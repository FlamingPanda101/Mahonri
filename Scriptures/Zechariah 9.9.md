# Zechariah 9:9

> Rejoice greatly, O daughter of Zion; shout, O daughter of Jerusalem: behold, thy King cometh unto thee: he is just, and having salvation; lowly, and riding upon an ass, and upon a colt the foal of an ass. *(KJV)*

## Significance
A messianic prophecy (fulfilled at Christ's triumphal entry). The episode suggests [[Zechariah son of Jehoiada]] may be the same figure — a prophet killed precisely because he preached the Messiah, fitting the pattern of suppressed messianic prophecy under [[Deuteronomistic Corruption of the Old Testament|Deuteronomism]].
