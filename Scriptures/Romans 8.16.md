# Romans 8:16

> The Spirit itself beareth witness with our spirit, that we are the children of God: and if children, then heirs; heirs of God, and joint-heirs with Christ. *(KJV, v. 16-17)*

## Significance
Paul's "the Spirit... beareth witness... that we are the children of God" — and "joint-heirs with Christ" — is, the Ward Radio panel argues, an unambiguous canonical affirmation of literal divine sonship and of human deification/inheritance, against the "God Loves Mormons" claim that we are not really God's children. They pair it with the "co-participants in his glory" and "thrones with him" language to ask why, if humans are not God's offspring, scripture promises them a joint inheritance.
