# Hebrews 9:11-12

> But when Christ came as a high priest... he entered once for all into the [heavenly] Holy Place, not with the blood of goats and calves, but with his own blood, thus obtaining eternal redemption. *(NRSV)*

**Context:** Hebrews presents Christ as both **priest and offering**, performing the final, perfect sacrifice "once for all." Drawn on by early Fathers for the **Christ the Victim** (sacrifice) atonement model, in which Christ's death is the effective sacrifice that satisfies God's justice — the seedbed for the later satisfaction theory (Anselm).
