# 2 Samuel

The second book of Samuel, covering David's reign as king, including the Davidic covenant, the Bathsheba affair, and Absalom's rebellion. [[Jacob Wright]] treats 2 Samuel's Davidic material as part of the "palace history" strand of the Bible — originating in Judah and edited into a final form that subordinates the monarchy to the older peoplehood narrative.
