# Daniel 5:25

> **25** And this is the writing that was written, MENE, MENE, TEKEL, UPHARSIN.

The mysterious "writing on the wall" at [[Belshazzar]]'s feast. The Aramaic terms (*mene* "numbered," *tekel* "weighed," *upharsin/peres* "divided") are interpreted by Daniel as God's judgment that Belshazzar's kingdom is finished. Cited in atheist "what would convince me" hypotheticals — a skeptic proposes that stars rearranging to spell these words would prove theism.
