# Deuteronomy 6:4

> "Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God is one LORD." (KJV)

The **Shema**: *Shema Yisrael, Adonai eloheinu, Adonai echad*. Key terms:
- **eloheinu** (אֱלֹהֵינוּ) — "**our** God," marking Yahweh as the deity that pertains to *us*, presupposing other gods.
- **echad** (אֶחָד) — "one"; on the exclusive-relationship reading this means "the only one for us," grammatically paralleled by *achat* ("she is one") in [[Song of Songs 6.8-9|Song of Songs 6:8–9]], where other women are explicitly acknowledged.
