# Isaiah 11:1 (KJV)

> And there shall come forth a rod out of the stem of Jesse, and a Branch shall grow out of his roots:

## Netzer / Nazarene Connection

The Hebrew word for "Branch" is _netzer_ (נֵצֶר). Some apologists claim this is the prophecy behind [[Matthew 2.23]]'s "He shall be called a Nazarene," since "Nazareth" sounds similar. [[Dan McClellan|McClellan]] characterizes this as "squinting at this pretty hard" — a strained connection to invent a prophecy that isn't actually there.[^1]

## Davidic Context

Jesse is David's father, so this is a Davidic prophecy. The branch imagery represents a future Davidic ruler. This is the only plausible link apologists can find for Matthew's otherwise unattested "Nazarene prophecy."[^1]
