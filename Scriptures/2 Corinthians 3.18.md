# 2 Corinthians 3:18

> And all of us, with unveiled faces, seeing the glory of the Lord as though reflected in a mirror, are being transformed into the same image from one degree of glory to another; for this comes from the Lord, the Spirit. *(NRSV)*

**Context:** Paul describes believers progressively transformed into the divine image "from one degree of glory to another" — a process, not a single event. Becerra lists it among the New Testament texts on which early Christian **deification** doctrine was built, and Gregory of Nyssa's image of the soul as a mirror reflecting God's light echoes its "mirror" language.
