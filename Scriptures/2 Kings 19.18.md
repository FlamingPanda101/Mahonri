# 2 Kings 19:18

> "And have cast their gods into the fire: for they were no gods, but the work of men's hands, wood and stone: therefore they have destroyed them." (KJV)

Part of Hezekiah's prayer (paralleled in Isaiah 37:19). The clause *lo elohim* ("not gods") is a classic statement of the **idol polemic**: the nations' gods that were burned were "the work of men's hands, wood and stone." In critical scholarship the destructibility is predicated of the manufactured *idol*, not of the deity the idol indexes.
