# Luke 15:3-7 (KJV) — The lost sheep

> What man of you, having an hundred sheep, if he lose one of them, doth not leave the ninety and nine... and go after that which is lost, until he find it?

## Key Detail

The scriptural root of the "gardening" (vs. "crop-dusting") retention ideal restored in the 2018 ministering program — leaving the ninety and nine for the one. Paired with central-order texts ([[1 Corinthians 12.12-27|1 Cor 12]]) so the wiser landing point is *both* individual hands-on care and church order, not one against the other.
