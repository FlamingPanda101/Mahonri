# Alma 36:22 — "Yea, methought I saw, even as our father Lehi saw"

> Yea, methought I saw, even as our father Lehi saw, God sitting upon his throne, surrounded with numberless concourses of angels, in the attitude of singing and praising their God; yea, and my soul did long to be there. *(Alma 36:22)*

## Key Detail
The exact center of Alma's chiasm in [[Alma 36]], this verse quotes [[1 Nephi 1.8|Lehi's throne-theophany]] almost verbatim ("numberless concourses of angels"). For [[Mike Day]] it is a breadcrumb linking the (later-dictated) small plates to the (earlier-dictated) middle of the record — and [[Hugh Nibley]]'s note that a "concourse" can mean a circle ties it to ancient circular worship around an altar.
