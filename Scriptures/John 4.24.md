# John 4:24

> God is a Spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth. *(KJV)*

## Used against the Church
"God *is* spirit — so the LDS teaching that God the Father has a body of flesh and bone is false."

## In context
Spoken to the **Samaritan woman at the well**, whose concern was *where* to worship (the Samaritans were barred from the Jerusalem temple). Jesus answers that God can be worshipped **anywhere, in spirit** — not that God is *only* immaterial. The Greek *pneuma* here is **qualitative, not possessive** — "God is spirit" reads like "God is love" / "God is truth," describing how he is worshipped, not denying that he also has a glorified body.
