# Moroni 4:3 — The sacrament prayer on the bread

> O God, the Eternal Father, we ask thee in the name of thy Son, Jesus Christ, to bless and sanctify this bread to the souls of all those who partake of it...

## Key Detail

David Butler argues the sacrament prayers of Moroni 4-5 — given with *no church context* in the text — may be **temple** texts, evidence that the ancient temple had a literal feast (Exodus 24's elders "ate and drank" with God; Psalm 23's "table"). He frames endowment and sacrament as "two halves" of one anciently-unified ordinance.
