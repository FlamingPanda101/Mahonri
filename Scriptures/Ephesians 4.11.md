# Ephesians 4:11-13

> And he gave some, apostles; and some, prophets; and some, evangelists; and some, pastors and teachers; for the perfecting of the saints, for the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ: till we all come in the unity of the faith, and of the knowledge of the Son of God, unto a perfect man, unto the measure of the stature of the fulness of Christ. *(KJV)*

## In context
A central Latter-day Saint proof-text for continuing apostles and prophets. [[Shiloh Lofgren]] argues that the offices are given **"till we all come in the unity of the faith"** — a goal manifestly not yet reached (the thousands of disagreeing denominations) — so there is no scriptural warrant for cessation: the apostles and prophets were to continue until a unity that has not arrived. Reformed respondents (e.g., [[Timothy J. Whitehead]]) reply that the foundational apostolic-prophetic office was completed in the apostolic age and the "unity" is an eschatological telos pursued through the *completed* Scripture, not an argument for ongoing canonical revelation.
