# Alma 32 — Faith as a seed; experiment upon the word

> But behold, if ye will awake and arouse your faculties, even to an experiment upon my words, and exercise a particle of faith... we will compare the word unto a seed. (vv. 27-28)

## Key Detail

Alma's seed is the vault's central proof-text for **evidence-based, experimental faith** (belief precedes knowledge but is tested by fruits) and for the "heart as soil" image: the word grows only in a heart that gives it "place." Cited across faith-crisis, retention, and Heavenly-Mother discussions as the LDS answer to "faith is blind."
