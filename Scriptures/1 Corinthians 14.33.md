# 1 Corinthians 14:33

> "For God is not the author of confusion, but of peace, as in all churches of the saints." (KJV)

## Use in the vault

Quoted by [[Ether's Elephant]] alongside [[James 1.13|James 1:13]] to argue that, on the Latter-day Saint view, God did not create evil or Satan's rebellious nature — "God is not the author of confusion." Used in [[Jesus and Satan Are Brothers Only If Nothing Is Created]] to ground the claim that the LDS co-eternal model keeps God innocent of evil's origin better than the creedal *creatio ex nihilo* model.
