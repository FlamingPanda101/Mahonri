# 1 Nephi 5:11

> And he beheld that they did contain the five books of Moses, which gave an account of the creation of the world, and also of Adam and Eve, who were our first parents. *(1830 / current text)*

## In context

When [[Lehi]] examines the [[brass plates]] [[Laban]] held, [[Nephi]] records that they contained "the five books of Moses." Latter-day Saint commentators on [[Ward Radio]] make this verse load-bearing: [[Jonah Barnes]] argues that *which* five books Lehi held is not specified, and proposes that the fifth book was not Deuteronomy (a product of the post-Lehi [[Josiah]]/Deuteronomist reform) but the **Assumption of Moses** — the dying prophet's last words. See [[The Assumption of Moses Was the Fifth Book of Moses Replaced by Deuteronomy]].
