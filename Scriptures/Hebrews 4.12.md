# Hebrews 4:12

> For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart. *(KJV)*

**Key Greek:** "word of God" is *logos tou theou*. Often memed as a description of the Bible, but the surrounding verses (4:13–14: "all are open and laid bare to the eyes of him… we have a great high priest… Jesus the Son of God") personalize the "word" as a living, judging agent — read by Trent Horn as God's commands or Christ himself, paralleling Rev 19:13, where the rider's name "is called the Word of God."
