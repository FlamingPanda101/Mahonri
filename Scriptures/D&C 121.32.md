# D&C 121:32

> "According to that which was ordained in the midst of the Council of the Eternal God of all other gods before this world was, that should be reserved unto the finishing and the end thereof, when every man shall enter into his eternal presence and into his immortal rest." *(LDS)*

The phrase "the **Council of the Eternal God of all other gods**" maps directly onto the Hebrew *Adat El* — the council of El — of [[Psalm 82.1|Psalm 82:1]]. [[Alex Arnold]] cites it (published 1835) as explicit LDS divine-council language predating the [[Ugaritic Texts Reveal Israels Original Pantheon|Ugaritic]] recovery of *Adat El*.
