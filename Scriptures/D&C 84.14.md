# D&C 84:14 — The priesthood through the lineage of the fathers, even Noah

> Which Abraham received the priesthood from Melchizedek, who received it through the lineage of his fathers, even till Noah. *(D&C 84:14)*

## Key Detail
This verse traces the Melchizedek Priesthood line: Abraham received it from **[[Melchizedek]]**, who received it "through the lineage of his fathers, even till **Noah**." In the [[Ward Radio - The Secret Identity of Melchizedek Revealed|Ward Radio episode]], the hosts treat the immediate jump from Melchizedek to Noah as their "smoking gun": if the priesthood descends Noah → his son → ... → Melchizedek by lineage, and **[[Shem]]** (Noah's son) is conspicuously the next priesthood-holder after Noah yet is otherwise silent in the record, then Shem and Melchizedek may be one and the same. The hosts note the phrasing is "fuzzy" — whether the priesthood comes *through* Noah or *after* him via Shem. See [[Shem Was Melchizedek]].
