# Mosiah 17:2 — Alma "was a young man"

> But there was one among them whose name was Alma, he also being a descendant of Nephi. And he was a young man, and he believed the words which Abinadi had spoken. *(Mosiah 17:2, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Term
- **Alma** — a Semitic name plausibly meaning "lad / young man," likely a hypocoristicon (shortened theophoric name) of something like "young man of God." The narrative detail "he was a young man" mirrors the name's meaning. The same consonants overlap with Hebrew *ʿlm* (*alam*), "to hide / conceal," foreshadowing Alma's hiding in the wilderness of Mormon.
