# Mosiah 4:11 — King Benjamin on our nothingness

> ...always retain in remembrance, the greatness of God, and your own nothingness, and his goodness and long-suffering towards you, unworthy creatures...

## Key Detail

King Benjamin's "nothingness" language is the Book of Mormon's steelman of human unworthiness in the **divine-childhood vs. total-depravity** debate. Paired with [[Moses 1.10]]: Latter-day Saints grant we are fallen and "lost without the great mediator," but unworthiness "has no bearing on whether he gives" grace — it is offered, not withheld.
