# Matthew 15:6

> And honour not his father or his mother, he shall be free. Thus have ye made the commandment of God of none effect by your tradition. *(KJV)*

**Textual note:** the KJV reflects manuscripts reading "the commandment of God" (*tēn entolēn tou theou*); other manuscripts read "the law of God" (*ton nomon*), and still others "the word of God" (*ton logon*). Trent Horn notes the "command/law of God" variants point the referent to Yahweh's **orally delivered commandment** (honor father and mother, Exodus 20 / Deuteronomy), not to the written text of Exodus describing it. The chapter does not contain the Greek word for "Scripture" (*graphē*).
