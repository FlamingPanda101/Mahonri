# Psalm 2:7 — "this day have I begotten thee"

> I will declare the decree: the LORD hath said unto me, Thou art my Son; this day have I begotten thee. *(Psalm 2:7, KJV)*

## Significance
A **royal coronation psalm**: at the enthronement of the Davidic king, God declares him "son" and "this day" begets him (an adoption/installation formula). King Benjamin quotes this in [[Mosiah 5.7-9|Mosiah 5:7-9]] ("this day he hath spiritually begotten you") but extends it to the whole covenant people — a biblical *control* confirming the enthronement framework of his speech.
