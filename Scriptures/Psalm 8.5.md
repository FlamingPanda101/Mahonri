# Psalm 8:5

> For thou hast made him a little lower than the angels, and hast crowned him with glory and honour. *(KJV)*

## Key Term: *elohim* ("angels" / "God")
The KJV "the angels" renders Hebrew *elohim*; some translations read "a little lower than God." [[Gavin Ortlund]] references this verse as an example where the biblical *elohim* can denote heavenly beings other than Yahweh, while invoking [[Michael Heiser]]'s point that the one God remains "species-unique" — God in a different, uncreated sense. The verse figures in the broader divine-council vs. creatio-ex-nihilo discussion.
