# 1 Corinthians 11:3

> But I would have you know, that the head of every man is Christ; and the head of the woman is the man; and the head of Christ is God. *(KJV)*

## Key point
Paul places **God as the "head" of Christ** in the same chain as Christ over man and man over woman — a relational hierarchy. The panel uses it to identify "the one who" of "one God" with the Father, while distinguishing **relational** subordination from **ontological** equality (the Son shares the divine nature but is, *as Son*, dependent on the Father). See [[Ontological Equality With Relational Subordination]], [[Subordination Within the New Testament]].
