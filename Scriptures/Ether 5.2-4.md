# Ether 5.2-4

> "And unto three shall they be shown by the power of God; wherefore they shall know of a surety that these things are true. And in the mouth of three witnesses shall these things be established; and the testimony of three, and this work... shall stand as a testimony against the world at the last day." — Ether 5:3-4

The Book of Mormon's own prophecy of the **Three Witnesses**, invoked as the scriptural mandate that they would see the plates "by the power of God" and testify. Underwrites the claim that the witnesses were divinely foretold rather than convened to deceive.
