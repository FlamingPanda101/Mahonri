# Daniel 7:13 — The Son of Man on the Clouds

> I saw in the night visions, and, behold, one like the Son of man came with the clouds of heaven, and came to the Ancient of days, and they brought him near before him. And there was given him dominion, and glory, and a kingdom, that all people, nations, and languages, should serve him. *(Daniel 7:13-14, KJV)*

## Key Detail
- The "Son of Man" who rides "the clouds of heaven" and receives universal dominion. [[Michael Jones]] notes that in the Old Testament the cloud-rider is **Yahweh**, so Jesus's trial claim to be this figure (Mark 14:62) is a claim to divine status — evidence that Mark's Christology is "extremely high," not a late development.
- Latter-day Saint apologetics also reads the Enochic expansion of this Son of Man as a preexistent divine Messiah matching LDS (non-Nicene) Christology. See [[The Enochic Son of Man Matches LDS Christology Not Nicene Homoousios]].
