# Matthew 22:30

> For in the resurrection they neither marry, nor are given in marriage, but are as the angels of God in heaven. *(KJV)*

## Used against the Church
"There is no marriage after the resurrection — so LDS temple sealing of spouses/families for eternity is unbiblical."

## In context
The verse addresses the act of **marrying** *in the resurrection itself* — not the persistence of marriages **sealed before** it. In LDS theology there is a long interval between death and resurrection (where proxy ordinances and acceptance occur); a marriage sealed and kept before the resurrection continues, while no new marrying happens *in* the resurrection. See [[Matthew 22-30 - No Marriage in Resurrection Rebutted]].
