# Romans 5:12

> Wherefore, as by one man sin entered into the world, and death by sin; and so death passed upon all men, for that all have sinned. *(KJV)*

## Significance
The proof text for the "appeal to the fall" defense of hell — sin and death entered through Adam. The video argues this fails to explain why a loving God would keep creating fallen, damnation-worthy souls from nothing. See [[Hell - Appeal to the Fall Rebutted]].
