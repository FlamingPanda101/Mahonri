# Moroni 10:26

> "And wo unto them who shall do these things away and die, for they die in their sins, and they cannot be saved in the kingdom of God; and I speak it according to the words of Christ; and I lie not." (current edition)

## Use in the vault

Cited by [[Ether's Elephant]] as a clear example of the most common kind of edit between the 1830 and modern editions of the Book of Mormon: a grammatical "what → who" change for more proper English, which leaves the meaning intact. Used in [[Edit-Pattern Stylometry as Evidence of Multiple Ancient Authors]] to illustrate that the bulk of the ~thousands-of-changes critique concerns bland error-correction, not substantive doctrinal shifts.
