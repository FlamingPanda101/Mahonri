# Mosiah 1–6 — King Benjamin's speech

> And it came to pass that king Benjamin... began to declare unto his people the things which the Lord had commanded him. *(Mosiah 1, summary)*

## Key Detail
King Benjamin's farewell address (Mosiah 1–6) is, on [[Mike Day]]'s reading, structured as an ancient **vassal/covenant treaty** — preamble (he is their king), antecedent history (he has not taxed or oppressed them), terms of the covenant (the people become "sons and daughters of Christ," [[Mosiah 5.7|Mosiah 5]]), formal witnesses, blessings and cursings, and the depositing/recital of the covenant. Day argues the same pattern underlies Deuteronomy and the 1842 temple endowment.
