# 1 Nephi 16:34 — Ishmael buried at Nahom

> And it came to pass that Ishmael died, and was buried in the place which was called Nahom. *(1 Nephi 16:34, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Term
- **Nahom** — corresponds to the consonantal **NHM** (Nihm / Nehem), a South Arabian tribe and territory attested epigraphically back to the 7th century BC. Because South Arabian writes no vowels, NHM is transliterated variously; "Nahom" fits. It sits at the Wadi Jawf area where ancient caravan trails turn eastward — precisely where the text says the party turned "nearly eastward" after the burial (1 Nephi 17:1). A looted funerary stela bearing **Yasmaʿil** (= Ishmael), dated ~6th century BC, comes from this region.
