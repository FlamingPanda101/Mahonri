# Colossians 2.9

> For in him dwelleth all the fulness of the Godhead bodily. (Colossians 2:9, KJV)

**Key Greek terms.** *theotēs* (θεότης) — KJV "Godhead"; the source argues the verse's force is "the fulness of all divine glory shared." *plērōma* (πλήρωμα) = "fulness," tied to the *exchange formula*: the fullness dwells in Christ, and through Christ believers "have been brought to fulness" (v.10). Christ is thereby "head over every power and authority."
