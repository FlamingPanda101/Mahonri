# Deuteronomy 1:1 — "on the other side Jordan"

> These be the words which Moses spake unto all Israel on this side Jordan in the wilderness... *(Deuteronomy 1:1, KJV)*

## Key Detail
The book's title in Hebrew is *Devarim* ("the words"). [[Mike Day]] (following [[Moshe Weinfeld]]) notes that the Hebrew of the opening literally locates Moses' words "on the **other** side of the Jordan" — a standpoint Moses (who never crossed into the land) could not occupy, betraying a later author writing from inside Israel. The verse is offered as a tell of the book's post-Mosaic, Deuteronomist composition.
