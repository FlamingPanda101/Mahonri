# Mark 2:28 — "Lord even of the Sabbath"

> Therefore the Son of man is Lord also of the sabbath. *(Mark 2:28, KJV)*

## Key Detail
- [[Michael Jones]] cites this as evidence of a **high Christology already in Mark**: the Lord of the Sabbath is the one who *gave* the Sabbath — Yahweh — so to claim to be "Lord of the Sabbath" is "to claim to be on par with the Father." Used against the claim that Jesus's divinity is a *late* development reaching full form only in John.
- Pairs with Mark 1's application of Yahweh's herald to Jesus and the [[Daniel 7.13|Daniel 7]] Son-of-Man claim at the trial (Mark 14:62).
