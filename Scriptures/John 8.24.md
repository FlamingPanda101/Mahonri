# John 8:24

> I said therefore unto you, that ye shall die in your sins: for if ye believe not that I am he, ye shall die in your sins. *(KJV)*

## Key Term: *ego eimi* ("I am")
The Greek is *ego eimi* — literally "I am." The KJV supplies "he" ("I am he"), but the absolute phrase echoes the divine self-identification of [[Exodus 3.14|Exodus 3:14]] and the *ani hu* ("I am he") of Deutero-Isaiah, both rendered *ego eimi* in the Septuagint. Read with [[John 8.58|John 8:58]] ("before Abraham was, I am"), the saying is widely taken as a coded participation in the divine name. Whether this entails ontological identity with Yahweh or *authorized agency of* the divine name is the disputed point.
