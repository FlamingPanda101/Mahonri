# Romans 10:6-9

> 6 But the righteousness which is of faith speaketh on this wise, Say not in thine heart, Who shall ascend into heaven? (that is, to bring Christ down from above:) 7 Or, Who shall descend into the deep? (that is, to bring up Christ again from the dead.) 8 But what saith it? The word is nigh thee, even in thy mouth, and in thy heart: that is, the word of faith, which we preach; 9 That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved. *(KJV)*

## In context

[[Paul the Apostle|Paul]] here reworks [[Deuteronomy 30.6,11-14|Deuteronomy 30]]: the *dabar* placed "in thy heart" is identified with Christ himself. [[Blake Ostler]] cites this to show that, scripturally, "the very basis of this religious knowledge is something that's been implanted into us by God" — the heart is the organ of saving knowledge, where one believes "unto righteousness."
