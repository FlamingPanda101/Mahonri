# Acts 17:28-29

> For in him we live, and move, and have our being; as certain also of your own poets have said, For we are also his offspring. Forasmuch then as we are the offspring of God, we ought not to think that the Godhead is like unto gold, or silver, or stone, graven by art and man's device. *(KJV)*

## Key Term: "offspring" (*genos*)
Paul (quoting the poet Aratus) calls humans the **offspring** of God; the Greek *genos* means kind/kindred/species. The panel reads this as scriptural support that humans are the *same species* as God — and notes the very next words use the word **"Godhead"** (Greek *to theion*), a biblical term, in contrast to the unbiblical word "Trinity."
