# John 10:34-36

> Jesus answered, "Is it not written in your law 'I said, you are gods'? If those to whom the word of God came were called 'gods' and the scripture cannot be annulled — can you say that the one whom the Father has sanctified and sent into the world is blaspheming because I said, 'I am God's Son'?" *(NRSV)*

**Context:** Jesus cites [[Psalm 82.6|Psalm 82:6]] ("ye are gods") to defend his claim of divine sonship. Becerra lists this among the core scriptures on which early Christians built their teaching of **deification**: if those who received the word of God were called "gods," the category of human godhood is scripturally grounded.
