# Numbers 6

The Nazirite vow: a temporary vow of separation to the LORD involving abstention from wine, uncut hair, and avoidance of corpses. Apologists attempt to connect "Nazarene" to "Nazirite" to find a Hebrew Bible source for Matthew 2:23's prophecy — but the concepts are unrelated.
