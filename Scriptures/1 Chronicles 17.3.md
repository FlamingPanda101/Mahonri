# 1 Chronicles 17:3

> And it came to pass the same night, that the word of God came to Nathan, saying, *(KJV)*

**Key term:** "the word of God came to Nathan" (cf. Hebrew *dəbar elohim/YHWH*). A classic Old Testament use of "word of God" as a **prophetic message delivered to a prophet** — a speech-act, not a document. Trent Horn cites it (alongside Shemaiah, 1 Kings 12:22; and John the Baptist, Luke 3:2) to show the OT pattern.
