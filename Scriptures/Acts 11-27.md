# Acts 11:27

> "And in these days came prophets from Jerusalem unto Antioch."

## Apologetic Significance
Explicitly names **multiple prophets** operating in the church AFTER Christ's
ascension. Directly refutes the claim that Hebrews 1:1-2 meant prophecy ceased
with Jesus. These prophets come FROM the Jerusalem church — they are recognized,
authoritative figures in the apostolic community.
