# 1 Samuel 9:27

> And as they were going down to the end of the city, Samuel said to Saul, Bid the servant pass on before us, (and he passed on,) but stand thou still a while, that I may shew thee the word of God. *(KJV)*

**Key term:** Samuel promises to "shew thee the word of God," then (9:25–10:1) **anoints Saul with oil** — he does not read from a text. Trent Horn highlights this as a vivid case where "word of God" is a delivered divine message/action, the opposite of a written document.
