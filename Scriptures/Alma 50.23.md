# Alma 50:23 — "there never was a happier time"

> But behold there never was a happier time among the people of Nephi, since the days of Nephi, than in the days of Moroni, yea, even at this time, in the twenty and first year of the reign of the judges. *(Alma 50:23)*

## Key Detail
Set amid the war chapters, this verse echoes [[2 Nephi 5.27|2 Nephi 5:27]] ("we lived after the manner of happiness") — an earlier event recorded on the *small* plates that, in the [[The Book of Mormon Was Translated Mosiah-First|Mosiah-first translation order]], had not yet been dictated. [[Mike Day]] uses the parallel both as an internal cross-reference and as a devotional point: one can "live after the manner of happiness" in a time of turmoil.
