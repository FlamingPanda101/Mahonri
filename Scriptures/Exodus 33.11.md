# Exodus 33:11 (KJV) — The LORD spake unto Moses face to face

> And the LORD spake unto Moses face to face, as a man speaketh unto his friend.

## Key Detail

A key text for the **anthropomorphic God of the Hebrew Bible**: God is depicted speaking with Moses face to face, as a friend — read alongside [[Genesis 32.30|Genesis 32:30]] against the later abstract/incorporeal deity. (The tension with [[Exodus 33.20-23|Exodus 33:20-23]], "thou canst not see my face," is itself part of the discussion.) Supports the LDS embodied-God doctrine.
