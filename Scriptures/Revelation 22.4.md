# Revelation 22:4 (KJV)

> And they shall see his face; and his name shall be in their foreheads.

## Significance
[[Margaret Barker]] treats this as the climactic reversal of what she calls the "age of wrath" — the period (begun, per *1 Enoch* 93, when Isaiah ascends and the people in the temple "forsake wisdom" and lose their spiritual sight) in which the Lord hides his face. On the last page of the Bible the servants of the Lord are back in the holy of holies of the new temple and "they shall see his face," with his name on their foreheads — recovering the first-temple promise of the beatific vision and the mark of the Lord that the Deuteronomists removed.
