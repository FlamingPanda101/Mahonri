# Alma 24:7 (Book of Mormon)

> "I thank my God, my beloved people, that our great God has in goodness sent these our brethren, the Nephites, unto us to preach unto us, and to convince us of the traditions of our wicked fathers."

Opening of King **Anti-Nephi-Lehi**'s thanksgiving (verses 7-11), which lists five things he is grateful for and frames the Nephites as "brethren" who came to convert the Lamanites away from "the traditions of our wicked fathers." In the Ward Radio episode this verse anchors both the five-fold thanksgiving (part of [[The Five Motif in Alma 24|the five motif]]) and a joke that Mesoamerican-model believers can "repent" of their "wicked tradition."
