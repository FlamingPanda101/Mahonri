# Acts 20:29–30 — Grievous Wolves Shall Enter In

> "For I know this, that after my departing shall grievous wolves enter in among you, not sparing the flock. Also of your own selves shall men arise, speaking perverse things, to draw away disciples after them." (KJV)

Paul's farewell to the Ephesian elders, prophesying internal corruption after his departure. Day lists it among the New Testament's own apostasy warnings (with [[1 Corinthians 11.18-19|1 Cor 11]], [[Galatians 1.8|Galatians]], [[2 Thessalonians 2.3|2 Thessalonians]], the Pastorals, and the Johannine epistles). See [[Apostasy as Gradual Falling Away Not Single Event]].
