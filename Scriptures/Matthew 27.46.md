# Matthew 27:46

> "And about the ninth hour Jesus cried with a loud voice, saying, Eli, Eli, lama sabachthani? that is to say, My God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me?" (KJV)

On the cross Jesus addresses the Father as **"my God"** (Aramaic *Eli*, transliterating Hebrew *Eli* / *Elohi*). Quoting Psalm 22:1. Used to argue that Jesus *has* a God distinct from himself — incoherent if he is "the same being" as God.
