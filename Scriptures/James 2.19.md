# James 2:19

> Thou believest that there is one God; thou doest well: the devils also believe, and tremble. *(KJV)*

## Key point
A standard "one God" proof-text. The panel's reply: yes, **one God** — meaning one *what* (the Godhead). The word "God" is not univocal: it can name any one of the three persons or the one Godhead. So a verse affirming "one God" is fully compatible with the LDS view and does not establish that the three persons are one *being*. See [[Three Whos and One What Framework]], [[Latter-day Saints Are Not Tritheists]].
