# Genesis 50:33 (JST)

There is **no Genesis 50:33 in the King James / Masoretic Bible** — Genesis 50 ends at verse 26. The reference belongs to [[Joseph Smith]]'s **Joseph Smith Translation (JST)** expansion of Genesis 50, which adds a prophecy attributed to [[Joseph of Egypt]] foretelling a latter-day seer named Joseph. A parallel prophecy appears in [[2 Nephi 3]].

## Significance
Critics (popularized by Mike Winger) cite "the missing Genesis 50:33" as a self-certifying-prophet gotcha: Joseph Smith allegedly invented a verse predicting himself. Ward Radio's reply: the verse is part of the JST — a commentary-like expansion never published in Joseph's lifetime, never canonized, and **never used by Joseph or his contemporaries to prove his prophetic calling**. You cannot be "self-certifying" with a text you never deployed as certification. See [[The Self-Certifying Prophet Charge Misreads the JST]].
