# Alma 53:10-22 — reciprocal compassion and the sons of the Anti-Nephi-Lehies

> ...the people of Ammon ... were moved with compassion ... *(Alma 53, Book of Mormon)*

## Significance
The converted Lamanites (people of Ammon), settled in [[Jershon Means Place of Inheritance|Jershon]], are "moved with **compassion**" for the Nephites and send their sons (the stripling warriors) into battle on the Nephites' behalf. This forms a **reciprocal** of the compassion the people of Zarahemla earlier showed them, completing an inverted wordplay on the *-hemla* ("compassion/mercy") element of **Zarahemla**. The expression occurs in only these two contexts.
