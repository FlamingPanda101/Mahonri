# Psalm 82:6

> I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are children of the most High. *(KJV)*

## Jesus's Use in John 10
Jesus quotes this verse in response to the accusation of blasphemy in [[John 10.33|John 10:33]]. The logic: "If scripture calls humans 'gods' and 'sons of the Most High,' then my claiming to be the Son of God is not blasphemous." This only works if the accusation is about claiming *divine status*, not about claiming to be THE God of Israel.

## Divine Plurality in the Hebrew Bible
Psalm 82 depicts a divine council where God presides over other divine beings (*elohim*). This is part of the broader [[Divine Name Theology|divine council]] / divine plurality tradition in the Hebrew Bible, distinct from later philosophical monotheism.
