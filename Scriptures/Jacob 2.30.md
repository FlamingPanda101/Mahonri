# Jacob 2:30

> For if I will, saith the Lord of Hosts, raise up seed unto me, I will command my people; otherwise they shall hearken unto these things. *(current text)*

## In context

In a chapter otherwise condemning unauthorized polygamy among the Nephites, the Lord reserves the right to "raise up seed" by commanding plural marriage. Latter-day Saints on [[Ward Radio]] pair this with [[Doctrine and Covenants 132.63|D&C 132:63]] as the openly stated rationale for 19th-century plural marriage — pre-empting the critic's claim that the reason was hidden. [[Stephen Cranney]] argues the fertility data is consistent with that stated purpose. See [[Polygamy Raised Fertility by Raising the Marriage Rate]].
