# 1 Thessalonians 5:22

> Abstain from all appearance of evil. *(KJV)*

**Key Greek:** the word the KJV renders "appearance" is *eidos* (εἶδος). It can mean "the form / outward appearance" of a thing, but it also commonly means the **kind, type, or variety** of something. Scholars today almost unanimously read the latter sense here, so modern translations render the verse "abstain from every **kind** of evil" (cf. ESV, NRSVUE, NIV) — a command to avoid evil in all its varieties, not a command to avoid morally neutral acts that merely *look* bad to onlookers.[^style]

[^style]: On *eidos* meaning "kind/type" rather than "outward appearance," see the concept note below.
