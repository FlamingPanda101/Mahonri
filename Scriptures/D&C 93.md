# D&C 93

Doctrine and Covenants Section 93 — an 1833 revelation to [[Joseph Smith]] that
taught spirits existed "in the beginning with God." This was an early hint at the
co-eternal nature of spirits that would be developed more fully in the [[King Follett Discourse]].[^1]

## Used in

- [[God Was Once a Man]] — cited as an early revelation laying groundwork for the
  later teaching that spirits are co-eternal with God
- [[Spirit Birth and Intelligences]] — part of the pre-Nauvoo theological development
  that preceded the King Follett Discourse
