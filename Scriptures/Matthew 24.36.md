# Matthew 24:36

> "But of that day and hour knoweth no man, no, not the angels of heaven, but my Father only." (KJV)

Jesus declares that not even the Son knows the day or hour of the end (parallel [[Mark 13.32|Mark 13:32]] adds "neither the Son"). The Greek **οὐδὲ ὁ υἱός** (*oude ho huios*, "not even the Son") makes the limitation explicit in the parallel. Used in LDS argument to show Jesus' genuinely limited knowledge — incompatible with the classical-theist claim that divinity entails omniscience held in "one mind."
