# Genesis 15:6

> And he believed in the LORD; and he counted it to him for righteousness. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Paul's proof text for justification by faith (Romans 4). James 2 cites it too, saying it was **fulfilled** when Abraham later offered Isaac ([[Genesis 22]]) — evidence (per Trent Horn) that Abraham's faith was *completed by works* over a life of obedience, not merely demonstrated.
