# 2 Nephi 32:5-6

**Reference:** 2 Nephi 32:5-6 (Book of Mormon)

## Text
> "For behold, again I say unto you that if ye will enter in by the way, and receive the Holy Ghost, it will show unto you all things what ye should do... behold, this is the doctrine of Christ, and there will be no more doctrine given until after he shall manifest himself unto you in the flesh. And when he shall manifest himself unto you in the flesh, the things which he shall say unto you shall ye observe to do." (2 Nephi 32:5-6)

## Significance
The climax of Nephi's two-chapter discourse on the "doctrine of Christ." Read by [[Dave Butler]] as the endpoint of a **temple ascent**: having entered the gate (baptism), spoken with the tongue of angels, and pressed forward, the initiate "knocks" and enters into the divine presence to be manifested unto by Christ "in the flesh." Nephi then says the Spirit stops his utterance and he can say no more — which Butler reads as the deliberate veiling of temple content.
