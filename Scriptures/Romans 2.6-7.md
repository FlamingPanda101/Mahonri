# Romans 2:6-7

> Who will render to every man according to his deeds: To them who by patient continuance in well doing seek for glory and honour and immortality, eternal life. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Paul, in the very letter most cited for *sola fide*, says God "will **render to every man according to his deeds**" and connects "patient continuance in well doing" with "eternal life" — a chapter *before* the [[Romans 3.28|Romans 3:28]] faith-alone prooftext. [[Alex Arnold]] uses this to argue that reading Paul whole yields a coherent picture: a person is not justified by *performing the law of Moses* as if it earned righteousness, but a saved person lives a life of active faithfulness. The two statements are not in conflict. See [[Paul Read Whole Rejects the Law of Moses Not Good Works]].
