# 1 John 4:1

> **1** Beloved, believe not every spirit, but try the spirits whether they are of God: because many false prophets are gone out into the world.

The Johannine command to **test the spirits**. Cited both ways in the personal-revelation debate: evangelicals use it to argue that spiritual *feelings* alone cannot certify truth, while Latter-day Saints note it presupposes spiritual experience is real and evaluable, not to be discarded. Greek *dokimazete* ("test, prove, examine") implies an active discernment process.
