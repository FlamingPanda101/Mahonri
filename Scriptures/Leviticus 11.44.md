# Leviticus 11:44 (KJV)

> "For I am the LORD your God: ye shall therefore sanctify yourselves, and ye shall be holy; for I am holy..."

**Note:** A foundational *imitatio Dei* ("imitation of God," *imitatio Dei*) text — Israel is to be holy *because* God is holy. The "be X as God is X" pattern (cf. [[Leviticus 19.2|Leviticus 19:2]]) is the Torah template behind [[Matthew 5.48|Matthew 5:48]] ("be perfect, as your Father is perfect") and [[Luke 6.36|Luke 6:36]] ("be merciful, as your Father is merciful").
