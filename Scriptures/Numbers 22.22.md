# Numbers 22:22 (KJV / Hebrew note)

> "And God's anger was kindled because he went: and the angel of the LORD stood in the way for an adversary against him. Now he was riding upon his ass, and his two servants were with him."

**Key Hebrew term:** the phrase rendered "for an adversary" is *lə-satan* (לְשָׂטָן) — the angel of the LORD stands as "a satan" against Balaam. A primary datum that *satan* in the Hebrew Bible is a **generic noun** ("adversary/opponent"), here applied to a *divine* figure who works for God — not a proper name for a cosmic enemy.
