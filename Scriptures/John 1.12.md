# John 1:12

> But as many as received him, to them gave he power to become the sons of God, even to them that believe on his name: *(KJV)*

## The Greek
*Edōken autois exousian tekna theou genesthai* — "he gave them authority/power to **become** children of God." The verb *genesthai* ("to become") is what draws attention: believers are given the capacity to *become* children of God.

## Significance
Dan McClellan raises this verse in connection with **theosis / divinization / deification** as a theme in Johannine theology — humans given "power to become the children of God." It dovetails with Jesus' use of [[Psalm 82.6|Psalm 82:6]] ("ye are gods... children of the most High") in John 10.
