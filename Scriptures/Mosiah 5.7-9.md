# Mosiah 5:7-9 — sons and daughters of Christ, the name on the right hand

> And now, because of the covenant which ye have made ye shall be called the children of Christ, his sons, and his daughters; for behold, this day he hath spiritually begotten you; for ye say that your hearts are changed through faith on his name; therefore, ye are born of him and have become his sons and his daughters. ... And ... whosoever doeth this shall be found at the right hand of God, for he shall know the name by which he is called; for he shall be called by the name of Christ. *(Mosiah 5:7-9, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Connections
- **"this day he hath spiritually begotten you"** quotes the coronation liturgy of **Psalm 2:7** ("Thou art my Son; this day have I begotten thee"). This gives a biblical *control* confirming a deliberate allusion.
- **"found at the right hand of God"** echoes the enthronement language of **Psalm 110:1**.
- King Benjamin (Hebrew **Benjamin** = "son of the **right hand**") delivers this during the enthronement of his son Mosiah, but **democratizes** the royal name and station: every covenanter becomes a son/daughter at God's right hand, bearing the name of Christ (Messiah, "anointed one").
