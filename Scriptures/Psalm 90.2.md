# Psalm 90:2

> Before the mountains were brought forth, or ever thou hadst formed the earth and the world, even from everlasting to everlasting, thou art God. *(KJV)*

## Key Term: "from everlasting to everlasting" (*me'olam ad olam*)
Cited by [[Gavin Ortlund]] for the eternity and aseity of God in classical theism — God exists "before" the world is formed and "from everlasting to everlasting." Used to support creatio ex nihilo and the Creator/creature distinction (God alone is uncreated and necessary). Latter-day Saints affirm God's eternality too but read it within an eternal cosmos of pre-existing intelligences and matter rather than aseity over a created-from-nothing universe.
