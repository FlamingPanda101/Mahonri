# Ezekiel 18:20 (KJV) — The soul that sinneth, it shall die

> The soul that sinneth, it shall die. The son shall not bear the iniquity of the father, neither shall the father bear the iniquity of the son.

## Key Detail

The prophetic principle (with Deuteronomy 24:16) that guilt is *not* inherited — the biblical content of the LDS Second Article of Faith. Deployed against federal-headship / original-guilt: you may inherit a disease (mortality), but punishment is reserved for one's own acts, so "the contract you never signed" (Adam's sin imputed) is unjust.
