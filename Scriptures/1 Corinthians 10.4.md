# 1 Corinthians 10:4 — That Rock Was Christ

> **4** And did all drink the same spiritual drink: for they drank of that spiritual Rock that followed them: and that Rock was Christ. (1 Corinthians 10:4)

Paul's identification of the wilderness rock — the source of the water in [[Exodus 17.6|Exodus 17:6]] — as Christ himself. In the [[Salty Saints - Why Water Is Better Than Wine in the Sacrament|Salty Saints sacrament episode]] this underwrites the panel's "triple analogy" reading: the water that saved Israel from thirst was Christ, so the sacramental water points to Christ at least as directly as the sacramental wine points to his blood, strengthening the case that **water is a better symbol of Christ than wine**.[^1] See [[Water Is a Better Symbol of Christ Than Wine]].
