# Matthew 1:16

> And Jacob begat Joseph the husband of Mary, of whom was born Jesus, who is called Christ. *(KJV)*

**Key term:** "Jacob begat Joseph" — Matthew's genealogy names **Jacob** as the father of Joseph (Mary's husband). Compare [[Luke 3.23|Luke 3:23]], which names **Heli** as Joseph's father. The two named fathers are the crux of a frequently raised Bible-contradiction challenge.
