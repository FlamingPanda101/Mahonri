# 2 Thessalonians 2:3 — "A Falling Away First"

> "Let no man deceive you by any means: for that day shall not come, except there come a falling away first, and that man of sin be revealed, the son of perdition." (KJV)

**Key Greek:** *hē apostasia* (ἡ ἀποστασία), "the falling away / the apostasy" — from *apo* ("away") + *histēmi* ("to stand"); literally "a standing away" or "departure." The same root underlies "apostasy." The accent falls on the *-sia*: *apostasía*. See [[Apostasy as Gradual Falling Away Not Single Event]].

The most-cited proof text in LDS missionary discussions and apologetics that an apostasy must precede the Second Coming. Paul writes to correct Thessalonians who thought the Day of the Lord had already come.
