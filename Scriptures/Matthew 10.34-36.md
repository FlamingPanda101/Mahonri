# Matthew 10:34-36

> Think not that I am come to send peace on earth: I came not to send peace, but a sword. For I am come to set a man at variance against his father, and the daughter against her mother, and the daughter in law against her mother in law. And a man's foes shall be they of his own household. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Cited by [[Lorne Dawson]] to note that being drawn toward what one sees as truth can set a person in tension with their own family — the same family-rupture dynamic critics misread as evidence of "brainwashing." Christ frames such division as a natural consequence of conviction, not coercion.
