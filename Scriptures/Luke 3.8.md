# Luke 3:8

> Bring forth therefore fruits worthy of repentance, and begin not to say within yourselves, We have Abraham to our father: for I say unto you, That God is able of these stones to raise up children unto Abraham. *(KJV)*

## Context: Fruit of repentance
The Lukan parallel to [[Matthew 3.9|Matthew 3:9]]. [[Jacob Hansen]] quotes a conflated form of this passage ("the axe lies ready at the root of the trees, and every tree that does not produce good fruit will be cut down") to support the claim that Jesus and John prioritized *fruits* and a "lived walk with God" over correct theological formulation. See the rebuttal in which [[Gavin Ortlund]] argues fruit and the confession of Jesus' identity are organically connected.
