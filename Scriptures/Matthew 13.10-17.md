# Matthew 13:10-17 (KJV) — Why Jesus speaks in parables

> Because it is given unto you to know the mysteries of the kingdom of heaven, but to them it is not given... Therefore speak I to them in parables: because they seeing see not. (vv. 11, 13)

## Key Detail

Jesus quotes Isaiah 6:9-10 ("hear... but understand not") to explain teaching the crowd in parables while initiated disciples "know the mysteries." David Butler uses this to ground the "written in code" temple reading of scripture — the text encodes mysteries for the initiated, paralleling Isaiah's coded commission.
