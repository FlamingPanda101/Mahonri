# Alma 24:22 (Book of Mormon)

> "Now when the people saw that they were coming against them they went out to meet them, and prostrated themselves before them to the earth, and began to call on the name of the Lord; and thus they were in this attitude when the Lamanites began to fall upon them, and began to slay them with the sword. And thus without meeting any resistance, they did slay a thousand and five of them; and we know that they are blessed, for they have gone to dwell with their God."

The aftermath of the bury-the-weapons covenant: the pacifist Anti-Nephi-Lehies let themselves be killed without resistance. The casualty figure **"a thousand and five" (1,005)** is highlighted in the episode as a "weird number" that feeds the [[The Five Motif in Alma 24|five motif]].

## Used in
- [[The Five Motif in Alma 24]] — the odd "1,005" slain as another instance of "five."
- [[Hiawatha Belt Parallel - Buried Weapons Under a Tree]] — the buried-weapons / non-resistance covenant compared to the Iroquois peace tradition.
