# Leviticus 19:2 (KJV)

> "Speak unto all the congregation of the children of Israel, and say unto them, Ye shall be holy: for I the LORD your God am holy."

**Note:** The header verse of the Holiness Code (Leviticus 19), which also contains "love thy neighbour as thyself" ([[Leviticus 19.18|Leviticus 19:18]]). Together with [[Leviticus 11.44|Leviticus 11:44]] it supplies the *imitatio Dei* ("be holy as God is holy") pattern that [[Matthew 5.48|Matthew 5:48]] reworks as "be perfect as your Father is perfect."

## Used in
- [[Be Perfect Defined Elsewhere as Caring for the Poor]] — cited by Trevan Hatch as the Torah background for Jesus's perfection command.
