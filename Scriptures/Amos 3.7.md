# Amos 3:7

> Surely the Lord GOD will do nothing, but he revealeth his secret unto his servants the prophets. *(KJV)*

## Significance
A standing principle that God works **through prophets** — invoked to argue God's pattern of prophetic revelation did not cease with Christ. Supports the case in [[Hebrews 1-1-2 - Jesus the Last Prophet Rebutted]].
