# 2 Nephi 2:25 — "men are, that they might have joy"

> Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy. *(2 Nephi 2:25, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Detail
- Lehi's summary of the purpose of the Fall and of mortal existence: **joy**, not innocence, is the goal. [[David Paulsen]] pairs it with [[2 Nephi 2.11]] to argue that even God cannot bring about this joy without righteousness, righteousness without freedom, or freedom without opposition — so moral evil is an inevitable by-product of a world built for joy and soul-making.
