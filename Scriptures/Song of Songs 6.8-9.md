# Song of Songs 6:8-9

> "There are threescore queens, and fourscore concubines, and virgins without number. My dove, my undefiled is but one; she is the only one of her mother, she that bare her." (KJV)

The beloved is *achat hi* — "she is **one**" — *amid* sixty queens, eighty concubines, and maidens beyond number. The grammar (the feminine of *echad*) is the closest analog to *echad* in the Shema ([[Deuteronomy 6.4|Deut 6:4]]): "one" plainly means "the only one *for me*," an exclusive relationship, not "the only one who exists."
