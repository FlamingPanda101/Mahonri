# 2 Corinthians 4:17

> "For our light affliction, which is but for a moment, worketh for us a far more exceeding and eternal weight of glory." (KJV)

A key soul-making proof-text. In the Freethinking Ministries discussion, the Molinist host leans on this verse to argue that the present world's "pain, evil, and suffering" actually *prepares* free agents for "an eternal weight of glory" — the suffering is a blessing that fits us "to live as free people... into the infinite future." Used to support a soul-making theodicy against [[The Creation Dilemma]]. See [[Soul-Making Theodicy - Hick and Joseph Smith]].
