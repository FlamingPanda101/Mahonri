# Mosiah 3:8

> And he shall be called Jesus Christ, the Son of God, the Father of heaven and earth, the Creator of all things from the beginning; and his mother shall be called Mary. *(Book of Mormon)*

## Key point
Spoken by King Benjamin a few chapters before Abinadi's sermon, this already calls Christ **"the Father of heaven and earth"** by virtue of creation — laying the groundwork for the dual "Father and Son" titles in [[Mosiah 15.1-5|Mosiah 15]]. The panel uses it to show that "Father," applied to Christ, is a *creator/role* title, not a claim that the Son is the person of the Father. See [[Mosiah 15 Father and Son Explained]].
