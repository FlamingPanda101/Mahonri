# Isaiah 40:17

> "All nations before him are as nothing; and they are counted to him less than nothing, and vanity." (KJV)

A key control text for reading Deutero-Isaiah's negation rhetoric. The **nations** — which manifestly exist — are called "nothing" (*ayin*), "less than nothing" (*efes*), and "vanity" (*tohu*). Since the same three words are applied to idols (Isa 41:24), the rhetoric expresses incomparability and irrelevance before Yahweh, not ontological non-existence.
