# 1 Nephi 1:8

> "And being thus overcome with the Spirit, he was carried away in a vision, even that he saw the heavens open, and he thought he saw God sitting upon his throne, surrounded with numberless concourses of angels in the attitude of singing and praising their God." *(LDS)*

Lehi's inaugural throne-room vision places God enthroned amid a heavenly multitude — the same divine-assembly imagery as [[Isaiah 6.1|Isaiah 6]] and [[1 Kings 22|1 Kings 22]]. [[Alex Arnold]] cites it as a Book of Mormon text (published 1830) describing the divine council decades before the [[Ugaritic Texts Reveal Israels Original Pantheon|Ugaritic tablets]] (1929) supplied the framework to understand it.
