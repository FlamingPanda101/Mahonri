# Deuteronomy 4:2

> Ye shall not add unto the word which I command you, neither shall ye diminish ought from it, that ye may keep the commandments of the LORD your God which I command you. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Near-identical "add not / diminish not" language appears here, early in the Old Testament. If such a formula forbade *all* later scripture, the rest of the Old Testament and the entire New Testament would themselves be illicit "additions" — which shows the formula seals a *specific* text, not the whole future canon.
