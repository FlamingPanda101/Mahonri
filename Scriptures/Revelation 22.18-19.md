# Revelation 22:18-19

> For I testify unto every man that heareth the words of the prophecy of this book, If any man shall add unto these things, God shall add unto him the plagues that are written in this book: And if any man shall take away from the words of the book of this prophecy, God shall take away his part out of the book of life, and out of the holy city, and from the things which are written in this book. *(KJV)*

## Used against the Church
Cited to argue the Bible forbids any addition, so the Book of Mormon (and [[Joseph Smith]]) stand condemned.

## In context
The warning is bound to **"the words of the prophecy of this book"** — the book of Revelation itself, written ~90s AD on Patmos, long before the biblical canon was compiled. Such "curse formulas" were common closings on ancient writings and even legal contracts. See the full rebuttal in [[Revelation 22 - Add or Take Away Rebutted]].

## Cross-references
- [[Deuteronomy 4.2|Deuteronomy 4:2]] — identical "add not / diminish not" language near the *start* of the canon, which would prove too much.
