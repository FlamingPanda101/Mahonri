# Isaiah 53 — The Suffering Servant

> He is despised and rejected of men; a man of sorrows... he was wounded for our transgressions, he was bruised for our iniquities... and with his stripes we are healed. (vv. 3-5)

## Key Detail

Cited via Avraham Gileadi: a chiasm "spanning the whole book of Mosiah" culminates in Isaiah 53, offered as evidence of the Book of Mormon's "very sophisticated" Hebraisms and of Isaiah's literary integrity (against the [[Deutero-Isaiah Theory]] division). The Servant Song is the messianic core both Christians and the Book of Mormon read as the atoning Christ.
