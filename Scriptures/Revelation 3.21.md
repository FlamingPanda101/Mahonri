# Revelation 3.21

> To him that overcometh will I grant to sit with me in my throne, even as I also overcame, and am set down with my Father in his throne. (Revelation 3:21, KJV)

**Key point.** The promise to the *victorious disciple* mirrors Christ's own victory and enthronement. The source reads "my throne" as a single shared throne — "we're all sitting on the same throne together... equal in power and authority" — so that what is predicated of Christ (a throne, the attributes of the divine King) is predicated of overcoming believers.
