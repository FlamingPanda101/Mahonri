# Moroni 10:27-34 — Moroni's farewell, quoting the small-plates prophets

> And again I would exhort you that ye would come unto Christ, and lay hold upon every good gift... *(Moroni 10:30)*

## Key Detail
In his closing "mic drop," Moroni weaves together quotations and allusions drawn from the small-plates authors (Nephi, Jacob, Enos, Omni) — "five quotations off the small plates," per [[Mike Day]] — incorporating their words into his own testimony. Because Moroni was dictated long *before* the small plates in the [[The Book of Mormon Was Translated Mosiah-First|Mosiah-first translation order]], these back-references are offered as evidence that Joseph could not have been composing.
