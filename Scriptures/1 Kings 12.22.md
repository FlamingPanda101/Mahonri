# 1 Kings 12:22

> But the word of God came unto Shemaiah the man of God, saying, *(KJV)*

**Key term:** "the word of God came unto Shemaiah." Another OT instance of "word of God" as a **prophetic oracle to a prophet**, cited by Trent Horn to establish that the phrase denotes a divine communication event, not a written text.
