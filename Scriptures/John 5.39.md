# John 5:39 — "ye think ye have eternal life" in the scriptures

> Search the scriptures; for in them ye think ye have eternal life: and they are they which testify of me. *(John 5:39, KJV)*

## Key Detail
[[Jared Halverson]] cites this as a place where Jesus "almost apologizes" for appeals to scripture: the scribes locate eternal life *in the texts*, but Jesus redirects it to himself — illustrating Halverson's contrast between a scribal/biblical religion and one grounded in living, authoritative revelation. (It is also the chapter whose JST, Joseph Smith said, prompted the degrees-of-glory revelation, [[D&C 76]].)
