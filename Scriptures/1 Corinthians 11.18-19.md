# 1 Corinthians 11:18–19 — Divisions and Heresies Among You

> "For first of all, when ye come together in the church, I hear that there be divisions among you; and I partly believe it. For there must be also heresies among you, that they which are approved may be made manifest among you." (KJV)

**Key Greek:** *schismata* ("divisions/splits") and *haireseis* ("factions/heresies"). Day cites 1 Corinthians 11 as "a case study" of a first-century congregation getting things wrong — direct New Testament evidence of the divisions that form the "base" of the apostolic-era church.
