# James 2:24

> Ye see then how that by works a man is justified, and not by faith only. *(KJV)*

## Significance
The only verse in scripture containing the phrase "faith alone" — and it *negates* it. The center of the Catholic case against *sola fide* (read via initial vs ongoing justification). Part of the broader [[James 2.14-26|James 2:14-26]] passage.
