# Ephesians 2:8-9

> For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God: Not of works, lest any man should boast. *(KJV)*

## Used against the Church
"Salvation is by grace, *not works* — so the LDS emphasis on works/obedience is a false, works-based gospel."

## In context
Salvation is by grace **through faith** — and faith that is real *demonstrates itself in action* (cf. [[James 2.14-26|James 2]]). Grace and works are not rivals: [[C.S. Lewis]]'s image is two blades of a scissors. The [[Faith and Works - Two Blades of the Scissors|Peter-walking-on-water]] analogy: faith without the step, or the step without faith, saves no one — both put Peter in position to be saved by **Christ's grace**. See [[Ephesians 2-8-9 - Saved by Grace Not Works Rebutted]].
