# Luke 10:16

> He that heareth you heareth me; and he that despiseth you despiseth me; and he that despiseth me despiseth him that sent me. *(KJV)*

## In context
Jesus' charge to the seventy disciples he sends out to preach. The authority to represent God is vested in the **commissioned persons**, not (yet) in any text they produce. Latter-day Saint apologist [[Shiloh Lofgren]] cites it against sola scriptura: from the first, the structure of authority runs through living, commissioned representatives — "the one who hears you hears me" — and nothing in the New Testament announces that this structure would be replaced by a closed book.
