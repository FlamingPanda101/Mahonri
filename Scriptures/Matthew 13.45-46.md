# Matthew 13:45-46

> Again, the kingdom of heaven is like unto a merchant man, seeking goodly
> pearls: Who, when he had found one pearl of great price, went and sold all
> that he had, and bought it.

## Note

This is the **Pearl of Great Price** parable, immediately following the
treasure-in-the-field parable ([[Matthew 13.44|Matthew 13:44]]). The source
distinguishes the two as separate but parallel parables — "one he finds a
treasure, one he finds a pearl of great price."[^1] The phrase also names the
LDS standard work, the Pearl of Great Price, which contains the Joseph Smith
History narrative referenced in this episode.[^2]
