# 1 Corinthians 1:17

> For Christ sent me not to baptize, but to preach the gospel: not with wisdom of words, lest the cross of Christ should be made of none effect. *(KJV)*

> "For Christ did not send me to baptize but to preach the gospel, and not with eloquent wisdom, lest the cross of Christ be emptied of its power." *(RSV — Heal's chapter epigraph)*

## In context
The epigraph to Heal's chapter "Preaching Christ." Paul's contrast between the gospel and "eloquent wisdom" frames the chapter's recurring theme that the power of preaching lies not in rhetorical polish but in the message of Christ and the goodness of the preacher — a theme developed through Augustine's response to Ambrose and Jerome's advice to Nepotian against the "windbag" preacher.
