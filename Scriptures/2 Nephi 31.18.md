# 2 Nephi 31:18 — The strait and narrow path

> And then are ye in this strait and narrow path which leads to eternal life; yea, ye have entered in by the gate... and ye have received the Holy Ghost.

## Key Detail

Cited for the "structure and succor together" reading of discipleship: the "strait and narrow" path (firm standards) is paired with the Shepherd who feeds the sheep ([[John 21.15-17]]), so the gospel's enduring core balances protection with nourishment rather than collapsing into one or the other.
