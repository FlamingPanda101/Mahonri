# Alma 11:38-39

> Now Zeezrom saith again unto him: Is the Son of God the very Eternal Father? And Amulek said unto him: Yea, he is the very Eternal Father of heaven and of earth, and all things which in them are; he is the beginning and the end, the first and the last. *(Book of Mormon)*

## Key point
Like [[Mosiah 15.1-5|Mosiah 15]], a Book-of-Mormon text calling the Son "the very Eternal Father" — raised in the recap as another apparent Modalist proof-text. The panel's reply is the same: "Father" here is a *role/title* of Christ (Father of heaven and earth by creation; cf. [[Mosiah 3.8|Mosiah 3:8]]), not a claim that the Son is the person of the Father. See [[Mosiah 15 Father and Son Explained]], [[Three Whos and One What Framework]].
