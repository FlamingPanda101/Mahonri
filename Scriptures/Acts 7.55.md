# Acts 7:55

> "But he, being full of the Holy Ghost, looked up stedfastly into heaven, and saw the glory of God, and Jesus standing on the right hand of God." (KJV)

As [[Stephen]] is being stoned, he sees **two distinct beings** — "the Son of Man standing at the right hand of God" — one standing and one seated. The Holy Ghost is also present as the power filling Stephen. Used to argue the plain text shows distinct persons in a relational hierarchy (the Godhead model), not one indivisible substance: "the distinction couldn't be more clear... the entire substance identity is assumed, not shown."
