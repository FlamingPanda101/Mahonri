# 2 Corinthians 3:13-16

> And not as Moses, which put a vail over his face… But their minds were blinded: for until this day remaineth the same vail untaken away in the reading of the old testament; which vail is done away in Christ. …Nevertheless when it shall turn to the Lord, the vail shall be taken away. *(KJV)*

## In context
Paul's own veil-of-Moses typology, the New Testament backdrop to [[Jacob of Serugh]]'s homily *The Veil on Moses' Face*. Where Paul says the veil over the Old Testament "is done away in Christ," Jacob expands the figure: the whole Old Testament is "veiled after the fashion of Moses," and Christ, in coming, "uncovered Moses' face" so that the light of prophecy shines out over the whole earth. Heal references 2 Corinthians 4:6 alongside Jacob's claim that "within the veil which lies over scripture there sits resplendent Christ."
