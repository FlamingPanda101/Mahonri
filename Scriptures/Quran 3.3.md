# Quran 3:3

**Reference:** Surah Al-Imran (3) ayah 3

## Text
> "He has sent down upon you, [O Muhammad], the Book in truth, confirming what was before it. And He revealed the Torah and the Gospel." (Quran 3:3)

## Significance
One of the Quranic texts in which the Torah (*Tawrat*) and the Gospel (*Injil*) are said to be **"sent down"** (*nazzala*) by God — the same verb the Quran uses of itself. [[Wes Huff]] argues that for these commands to be intelligible to their seventh-century audience, the Torah and Gospel they referenced must have been the texts the Jews and Christians of Muhammad's day actually possessed — texts we can reconstruct and which, Huff says, contradict the Quran. This forms part of the "Bible is the standard" line of argument.
