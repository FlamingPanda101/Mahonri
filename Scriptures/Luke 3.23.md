# Luke 3:23

> And Jesus himself began to be about thirty years of age, being (as was supposed) the son of Joseph, which was the son of Heli, *(KJV)*

**Key term:** "the son of Heli" — Luke's genealogy (which runs in reverse, Jesus back to Adam) names **Heli** as the father of Joseph. Matthew [[Matthew 1.16|Matthew 1:16]] instead names **Jacob**. The discrepancy is the lever for a standing Bible-inerrancy challenge; the common harmonization is that Luke traces **Mary's** line (Heli as Joseph's father-in-law), a reading the text does not state on its face.
