# Zechariah 8:4-5 (KJV) — Old men and children in the streets of restored Jerusalem

> There shall yet old men and old women dwell in the streets of Jerusalem... And the streets of the city shall be full of boys and girls playing in the streets thereof.

## Key Detail

A postexilic image of peaceful restoration that (with [[Ezekiel 47.8]], Isaiah 65, Joel 2) supplied the "Day of the Lord" eschatology later Christians reused. Used to show Christian end-time hope was *inherited* from Second Temple Judaism — a matter of "when, not if."
