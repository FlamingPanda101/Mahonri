# Abraham 3:22-23

> Now the Lord had shown unto me, Abraham, the intelligences that were organized before the world was; and among all these there were many of the noble and great ones; And God saw these souls that they were good, and he stood in the midst of them, and he said: These I will make my rulers... *(Abraham 3:22-23, Pearl of Great Price)*

## Significance
The classic LDS premortal-existence text: Abraham is shown the "intelligences... organized before the world was," including "the noble and great ones." In the Ward Radio "God Loves Mormons" rebuttal, [[Jonah Barnes]] parallels this with the Apocalypse of Abraham (Abraham shown the spirits "born before your day and afterwards"), arguing the apocrypha independently corroborate the premortal council Joseph Smith restored.
