# John 21:15-17 (KJV) — Feed my sheep

> He saith unto him the third time, Simon, son of Jonas, lovest thou me?... Jesus saith unto him, Feed my sheep.

## Key Detail

The "Shepherd who feeds the sheep" half of the structure-and-succor pairing: balanced against the "strait and narrow" path ([[2 Nephi 31.18]]), it shows the gospel joins firm standards with nourishment. The thrice-repeated charge also underwrites the ministering ideal of tending the one.
