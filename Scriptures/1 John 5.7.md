# 1 John 5:7

> For there are three that bear record in heaven, the Father, the Word, and the Holy Ghost: and these three are one. *(KJV)*

## Used against the Church
The clearest "Trinity in the Bible" proof text.

## In context — the Johannine Comma
This clause (the **Comma Johanneum**) is **absent from the earliest Greek manuscripts** and appears only centuries later — a textual **interpolation**, likely a marginal gloss that a later copyist absorbed into the text. Modern critical translations (NRSV, etc.) drop it; there the verse reads of "the Spirit, the water, and the blood." It is widely regarded as not original to the epistle. See [[1 John 5-7 - Johannine Comma Interpolation]].
