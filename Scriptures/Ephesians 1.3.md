# Ephesians 1:3

> Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly places in Christ: *(KJV)*

## Key point
Paul calls the Father **"the God... of our Lord Jesus Christ."** The panel strings this with Ephesians 1:17, [[2 Corinthians 1.3|2 Cor 1:3]], and 1 Peter 1:3 (all "the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ") as evidence that the New Testament habitually treats the Father as Christ's *God* — a subordinationist pattern incompatible with the Son being numerically the same being as the Father. See [[Subordination Within the New Testament]].
