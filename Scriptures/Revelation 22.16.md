# Revelation 22.16 — "the bright and morning star"

> I Jesus have sent mine angel to testify unto you these things in the churches. I am the root and the offspring of David, and the bright and morning star. *(Revelation 22:16, KJV)*

## Key Detail
- The risen Christ's self-identification as **"the root and the offspring of David, and the bright and morning star."** In [[Numbers 24.17|Numbers 24:17]]'s note it closes the messianic-star tradition: the "Star out of Jacob" that the temple-covenant magi watched for ([[The Wise Men Were Bearers of Preserved First-Temple Covenant]]) is fulfilled in Jesus, who calls himself the morning star (cf. Revelation 2:28). Both Davidic-kingship and the star-prophecy converge on Christ.
