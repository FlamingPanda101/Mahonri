# Matthew 7:7-8 (KJV) — Ask, seek, knock

> Ask, and it shall be given you; seek, and ye shall find; knock, and it shall be opened unto you. For every one that asketh receiveth.

## Key Detail

In David Butler's Sermon-on-the-Mount-as-ordinance reading, the triple petition (ask/seek/knock) immediately precedes the **narrow gate** that "leadeth unto life" ([[Matthew 7.13-14]]) — an initiate passing through to the tree of life. The structure stages the Sermon as a three-room temple ascent.
