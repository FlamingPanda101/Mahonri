# Psalm 82:1

> "God standeth in the congregation of the mighty; he judgeth among the gods." (KJV)

*Elohim nitzav ba-adat el* — "God takes his stand in the divine council (*adat el*)" — "in the midst of the **gods** (*elohim*)" he holds judgment. The psalm depicts Yahweh sentencing the other gods to die "like men" (v. 7). The angelic/divine-council reading is associated with [[Michael Heiser]]; [[Dan McClellan]] develops an alternative in his 2018 *Journal of Biblical Literature* article "The Gods' Complaint."
