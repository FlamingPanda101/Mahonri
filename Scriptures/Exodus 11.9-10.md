# Exodus 11:9-10

> And the LORD said unto Moses, Pharaoh shall not hearken unto you; that my wonders may be multiplied in the land of Egypt. And Moses and Aaron did all these wonders before Pharaoh: and the LORD hardened Pharaoh's heart, so that he would not let the children of Israel go out of his land. *(KJV)*

## The Hebrew
The purpose clause לְמַעַן רְבוֹת מוֹפְתַי ("that my wonders may be multiplied") states the rationale for Pharaoh's non-compliance, and וַיְחַזֵּק ("the LORD strengthened/hardened") again names God as the agent. The verse pairs the divine *purpose* (multiplied wonders) with the divine *act* (hardening) in a single breath.
