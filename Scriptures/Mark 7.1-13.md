# Mark 7:1-13 (KJV) — The traditions of men nullify the commandment of God

> Howbeit in vain do they worship me, teaching for doctrines the commandments of men... Full well ye reject the commandment of God, that ye may keep your own tradition. (vv. 7-9)

## Key Detail

Ortlund and Cooper invoke this passage to frame the Reformers' refusal of the papacy as *protest, not schism*: declining submission to a man-made structure that "yokes our consciences" without divine warrant is the territory of Jesus rebuking the Pharisees' traditions, not departure from the true church.
