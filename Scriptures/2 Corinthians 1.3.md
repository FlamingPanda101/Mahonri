# 2 Corinthians 1:3

> Blessed be God, even the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of mercies, and the God of all comfort; *(KJV)*

## Key point
One of the recurring Pauline formulas — "the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ" — strung together by the panel ([[Ephesians 1.3|Eph 1:3]], Eph 1:17, this verse, 1 Peter 1:3) to show the NT habitually names the Father as Christ's *God*, a subordinationist pattern. See [[Subordination Within the New Testament]].
