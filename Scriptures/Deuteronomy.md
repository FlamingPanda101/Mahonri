# Deuteronomy

The fifth book of the Torah/Pentateuch, presented as Moses's final speeches to Israel before entering the Promised Land. [[Jacob Wright]] points to Deuteronomy's claim that Israel's greatness on the world stage will come not from military power but from "wisdom and devotion to learning" — a key text for his [[Bible as Pedagogical Curriculum]] thesis.
