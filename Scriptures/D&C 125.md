# D&C 125 — a city named Zarahemla in Iowa

> Let them build up a city unto my name upon the land opposite the city of Nauvoo, and let the name of Zarahemla be named upon it. *(D&C 125:3)*

## Key Detail
This short 1841 revelation directs the Saints to build a city "opposite Nauvoo" (in Iowa Territory) and name it Zarahemla. [[Jonathan Neville]] treats it as a "pin in the map": because the Lord said "let the name of *Zarahemla* be named upon it" (not "New Zarahemla"), Neville reads it as plausibly identifying the *ancient* Book of Mormon city — paralleling the Nephite practice of naming sites after their first inhabitant. (The revelation was never canonized by the Community of Christ.)
