# D&C 9

A revelation given through [[Joseph Smith]] to [[Oliver Cowdery]] at Harmony,
Pennsylvania, April 1829, after Cowdery attempted and failed to translate.[^1]

## Study It Out in Your Mind

The source summarizes this section as the revelation where Cowdery is told "you
didn't continue on as you started; you thought all you had to do is ask. You
actually need to study it out in your mind and then ask me if it's right."[^2]
It belongs to the same cluster of early revelations (sections 6–9) that also
describe Cowdery's "gift of the rod," underscoring that the same revelatory
context produced both the instruction on translation and the affirmation of the
rod as a spiritual gift.[^3]
