# D&C 76

Doctrine and Covenants Section 76 — an 1832 revelation to [[Joseph Smith]] and
[[Sydney Rigdon]] known as "The Vision." It describes three degrees of glory
(Celestial, Terrestrial, Telestial) and introduced the concept of exaltation into
LDS theology, though "not as specifically as later LDS teachings would clarify."[^1]

## Used in

- [[Human Deification in Mormonism]] — cited as an early revelation that "spoke
  about exaltation," laying groundwork for the [[King Follett Discourse]] doctrines
