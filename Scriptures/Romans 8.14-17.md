# Romans 8:14-17

> For as many as are led by the Spirit of God, they are the sons of God. For ye have not received the spirit of bondage again to fear; but ye have received the Spirit of adoption, whereby we cry, Abba, Father. The Spirit itself beareth witness with our spirit, that we are the children of God: And if children, then heirs; heirs of God, and joint-heirs with Christ. *(KJV)*

## Significance
The epigraph to [[Gaye Strathearn]]'s chapter on human nature, framing humanity's divine sonship and joint-heirship with Christ. [[Origen]] used the "Spirit of adoption" language to distinguish how Christ is Son "by nature" (eternal, "born of [God] but … without any beginning") from how humans become "children of God … through adoption in the Spirit." The passage grounds the patristic and LDS theme that humans are God's children with the potential to become "heirs of God, and joint-heirs with Christ" (theosis).
