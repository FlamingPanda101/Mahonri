# 2 Nephi 5:27 — "we lived after the manner of happiness"

> And it came to pass that we lived after the manner of happiness. *(2 Nephi 5:27)*

## Key Detail
After separating from his murderous brothers and building a temple ([[2 Nephi 5.21|2 Nephi 5]]), Nephi reports that his people "lived after the manner of happiness." Recorded on the small plates — dictated *last* in the [[The Book of Mormon Was Translated Mosiah-First|Mosiah-first order]] — yet the phrase is reused in the earlier-dictated [[Alma 50.23|Alma 50:23]], a cross-reference [[Mike Day]] cites as evidence against composition.
