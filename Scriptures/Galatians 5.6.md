# Galatians 5:6

> For in Jesus Christ neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision; but faith which worketh by love. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Paul himself describes saving faith as "faith **working** through love" — used to show that for Paul, as for James, living faith is inseparable from works of love.
