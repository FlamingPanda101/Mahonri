# Alma 12 — Alma confronts Ammonihah over the mysteries of God

Alma rebuts Zeezrom at Ammonihah, teaching the doctrine of resurrection and judgment and warning that those who harden their hearts receive "the lesser portion of the word" until they know nothing of the mysteries.

## Key Detail

Cited by David Butler as evidence that Ammonihah is a city that "once knew the mysteries" and has weaponized the liturgy — making the seven cheek-smitings of the bound Alma and Amulek ([[Alma 14.14]]) read as a *reversed* temple ordinance ("smiting... that which was sacred," [[Helaman 4.12]]) rather than random violence.
