# Ephesians 5:23

> For the husband is the head of the wife, even as Christ is the head of the church: and he is the saviour of the body.

Paul's analogy of husband-as-head to Christ-as-head of the church. Cited in Latter-day Saint and broader Christian discussions of presiding/headship in marriage as a *servant-leadership* rather than tyranny.
