# Abraham 5:14-21 (PGP) — Eve found for Adam, not made from his rib

> And the Gods caused a deep sleep to fall upon Adam... and they took one of his ribs... and they organized into a woman the rib which they had taken from man, and brought her unto the man. (vv. 14-21)

## Key Detail

In the Book of Abraham rendition, Joseph Smith's wording shifts Eve from being surgically *made from* Adam's rib toward Eve being *found for* Adam. Charles Lambert reads this as matching what the Hebrew grammar already implies — "took" (*wayyiqach*) is the language of going out and searching — supporting the reading of Eve's removal as a sanctified group "taken out" of humanity and recreated in Eden in the image of the divine Mother.
