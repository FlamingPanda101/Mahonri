# Genesis 4 — Cain and Abel

[[Cain]] murders [[Abel]], the first murder. Verse 10:

> And he said, What hast thou done? the voice of thy brother's blood crieth unto me from the ground. *(KJV)*

## Significance
The "crying blood" of Abel is the seed the apocryphal traditions expand — earth refusing his body, blood that would not dry. The canonical anchor for the [[Blood of Abel - Unburiable Tradition]] and for Jesus's "blood of Abel" in [[Luke 11.47-51|Luke 11:47-51]].
