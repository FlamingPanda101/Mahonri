# Romans 5:19

> For as by one man's disobedience many were made sinners, so by the obedience of one shall many be made righteous. *(KJV)*

**Context:** Paul's Adam–Christ typology (Romans 5:12-21): the trespass of the one (Adam) is answered and overturned by the obedience of the one (Christ). This verse is the scriptural foundation Irenaeus builds on for his doctrine of **recapitulation** — Christ reliving Adam's history but succeeding where Adam failed.
