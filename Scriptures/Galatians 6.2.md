# Galatians 6:2

> Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ. *(KJV)*

## Key phrase
"**Bear ye one another's burdens**" — Paul's call to mutual support within the body of Christ.

## Apologetic / pastoral application
[[Trent Horn]] uses this verse as the conclusion of his case that pornography is a *shared* human struggle, not a male-only sin. Rather than "setting men and women against each other," the Church should "unite them together to carry each other's burdens… so we can help one another hold fast to the gift of eternal life in Christ." It anchors his reframing of the message from "men should reject this" to "people should reject this." See [[Pornography Corrupts Distinct Gendered Desires]].
