# Genesis 22 — The Binding of Isaac

Abraham, tested by God, prepares to offer his son Isaac; God stays his hand and provides a ram. Verse 5:

> And Abraham said unto his young men, Abide ye here with the ass; and I and the lad will go yonder and worship, and come again to you. *(KJV)*

## Significance
James cites this as where Abraham's faith was "active along with works." Crucially, **Abraham and Isaac went away from the servants** — no one witnessed the act — which refutes the claim that James means Abraham was "justified before men." See [[James - Justification Before Men Reply Rebutted]].
