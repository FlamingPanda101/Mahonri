# 2 Corinthians 11:14

> **14** And no marvel; for Satan himself is transformed into an angel of light.

Paul's warning that deception can masquerade as the divine. Central to the evangelical objection that a spiritual "burning in the bosom" cannot, by itself, certify its own source — since a counterfeit can imitate light. Latter-day Saints respond that the existence of counterfeits implies a genuine article and a means of discernment ([[1 John 4.1|1 John 4:1]]).
