# Alma 29:8 — God Grants Each Nation His Word

> **8** For behold, the Lord doth grant unto all nations, of their own nation and tongue, to teach his word, yea, in wisdom, all that he seeth fit that they should have; therefore we see that the Lord doth counsel in wisdom, according to that which is just and true. (Alma 29:8)

Alma's reflection that God gives "all nations" His word "in their own... tongue," at the level "he seeth fit that they should have."

[[Shad Brooks]] reads "all that he seeth fit" as God **calibrating revealed truth to each people's readiness** — the level that "is going to most optimally help them become like him" — and as support for the idea that God "has revealed many teachings to many different cultures."[^1] He pairs it with [[D&C 29]]:33 to argue God accommodates truth to the hearer. See [[God Frames Truth Pedagogically to Change Behavior]]; compare the vault's [[Embrace All Truth From Any Source]] and [[The Gathering of Truths from All Churches]].
