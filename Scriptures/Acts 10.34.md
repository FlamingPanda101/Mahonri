# Acts 10:34 (KJV) — God is no respecter of persons

> Then Peter opened his mouth, and said, Of a truth I perceive that God is no respecter of persons.

## Key Detail

Part of the LDS canon against divine-command metaethics: alongside [[Alma 42.13|Alma 42:13]] ("God would cease to be God") and [[2 Nephi 2.13|2 Nephi 2:13]], it grounds morality in principles God *honors* rather than arbitrarily authors — answering the Euthyphro horn that "whatever God does is good."
