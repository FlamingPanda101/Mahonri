# Exodus 20:12

> Honour thy father and thy mother: that thy days may be long upon the land which the LORD thy God giveth thee. *(KJV)*

**Context:** the fifth commandment of the Decalogue, **orally spoken by God to Moses** at Sinai. In Mark 7 / Matthew 15 Jesus charges the Pharisees with voiding "the word/command of God" — Trent Horn argues the referent is precisely this *spoken* commandment (and its Deuteronomic reiteration), not the written text of Exodus that records it.
