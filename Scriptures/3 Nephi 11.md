# 3 Nephi 11 — The Risen Christ Appears at Bountiful

> Behold, I am Jesus Christ, whom the prophets testified shall come into the world. And behold, I am the light and the life of the world... I am the God of Israel, and the God of the whole earth, and have been slain for the sins of the world. *(3 Nephi 11:10–11, 14, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Detail
- The resurrected Christ descends to the Nephites and declares himself "the God of Israel, and the God of the whole earth." The Thoughtful Faith narrator cites it to rebut the charge that Latter-day Saints "don't believe Jesus is God": Latter-day Saints affirm Christ's **full divinity** ("a fullness of divinity") while rejecting the creedal *definition* of Godhood as a simple, timeless, immaterial abstraction.
- Demonstrates the LDS move: deny the Trinity's metaphysics, not the divinity of Christ. See [[The Creedal God Is the Platonic One Not the God of the Bible]].
