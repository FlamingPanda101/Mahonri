# John 14:6 — "no man cometh unto the Father, but by me"

> I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. *(John 14:6, KJV)*

## Key Detail
- One of the two proof-texts for **soteriological exclusivism** (salvation only through Christ) that generate the [[The Soteriological Problem of Evil - The Inconsistent Triad|soteriological problem of evil]]: if salvation comes only through Christ, what of the millions who never heard of Him? [[David Paulsen]] argues the tension is resolved by restored knowledge of **postmortem evangelization** ([[Postmortem Salvation Resolves the Soteriological Problem]]).
