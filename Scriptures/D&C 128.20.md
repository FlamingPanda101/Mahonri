# D&C 128:20 — "Glad tidings from Cumorah!"

> And again, what do we hear? Glad tidings from Cumorah! Moroni, an angel from heaven, declaring the fulfilment of the prophets — the book to be revealed. *(D&C 128:20)*

## Key Detail
Written by Joseph Smith in 1842, this verse names "Cumorah" in connection with Moroni's announcement of the book *before* the plates were uncovered. [[Jonathan Neville]] treats it as canonized confirmation that the New York hill was identified as Cumorah from the first visit (corroborating [[Lucy Mack Smith]]'s account), and argues the Mesoamerican reading must strain the verse to deny it. Mesoamerican advocates read "Cumorah" here as a later label.
