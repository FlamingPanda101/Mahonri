# 1 Corinthians 15:1-4

> Moreover, brethren, I declare unto you the gospel... how that Christ died for our sins according to the scriptures; And that he was buried, and that he rose again the third day according to the scriptures. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Paul's own definition of "the gospel" — Christ's death, burial, and resurrection for our sins. Latter-day Saints affirm *this* gospel, which is why [[Galatians 1.8|Galatians 1:8]]'s "another gospel" does not apply to them. Anchors the [[Definition of Gospel]].
