# Psalm 23:4 (KJV) — The valley of the shadow of death

> Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil: for thou art with me; thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.

## Key Detail

Cited as the scriptural antecedent for the near-death-experience "dark tunnel/valley" motif: passage through darkness without fear because of a divine presence. Used to show NDE imagery resonates with long-standing biblical language of death's threshold.
