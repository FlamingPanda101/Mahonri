# Leviticus 17

The blood prohibition: "For the life of the flesh is in the blood... for it is the life of all flesh; the blood of it is for the life thereof" (vv. 11, 14). Israel is forbidden to consume blood because blood *is* the life of the creature.

This datum is invoked in apocryphal/Restoration readings of the Cain narrative: the ancient idea that drinking a victim's blood transfers or lengthens life — a belief the book of Enoch ascribes to the antediluvians — frames why the apocryphal Cain traditions have him drink Abel's blood.
