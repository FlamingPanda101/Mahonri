# Romans 8:28

> And we know that all things work together for good to them that love God, to them who are the called according to his purpose. *(KJV)*

> "For those who love Him, God makes all things conspire to good… For those who are called in accordance with a resolve." *(as quoted by Cyril of Jerusalem)*

## In context
Cited by [[Cyril of Jerusalem]] in the prologue (procatechesis) to his catechetical lectures, to instruct baptismal candidates that being "called" depends on the sincerity of their resolve: "It is the sincerity of your resolution that makes you 'called.' … It is of no use your body being here if your thoughts and heart are elsewhere." Heal highlights how Cyril weaves the scriptural language of calling into the elevated rhetoric preparing candidates for baptism.
