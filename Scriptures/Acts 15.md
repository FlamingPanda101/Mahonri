# Acts 15 — The Jerusalem Council

The early church's council deciding whether Gentile converts must be circumcised and keep the law of Moses. The apostles and elders conclude they need not — settling the **Judaizer** controversy.

## Significance
This is the very dispute driving the letter to the Galatians. It establishes that the "another gospel" Paul anathematizes in [[Galatians 1.8|Galatians 1:8]] is **Judaizing legalism**, not angelic revelation.
