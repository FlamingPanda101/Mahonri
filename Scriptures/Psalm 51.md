# Psalm 51 — "In sin did my mother conceive me"

> Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother conceive me. (v. 5)

## Key Detail

A standard original-sin prooftext (David's penitential psalm) that the vault engages in the **infant-damnation reductio**: if inherited guilt plus a *required* profession of faith damns those who cannot profess (infants), the doctrine yields an intolerable conclusion — used to press inherited-guilt theology rather than to endorse it.
