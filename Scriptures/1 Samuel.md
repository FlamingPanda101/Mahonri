# 1 Samuel

The first book of Samuel in the Hebrew Bible, covering the transition from the period of the judges to the monarchy — the stories of Samuel, Saul, and David's rise. [[Jacob Wright]] argues that the Davidic material in 1–2 Samuel represents the "palace history" of Judah, which was editorially subordinated to the older "people's history" (Patriarchs, Exodus) from the northern tradition.
