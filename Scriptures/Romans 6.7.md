# Romans 6:7

> For he that is dead is freed from sin. *(KJV)*

## Significance
The word "freed" is a form of the Greek *dikaioō* ("to justify"). Paul literally writes "he who has died is **justified** from sin" — used to show Paul's own vocabulary of justification is broader than a one-time forensic declaration.
