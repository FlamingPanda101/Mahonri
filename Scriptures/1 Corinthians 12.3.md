# 1 Corinthians 12:3

> Wherefore I give you to understand, that no man speaking by the Spirit of God calleth Jesus accursed: and that no man can say that Jesus is the Lord, but by the Holy Ghost. *(KJV)*

## Key Term: "Jesus is Lord" (*Kyrios Iesous*)
The confession "Jesus is Lord" (*Kyrios* rendering YHWH/*kyrios*) is presented by Paul as Spirit-given. [[Gavin Ortlund]] cites this with [[Philippians 2.9-11|Philippians 2:9-11]] to argue that the *confession and apprehension of Jesus' identity* is "baked right into the fruit" of true faith — i.e., correct Christology and faithful living are not separable. The disputed question (with [[Jacob Hansen]]) is what "Lord" metaphysically entails.
