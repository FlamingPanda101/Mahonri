# John 1:9

> 9 That was the true Light, which lighteth every man that cometh into the world. *(KJV, John 1:9)*

## Significance
John's prologue identifies Christ as "the true Light, which lighteth **every man**" — a light universal to all humanity, not confined to one people or creed. This fits the striking pattern of the [[NDE - The Being of Light]]: across religiously diverse subjects, people of every faith and of none encounter the same loving luminous presence, and only afterward name it according to their own tradition. It pairs with the Restoration's expansion of the same idea — the [[D&C 88|D&C 88:11–13]] "light which is in all things" — to read the being of light as the **Light of Christ**.
