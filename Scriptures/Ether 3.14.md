# Ether 3:14

> Behold, I am he who was prepared from the foundation of the world to redeem my people. Behold, I am Jesus Christ. I am the Father and the Son. In me shall all mankind have life, and that eternally, even they who shall believe on my name; and they shall become my sons and my daughters. *(Book of Mormon)*

## Key Term: "I am the Father and the Son"
Like [[Mosiah 15.1-5|Mosiah 15]], this is cited against Latter-day Saints as teaching that the Father and the Son are the same being. [[Blake Ostler]] argues the surrounding context supplies the justification: in revealing himself to the brother of Jared, the premortal Christ explains that he will come in the flesh and be *begotten by the Father* — and in that sense (creator/redeemer who will be born of the Father) he is "the Father and the Son." The very same Book of Mormon elsewhere distinguishes the Father and Son rather radically. See [[Book of Mormon Father-and-Son Texts Resolve in Context]].
