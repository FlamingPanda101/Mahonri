# Romans 10:17

> So then faith cometh by hearing, and hearing by the word of God. *(KJV)*

## In context
Heal uses this verse in the "Why It Matters" box to ground the centrality of preaching: "Faith in the gospel comes from hearing it proclaimed." Preaching is therefore "foundational not only to the spreading of the Christian message but to the ongoing project of teaching, inspiring, guiding, chastening, and caring for the souls of those who turn to Christ." The verse anchors the chapter's thesis that early Christianity was a preaching religion.
