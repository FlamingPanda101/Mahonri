# 1 Nephi 17:1 — "we did travel nearly eastward from that time forth"

> "And we did again take our journey in the wilderness; and we did travel nearly eastward from that time forth. And we did travel and wade through much affliction in the wilderness..." *(1 Nephi 17:1, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Detail

- This verse supplies the **eastward turn** that anchors the whole Arabian correlation. After traveling generally south-southeast (1 Nephi 16:13) to [[1 Nephi 16.34|Nahom]], the party turns "nearly eastward." Along the Frankincense Trail, eastward branches are available only in a ~70-mile window around the Wadi Jawf — which is also where the NHM tribal region sits.
- The turn fixes Nahom's position as **inland**, not coastal, and points the journey toward the Dhofar region candidate for Bountiful.
