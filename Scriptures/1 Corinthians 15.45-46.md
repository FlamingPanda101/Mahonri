# 1 Corinthians 15:45-46

> And so it is written, The first man Adam was made a living soul; the last Adam was made a quickening spirit. Howbeit that was not first which is spiritual, but that which is natural; and afterward that which is spiritual. *(KJV)*

## Significance
The evangelical "God Loves Mormons" video uses "not first which is spiritual, but… the natural" to argue against a premortal spiritual existence. The Ward Radio panel answers that the context is Paul's first-Adam / last-Adam (Christ) typology — "the first man is of the earth, earthy; the second man is the Lord from heaven" (v. 47) — a contrast between mortal man and the resurrected/heavenly Christ, not between premortal and mortal life. Reading it as a denial of pre-existence imports a "mechanics of creation" meaning Paul never intended.
