# Romans 2:6-13

> Who will render to every man according to his deeds: To them who by patient continuance in well doing seek for glory and honour and immortality, eternal life... For not the hearers of the law are just before God, but the doers of the law shall be justified. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Paul's own statement that God judges "according to deeds" and that "the **doers** of the law shall be justified" — used to show that even Paul ties final justification to works (the ongoing/eschatological sense), harmonizing with James.
