# Matthew 5:45

> That ye may be the children of your Father which is in heaven: for he maketh his sun to rise on the evil and on the good, and sendeth rain on the just and on the unjust. *(KJV)*

## Key phrase
"**His sun to rise on the evil and on the good**" — the classic proof-text for **common grace** / non-discriminating divine benevolence: God's good is not metered out by worthiness.

## Apologetic application
[[Gavin (Catholic Debater)]] cites it as evidence God's good is extended to all without exception. See [[Scripture Explicitly Affirms Universal Salvific Will]] and [[Omnibenevolence Is Unconditional Grace Not a Contract]].
