# Matthew 7:15-20

> Beware of false prophets, which come to you in sheep's clothing, but inwardly they are ravening wolves. Ye shall know them by their fruits. Do men gather grapes of thorns, or figs of thistles? Even so every good tree bringeth forth good fruit; but a corrupt tree bringeth forth evil fruit. A good tree cannot bring forth evil fruit, neither can a corrupt tree bring forth good fruit... Wherefore by their fruits ye shall know them. *(KJV)*

## Key phrase
"**By their fruits ye shall know them**" — Jesus's test for distinguishing true from false prophets: not by claims or credentials but by what their teaching produces. A good tree cannot bring forth evil fruit, nor a corrupt tree good fruit.
