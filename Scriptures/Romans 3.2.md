# Romans 3:2

> Much every way: chiefly, because that unto them were committed the oracles of God. *(KJV)*

**Key Greek:** "oracles" translates *ta logia tou theou* — *logia*, divine **oracles/utterances**, not the more common *logos* ("word"). The NIV's "the very words of God" is the outlier rendering that makes the verse sound like a statement about Scripture-as-text; most translations read "oracles." N.T. Wright notes Israel's Scriptures are nowhere else called God's "oracles"; A.T. Robertson takes the term as God's commands and promises rather than Scripture itself.
