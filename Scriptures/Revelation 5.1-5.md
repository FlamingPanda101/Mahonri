# Revelation 5:1-5 (KJV) — The sealed book and the Lamb who is worthy to open it

> And I saw in the right hand of him that sat on the throne a book written within and on the backside, sealed with seven seals... and no man was able to open the book... the Lion of the tribe of Juda, the Root of David, hath prevailed to open the book.

## Key Detail

Read alongside [[D&C 110]] in the framework of **priesthood keys across the seven dispensations**: the seven-sealed scroll opened only by the worthy Lamb is taken as imagery for the keys and the unfolding of God's dispensational plan — supporting that authority/keys, once departed, mark the apostasy and must be restored.
