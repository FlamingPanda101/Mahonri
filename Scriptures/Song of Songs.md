# Song of Songs

The Song of Songs (Song of Solomon) is a collection of love poetry celebrating romantic and erotic love between a man and a woman. [[Jacob Wright]] addresses the question of what love poetry has to do with his educational thesis: Song of Songs models an "I-Thou" intimate relationship in which the woman is equal to the man — a society cannot exist without such deep intimacy, and the poetry itself has "educational capacity" that shapes readers.
