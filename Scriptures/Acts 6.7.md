# Acts 6:7

> And the word of God increased; and the number of the disciples multiplied in Jerusalem greatly; and a great company of the priests were obedient to the faith. *(KJV)*

**Key term:** "the word of God increased/grew" (*ho logos tou theou ēuxanen*). Luke uses this idiom on three occasions (cf. Acts 12:24; 19:20). Trent Horn, citing Jerome Kodell, notes the phrase treats the word of God as **embedded in the growing Christian community** — "the word of God grew when the church adds new members" — never tying it to a written text.
