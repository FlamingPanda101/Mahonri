# Genesis 6:1-4 — The sons of God and the daughters of men

> ...the sons of God saw the daughters of men that they were fair; and they took them wives of all which they chose... There were giants in the earth in those days. (vv. 2, 4)

## Key Detail

The cryptic "sons of God" passage that [[1 Enoch]]'s Book of the Watchers expands into the fallen-angel/Watchers narrative. Cited to show Second Temple traditions (and the divine-council "sons of God" theology) standing behind the terse biblical text — relevant to LDS readings of a literal divine family and council.
