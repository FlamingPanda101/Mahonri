# Alma 37:3-5 — The brass plates shall retain their brightness

> And now behold, if they are kept they must retain their brightness; yea, and they will retain their brightness; yea, and also shall all the plates which do contain that which is holy writ. *(Alma 37:5)*

## Key Detail
Alma charges Helaman with the **brass plates** and the other records, promising the plates of brass will "retain their brightness" and never be dimmed — a record of scripture and genealogy preserved by divine purpose. In the [[Ward Radio - The Sword of Laban Was the Sword of Joshua|Don Bradley interview]], [[Don Bradley]] reads a symbolic contrast: the **sword of Laban** is reported (via Joseph Smith Sr.) to have cankered and rusted to only its hilt, while the brass **plates** endure — "the word of God stands forever... ultimately the power is not in the sword, it is in the word."
