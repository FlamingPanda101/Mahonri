# Job 1-2

> "Now there was a day when the sons of God came to present themselves before the LORD, and Satan came also among them." *(Job 1:6, KJV)*

The *bene ha-elohim* ([[Bene Elohim Are Sons Not a Different Species|sons of God]]) present themselves before Yahweh's throne on a scheduled basis — the language of a subordinate body reporting to a superior, one of the clearest [[Psalm 82 Is About Divine Council Not Human Judges|divine-council]] scenes in the Hebrew Bible. [[Alex Arnold]] lists it among the texts that describe a populated, deliberating heaven.
