# Official Declaration 2 (1978) — Priesthood extended to all worthy male members

> He has heard our prayers, and by revelation has confirmed that the long-promised day has come when every faithful, worthy man in the Church may receive the holy priesthood... without regard for race or color.

## Key Detail

The 1978 revelation ending the priesthood-and-temple ban on Black members. Cited as the culmination Spencer W. Kimball reached as prophet — *decades after* he had already penciled question marks beside the [[Pearl of Great Price]] passages used to justify the ban, evidence he privately doubted the prooftexts long before the revelation came.
