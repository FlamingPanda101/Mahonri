# John 6:35 — I Am the Bread of Life

> **35** And Jesus said unto them, I am the bread of life: he that cometh to me shall never hunger; and he that believeth on me shall never thirst. (John 6:35)

Cited in the [[Salty Saints - Why Water Is Better Than Wine in the Sacrament|Salty Saints sacrament episode]] to seal the episode's central thesis: the sacrament's bread and water *sustain* the new life entered at baptism. Jesus is the bread that satisfies hunger and (paired with the living-water sayings) the drink that satisfies thirst, so eating and drinking the emblems is the spiritual nourishment of the reborn disciple.[^1] See [[The Sacrament Sustains the New Life of Baptism]].
