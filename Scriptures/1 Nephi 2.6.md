# 1 Nephi 2:6 — the valley with a river

> And it came to pass that when he had traveled three days in the wilderness, he pitched his tent in a valley by the side of a river of water. *(1 Nephi 2:6, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Detail
- **"a river of water"** — Nephi specifies a *continually flowing* river that empties into the Red Sea (cf. 1 Nephi 2:8–9). For over a thousand miles of Arabian coast, only one perennial stream flows toward the Red Sea; it was discovered in **1995**, 166 years after Joseph Smith dictated the text, and after a 1980s survey reported no such rivers existed. Located ~75 miles south of the Gulf of Aqaba, fitting the "three days" at ~25 miles/day.
