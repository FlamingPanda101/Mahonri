# Romans 6:23

> For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Cited by [[Wes Huff]] for the "appeal to sinfulness" defense — we *earn* death by sin, while eternal life is an unearned gift. The video argues this cannot justify *infinite* punishment for finite acts. See [[Hell - Appeal to Sinfulness Rebutted]].
