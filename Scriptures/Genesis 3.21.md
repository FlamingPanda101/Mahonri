# Genesis 3:21 — God clothes Adam and Eve in coats of skins

> Unto Adam also and to his wife did the LORD God make coats of skins, and clothed them. *(KJV)*

## Key Detail
After the Fall, Adam and Eve cover themselves with **fig leaves** (Gen 3:7) — a self-made covering — but God replaces it with **coats of skins**, a covering that requires the death of an animal. Latter-day Saint and many patristic readers take the divinely-made skin garment as a type of the **covering, atoning grace of Jesus Christ**: the first thing God does for fallen humanity is *clothe* it, at the cost of blood. The progression fig-leaves → skins maps onto inadequate human effort being superseded by a God-given covering. This is the seed-text behind the temple teaching that the garment "represents Jesus Christ" because the wearer is daily clothed-upon with the Savior. See [[The Garment Is the Covering of Christ]].
