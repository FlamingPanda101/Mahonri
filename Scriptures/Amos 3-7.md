# Amos 3:7

> "Surely the Lord GOD will do nothing, but he revealeth his secret unto his
> servants the prophets."

## Apologetic Significance
Establishes the **pattern** of prophetic revelation: God consistently works through
prophets. LDS apologists cite this to argue that if God is "the same yesterday,
today, and forever" (Hebrews 13:8), the prophetic pattern should continue. The
burden shifts to the cessationist: when did God announce He was changing this
pattern?
