# Galatians 1:8

> But though we, or an angel from heaven, preach any other gospel unto you than that which we have preached unto you, let him be accursed. *(KJV)*

## Used against the Church
Read as a prophecy against [[Angel Moroni]] (or Muhammad's angel) — "an angel brought a new gospel, so it is accursed."

## In context
Galatians is **one undivided letter** whose driving crisis is the **Judaizers** demanding circumcision and the law of Moses — the same dispute settled at the [[Acts 15]] Jerusalem council. The "another gospel" is Judaizing legalism, not angelic visitation; angels deliver God's messages throughout scripture. See [[Galatians 1-8 - Angel Preaching Another Gospel]].

## Cross-references
- [[Acts 15]] — the circumcision controversy Paul is addressing.
- [[1 Corinthians 15.1-4|1 Corinthians 15:1-4]] — Paul's own definition of "the gospel."
