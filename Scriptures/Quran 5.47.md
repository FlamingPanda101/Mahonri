# Quran 5:47 — let the people of the Gospel judge by it

> And let the People of the Gospel judge by what Allah has revealed therein. And whoever does not judge by what Allah has revealed — then it is those who are the defiantly disobedient. *(Sahih International)*

## Key Detail
A command for Christians ("the People of the Gospel") to **judge by** the revelation in the Gospel, with a threat against those who fail to do so. [[Avery (God Logic)|Avery]] argues that one cannot be commanded to adjudicate by a book unless that book is *present and trustworthy* — so the verse presupposes an authentic, accessible Gospel in Muhammad's own day, which is the positive premise of [[The Islamic Dilemma]]. Read alongside [[Quran 5.43|5:43]] (the same logic for Jews and the Torah). The companion verse [[Quran 5.48|5:48]] introduces the *muhaymin* ("guardian") description that Muslims invoke in reply — see [[The Guardian and Criterion Reply to the Islamic Dilemma]].
