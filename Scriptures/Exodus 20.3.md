# Exodus 20:3

> "Thou shalt have no other gods before me." (KJV)

The first commandment of the Decalogue. [[Dan McClellan]] reads the prohibition as presupposing the *real existence* of the other gods it forbids Israel to prefer: a command not to put them "before" Yahweh only makes sense if they are taken to exist. He stresses the Hebrew says *elohim* ("gods"), not "idols." See [[Negation Texts Assert Incomparability Not Non-Existence]] and [[Other Gods Are Real Divine Beings Not Merely Idols]].
