# 1 Timothy 6:6-8 (KJV) — Godliness with contentment

> But godliness with contentment is great gain... And having food and raiment let us be therewith content.

## Key Detail

Used in the *sola scriptura* rebuttal (against the [[2 Timothy 3.16-17|2 Tim 3:16-17]] "profitable" prooftext): the Pastorals themselves deploy *sufficiency*/contentment language here for material needs — showing the authors knew how to say "sufficient" when they meant it, and did not apply it to scripture as the whole rule of faith.
