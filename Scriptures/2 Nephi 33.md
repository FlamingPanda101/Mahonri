# 2 Nephi 33 — "I glory in plainness"

> For I glory in plainness; I glory in truth; I glory in my Jesus, for he hath redeemed my soul from hell. *(2 Nephi 33:6, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Detail
- Nephi's closing testimony: "I glory in plainness; I glory in truth; I glory in my Jesus." [[Josh Galey]] invokes this in [[Ward Radio - Fake Book of Mormon Artifacts]] as the spirit in which the panel debunks forged artifacts — delighting in truth and denouncing deception, while still respecting believers whose paradigm is being challenged.
