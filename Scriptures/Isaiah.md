# Isaiah

The Book of Isaiah is a major prophetic book spanning pre-exilic, exilic, and post-exilic periods. "Deutero-Isaiah" (chapters 40–55) contains the "light to the nations" passages and Zion theology that represent the triumphalist strand within the biblical tradition — which [[Jacob Wright]] contrasts with the defeat-centered, smallness-oriented strand of his "peoplehood" thesis.
