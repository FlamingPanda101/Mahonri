# Acts 21:10

> "And as we tarried there many days, there came down from Judaea a certain
> prophet named Agabus."

## Apologetic Significance
A **named prophet** (Agabus) operates in the post-ascension church — he even
performs a symbolic prophetic act (binding his own hands/feet with Paul's belt).
This is a specific, named counter-example to any claim that prophecy ended with
Christ.
