# 1 Kings 18:24

> "And call ye on the name of your gods, and I will call on the name of the LORD: and the God that answereth by fire, let him be God. And all the people answered and said, It is well spoken." (KJV)

Elijah's framing of the contest on Mount Carmel against the prophets of Baal. The test — "the God that answereth by **fire**" — is, in comparative scholarship, a demand for a **storm-deity** demonstration (fire/lightning from heaven). The people's later cry is *Adonai hu ha-Elohim* — "Yahweh, he is **the** god" (1 Kings 18:39) — read as a claim about purview, not bare existence.
