# Genesis 10

The "Table of Nations." Genesis 10:6 lists the sons of [[Ham]]: "Cush, and Mizraim, and Phut, and Canaan." **Mizraim** (מִצְרַיִם, *Mitsrayim*) is the standard Hebrew term for **Egypt** — so Genesis itself derives the Egyptians from Ham. Read together with the curse on Canaan in Genesis 9:25 ("Cursed be Canaan; a servant of servants shall he be"), this genealogy became the textual backbone of the "[[curse of Ham]]" used in the 18th–19th centuries to justify the enslavement of people of African descent.

> 6 And the sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, and Phut, and Canaan.
> (Genesis 9:25) And he said, Cursed be Canaan; a servant of servants shall he be unto his brethren.
