# 1 Kings 22:19-23

> "I saw the LORD sitting on his throne, and all the host of heaven standing by him on his right hand and on his left. And the LORD said, Who shall persuade Ahab...? And there came forth a spirit, and stood before the LORD, and said, I will persuade him." *(KJV)*

The prophet Micaiah describes a divine council in session: Yahweh deliberating with the heavenly host about Ahab's fate, members offering competing proposals before a decision is reached — a governing body, not a monologue. [[Alex Arnold]] treats it as a structural template for the deliberative councils of [[Moses 4|Moses 4]] and [[1 Nephi 1.8|1 Nephi 1:8]].
