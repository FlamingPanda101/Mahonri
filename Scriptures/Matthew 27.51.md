# Matthew 27:51 — The veil of the temple was rent in twain

> And, behold, the veil of the temple was rent in twain from the top to the bottom; and the earth did quake, and the rocks rent. *(KJV)*

## Key Detail
At the moment of Christ's death the **veil of the temple** — the curtain separating the Holy Place from the Holy of Holies — is torn from **top to bottom**, an act read in Hebrews 10:19-20 as opening "a new and living way" into the presence of God through Christ's flesh. The top-to-bottom direction signals a divine, not human, rending. In the [[Ward Radio - The Secret Birth Allegory in the Temple Rites Part II|Ward Radio birth-allegory episode]] this rent veil is the anchor for the birth typology: as Christ rent the temple veil to admit worshippers to the Father, a mother's "veil" (the amniotic sac) is rent to deliver a child into the world.
