# John 4:10-14 — Living Water at the Well

> **10** …if thou knewest the gift of God… thou wouldest have asked of him, and he would have given thee living water… **14** But whosoever drinketh of the water that I shall give him shall never thirst; but the water that I shall give him shall be in him a well of water springing up into everlasting life. (John 4:10–14)

In the [[Salty Saints - Why Water Is Better Than Wine in the Sacrament|Salty Saints sacrament episode]], David (Sword of Laban) uses Christ's "living water" offer to the Samaritan woman as a centerpiece of his argument that **water is a stronger symbol of Jehovah than wine**. He connects it to the Exodus water-from-the-rock motif ([[Exodus 17.6|Exodus 17:6]]) and the "fountain of living waters" of Jeremiah, where Jehovah himself is identified as the living water.[^1] See [[Water Is a Better Symbol of Christ Than Wine]].
