# Philippians 2:12-13

> Work out your own salvation with fear and trembling. For it is God which worketh in you both to will and to do of his good pleasure. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Paul's synergistic language — believers "work out" their salvation *while* God works in them. Cited against a faith-alone-with-no-works-required reading; salvation involves active human cooperation with grace.
