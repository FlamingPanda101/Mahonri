# John 17.11

> And now I am no more in the world, but these are in the world, and I come to thee. Holy Father, keep through thine own name those whom thou hast given me, that they may be one, as we are. (John 17:11, KJV)

**Key term.** "Thy name... which thou hast given me" — read here as an *honorific* divine name (the Name) conferred on the Son, with power "to make them one." The oneness is the indwelling unity (*they may be one, as we are one*) extended to the disciples, not consubstantiality.
