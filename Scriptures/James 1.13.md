# James 1:13

> "Let no man say when he is tempted, I am tempted of God: for God cannot be tempted with evil, neither tempteth he any man." (KJV)

## Use in the vault

Cited by [[Ether's Elephant]] (with [[1 Corinthians 14.33|1 Corinthians 14:33]]) to argue that God is not the author of evil or of Satan's rebellious nature — Lucifer *chose* to rebel. This supports the Latter-day Saint claim, developed in [[Jesus and Satan Are Brothers Only If Nothing Is Created]], that evil is self-originated through agency rather than designed into a created being. See also [[Moses 4]] and the broader cluster at [[Satan - Development of the Figure]].
