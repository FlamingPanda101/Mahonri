# Matthew 28:19

> Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. *(KJV)*

## Key phrase
"**Go ye therefore, and teach all nations**" — the Great Commission, the epigraph of Miranda Wilcox's Afterword on medieval Christians.

## Apologetic / historical application
[[Miranda Wilcox]] opens the Medieval Afterword with this verse to frame her thesis: "Ancient and medieval Christians followed Christ's command to preach the gospel of Christ across Europe, Africa, and Asia." The geographic spread of Christianity "from China to Ireland, from Ethiopia and India to Iceland and Poland" is presented as obedience to this commission — a reason to revere medieval Christians rather than dismiss them as apostates. See [[Medieval Christians Kept the Light of Christ Alive Not Apostates]].
