# Mormon 7:5

> Know ye that ye must come to the knowledge of your fathers, and repent of all your sins and iniquities, and believe in Jesus Christ, that he is the Son of God, and that he was slain by the Jews, and by the power of the Father he hath risen again, whereby he hath gained the victory over the grave; and also in him is the sting of death swallowed up. *(Mormon 7:5, Book of Mormon)*

[[Mormon]]'s closing exhortation again deploys the "sting of death swallowed up" language.

## Why it's cited
The latest of the Book of Mormon occurrences of the "sting of death" motif (after [[Mosiah 16.7-8|Mosiah 16]] and [[Alma 22.14|Alma 22]]). The Ward Radio panel cites the chain as a deliberate intertextual thread traceable to a brass-plates Isaiah source — Paul's [[1 Corinthians 15.55|1 Corinthians 15:55]] quoting the same lost Isaiah preserved in the Gospel of Nicodemus.
