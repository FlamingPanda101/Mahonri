# Twelfth Article of Faith — Subject to kings, presidents, rulers, and magistrates

> We believe in being subject to kings, presidents, rulers, and magistrates, in obeying, honoring, and sustaining the law.

## Key Detail

The scriptural basis LDS leaders cited (with [[Doctrine and Covenants 134]]) for the Church's accommodationist posture in Nazi Germany — the "go-to response" when Nazi officials probed members' view of the government. Used in the historical analysis of whether that posture was cowardice or a documented response to explicit Gestapo coercion.
