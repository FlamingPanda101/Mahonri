# Exodus 4:21

> And the LORD said unto Moses, When thou goest to return into Egypt, see that thou do all those wonders before Pharaoh, which I have put in thine hand: but I will harden his heart, that he shall not let the people go. *(KJV)*

## The Hebrew
The verb here is אֲחַזֵּק (*ăḥazzēq*, piel of חזק *ḥāzaq*), "I will make strong / make firm / strengthen" the heart — one of three distinct Hebrew verbs used for the hardening across Exodus (also כבד *kāḇēḏ*, "make heavy," and קשׁה *qāšâ*, "make hard/stubborn"). The first-person form makes God the explicit agent *before* the plagues begin.
