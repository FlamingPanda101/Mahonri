# 1 Nephi 15:9 — "The Lord maketh no such thing known unto us"

> And I said unto them: Have ye inquired of the Lord? And they said unto me: We have not; for the Lord maketh no such thing known unto us.

## Key Detail

David Butler / Bryce Day read Laman and Lemuel's reply as *ideological*, not a mere personality defect: they are "good deuteronomists" who deny personal revelation and dismiss Lehi as a "visionary man" — the very thing Deuteronomy says to reject. This frames the family conflict as egalitarianism (the mysteries are for everyone) versus deuteronomist elitism.
