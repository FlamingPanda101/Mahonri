# Moroni 7 — The Light of Christ; judging good from evil

> For behold, the Spirit of Christ is given to every man, that he may know good from evil; wherefore, I show unto you the way to judge. (v. 16)

## Key Detail

Mormon's sermon grounds an *eternal* (not emergent) moral sense: the Light of Christ gives every person the ability to know good from evil. Cited as the LDS answer to the moral grounding problem — objective morality rooted in "things as they really are" and an uncreated moral agency, against Dawkins' contingent "zeitgeist."
