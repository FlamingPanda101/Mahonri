# Mosiah 5:7

> And now, because of the covenant which ye have made ye shall be called the children of Christ, his sons, and his daughters; for behold, this day he hath spiritually begotten you; for ye say that your hearts are changed through faith on his name; therefore, ye are born of him and have become his sons and his daughters. *(Book of Mormon)*

## Key point
By **covenant** (baptism), believers are "spiritually begotten" and become Christ's "sons and daughters" — so Christ is "Father" in a *covenantal/adoptive* sense. The panel uses this to disambiguate the "Father and Son" language of [[Mosiah 15.1-5|Mosiah 15]]: Christ is Father by creation ([[Mosiah 3.8|Mosiah 3:8]]) and by covenant adoption (here), and Son by the flesh — none of which makes him the *person* of the Father. See [[Mosiah 15 Father and Son Explained]].
