# Luke 3:2

> Annas and Caiaphas being the high priests, the word of God came unto John the son of Zacharias in the wilderness. *(KJV)*

**Key term:** "the word of God came unto John" — the OT prophetic idiom ("the word of God came to…") applied to **John the Baptist**, marking him as a prophet who receives a divine message. Trent Horn cites it as the NT continuation of the OT pattern where "word of God" = prophetic speech-event, not Scripture.
