# Ether 2:3

> And they did also carry with them deseret, which, by interpretation, is a honey bee; and thus they did carry with them swarms of bees, and all manner of that which was upon the face of the land, seeds of every kind. *(KJV/BoM)*

## Key phrase
"**Deseret, which, by interpretation, is a honey bee... seeds of every kind.**" The Jaredites carry honeybees (*deseret*) and "seeds of every kind" as they leave the tower toward the promised land.

## Apologetic / historical application
[[Mike Day]] connects this to the holy of holies in the "Lehi's Journey" episode: the **bee** is *Deborah* (*d'vorah*), and the temple's innermost room, the *debir*, shares the consonants — so the holy of holies is "the room of the bee," the place of manna (which tastes like honey) and the land of "milk and honey." The Jaredites carrying bees and "seeds of every kind" echoes God in the holy of holies beckoning "everyone to come" — seeds of every kind (cf. the land Bountiful = Eden).
