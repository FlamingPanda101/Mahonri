# D&C 116 — Adam-ondi-Ahman

> Spring Hill is named by the Lord Adam-ondi-Ahman, because, said he, it is the place where Adam shall come to visit his people, or the Ancient of Days shall sit, as spoken of by Daniel the prophet. *(D&C 116)*

## Key Detail

The brief revelation identifying **Spring Hill, Daviess County, Missouri** as Adam-ondi-Ahman — the place tied to [[Adam]] and the future gathering "where the Ancient of Days shall sit" (cf. [[Daniel 7.13-14|Daniel 7]]). Together with [[D&C 107|D&C 107:53–56]] (Adam gathering his righteous posterity three years before his death to bestow a last blessing, and prophesying to the last generation), it forms the textual basis of the Latter-day Saint Adam-ondi-Ahman teaching.

On [[Ward Radio - Adam-Ondi-Ahman Revealed in the Apocrypha]], [[Jonah (Plain and Precious Publishing)|Jonah Barnes]] argues the *details* around this site — Adam as a righteous priest/prophet, the pre-death assembly of his posterity, the prophecy to the last generation, and a "Book of Eve" — match recently recovered Adam apocrypha (the *Life of Adam and Eve* in its Greek, Latin, Armenian, Georgian, and Egyptian versions) that Joseph Smith could not have known. See [[Adam Gathering His Posterity Before Death Matches the Adam Apocrypha]] and [[The Book of Eve Has Eve Dictate the First Scripture]].
