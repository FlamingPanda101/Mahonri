# Leviticus 19:18

> Thou shalt not avenge, nor bear any grudge against the children of thy people, but thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself: I am the LORD.

**Hebrew note.** "Love thy neighbour as thyself" renders *wĕ'āhavtā lĕrēʿăkā kāmôkā* (וְאָהַבְתָּ לְרֵעֲךָ כָּמוֹךָ). Jesus elevates this verse to the second-greatest commandment (cf. [[Matthew 22.37-39|Matt 22:37-39]], [[Mark 12.30-31|Mark 12:30-31]]); it is the Old Testament root of [[Kyle Beshears]]'s argument that loving the neighbor presupposes understanding the neighbor.
