# Acts 4:12 — "none other name... whereby we must be saved"

> Neither is there salvation in any other: for there is none other name under heaven given among men, whereby we must be saved. *(Acts 4:12, KJV)*

## Key Detail
- Peter's declaration, the second proof-text (with [[John 14.6]]) for **soteriological exclusivism**. [[David Paulsen]] grants the premise is "clearly warranted on biblical authority," which is what makes the [[The Soteriological Problem of Evil - The Inconsistent Triad|inconsistent triad]] bite — and why the resolution must *add* a premise (postmortem opportunity) rather than deny exclusivism.
