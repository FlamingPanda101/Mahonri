# Ezekiel 47:8 (KJV) — Healing waters flowing from the temple

> Then said he unto me, These waters issue out toward the east country... which being brought forth into the sea... the waters shall be healed.

## Key Detail

One of the postexilic prophetic images (with [[Zechariah 8.4-5]], Isaiah 65, and Joel 2) that supplied the "Day of the Lord" eschatology later Christians reused. Used to show Christian end-time hope was *inherited* from Second Temple Judaism — the question for early believers was "when, not if."
