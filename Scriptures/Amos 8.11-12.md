# Amos 8:11–12 — A Famine of Hearing the Words of the LORD

> "Behold, the days come, saith the Lord GOD, that I will send a famine in the land, not a famine of bread, nor a thirst for water, but of hearing the words of the LORD: And they shall wander from sea to sea, and from the north even to the east, they shall run to and fro to seek the word of the LORD, and shall not find it." (KJV)

A standard LDS apostasy proof text (alongside [[Amos 3.7|Amos 3:7]]), invoked by Day as a thematic backdrop to the New Testament's own apostasy warnings: a coming "famine" of divine revelation. The original oracle is an 8th-century-BCE judgment on the northern kingdom of Israel.
