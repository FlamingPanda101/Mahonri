# 1 Kings

The first book of Kings, covering Solomon's reign, the division of the kingdom into Israel (north) and Judah (south), and the subsequent histories of both kingdoms. [[Jacob Wright]] argues that the book of Kings (along with the Davidic stories) represents the "palace history" of Judah, and that the "united monarchy" narrative of 1 Kings 1–11 is a later political fabrication designed to claim the north's legacy.
