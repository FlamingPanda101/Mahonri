# Doctrine and Covenants 122:9 — "thy days are known"

> ...therefore, hold on thy way ... fear not what man can do, for God shall be with you forever and ever. ... thy days are known, and thy years shall not be numbered less... *(D&C 122:9, revelation given to Joseph Smith in Liberty Jail, March 1839)*

## Significance
Part of the Liberty Jail revelation (D&C 121–123). The promise that Joseph's "days are known" and "years shall not be numbered less" is cited in the martyrdom discussion as evidence **against** the "inside job" theory: if the Lord had fixed Joseph's term, his death at the appointed time was foreknown, and he returned from Liberty Jail acting like "a man in a hurry" who knew his time was short. It pairs with the separate "promise of 5 years" reminiscence.
