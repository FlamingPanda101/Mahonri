# Romans 11:26

> And so all Israel will be saved … *(NRSV)*

## Significance
[[Thomas A. Wayment]] points to this verse as Paul's own counter to the **supersessionism** of later writers like [[Justin Martyr]], who claimed God had ended his covenant with the Jews and withdrawn the prophetic Spirit. Paul affirms a continuing covenant relationship, contrasting with the "prophecy has ceased among the Jews" argument used to police revelation. See [[Closing the Canon Was Used to Declare Prophecy Ended]].
