# Revelation 2:17

> "To him that overcometh will I give to eat of the hidden manna, and will give him a white stone, and in the stone a new name written, which no man knoweth saving he that receiveth it." — Revelation 2:17 (KJV)

The promise of a **white stone bearing a new name "which no man knoweth"** to the one who overcomes. [[Joseph Smith]] quotes the verse in [[D&C 130.20-21|D&C 130]] and teaches that everyone who enters the celestial kingdom receives such a stone. [[Don Bradley]] links it to the **brother of Jared's white stones and withheld name** in [[Ether 3.14|Ether 3]] (the man is named only "the brother of Jared" because his name is sacredly withheld) — the same theme of a secret/new name conferred when one is admitted into the divine presence, which he reads as an endowment motif already present in the Book of Mormon.
