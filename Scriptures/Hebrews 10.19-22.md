# Hebrews 10:19-22

> Having therefore, brethren, boldness to enter into the holiest by the blood of Jesus, by a new and living way, which he hath consecrated for us, through the veil, that is to say, his flesh; And having an high priest over the house of God; Let us draw near with a true heart in full assurance of faith... *(KJV)*

## Key phrase
"**Boldness to enter into the holiest by the blood of Jesus... through the veil, that is to say, his flesh**" — the torn veil signifies unrestricted access to God's presence through Christ's atonement.

## Apologetic application
[[Gavin (Catholic Debater)]] cites it as "Christ is the veil torn open," arguing access to God is universal and not institutionally gated by progressive worthiness. See [[Omnibenevolence vs Conditional Access Models]].
