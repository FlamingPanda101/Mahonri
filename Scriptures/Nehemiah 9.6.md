# Nehemiah 9:6

> Thou, even thou, art LORD alone; thou hast made heaven, the heaven of heavens, with all their host, the earth, and all things that are therein, the seas, and all that is therein, and thou preservest them all; and the host of heaven worshippeth thee. *(KJV)*

## Key Term: "the host of heaven"
[[Gavin Ortlund]] cites this verse to argue the "host of heaven" — the *elohim* / divine beings (angels) — are themselves *made* by God and worship him, supporting the view that only Yahweh is uncreated ("species-unique," per [[Michael Heiser]]). On Ortlund's reading God "draws them into being; they are not eternal." Latter-day Saints, rejecting creatio ex nihilo, read "made" as *organization* of pre-existing beings/intelligences rather than creation from nothing.
