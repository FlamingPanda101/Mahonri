# Words of Mormon 1:7 — Mormon adds the small plates "for a wise purpose"

> And I do this for a wise purpose; for thus it whispereth me, according to the workings of the Spirit of the Lord which is in me. And now, I do not know all things; but the Lord knoweth all things which are to come; wherefore, he worketh in me to do according to his will. *(Words of Mormon 1:7, KJV-style)*

## Key Detail
Mormon explains that, having already abridged the large plates down to King Benjamin, he found the small plates of Nephi and was prompted to append them "for a wise purpose" he does not understand (vv. 3–7). On the LDS reading this is providential: the small plates replace the content of the lost 116 pages. The verse is central to [[Mike Day]]'s argument that the small plates were translated *last* yet are cross-referenced throughout the earlier-dictated text.
