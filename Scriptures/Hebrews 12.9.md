# Hebrews 12:9

> Furthermore we have had fathers of our flesh which corrected us, and we gave them reverence: shall we not much rather be in subjection unto the Father of spirits, and live? *(KJV)*

## Significance
"The Father of spirits" is a frontline LDS prooftext for literal divine sonship and premortal existence: God is contrasted with "fathers of our flesh" as the Father of our *spirits*. The Ward Radio panel stacks it with [[Acts 17.28-29|Acts 17:28-29]] ("offspring of God"), [[Romans 8.16|Romans 8:16]] ("children of God"), and [[Jeremiah 1.5|Jeremiah 1:5]] against the creedal claim that humans are God's children only by adoption.
