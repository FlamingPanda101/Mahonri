# Doctrine and Covenants 132:63

> But if one or either of the ten virgins, after she is espoused, shall be with another man, she has committed adultery... for they are given unto him to multiply and replenish the earth, according to my commandment, and to fulfil the promise which was given by my Father before the foundation of the world, and for their exaltation in the eternal worlds, that they may bear the souls of men; for herein is the work of my Father continued, that he may be glorified. *(current text)*

## In context

In the revelation on eternal and plural marriage, plural wives are said to be given to a man "to multiply and replenish the earth" and "to bear the souls of men." Latter-day Saints on [[Ward Radio]] cite this (with [[Jacob 2.30]]) as the **explicit, scripturally stated purpose** of polygamy — "to raise up seed" — rebutting critics who say the reason was secret. [[Stephen Cranney]] then argues the demographic data supports that purpose: plural marriage raised the *marriage rate*, and societies practicing polygyny show higher fertility. See [[Polygamy Raised Fertility by Raising the Marriage Rate]].
