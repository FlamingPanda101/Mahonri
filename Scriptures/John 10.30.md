# John 10:30

> I and my Father are one. *(KJV)*

## Used against the Church
"Checkmate — the Father and Son are *one*, so the Trinity (one essence) is taught in the Bible."

## In context
Latter-day Saints affirm the Father and Son are "one" — but **one in unity, mission, and purpose**, not one essence/substance. Read alongside [[John 17.20-23|John 17:20-23]], where Jesus prays that the disciples "may be one" *as* he and the Father are one — no one thinks the disciples become one substance. The whole dispute turns on the [[Definition of Gospel|definition of "one"]]. See [[John 10-30 - I and the Father Are One Rebutted]].
