# Ether 3 — The brother of Jared sees the Lord

> And it came to pass that when the brother of Jared had said these words, behold, the Lord stretched forth his hand and touched the stones one by one with his finger ... And ... the brother of Jared ... saw the finger of the Lord; and it was as the finger of a man, like unto flesh and blood ... Behold, I am he who was prepared from the foundation of the world to redeem my people ... Behold, this body, which ye now behold, is the body of my spirit ... and even as I appear unto thee to be in the spirit will I appear unto my people in the flesh. *(Ether 3:6-16, abridged — Book of Mormon)*

## Key Detail
In Ether 3 the **[[Brother of Jared]]** ascends Mount Shelem with sixteen small molten stones and prays for the Lord to touch them so they will give light inside the Jaredite barges. When the Lord touches the stones, the brother of Jared sees **the Lord's finger** "as the finger of a man," is overcome, and — because of his extraordinary faith — is then brought **through the veil** into the full presence of the premortal **Christ**, who reveals that the body shown is "the body of my spirit." He receives a sweeping revelation of all things and is given **two interpreters** (white stones) sealed up with the record. The chapter is the Book of Mormon's most explicit pre-incarnation theophany.

## Why it's cited
Ether 3 is load-bearing for several Restoration apologetics:
- **An embodied, pre-existent, distinct Christ.** The Lord appears with a spirit-body in the form of a man before the incarnation — used to argue the Book of Mormon preserves the corporeal, pre-mortal Messiah that the vault's Deuteronomist thesis says was later edited out.
- **A temple-endowment ascent pattern.** [[Don Bradley]] and others read the veil dialogue, the redemption-from-the-fall, the new white stones, and the *withheld name* (LDS tradition: Mahonri Moriancumer) as a depiction of the temple endowment predating Joseph Smith's 1842 Freemasonry. See [[The Brother of Jared Models the Endowment Ascent Through the Veil]] and [[The Jaredite and Nephite Interpreters Carry the Endowment Ascent Pattern]].
- **Interpreters shaped like compass and square.** Bradley argues the interpreters received here were shaped like the Masonic compass and square (per [[Joseph Smith Sr]] and Lucy Mack Smith), grounding that symbolism in antiquity rather than 1840s Masonry. See [[The Nephite Interpreters Were Shaped Like the Masonic Compass and Square]].
