# Hebrews 11:17

> By faith Abraham, when he was tried, offered up Isaac: and he that had received the promises offered up his only begotten son. *(KJV)*

## Key phrase
"**Offered up his only begotten son**" — [[Isaac]] is called Abraham's "only begotten son" even though [[Ishmael (son of Abraham)|Ishmael]] was born first.

## Apologetic / historical application
[[Stick of Joseph|The Stick of Joseph]] uses this in the "Jesus and Satan are brothers" episode as evidence that **the chosen son need not be the biological firstborn**: Isaac, the clear type of Christ in the Akedah, is named the "only begotten" though Ishmael preceded him. The hosts generalize the pattern — across Cain/Abel, Ishmael/Isaac, Esau/Jacob, the firstborn-by-birth is repeatedly passed over for the chosen son — and apply it speculatively to premortal Christ (the chosen, "firstborn" by righteousness) and Lucifer ("son of the morning"). See [[Two Brothers Where the Younger Is Chosen Are Types of Christ and Lucifer]].
