# 2 Nephi 9:21 — Christ suffers the pains of every living creature

> And he cometh into the world that he may save all men if they will hearken unto his voice; for behold, he suffereth the pains of all men, yea, the pains of every living creature, both men, women, and children, who belong to the family of Adam. *(2 Nephi 9:21, Book of Mormon)*

## Key Term
- **"every living creature… who belong to the family of Adam"** — [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|David Butler]] reads "the family of Adam" as extending beyond human beings to *every living thing*, because **Eve (Ḥawwah) is "the mother of every living thing"** ([[Genesis 3.21|Genesis 3]]). This connects Jacob's atonement doctrine to the divine-feminine motif that **Shaddai is the Mistress of the *hayot* (living creatures)** — the beasts that, at the Waters of Mormon, mark the presence of the Mother.
