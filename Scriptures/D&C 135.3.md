# D&C 135:3 — "done more, save Jesus only"

> Joseph Smith, the Prophet and Seer of the Lord, has done more, save Jesus only, for the salvation of men in this world, than any other man that ever lived in it. *(D&C 135:3, penned by [[John Taylor]])*

## Key Detail
- [[John Taylor]]'s tribute in the martyrdom announcement. [[David Paulsen]] invokes it precisely in the soteriological context: Joseph's claim to have done "more, save Jesus only, for the salvation of men" rests on his having restored the knowledge and priesthood powers that make the **redemption of the dead** possible — reopening salvation to the uncounted millions who never heard of Christ.
