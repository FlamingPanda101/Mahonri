# D&C 110

> "The veil was taken from our minds, and the eyes of our understanding were opened. We saw the Lord standing upon the breastwork of the pulpit... After this vision closed, the heavens were again opened unto us; and Moses appeared before us, and committed unto us the keys of the gathering of Israel... Elias appeared, and committed the dispensation of the gospel of Abraham... Elijah the prophet... stood before us." — D&C 110:1, 11-13, 15

The record of the Kirtland Temple vision (3 April 1836) in which [[Joseph Smith]] and [[Oliver Cowdery]] report seeing Jesus, Moses, Elias, and Elijah, who deliver priesthood keys. One of Oliver Cowdery's additional supernatural claims.
