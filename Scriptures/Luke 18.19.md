# Luke 18:19

> And Jesus said unto him, Why callest thou me good? none is good, save one, that is, God. *(KJV)*

## Significance
The "no one is good but God" half of the dilemma in the appeal to sinfulness: if all good people go to heaven but none is good but God, then (taken woodenly) no one is saved. The video presses: "if none is good but God, *whose fault is that?*" — God being the creator and sustainer of every soul. See [[Hell - Appeal to Sinfulness Rebutted]] and [[Hell - Finite Sin vs Infinite Punishment]].
