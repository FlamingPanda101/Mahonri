# James 1:5

> "If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him." (KJV)

The verse Joseph Smith reads in [[Joseph Smith History 1.19|Joseph Smith—History 1:11–13]] that prompts the First Vision. [[Kerry Muhlestein]] describes it (to a Protestant pastor) as "a blast of light in the midst of [the] cloud of chaos" — moving the young Joseph from feeling "very powerless" to realizing "there is something I can do here": ask God directly for wisdom, after he had given up finding answers from family or competing ministers. See [[The Smith Family Religious Division Framed the First Vision]].
