# 2 Timothy 2:2 (KJV) — Commit to faithful men who shall teach others

> And the things that thou hast heard of me among many witnesses, the same commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others also.

## Key Detail

Used in the *sola scriptura* rebuttal: Paul commits the "deposit" to faithful men for an *ongoing oral* transmission ("able to teach others also"), not to the bare written text. This cuts against reading [[2 Timothy 3.16-17|2 Tim 3:16-17]]'s "profitable" as a claim that scripture alone is the complete and sufficient rule of faith.
