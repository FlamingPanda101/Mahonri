# Psalm 8:4

> What are human beings that you are mindful of them, mortals that you care for them? *(NRSV)*

## Significance
The psalmist's question opens [[Gaye Strathearn]]'s discussion of human nature. His own answer reflects "a very positive understanding of human nature": God made humans "a little lower than" himself, "crowned them with glory and honor," and gave them dominion (Psalm 8:5–8 NRSV). The NRSV here follows the Septuagint ("a little lower than [God/himself]") rather than the KJV/Masoretic "a little lower than the angels." Early Christians were fascinated by why God placed humans in this privileged, near-divine position above the rest of creation.
