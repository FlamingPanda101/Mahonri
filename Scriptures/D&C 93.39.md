# D&C 93.39 — light and truth lost "through the tradition of their fathers"

> And that wicked one cometh and taketh away light and truth, through disobedience, from the children of men, and because of the tradition of their fathers. *(D&C 93:39)*

## Key Detail
- The verse names **inherited "tradition of their fathers"** — alongside personal disobedience — as how "that wicked one" drains light and truth from people. [[Todd McLauchlin]] uses it to argue that [[Magnifying a Calling Means Coming Clean From the Blood and Sins of This Generation|coming clean from the blood and sins of this generation]] must include repenting of *inherited* patterns, not only one's own sins — so that one "brings the generations with you" into Christ (a tie to redeeming-the-dead / temple work). Sits within D&C 93's "light of truth... was not created" framework.
