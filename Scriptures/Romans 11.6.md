# Romans 11:6

> And if by grace, then is it no more of works: otherwise grace is no more grace. But if it be of works, then is it no more grace: otherwise work is no more work. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Quoted by the Council of Trent to affirm that the **initial** grace of justification cannot be merited by works — grace and merit are mutually exclusive at that first moment. Anchors the "initial justification by grace alone" half of the Catholic position.
