# Joel 2:30-31

> And I will shew wonders in the heavens and in the earth, blood, and fire, and pillars of smoke. The sun shall be turned into darkness, and the moon into blood, before the great and terrible day of the LORD come. *(KJV)*

## Key phrase
"**The great and terrible day of the LORD**" — preceded by cosmic wonders: "blood, and fire, and pillars of smoke," the sun darkened and the moon turned to blood.

## Apologetic / historical application
[[Nicholas J. Frederick]] cites Joel among the postexilic Hebrew prophets whose "day of the Lord" eschatology Christianity *inherited*. The imagery of cosmic upheaval before divine vindication shaped early Christian expectations of the end. See [[Christianity Inherited Jewish Day-of-the-Lord Eschatology]].
