# Jeremiah

The Book of Jeremiah contains the prophecies of [[Jeremiah|Jeremiah the prophet]], who proclaimed Jerusalem's destruction and was imprisoned for it. [[Jacob Wright]] uses Jeremiah as evidence of the tension within the biblical tradition between those who insisted on invulnerability ("Jerusalem is never going to be destroyed") and those who insisted on facing the reality of defeat — a tension that continues "into modern Israeli politics."
