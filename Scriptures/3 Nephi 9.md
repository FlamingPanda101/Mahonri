# 3 Nephi 9 — the voice of Christ in the darkness; "baptized... with fire"

> And behold, that great city Zarahemla have I burned with fire... O all ye that are spared because ye were more righteous than they, will ye not now return unto me, and repent of your sins, and be converted, that I may heal you?... And ye shall offer for a sacrifice unto me a broken heart and a contrite spirit. *(3 Nephi 9:3, 13, 20)* — "...and as many of the Lamanites as believed in their preaching... were baptized with fire and with the Holy Ghost, and they knew it not." *(cf. 3 Nephi 9:20)*

## Key Detail
- After the destruction at the Crucifixion, the voice of the risen Christ calls survivors to repentance and a sacrifice of "a broken heart and a contrite spirit." [[Todd McLauchlin]] pairs its "baptized with fire... and they knew it not" with Lehi and Nephi's mass conversion of the Lamanites as a model of redemption [[Magnifying a Calling Means Coming Clean From the Blood and Sins of This Generation|"magnified into" a whole people through a leader's sacrifice]] — alongside [[Ether 12|Ether 12]] and [[Mosiah 5.7|Mosiah 5:7]].
