# Acts 17:10-11

> And the brethren immediately sent away Paul and Silas by night unto Berea: who coming thither went into the synagogue of the Jews. These were more noble than those in Thessalonica, in that they received the word with all readiness of mind, and searched the scriptures daily, whether those things were so. *(KJV)*

## Key Greek term
**ἀνακρίνω (anakrinō)** — "examined, scrutinized, sifted" (rendered "searched"). A term of judicial investigation; the Bereans actively tested Paul's preaching against scripture.

## Significance
Kelly's model text for *sola scriptura* in practice: even an apostle's preaching is checked against the (already-recognized) scriptures. **Counter-reading in the debate:** the "scriptures" the Bereans searched were the **Old Testament**, which presupposes an already-existing recognized body of writings rather than establishing the boundaries or infallibility of a 66-book canon. Hayden grants scripture is *used* as a test but denies this designates the modern Protestant canon as the *ultimate* authority.
