# Luke 11:47-51

> Woe unto you! for ye build the sepulchres of the prophets, and your fathers killed them... That the blood of all the prophets, which was shed from the foundation of the world, may be required of this generation; From the blood of Abel unto the blood of Zacharias, which perished between the altar and the temple: verily I say unto you, It shall be required of this generation. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Jesus rebukes hand-washing critics by invoking "the blood of Abel unto the blood of Zacharias." Per this episode, this is not generic "A to Z" but a reference to two specific **apocryphal blood traditions** — blood that could not be washed away — known to his audience. See [[Blood of Abel to Zechariah - Not A to Z]].
