# Isaiah 2:9

> And the mean man boweth down, and the great man humbleth himself: therefore forgive them not. *(Isaiah 2:9, KJV)*

> Enter into the rock, and hide thee in the dust, for fear of the LORD, and for the glory of his majesty. *(Isaiah 2:10, KJV)*

## Textual Note

The second half of Isaiah 2:9 ("therefore forgive them not") together with all of Isaiah 2:10 is **absent from the Great Isaiah Scroll** ([[Donald W. Parry|1QIsaᵃ]]). [[Alex OConnor|Alex O'Connor]] cited this gap as a concrete example that the Qumran Isaiah is not "word for word identical" to the Masoretic Text; [[Gavin Ortlund|Gavin Ortlund]] countered that ~20 other Qumran Isaiah copies do contain the passage. See [[Dan McClellan - Correcting Alex OConnor and Gavin Ortlund]].

[[Dan McClellan]] supplies the critical-scholarship verdict: scholars are in "pretty widespread agreement" that 1QIsaᵃ preserves the **earlier reading** and that 2:9b–2:10 is a **secondary addition** to the text. One piece of supporting evidence is that the other Qumran manuscripts which *do* contain 2:9b–2:10 disagree among themselves in how the verses read — the instability expected of a later insertion. So Ortlund's appeal that "nothing was lost" is misleading: the shorter 1QIsaᵃ text is likely the more original, and the Masoretic plus is the secondary growth.
