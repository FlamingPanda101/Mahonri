# Acts 11:27

> And in these days came prophets from Jerusalem unto Antioch. *(KJV)*

## Significance
A plain New Testament record of **prophets active after Christ's ministry** — direct counter-evidence to the claim (from [[Hebrews 1.1-2|Hebrews 1:1-2]]) that Jesus was the last prophet.
