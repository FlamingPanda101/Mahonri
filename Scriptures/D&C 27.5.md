# D&C 27:5 — The Keys of the Record of the Stick of Ephraim

> Behold, this is wisdom in me; wherefore, marvel not, for the hour cometh that I will drink of the fruit of the vine with you on the earth, and with **Moroni**, whom I have sent unto you to reveal the Book of Mormon, containing the fulness of my everlasting gospel; to whom I have committed **the keys of the record of the stick of Ephraim**. *(LDS Doctrine and Covenants)*

## Key Detail
The verse names [[Angel Moroni|Moroni]] not merely as the messenger who delivered the plates but as the holder of **priesthood keys tied to the ancient record itself** — "the stick of Ephraim" (cf. Ezekiel 37's stick of Joseph/Ephraim, the LDS reading of the Book of Mormon as the joined stick). In the [[The Stick of Joseph - Sacred Temple Connection to Priesthood Keys|Stick of Joseph interview]], this is offered as the concrete, citable instance of the broader pattern that *each dispensation head was committed keys over his stewardship* — here, keys over a record rather than over a people or an ordinance. The hosts use it to make the otherwise-abstract word "keys" land as something specific.
