# Acts 21:10

> And as we tarried there many days, there came down from Judaea a certain prophet, named Agabus. *(KJV)*

## Significance
Names a specific post-Christ prophet, **Agabus** — again refuting the "Jesus was the last prophet" reading of [[Hebrews 1.1-2|Hebrews 1:1-2]].
