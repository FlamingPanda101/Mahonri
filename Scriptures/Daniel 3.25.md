# Daniel 3:25

> He answered and said, Lo, I see four men loose, walking in the midst of the fire, and they have no hurt; and the form of the fourth is like the Son of God. *(KJV)*

## Significance
[[Nebuchadnezzar]]'s description of the fourth figure in the fiery furnace with Shadrach, Meshach,
and Abednego. [[Dan McClellan]] notes the Aramaic *bar elahin* is grammatically indefinite and plural
— "a son of gods," not "the Son of God." When supervising the church's 2015 Portuguese Bible, he told
ecclesiastical reviewers (who worried about chapel paintings depicting Jesus in the furnace) that the
text says "a son of gods"; they ultimately kept the accurate rendering. The case shows that
non-English Latter-day Saints already use Bibles diverging from the KJV at theologically loaded points.
