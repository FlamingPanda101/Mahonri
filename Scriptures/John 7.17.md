# John 7:17 (KJV) — Do his will, and you shall know the doctrine

> If any man will do his will, he shall know of the doctrine, whether it be of God, or whether I speak of myself.

## Key Detail

The "disposition precedes knowing" proof-text: spiritual knowledge answers a heart already willing to obey (cf. Alma's seed, [[Alma 32]]). Used pastorally to distinguish intellectual doubt from relational/moral struggle — without weaponizing "it's just your heart" against sincere questioners.
