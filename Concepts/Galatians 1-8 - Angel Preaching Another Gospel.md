# Galatians 1:8 — "If an Angel Preach Another Gospel"

## The Charge
> "Paul says if even an angel preaches a different gospel, let him be accursed.
> Angel Moroni brought Joseph Smith a different gospel — therefore Mormonism is
> cursed."

## The Verse (KJV)
> "But though we, or an angel from heaven, preach any other gospel unto you than
> that which we have preached unto you, let him be accursed." (Galatians 1:8)

## Rebuttal Points

### 1. Historical Context: The Judaizer Controversy
- The entire letter to the Galatians addresses ONE issue: **Judaizers** — Jewish
  Christians who insisted Gentile converts must be circumcised and keep the Law of
  Moses.
- Paul's "different gospel" was specifically "you must add Mosaic law-keeping to
  faith in Christ." The entire book of Galatians and Acts 15 (the Jerusalem Council)
  revolve around this.
- Dalton: "Out of all the things Paul could address, this is the one that took his
  primary attention in that big council with all the leadership of the church."

### 2. Angels Deliver Messages Throughout the Bible
If "an angel can't bring a message" is the principle, then:
- Gabriel announcing Jesus's birth to Mary must be rejected
- The angel announcing Christ's birth to the shepherds must be rejected
- The angel delivering Revelation to John must be rejected
- The angel speaking to Cornelius (Acts 10) must be rejected

The Bible consistently shows God uses angels as messengers.

### 3. Define "Gospel"
- LDS define the gospel as: Jesus Christ is the Savior, salvation by His grace
  (consistent with 1 Corinthians 15:1-4, the same Paul).
- "We believe in the same gospel as lined out by Paul in 1 Corinthians" (Dalton).
- Differences are in theology (nature of God, etc.), not the core gospel message.
- "If you are under the presupposition that we follow a different gospel, then
  you'll think this verse has more weight than it actually does" (Braden).

### 4. The Uno Reverse Card
The LDS Church claims to be a **restoration** of the original church — so by the
critic's own logic, post-apostolic innovations are the "different gospel."

## Bottom Line
Paul was writing against Judaizers, not a 19th-century restoration movement.
Angels are God's messengers throughout scripture, and definitions of "gospel"
must be agreed upon before this verse can be weaponized.
