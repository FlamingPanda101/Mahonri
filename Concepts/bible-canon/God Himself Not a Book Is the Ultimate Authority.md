# God Himself Not a Book Is the Ultimate Authority

## The Point
Carroll's positive alternative to Sola Scriptura is not "the Book of Mormon instead of the Bible" — that would repeat the same error. The Latter-day Saint answer to "what is the ultimate authority?" is **God Himself**, who can reveal truth in any way He pleases, including directly to our spirits by His Spirit. Scripture is a *product* of that revelation, not its ceiling. [^1]

## Evidence
1. **Revelation precedes and exceeds the text.** "How did people receive truth from God before the Bible was compiled?... God gave it to them, through His Spirit." The authors of the Bible knew what to write only because God revealed it — so the Spirit is logically prior to and more ultimate than the book. [^1]
2. **Paul grounds knowledge in the Spirit, not the text.** 1 Corinthians 2:9-14 — "the things of God knoweth no man, but the Spirit of God"; the natural man cannot receive them "because they are spiritually discerned." [^2]
3. **Even a Reformed confession concedes it.** The Westminster Confession (1.5) locates "full persuasion and assurance" of scripture's truth in "the inward work of the Holy Spirit, bearing witness by and with the Word in our hearts." [^2] (See [[James White Appeals to the Spirit Not the Bible for Assurance]].)
4. **Continuing revelation follows.** God "has not constrained himself to any amount of books"; "God can and continues to speak. Why would He stop?" This is *not* "feelings alone" — see [[Triangulating Truth - Five Mediums of Revelation]]. [^3]

## Best Quotes
> "What do we believe our ultimate authority is, if not the Bible?... GOD! God is the ultimate authority. And He can reveal the truth in any way that He pleases." [^1]

## LDS Connection
This is the Restoration's epistemic foundation — a living God who still speaks (continuing revelation, modern prophets, personal revelation) rather than a closed text as the terminal authority. It is the constructive payoff of [[The Three Problems with Sola Scriptura - Interpretation Logic Canon]] and pairs with [[Fallible Humans Cannot Produce an Infallible Bible]].
