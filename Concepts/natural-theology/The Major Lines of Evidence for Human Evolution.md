# The Major Lines of Evidence for Human Evolution

## The Point

[[Seth M. Bybee]], an evolutionary biologist at BYU, argues that the scientific case for human evolution is not one argument but a convergence of **independent lines of evidence** — the same lines used for evolution generally, applied to our own species. Humans did **not** evolve "from" modern apes; rather, humans and apes share a common ancestor, like cousins sharing grandparents. [^1] Each line — anatomy, ontogeny (development), vestigial traits, molecular biology, phylogeny, biogeography, the fossil record, and directly observed microevolution — independently points to descent with modification, and together they make human evolution, to scientists, "a proven scientific theory" with the same standing as heliocentrism or gravity. [^2]

## Evidence

- **Comparative anatomy (homology).** Distant vertebrate relatives (bats, whales, birds, humans) share the same set of arm bones; the pattern of shared anatomy sharpens as one moves from apes to the hominin fossil record toward modern human traits (upright posture, foot/pelvis/skull changes, brain complexity). [^3] (see [[Comparative Anatomy and Homology as Evidence for Common Ancestry]])
- **Ontogeny (development).** Human embryos pass through stages with a prominent set of gills and a well-formed tail, governed by the same (homologous) genes that build gills in fish; the gill structures become the human ear, the tail is reduced. [^4]
- **Vestigial traits.** Grasping muscles in the wrist and foot are reduced or absent in ~10% of humans (present in all apes); the appendix and the coccyx are rudimentary; humans are occasionally born with tails, showing the genetics persist in our DNA. [^5]
- **Molecular biology / genetics.** All life shares DNA with the same four bases read the same way; human and chimp genomes are nearly identical, and the chromosome-count difference is explained by the **fusion that produced human chromosome 2** and by inversions. Modern humans also carry DNA from interbreeding with **Neanderthals and Denisovans**. [^6] (see [[Genetic Evidence for Human Evolution - Shared DNA and Chromosome 2 Fusion]])
- **Phylogeny.** Trees built from anatomy or genetics independently recover chimps/bonobos as our closest living relatives and place fossil hominins near humans; parasite phylogenies (lice, pinworms) track their host hominids. [^7]
- **Biogeography.** *Homo sapiens* originated in Africa and migrated out; island dwarfism appears among hominins (e.g. *Homo floresiensis* and modern Flores pygmies). [^8]
- **Fossils.** As of 2024 roughly **thirty fossil hominin species** are known since the chimp–human split, showing clear transitions in brain and chin morphology toward modern humans. [^9]
- **Direct observation (microevolution).** Antibiotic/pesticide resistance, dog breeding (artificial selection), and ongoing changes in human eye color, complexion, lactose tolerance, and convergent light-skin adaptation in Europeans and Asians show evolution operating now. [^10]

## Best Quotes

> "The overwhelming scientific evidence provides an emphatic 'Yes!' to the question of whether humans evolved on this earth. Does that then mean there is still room for God in the creative process? A correspondingly overwhelming amount of evidence from the religious perspective would provide an equally emphatic 'Yes!'" [^11]

> "You did not come from or originate from your cousins, but you do share a common ancestor with your cousins (your grandparents). Likewise, humans did not come from modern apes, but we do share a common ancestor with apes." [^1]

## LDS Connection

Bybee insists the science "does not and cannot comment directly on the existence of God, the actual existence of Adam and Eve, our place before God, or how we personally view the Creation"; it speaks only "from the agnostic vantage point of the scientific method." [^12] On this view a Latter-day Saint can affirm that humans were created **on purpose and in God's image** *and* that the body was assembled by descent with modification — the "how" being left open ("nothing has been revealed concerning evolution"). Notably, BYU students taught this evidence alongside accurate Church teaching report that their testimonies slightly *increase*. [^13] The remaining questions about Adam and Eve are precisely the ones Bybee leaves to continuing revelation (D&C 101:32–34). See [[A Body from the Physical Creation Joined to a Premortal Spirit]] for how he houses a divine spirit in an evolved body.
