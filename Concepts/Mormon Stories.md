# Mormon Stories

Mormon Stories is a long-running podcast founded by John Dehlin that interviews current, questioning, and former Latter-day Saints about faith, doubt, and disaffection. It is one of the most prominent platforms in the "faith-transition" / ex-Mormon ecosystem and frequently hosts critics of the Church.

## Relevance

In the vault it is referenced as the kind of venue where [[Jeremy Runnells]] gave interviews about the [[CES Letter]] — clips of which apologists replay to argue that his stated "I just wrote it for the CES director and a few people on Reddit" origin story is contradicted by his own marketing of the letter.
