# Cult Accusation as Political Weapon

## The Claim
"Cult" and "brainwashed" have migrated from religion into political rhetoric, where they function as thought-terminating insults that obscure more than they explain.

## Supporting Evidence
- The left calls Trump voters a "cult of Trump"; the right calls opponents brainwashed by a "cult of wokeness" — the same empty accusation, both directions *(observation)* [^1].
- The remedy: understand the other person's **"definition of the situation"** — empathetically grasp *why* a view is appealing before disputing it — which makes genuine dialogue possible *(scholar)* [^2].

## Best Quote
> "The term brainwashing, like so many others, even like the term cult, it hides more than it reveals... It actually throws everybody off track." — [[Lorne Dawson]] [^3]

## Counter-arguments / related
- Rooted in the same myth dismantled in [[Brainwashing Does Not Exist]] and the loaded history in [[Cult - A Contaminated Pejorative Term]].
