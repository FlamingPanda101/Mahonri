# Theology

## Overview

**Theology** is systematic reasoning about God — the nature, attributes, and acts of the divine, and the doctrines that follow. As a reference tag in this vault it gathers the many notes that argue *about* doctrine, and it also names a recurring **critique**: that creedal Christianity over-prioritizes propositional theology at the expense of relationship and fruit.

## The Claim

A repeated move across the cluster is to subordinate theology to relationship and discipleship rather than treating right doctrine as the test of salvation:

- Identity and salvation are about [[Covenant Relationship Over Theology Quiz|covenant relationship, not passing a theology quiz]] — "salvation by theology damns almost everyone."
- The vault distinguishes [[Theological History vs Historical Theology|theological history from historical theology]] (how doctrine is studied vs. how it is asserted).
- It probes whether a tree's [[Fruit Versus Theology - Are They Separable|fruit and its theology are separable]], and warns against pinning theology to transient science ([[The Aquinas Fallacy - Pinning Theology to Current Science]]).
