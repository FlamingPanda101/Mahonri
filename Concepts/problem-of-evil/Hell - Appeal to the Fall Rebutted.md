# Hell — The Appeal to the Fall Rebutted

## The Claim (Objection Rebutted)
Humans lack the attributes/beliefs needed for salvation because of **the Fall** ([[Romans 5.12|Romans 5:12]]); the Fall was Adam's choice, not God's; therefore God is not responsible.

## The Rebuttal
- God still created Adam with the capacity to sin and **continues to create depraved souls from nothing** for all of human history after the Fall [^1].
- If the Fall doesn't stop God from creating *some* humans who meet salvation's requirements, why doesn't he create *all* humans that way? [^2]
- So either God is bound by Adam's act (impotent), or he freely creates souls he knows are hell-bound when he could create them otherwise (unjust) [^3].

## Best Quote
> "Why would Adam's fall result in God perpetually creating trillions of beings that are worthy of damnation when he could have just created them in such a way as to avoid being worthy of damnation?" [^1]
