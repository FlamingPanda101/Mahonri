# Refusing the Pious Fraud Category

## The Claim
[[Kyle Beshears]] declines to slot Joseph Smith into any of the standard explanatory boxes — "outright con," "pious fraud," or the other theories — saying he is "not comfortable yet kind of coming down on the categories." He treats the sincerity he found in the sources as an irreducible datum the tidy categories cannot absorb. [^1][^2]

## Supporting Evidence

1. **The "pious fraud" model is named and set aside.** Beshears explicitly references the framework most associated with [[Dan Vogel]] — Joseph as a sincere deceiver who lies for what he believes is a good end — and refuses to adopt it: "I'm not comfortable yet kind of like coming down on the categories of like was he a pious fraud or any of those theories." [^1] *Rating: notable;* a critic declining the most sophisticated anti-prophetic model is conceding that the evidence resists it.

2. **Sincerity is the data the categories must, but cannot, explain away.** "I will never throw away this element of sincerity behind him. I just don't follow him in where his sincerity led him." [^2] *Rating: strong;* it locates the dispute in *direction* (where sincerity led) rather than *authenticity* (whether it was real).

3. **Complexity over taxonomy.** The whole posture flows from his "extremely complicated man" conclusion (see [[Joseph Smith Was Sincere Not a Fraud]]); a single label ("con," "fraud," "deluded") would flatten what the primary sources showed him. [^2] *Rating: moderate-to-strong* as a historiographical virtue — resisting premature categorization.

**Steelman of the categorizers.** Historians need explanatory frameworks; "he was complicated" is not itself an explanation of the gold plates, the witnesses, or the revelations. A defender of the "pious fraud" model can argue that Beshears's refusal is really a polite suspension of judgment that leaves the central evidential questions unanswered. Beshears would reply that an honest "I don't have a box for this yet" is preferable to forcing the man into a category the documents contradict — and that the believing reader will note a hostile scholar conceding the standard categories fail.
