# Pre-Mortal Existence Dissolves the Problem of Hell

## The Claim
The video's constructive resolution: God did **not** create you from nothing. "You existed with him before you came to earth." You are the literal child of a real Heavenly Father, and Jesus is "his only begotten son in the flesh." Because intelligences/spirits were not manufactured *ex nihilo*, God is not the unilateral author of anyone's existence-with-its-fixed-ending — so the button was never God's alone to press, and the five-attribute contradiction dissolves [^1]. The closing exhortation: stop letting "the contradictions and inventions of men" stand between you and the Father, and "find your actual father in heaven" [^2].

## Supporting Evidence
1. **Denies premise (3) of the inconsistent set — rate: structurally decisive within LDS premises.** Of the five exits in [[Five Divine Attributes Cannot All Be True]], LDS theology takes "people aren't created from nothing." If agents are co-eternal and self-existent, God's creative act is *organizing* and *parenting* pre-existing intelligences, not conjuring doomed beings from nothing. This is the same root move as [[Free Will - Atheist Determinism vs LDS Co-Eternality]] and [[Creation Ex Nihilo Leads to Determinism]]; the resolution it pairs with is [[LDS Afterlife - Degrees of Glory]]. **Steelman of the classical theist:** (a) denying *ex nihilo* is a steep metaphysical price — it limits divine aseity/sovereignty and arguably makes God a demiurge among co-eternal realities rather than the absolute ground of being; (b) it does not, by itself, remove *foreknowledge*: if God still foreknows an intelligence will end in eternal loss yet "spirit-births" and embodies it, a residual version of the problem returns; the LDS answer leans on agency being genuinely self-originating, which is a further claim.
2. **Replaces binary heaven/hell with degrees of glory — rate: moderate, the softer-eschatology assist.** The problem of *hell* presupposes eternal conscious torment for the many; LDS eschatology mostly replaces this with graded salvation ([[LDS Progression Model vs Binary Heaven and Hell]], [[Degrees of Glory vs Heaven]]), reserving outer darkness for the rare "sons of perdition." This blunts premise (4) as well, even if the video foregrounds the *ex nihilo* denial. **Caution:** the video itself does not detail degrees of glory; it leans on the relational/pre-existence framing.
3. **Frames the conclusion experientially.** The appeal is finally to the witness of the Spirit ("the spirit whispers it to you... you can feel your heavenly father's love"), consistent with the channel's [[Spirit Over Argument - The Limits of Apologetics]] register. **Honest note:** this is an experiential, not a probative, move and will not persuade a skeptic on logic alone.

## Best Quotes

> "God did not create us from nothing just to send most of us to hell. You know this deep in your heart." [^2]

> "Stop letting the contradictions and inventions of men stand between you and him. Find your heavenly father. He is waiting for you." [^2]
