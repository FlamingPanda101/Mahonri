# Public Square Magazine

Public Square Magazine is an online Latter-day Saint publication featuring faith-friendly essays and commentary on culture, politics, and Church-related controversies, written largely by volunteer lay contributors. It serves as a venue for apologetic and devotional writing aimed at a general LDS audience rather than peer-reviewed scholarship.

## Relevance

The vault notes Public Square Magazine as the entry point for lay writer [[Michael Peterson]], who began contributing around 2021 and later co-authored the report arguing that the marketed origin story of [[Jeremy Runnells]]'s [[CES Letter]] was fabricated.
