# Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is the restorationist Christian church founded by Joseph Smith in 1830, headquartered in Salt Lake City, Utah. Its distinctive scriptures include the Book of Mormon, the Doctrine and Covenants, and the Pearl of Great Price alongside the Bible, and it is led by a prophet-president and the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles. It is the institution whose history, doctrine, and scripture this vault is principally concerned to explain and defend.

## Relevance

As the subject church of the apologetic project, it is the referent behind most of the vault's positions and the institution converts such as [[Avraham Gileadi]] join. It anchors discussions of authority, doctrine, and continuing revelation that recur throughout the [[Concepts]] notes.
