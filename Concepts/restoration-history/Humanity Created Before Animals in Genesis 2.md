# Genesis 2 Creates Animals After Humanity (Contradicting Genesis 1)

## The Claim

In the second creation account, God forms "every animal of the field and every bird of the air" *after* the human, in search of a fitting partner ([[Genesis 2.18-19|Genesis 2:18–19]]). This contradicts [[Genesis 1.24-26|Genesis 1:24–26]], where land animals are made on day six *before* humanity, and fish and birds on still-earlier days.[^1]

## Supporting Evidence

1. **Explicit post-human creation (decisive).** Genesis 2:18–19: "It is not good that the man should be alone... So out of the ground the LORD God formed every animal of the field and every bird of the air and brought them to the man." The animals are made to address the human's solitude, hence necessarily after him.[^2] Evidence rating: **decisive**.

2. **"Field" means the earth, not a domestic pen (strong).** McClellan: "this does not mean of the fenced in domestic field. This means of the earth," and "all of the birds of the air" — i.e., the full range of land animals and birds, all after humanity.[^1] Evidence rating: **strong** — blocks the harmonizing move that only *some* animals are in view.

3. **Genesis 1 reverses the order (decisive).** In Genesis 1, "the fish of the sea and the birds of the air were all created on a previous day. And on day six, the animals of the earth were created prior to the creation of humanity."[^3] Mutually exclusive sequences. Evidence rating: **decisive**.

**Steelman:** Harmonizers translate Genesis 2:19 as a pluperfect — "the LORD God *had formed* every animal" — making it a flashback consistent with Genesis 1's prior creation. This is a real grammatical option (the *wayyiqtol* can occasionally bear a pluperfect sense, and some translations adopt it). McClellan's reading follows the updated NRSV ("formed"), and on the dominant reading the verb is sequential, not pluperfect; the pluperfect rendering is widely regarded as harmonization-driven rather than the most natural reading, and it does not rescue the *plants* contradiction in any case. See [[Humanity Created Before Plants in Genesis 2]].

## Best Quotes

> "This directly contradicts the account in Genesis 1 which very clearly describes the creation of animal life prior to the creation of the first humans." [^2]

> "They were all created after humanity was created in Genesis 2. But in Genesis 1, the fish of the sea and the birds of the air were all created on a previous day. And on day six, the animals of the earth were created prior to the creation of humanity." [^3]
