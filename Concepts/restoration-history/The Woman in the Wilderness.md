# The Woman in the Wilderness

The Woman in the Wilderness is a Latter-day Saint devotional podcast/YouTube channel hosted by Joselyn, focused on scripture, the divine feminine, and the reframing of figures such as Eve. Its name evokes the Revelation 12 "woman in the wilderness" imagery. Its content is devotional commentary rather than peer-reviewed scholarship (C-tier in the vault).

## Relevance

The channel is the venue for the interview with historical linguist [[J Richard Lambert]] arguing that the traditional reading of Eve is a long mistranslation. Host Joselyn contributes the point that whereas *ha-adam* is formed from the dust, Eve is created *in Eden* — a "higher, holier place" — supporting the vault's notes that recast Eve as an elevated, Heavenly-Mother-type figure.
