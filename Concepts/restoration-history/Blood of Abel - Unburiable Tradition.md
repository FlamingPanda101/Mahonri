# Blood of Abel — The Unburiable Tradition

## The Claim
Apocryphal traditions hold that after [[Cain]] murdered [[Abel]], the earth refused to receive Abel's body — it would not stay buried, and his blood never dried — until [[Adam]] himself died and was buried first.

## Supporting Evidence
- The Armenian *History of the Sons of Adam*: "Whenever they buried Abel's body, it came out of the face of the ground... his blood never dried and his body did not stink nor become worm eaten" *(apocryphal primary)* [^1].
- The reason given: "the earth said, 'I will not allow anyone to be buried until Adam is buried first'" — Adam was first taken from the earth, so first to return *(apocryphal tradition)* [^2].
- One tradition: Abel's blood would not leave Cain's skin — possibly the "mark of Cain" *(apocryphal variant)* [^3].
- The canonical seed is [[Genesis 4|Genesis 4:10]] — Abel's blood "crieth from the ground."

## Best Quote
> "It's Abel's blood that couldn't be buried and Zachariah's blood that couldn't be buried. It's this blood that stains forever." — [[Jonah (Plain and Precious Publishing)|Jonah]] [^4]
