# Mormonism Research Ministry

Mormonism Research Ministry (MRM) is an evangelical Christian counter-cult and outreach organization focused on the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Based in the Salt Lake City area and led for decades by Bill McKeever and Eric Johnson, it produces literature, podcasts (e.g. *Viewpoint on Mormonism*), and street-evangelism resources aimed at equipping Protestants to engage Latter-day Saints and "proclaim the Protestant gospel." It is an advocacy ministry rather than an academic body.

## Relevance

MRM is named as the ministry affiliation of evangelical apologist [[Aaron Shafovaloff]], who volunteers with it (and the Utah Christian Research Center) in his Salt-Lake-area outreach to Latter-day Saints. The vault treats such ministry commentary as informed but popular-level (C-tier) rather than peer-reviewed scholarship.
