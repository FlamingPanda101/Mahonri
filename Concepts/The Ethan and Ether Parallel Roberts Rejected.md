# The Ethan and Ether Parallel Roberts Rejected

## The Claim (Objection Rebutted)
**Objection:** The [[CES Letter]] (quoting [[B.H. Roberts]]) notes that "Ethan is prominently connected with the recording of the matter in the one case and Ether in the other" — the similarity of the names *Ethan* (Smith) and *Ether* (Book of Mormon prophet) hints at borrowing.[^1]

**Rebuttal:** This is the clearest example of a manufactured non-parallel, and the critic's own cited authority disowned it. Immediately after the quoted sentence, Roberts wrote that this "is not a serious suggestion; it's the only most bare possibility; I would not seriously consider it." [[Jeremy Runnells]] quotes the setup but omits Roberts's disclaimer.[^2]

## The Rebuttal

1. **The cited authority repudiated the parallel (rated: strong, primary).** Roberts: "this however is not a serious suggestion, it's the only most bare possibility, I would not seriously consider it."[^2] He was "scraping the bare bottom of the barrel."
2. **Selective quotation misleads the reader (rated: strong, documented).** Runnells reproduces Roberts's name-comparison "but does not quote in that same like literally in the next sentence" the repudiation.[^2] This is a methodological tell about the reliability of the parallel list. *Steelman:* a defender of the list might say one bad entry doesn't sink the rest, and that Roberts at least found the resemblance worth mentioning. Smoot's reply is that Roberts mentioned it precisely to *dismiss* it, and that the broader list fails for the same reasons (see [[The View of the Hebrews Parallels Are Parallelomania]] and [[A Parallel List Without Textual Analysis Carries No Burden]]).
3. **Phonetic name-matching is methodologically worthless (rated: moderate, logical).** A loose sound-similarity between two names, with no etymological or textual link, is the kind of coincidence parallelomania manufactures by the dozen.

## Best Quotes

> "What Jeremy Runnells does not quote ... literally in the next sentence in Elder Roberts' manuscript he says this however is not a serious suggestion; it's the only most bare possibility; I would not seriously consider it."[^2]

> "He himself is like saying he's just scraping the bare bottom of the barrel there."[^2]
