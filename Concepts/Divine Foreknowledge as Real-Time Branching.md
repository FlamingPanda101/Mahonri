# Divine Foreknowledge as Real-Time Branching

## The Claim
[[Gavin (Catholic Debater)]]'s model for reconciling foreknowledge with agency: God watches decisions branch in real time and adjusts, "still in control while respecting your personal agency." He offers a **barometer** image — "as that barometer changes, God sees the plans as they change in real time" — and recommends the film *The Adjustment Bureau* as "a real-time visual of what each decision point branches out into in the grander scheme of things."[^1]

## Supporting Evidence
1. **Preserves agency + providence** — rate: weak as stated. The picture gestures at a dynamic-providence or open-future intuition (God responds to choices as they happen) while still affirming control. *(illustrative)* [^1]
2. **Pop-culture anchor** — the *Adjustment Bureau* reference is a vivid teaching device for branching counterfactuals. *(illustration)* [^1]

**Why it backfires on the affirmative (Hayden's angle):** the "real-time branching" model sits in tension with what Gavin *concedes elsewhere* — that God knows the *absolute future* with certainty *before* creating ([[The Five Incompatible Claims of Creedal Theism]]). If God truly sees the final outcome in advance (not merely reacting in real time), then the barometer image collapses back into the dilemma: he knew the end-state and created anyway. Conversely, if foreknowledge is genuinely *open* (God learns as choices unfold), that abandons creedal omniscience — one of the five claims Hayden says must be revised. Either way the branching picture does not dissolve [[The Creation Dilemma]]; it either restates exhaustive foreknowledge or quietly limits it. Hayden in rebuttal: "What I heard him say is that God is in control, which I'm not exactly sure what that means if people have free will."

## Best Quotes
> "God knows at every given point as we decide or make those decisions, as that barometer changes, God sees the plans as they change in real time."[^1]

> "Go watch the movie The Adjustment Bureau where there's a real-time visual of what each decision point branches out into... and where God is still in control while respecting your personal agency."[^1]
