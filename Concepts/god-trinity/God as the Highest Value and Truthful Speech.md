# God as the Highest Value and Truthful Speech

## The Point

Pressed by [[Sam Harris]] to say what God he believes in, [[Jordan Peterson]] declines a literal supernatural deity ("I act as if he exists") and instead offers an evolutionary-psychological definition: God as the highest value in the hierarchy of values, the voice of conscience, "that which selects among men," and truthful speech embodied (the "Word").

## Argument (steelman)

1. **"I act as if he exists."** Peterson reframes belief as enacted rather than propositional: "I act as though God exists" — a "much more precise claim" than assent to a supernatural being. He adds that a "real atheist" (Raskolnikov) would have the temptations to misbehave "more at hand."[^1]

2. **The read-out list of definitions.** "God is how we imaginatively and collectively represent the existence and action of consciousness across time... God is that which eternally dies and is reborn in the pursuit of higher being and truth... the highest value in the hierarchy of values... the voice of conscience... the source of judgment and mercy... the future to which we make sacrifices."[^2]

3. **God as the evolutionary selector.** "If you're an evolutionary biologist, God is that which selects among men in the eternal hierarchy of men" — the principles (truthful articulation, competence, sound moral judgment, not tyrannical power) that raise a person in the hierarchy and increase reproductive fitness over vast timescales.[^3]

4. **The Word / truthful speech.** Peterson's deepest claim: redemption is found in truthful speech, embodied (not merely an idea) — "the embodiment of the word in flesh." Since all three already agreed free truthful expression is the antidote to dogmatism (see [[Free Speech as the Corrective to Dogmatism]]), Peterson asks why this is not the same claim Christianity makes.[^4]

5. **The transcendent-across-time framing.** God is "a transcendent reality only observable across the longest of iterated timeframes" — tying directly to his is/ought argument that our values come from structures evolved over billions of years. See [[You Cannot Get From Facts to Values Without an Interpretive Structure]].[^5]

6. **Harris's pushback (cross-linked).** Harris objects this is not what most believers mean and empties the word of content. See [[Redefining God Empties the Word - Harris's Deepak Objection]].[^6]

## Best Quotes

> "God is how we imaginatively and collectively represent the existence and action of consciousness across time... God is that which eternally dies and is reborn in the pursuit of higher being and truth."[^2]

> "If you're an evolutionary biologist, God is that which selects among men in the eternal hierarchy of men."[^3]
