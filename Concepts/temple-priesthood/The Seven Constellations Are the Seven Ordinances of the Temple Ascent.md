# The Seven Constellations Are the Seven Ordinances of the Temple Ascent

#type/claim

## The Claim

[[Kamron Coleman]] argues that the **ordinances of the gospel are constellations**, and the temple endowment is an astronomical ascent through **seven seals/constellations**. His linguistic hinge: the Hebrew **mazzaroth** (the "constellations") is, he claims, the same word as "ordinance" — so "the ordinances of the Gospel" can be read as "the constellations of the Gospel." The covenant path runs Pisces (baptism) → Aries (the lamb in the covenant) → Orion (the Good Shepherd) → the **veil / Milky Way / tree of life** (guarded by the Gemini "two witnesses") → **Leo** (the celestial kingdom, Father and Mother as lion and lioness). Crucially, the **human body is a fractal of this cosmos**, and the temple exists to teach that "you are the temple of the cosmos."

## Supporting Evidence (steelman)

### 1. Mazzaroth = constellation = ordinance (rating: weak-to-moderate; the lexical linchpin)
"The Hebrew word *mazzaroth*... is constellation. Is the same word for ordinance. So when we talk about the ordinances of the Gospel you can say the constellations of the Gospel."[^1] The covenant path is then "seven seals of initiation in sequence" corresponding to "the seven seals of initiation in the endowment ceremony, which is astronomical."[^1] *Rating: mazzaroth (Job 38:32) is a rare term of uncertain meaning; the equation with "ordinance" is Coleman's own and not standard lexicography.*

### 2. The ascent has a fixed astronomical order (rating: internally coherent; speculative)
The initiate begins as a **Pisces fish** (baptism — a fish in the womb's salt water, "born again"), becomes a **sheep** of Aries shepherded by **Orion** (the Good Shepherd, arm raised), reaches the **veil** (the Milky Way / tree of life) flanked by the **Gemini** twins as the "two sentinels / two olive-tree witnesses," and crosses into **Leo** (and neighboring Cancer) — Father and Mother lion and lioness — where "the lamb shall lie down with the lion" (Leo Minor).[^2] **Aquarius** marks the pre-mortal "other world" at the 6 o'clock (south) pole. See [[Astronomy Is the Lost Universal Language of Scripture]].

### 3. D&C 121 walks the seals of the body (rating: the strongest textual demonstration)
Coleman reads [[D&C 121.32|D&C 121:26-32]] — Joseph Smith in Liberty Jail — as descending the body's seals: the stayed **hand** (7th seal, Leo, the lion's paw that draws the initiate through the veil), the **eye** ("pure eye"/pineal, 6th seal, Cancer/the Mother, terrestrial), the **ear/mouth** (5th seal), the **heart**, and the **sword** ("with thy sword avenge our wrongs") as Orion's famously placed sword — the Orion Nebula being "where the seed of God is generated."[^3] [[D&C 88]] is "plain that it's describing this." See [[The Facsimiles Encode Abraham's Endowment Through the Osiris Myth]].

### 4. The body as fractal of the cosmos; chakras and the front seals (rating: syncretic; speculative)
The body holds "12 zodiacal constellations"; the **back of the spine** carries the seven that ascend (the Eastern chakras, an enlightenment/"solo trip"), while the **gospel deals with the seven seals on the front of the body** (marriage, a "physical reality that requires a body and a mate"). They converge at the heart and the crown.[^4] [[3 Nephi 11.10-11|3 Nephi 11]] is read as the three top degrees of revelation — hear (×3), see, then **touch** Christ (exaltation, drawn through the veil) — and [[John 9.1-3|John 9]] as a complete ascension text (Aquarius → Leo).[^5]

### 5. Steelman of the opposing view
The LDS endowment's covenant structure has well-attested ancient-temple antecedents (Barker, Nibley) without any astral-ordinance scheme; importing chakras, the Orion Nebula, and a mazzaroth=ordinance equation is **parallelomania** — see [[Avoiding Parallelomania Between Ancient Rites and LDS Temple]]. The "seven seals" of Revelation are read by Joseph Smith himself as the seven *millennial periods* of earth's history ([[Seven Seals and the Age of the Earth Show Continuing Revelation|D&C 77]]), not seven constellations, so the scriptural "seven seals" do not obviously license Coleman's mapping. Coleman's own framing answers this: he presents the scheme as an *imaginative* recovered language to "fire up the right hemisphere," explicitly "not supplying a dogma."
