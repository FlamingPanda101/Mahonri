# Book of Abraham

A book of Latter-day Saint scripture in the Pearl of Great Price, translated by [[Joseph Smith]] from Egyptian papyri and presenting a first-person account attributed to the biblical Abraham. A frequent subject of apologetic discussion: the recovered papyri, the facsimiles, the catalyst/translation theories, and Egyptological critique.
