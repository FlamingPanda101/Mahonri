# priesthood ban

The former Latter-day Saint restriction (in force until the 1978 revelation, Official Declaration 2) barring men of Black African descent from priesthood ordination and temple ordinances. A recurring subject in apologetic discussions of race, prophetic fallibility, folk doctrine, and continuing revelation.
