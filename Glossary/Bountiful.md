# Bountiful

**Definition.** The fertile coastal place in 1 Nephi 17 where Lehi's party arrives after turning eastward from [[Nahom]], described as having much fruit, honey, timber, and ore — the spot where Nephi builds a ship and the family departs the Old World. (The name is distinct from the later New World land of Bountiful in the Americas.) Candidates proposed on the Dhofar coast of Oman include Wadi Sayq / Khor Kharfot.

**Why it matters here.** Defenders argue that a green, tree-and-water-rich inlet due east of Nahom matches an otherwise improbable description of the famously arid Arabian Peninsula, strengthening the case that Nephi recorded a real journey. Critics question whether any single site satisfies every textual detail and note that more than one location has been nominated, so the identification is suggestive rather than settled.
