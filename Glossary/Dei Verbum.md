# Dei Verbum

The Second Vatican Council's *Dogmatic Constitution on Divine Revelation* (1965), a key Roman Catholic statement on the relationship of Scripture, Sacred Tradition, and the Magisterium in transmitting revelation. Cited in this vault's discussions of canon, sola scriptura, and Scripture-and-Tradition.
