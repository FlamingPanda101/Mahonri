# CES Letter

"Letter to a CES Director" (2013) by Jeremy Runnells — a widely circulated compilation of objections to Latter-day Saint truth claims (Book of Mormon and Book of Abraham translation, polygamy, history, science, the witnesses). It is a common reference point for many of the questions this vault's apologetic notes address.
