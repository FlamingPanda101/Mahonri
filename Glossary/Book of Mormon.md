# Book of Mormon

The keystone scripture of the Restoration, published by [[Joseph Smith]] in 1830 and presented as a translation of an ancient record of peoples in the Americas, written to testify of Jesus Christ. It is the subject of much of this vault's apologetic material — historicity, the witnesses, Hebraisms and chiasmus, Nephi's Arabian journey (Nahom/Bountiful), DNA, and anachronism debates.
