# Hayden Carroll — Ratio Christi Campus Talk

The script of a talk [[Hayden Carroll]] delivered to a **Ratio Christi** (Evangelical campus apologetics) chapter, aiming to "get inside the head of a Latter-day Saint." It moves through four stages: a grace-centered Plan of Salvation, a three-part critique of Sola Scriptura, a positive alternative (God / the Spirit as ultimate authority), and the Book of Mormon as the keystone evidence.

## Summary
- **Plan of Salvation (grace).** Establishes common ground — the Fall, the Atonement, and salvation by grace through faith manifested in works: "anyone who proclaims that salvation is caused by any human act preaches blasphemy." Shares the framing of [[Light and Truth - Saved by Grace or Works]] and reads 2 Nephi 25:23 as [[After All We Can Do Means In Spite of All We Can Do|"in spite of all we can do"]] [^1].
- **Rejection of Sola Scriptura** via three problems — interpretation, logic, and canon — which he calls the *root* of nearly every Latter-day Saint vs. Protestant disagreement, Trinity included [^2].
- **Positive alternative:** God Himself, not any book, is the ultimate authority; He reveals truth by His Spirit (1 Corinthians 2:9-14); fallible humans cannot produce an infallible Bible; even [[James White]] appeals to the Spirit (not the Bible) for his assurance of salvation [^3].
- **The greatest evidence:** the **Book of Mormon as keystone** — if it is an authentic revelation, the Restoration follows; and the dictation feat is inexplicable given Joseph Smith's documented lack of education [^4].
