# Ward Radio - 10 Reasons to Believe the Book of Abraham (with Kerry Muhlestein)

**Channel:** Ward Radio · **Host:** [[Cardon Ellis]] · **Guest:** [[Kerry Muhlestein]]
**URL:** https://youtu.be/twxJz_m5D-w

## Summary
- Muhlestein counts down ten reasons to *believe* (rather than merely defend) the [[Book of Abraham]]. Reasons 10–5 are intellectual: it reads authentically ancient; it matches Egyptian human-sacrifice practice; geographical/linguistic bullseyes like **Olishem** and **Shinehah**; the four Canaanite gods, the "pillars of heaven" palace-facade, and the **Sobek/crocodile = Pharaoh** facsimile details; and Egyptians themselves associating lion-couch scenes with Abraham.
- Reasons 4–1 are spiritual/revelatory: the profound truths about the **Abrahamic covenant** (incumbent on the covenant-holder to extend it to all), the **temple pattern** (facsimiles 1–2–3 ordered altar → cosmic dome → throne), the expanding structure of Abraham 1→3 (individual → world → cosmos), and Muhlestein's lived testimony that living the book yields its promised fruits.
- Recurring thesis: it takes *more* faith to call Joseph a fraud than a prophet, because there are more details to explain away on the fraud hypothesis.
