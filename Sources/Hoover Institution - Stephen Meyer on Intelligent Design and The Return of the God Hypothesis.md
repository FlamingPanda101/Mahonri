# Stephen Meyer on Intelligent Design and The Return of the God Hypothesis

On *Uncommon Knowledge*, Peter Robinson interviews **[[Stephen C. Meyer]]** about *Return of the God Hypothesis*, which argues that three twentieth-century scientific discoveries — the beginning of the universe, the fine-tuning of physical constants, and the digital information in DNA — are best explained by a transcendent, designing mind. Meyer frames worldviews (theism, deism, pantheism, materialism) as testable metaphysical hypotheses and argues that theism has superior explanatory power, offered as the best explanation rather than as deductive proof.

## Summary
- The **"God hypothesis"** treats the existence of God as an explanatory hypothesis with greater **explanatory power** than competing worldviews (deism, materialism, pantheism); worldviews are testable by how well they fit what we observe.[^1]
- Modern science **arose uniquely in the Judeo-Christian West (c. 1300–1750)** because of three theological presuppositions: the *contingency*, the *intelligibility*, and (given human *fallibility*) the testability of nature.[^2]
- **Discovery 1 — a beginning.** Big Bang cosmology (redshift, general relativity, Hawking's singularity theorem) shows matter, space, time, and energy began to exist, implying a cause that *transcends* the physical universe.[^3]
- **Discovery 2 — fine-tuning.** A "Goldilocks universe": the cosmological constant alone is tuned to ~1 part in 10^90, and dozens of constants and initial conditions fall within life-permitting tolerances; the firing-squad analogy answers the anthropic-principle dodge.[^4]
- **Discovery 3 — information in DNA.** DNA stores a four-character digital code; in our uniform experience information traces to a mind, so the information in the first cell (and the abrupt **Cambrian** infusions of new information) is best explained by intelligent design.[^5]
- Combining the three, Meyer argues the evidence points past an *immanent* (alien/panspermia) designer to a **transcendent, theistic** designer, by an abductive **inference to the best explanation**, not mathematical proof.[^6]
