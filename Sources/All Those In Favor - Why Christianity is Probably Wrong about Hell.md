# All Those In Favor — Why Christianity is Probably Wrong about Hell

A philosophical argument that **eternal conscious torment** cannot be reconciled with a perfectly loving, just God who creates each soul *from nothing* — the "problem of hell and creation" — followed by the LDS degrees-of-glory resolution.

## Summary
- The core problem: if God creates each person from nothing with their exact attributes and sustains them, he is ultimately responsible for their sins — so eternal damnation is unjust [^1].
- Five standard defenses are each rebutted: appeal to sinfulness, watchmaker, God's glory, the fall, and free will [^2].
- The LDS alternative (pre-existent souls, post-mortal progression, [[Degrees of Glory vs Heaven|degrees of glory]]) dissolves the problem [^3].
