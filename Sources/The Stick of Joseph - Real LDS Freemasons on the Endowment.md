# The Stick of Joseph — Real LDS Freemasons on the Endowment

A follow-up to the channel's long [[The Stick of Joseph - The Ultimate Guide to Freemasonry and the Temple|Freemasonry-and-the-Temple deep dive]], hosted by [[Mike Day]] and [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|David Butler]], featuring two practicing Master Masons who are also endowed Latter-day Saints — Josh Flansberg and Robert Kay (of "Mormon Yeshiva") — discussing what Freemasonry illuminates about [[Joseph Smith]]'s mind and the temple endowment.

## Summary
- Restoration is not creation *ex nihilo* in a vacuum: Joseph saw "an order of priesthood" in Masonry and gathered preserved fragments; they cite [[Heber C. Kimball]]'s 1842 letter that "Masonry was taken from priesthood but has become degenerated" [^1].
- The "Masons worship Lucifer / Albert Pike said so" charge is a misreading of *Morals and Dogma* pushed by anti-Masonic (often Catholic) writers; the same kind of conspiracy slur is leveled at Latter-day Saints, and Freemasonry does not match the Book of Mormon's "secret combinations" [^2].
- A "sealed" record can mean *concealed in plain sight*; the guests read scripture as multivalent and rhythmic-mnemonic (catechism, cipher), claiming Joseph's Egyptian Alphabet & Grammar draws on a Royal-Arch / Hebrew cipher tradition rather than being invented [^3].
