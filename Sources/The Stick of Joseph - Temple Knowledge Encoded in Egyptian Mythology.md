# The Stick of Joseph — Temple Knowledge Encoded in Egyptian Mythology

A long-form conversation on the [[The Stick of Joseph - Are Christians Becoming Mormon with Brian Thomassen|Stick of Joseph]] channel with **Greg Matsen** (Go and Do Travel, who guides Egypt tours) and co-host [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|David Butler]], arguing that ancient Egyptian temple religion preserves a "form of the truth" that parallels the Latter-day Saint [[The Book of Mormon Already Contains the Endowment|endowment]] — from serpent iconography and guardian-figures to the [[Osiris Is a Type Not a Person - The Blessed Dead Become Osiris|Osiris myth]] and the [[The Facsimiles Encode Abraham's Endowment Through the Osiris Myth|Book of Abraham facsimiles]]. The framing claim, returned to as a refrain, is that "what we have in the Book of Abraham and what we have in the temple can be found everywhere" in Egypt.

## Summary
- **All restored-gospel scripture traces to Egyptian-language records via a Josephite/Manasseh scribal school.** The hosts inventory Egypt's saturation of LDS scripture (Abraham, Joseph, Moses, Lehi, Nephi, Jeremiah, the flight to Egypt) and argue the Book of Abraham, Book of Joseph, Book of Moses, brass plates, and Book of Mormon are all Egyptian records carried down through the scribal school of Manasseh — citing Hugh Nibley and Noel Reynolds; Nephi's "language of the Egyptians" ([[1 Nephi 1.1|1 Nephi 1:1]]) reads as a scribe's testimony [^1][^2]. This is developed at length in [[Lehi Was of Manasseh So the Book of Mormon Is a Northern Israelite Record]] and [[The Northern Lineage Explains Laman and Lemuel and the Brass Plates]].
- **[[Helaman 8.7|Helaman 8:18]]'s "from Moses to Abraham" implies a line of holy prophets inside Egypt.** The backwards-running phrase (Moses *to* Abraham) brackets exactly the sojourn in Egypt, suggesting the patriarchal-to-Mosaic prophetic line ran *through* Egyptian soil and a scribal culture, not merely alongside a "Pharaoh = bad guy" frame [^3].
- **Egyptian serpent iconography illuminates the seraphim and the brazen serpent.** The seraph is a *winged serpent*; the hosts read Isaiah 6's throne-vision against winged-cobra throne iconography (King Tut's throne) and contrast the chaos-serpent [[Two Serpents - Mehen and Apophis Parallel the Brazen Serpent|Apophis with the protector-serpent Mehen]], paralleling Moses' fiery-serpents-vs-brazen-serpent and the five Book-of-Mormon Nehushtan passages [^4][^5].
- **Sphinxes, cherubim, and sentinels are interchangeable guardians of sacred space, gated by passwords.** The Sphinx guards the pyramids as cherubim guard Eden's tree of life; the [[Guardians of Sacred Space and the Password Motif|Oedipus riddle is a keyword]] and "Ehyeh asher ehyeh" a password given to Moses — mapping onto temple sentinels and keywords at the veil [^6][^7].
- **Osiris is a type/title one *becomes*, not a single god — refuting the "Jesus is just Osiris" copycat charge.** The dead person is "Bob Osiris," taking on the name of God as the king takes the name of Jehovah in the temple drama; "It's not that Jesus is *just* Osiris — it's, oh my goodness, Jesus *is* Osiris," with Christ as Wepwawet the "opener of the way" past judgment [^8][^9]. See [[Osiris Is a Type Not a Person - The Blessed Dead Become Osiris]].
- **The Book of Abraham facsimiles encode Abraham's endowment through the Osiris death-resurrection myth.** Feet-apart = alive, hands-up = speaking (the Metropolitan parchment vindicated Joseph's "alive" Abraham over critics' "dead Osiris"); facsimile 1 = death of the king, 2 = resurrection/ascension, 3 (missing) = presentation; even Robert Ritner "knows it inside and out and holds back" on the Osiris-type reading [^10][^11]. See [[The Facsimiles Encode Abraham's Endowment Through the Osiris Myth]].
- **The substitute-king coronation drama parallels the atoning death/rebirth pattern.** The pharaoh-as-Osiris dies and is reborn (a substitute is supplied — Abraham as substitute for Pharaoh/Nimrod, then Christ as the true substitute); the Judahite king's three-days-in-the-dark rebirth echoes [[Psalm 110.1|Psalm 110]] [^12][^13].
- **Egyptian exaltation language seeded biblical blessing-formulae and the Abrahamic-covenant veil.** *Maat/maar* ("blessed, happy") underlies the Beatitudes' *makarios*; an Edfu/Hatshepsut blessing-row ("I endow you with eternal life, posterity, priesthood, well-being, to become like God for eternity") and "ritual embrace" scenes at the veil parallel the endowment, washing/anointing with ankhs, and ankh as living water [^14][^15].
- **Egyptian temple worship was genuine religion, democratized over time — a typological anticipation of the restored endowment.** "The Egyptians didn't have mythology… they were religious," focused on life-after-death and exaltation; the texts moved [[Egyptian Funerary Texts Democratized From Pharaoh to Everyone|from royal-only Pyramid Texts to the Book of Breathings for the poor]], anticipating Joseph Smith's full democratization of the endowment to "kings and queens, priests and priestesses" [^16][^17].
