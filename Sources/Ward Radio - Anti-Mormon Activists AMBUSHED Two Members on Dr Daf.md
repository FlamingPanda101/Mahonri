# Ward Radio - Anti-Mormon Activists AMBUSHED Two Members on Dr. Daf and We Responded

**Channel:** Ward Radio · **Hosts:** [[Cardon Ellis]], [[Luke Hansen]], [[Jonah Barnes]], [[Austin Falter]]
**URL:** https://youtu.be/LUr-81zcxPA

## Summary
- Reaction to a "Christian versus Mormon women" segment ("The Bridge") on the Dr. Daff show, framed as an **ambush**: two devotional (not apologetically trained) LDS women — Mallerie and Julieta — set against a doctorate-holding host and prepped evangelical guests. Meta-thesis: Protestants treat their position as the cultural default and demand Latter-day Saints "defend yourself," and anti-Mormonism enjoys a social blind spot other religions don't.
- Rebuttals to the recycled tropes: the **self-certifying prophet** charge (the "missing Genesis 50:33" / JST) — Joseph never used that text to certify himself; the **Dead Sea Scrolls "match the Bible exactly"** claim — false, ~40% are biblical and ~60% are a *competing* narrative, and even the biblical scrolls (e.g. the Psalms scroll) differ; the **manuscript-attestation** confusion — many late copies attest *transmission*, not that *events happened*, whereas the Book of Mormon is an original; the **"do not add or take away"** (Revelation 22) trope — it seals Revelation, not a Bible compiled centuries later (nine NT books were written after it); and the **"Orthodox Christians"** self-label — evangelicalism is younger than the hosts' grandmothers.
