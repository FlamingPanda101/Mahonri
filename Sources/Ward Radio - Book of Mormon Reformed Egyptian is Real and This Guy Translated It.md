# Ward Radio - Book of Mormon Reformed Egyptian is Real and This Guy Translated It

Host [[Cardon Ellis]] with [[Ed Thomas]] and guest [[Jerry Grover]] (licensed geologist, civil engineer, and self-taught student of ancient number systems) on Grover's academic translation of the "characters document" — the page of copied plate characters long mislabeled the Anthon transcript.

## Summary
- **The characters document is not the Anthon transcript.** Handwriting analysis identifies it as [[John Whitmer]]'s (from [[David Whitmer]]), and [[Charles Anthon]]'s description (an emblem) does not match it — so it is a separate, well-provenanced copy of reformed-Egyptian characters. See [[The Characters Document Translated as Abbreviated Hieratic and Maya]].
- **Grover reads it as abbreviated Egyptian hieratic + demotic, with Maya-style date glyphs.** He claims the first four lines translate as a lost preface to Mosiah (a chronology keyed to "after Lehi left Jerusalem"), with logographic compression ("it came to pass" = one glyph) explaining how much text fit on the plates. Connects to [[Reformed Egyptian Characters Were Small]] and [[Translation Density Shows the Book of Mormon Text Fits]].
- **Name-glyphs carry layered meanings.** Grover argues "Lamanite" encodes "unbeliever" (Hebrew *lo-amān*) and even "filthy/excrement" in the Egyptian and Mesoamerican glyph forms — reflecting ancient tribal animosity rather than 19th-century invention. See [[Lamanite Means Filthy Unbeliever in the Reformed Egyptian Glyphs]].
