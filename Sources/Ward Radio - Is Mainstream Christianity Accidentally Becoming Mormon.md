# Ward Radio — Is Mainstream Christianity Accidentally Becoming Mormon?

- **Convergence Thesis:** Mainstream Christian scholars across the denominational spectrum are increasingly converging on what were once dismissed as "uniquely Mormon heresies" — pre-existence, an embodied God, the feminine divine, and a more capacious plan of salvation for those who die without hearing the gospel.
- **Publishing Wave:** A "flurry of books" in the last 20 years addresses distinctively LDS claims from within mainstream Christianity — *The Embodied God*, *Heavenly Flesh*, *Does God Have a Body?*, *God Had a Wife*, and *Did God Have a Wife?* — evidence that these are not fringe conversations.
- **LDS as Restoration, Not Invention:** As early Christianity becomes better understood historically, LDS theology looks increasingly plausible — "it turns out we weren't inventing new doctrines. We were just remembering old ones."
