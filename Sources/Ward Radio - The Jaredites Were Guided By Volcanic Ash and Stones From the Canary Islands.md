# Ward Radio — The Jaredites Were Guided By Volcanic Ash and Stones From the Canary Islands

[[Cardon Ellis]] hosts **[[Jerry Grover]]** (translator of the "characters document," Book of Mormon linguist/geologist) and **[[Josh Gailey]]** (author of *Witnessing Miracles*) on a naturalistic reconstruction of the Jaredite voyage: an **Atlantic crossing** from Sumer, guided in part by **volcanic eruptions** read as the "cloud," with the brother of Jared's mount and stones located in the **Canary Islands**.

## Summary
- **Origin in Sumer, Atlantic (not Pacific) crossing.** Grover argues the Jaredites began near Sumer (the city of Kish ↔ archaeological Kesh; the unnamed "tower" matching the ziggurats), then crossed the Mediterranean in low-grade barges, staged at the Canary Islands, and rode the Canary/North-Equatorial currents to Mesoamerica in the ~344 days of [[Ether 6|Ether 6:11]] — John Sorenson having shifted from a Pacific to an Atlantic view [^1].
- **"Led in a cloud" = volcanic eruption.** Wherever scripture has God speak/lead from a cloud (Sinai-as-volcano, [[3 Nephi 9|3 Nephi]]), Grover reads a possible volcanic mechanism; Teide (Canaries), Mount Pelée, and Pico de Zaza were all erupting in-frame and could serve as navigational "signal flares" out of the equatorial current — God working *through* natural law, not instead of it [^2].
- **The shining stones, "molten out of a rock."** The Canaries have ~30 translucent minerals; the original spelling "moulten" can mean merely *heat-and-crack*, so the brother of Jared need not have smelted crystals. The 16 stones may have been prepared to glow by kinetic ("shake-flashlight") or photoluminescent means; the two **interpreter** stones were a separate, God-given technology mounted later by the Nephites in platinum-rimmed spectacles [^3].
- **The "transparent as glass" anachronism** is met by natural glass — fulgurites (lightning-struck sand) and tektites/Libyan desert glass — known in antiquity [^4].
