# The Stick of Joseph - The Lost Scripture Language of the Stars

A roughly three-hour [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|Stick of Joseph]] conversation between host **David Butler**, co-host **Brad Whitbeck**, and artist **[[Kamron Coleman]]**, built around Coleman's massive in-progress artwork the *Apotheosis Scroll* (apotheosisscroll.com). The central thesis is that **ancient astronomy is a now-lost universal "language" in which the world's scriptures were written**, and that the restored gospel — through [[Joseph Smith]] and the Doctrine and Covenants — recovers it. Coleman reads the covenant path, the temple ordinances, the human body, and the life of Christ as encoded in the constellations, the zodiacal precession, and the shifting pole star.

The episode opens with Butler framing the whole project through Iain McGilchrist's left-brain/right-brain neuroscience: the art is "right-brain," an *initiatic experience* and a recovered language, not a doctrine or dogma. Coleman repeatedly insists he is "not supplying a doctrine" but trying to "teach a language that's been lost," and that what anyone does with it is "completely wide open."[^9]

## Summary

- **Astronomy as the universal lost language.** Coleman and Butler argue the night sky was humanity's "first great book," a pre-literate medium in which the same star-story was told from Persia to Egypt to Mesoamerica; Christianity spread "like wildfire" not via written Gospels but because "it's a star story" everyone already knew.[^1] See [[Astronomy Is the Lost Universal Language of Scripture]]. They lean on *Hamlet's Mill* (de Santillana & von Dechend) for the claim that the **precession of the equinoxes** — a ~26,000-year cycle — is encoded in myth older than writing.[^2]
- **Seven constellations = seven ordinances.** Coleman equates the Hebrew **mazzaroth** (constellation) with "ordinance," so "the ordinances of the gospel" are "the constellations of the gospel." The covenant path is an ascent through seven seals/constellations (Pisces → Aries → Orion → the veil/Milky Way → Leo), and the human body is a **fractal** of this cosmos taught in the temple. He reads [[D&C 121.32|D&C 121:26-32]] (Joseph in Liberty Jail) and [[D&C 88]] as walking through the seals of the body.[^3] See [[The Seven Constellations Are the Seven Ordinances of the Temple Ascent]].
- **Precession decodes lamb / ram / fish.** The ages of **Taurus → Aries → Pisces** map salvation history: bull-sacrifice in the age of Taurus (the council of El, all wearing bull horns); the ram of Aries (Israel, Solomon's temple); and the fish of Pisces (Christ, "the SUN of righteousness," fishermen-disciples). [[Genesis 22|Abraham and Isaac]] is the Taurus→Aries hinge — the ram caught in the thicket announces the bull-age is over.[^4] See [[The Precession of the Equinoxes Decodes the Lamb Ram and Fish]]. Builds on [[The Bull of Jacob Shows Yahweh Was Depicted as a Bull]] and [[Golden Calf and Bronze Serpent as Idolatry Tests]].
- **Pole-star shift = Bethlehem star and Second Coming.** A second, harder-to-observe precession track is the **pole star**: Thuban (in Draco, at the "udder" of a giant cow-constellation Coleman names "Bossy"/Hathor) → an empty "womb" of dark sky (the formless Yahweh, cult of Yahweh ~1000 BC) → Polaris (~500 AD onward). The **Star of Bethlehem** is the sign appearing in that empty womb — the "golden calf" born as the SUN/Son — and the Magi follow only the North Pole star.[^5] See [[The Pole Star Shift Marks the Star of Bethlehem and the Second Coming]].
- **The cosmic mill grinds the bread of life.** *Hamlet's Mill* becomes the mill that pulverizes grain into the **bread of life**: the world-axis/Milky Way grinds the saved "seeds" (universal salvation), and individual suffering is the grinding that turns many grains into one communal loaf — "He is risen," baked in the celestial fire, crowned.[^6] See [[The Cosmic Mill Grinds the Bread of Life]].
- **John 9 and 3 Nephi 11 as ascension texts.** [[John 9.1-3|John 9]] (the man born blind) is read as a self-contained "Gnostic ascension text" — Orion/Canis Major, Sirius as the healed eye, ascending Aquarius → Leo through seven seals while the Jews descend. [[3 Nephi 11.10-11|3 Nephi 11]] stages the three degrees of revelation (hear → see → touch) as the top seals.[^7]
- **Framing and caveats.** The art is "initiatic," a "covenant path" to be *traversed in order*, modeled on the **Gospel of Thomas** (non-narrative, "right-brain") and other Nag Hammadi material. Coleman ties astronomy's suppression to the Deuteronomic/Josianic reform and to Rome/Catholicism, with the thread preserved underground via Kabbalah (which, he says, "highly informed" Joseph Smith).[^8] He stresses repeatedly that the work is experiential, "not a doctrine," and invites disagreement.[^9]
