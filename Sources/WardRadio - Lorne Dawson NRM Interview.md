# Ward Radio — Lorne Dawson NRM Interview

A Ward Radio host interviews **[[Lorne Dawson]]**, a retired Canadian sociologist of new religious movements and radicalization, on how academia understands "cults" versus how popular culture does.

## Summary
- The word **"cult" is a contaminated pejorative**; scholars use "new religious movement" (NRM). Its negative sense was coined by mid-20th-c. evangelicals against the LDS, JWs, Christian Scientists, and Adventists [^1].
- **Brainwashing does not exist** — a CIA study of returned Korean War POWs and two large independent empirical studies refute it; courts (US v. Fishman) bar it as unscientific [^2].
- The **church / sect / cult** categories are relative over time *and* geography — the same group can be a church in Utah, a sect in Alberta, and a "cult" in Quebec [^3].
