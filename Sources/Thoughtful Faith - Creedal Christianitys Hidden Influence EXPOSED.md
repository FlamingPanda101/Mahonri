## Summary
- A guest commentator answers an Eastern Orthodox priest's charge that Latter-day Saint theology is "just insanity," arguing the priest **assumes** rather than argues that hard Trinitarian theology is apostolic — an "I'm right because I'm right" circularity that mirrors [[Trent Horn]]'s syllogistic style. See [[Assuming Your Conclusion Is Not an Argument - The Circular Creedal Move]].
- The core thesis: the creedal God (simple, timeless, immaterial, without body parts or passions) is the **Platonic "One"** read back into the Bible through Hellenization, not the dynamic, embodied God of Hebrew scripture. See [[The Creedal God Is the Platonic One Not the God of the Bible]] and [[The Hypostatic Union Is a Greek Solution to a Greek Problem]].
