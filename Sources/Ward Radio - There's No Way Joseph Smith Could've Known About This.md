# Ward Radio - There's No Way Joseph Smith Could've Known About This!

**Channel:** Ward Radio · **Guests:** [[Jonah Barnes]], [[Brad Whitbeck]]
**URL:** https://youtu.be/bOKVLrKoFco

## Summary
- [[Jonah Barnes]] argues an apocryphal "secret death of Jeremiah" (the **Paraleipomena of Jeremiah / 4 Baruch**, plus the *Apocalypse of Baruch*) records [[Jeremiah]] receiving a tree-of-life-style vision of God and the Son of God, then being stoned as a messianic prophet — material echoing [[Nephi]]'s vision that Joseph Smith could not have accessed. See [[Jeremiahs Secret-Death Vision Parallels Nephis Tree of Life]].
- Frames it through the **Deuteronomist** thesis: scribes under [[Josiah]] discovered a "second law" (Deuteronomy), centralized the temple, and redacted the pre-existent divine Messiah out of the canon — opposed by Jeremiah, Lehi, Isaiah. See [[The Deuteronomists Edited Out the Pre-Existent Divine Messiah]] and [[Apocryphal Prophet-Martyrdoms Corroborate the Book of Mormons Slain Messianic Prophets]].
- [[Brad Whitbeck]] repeatedly steelmans the other side, cautioning against projecting uniform bad motives onto Josiah's priests and against rigid conspiratorial thinking; the takeaway is "never worship a book" — it is always about Christ.
