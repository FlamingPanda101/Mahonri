# Ward Radio — Who Are the Giants in the Bible? (Jonah Barnes)

A [[Ward Radio]] segment in which [[Cardon Ellis]] interviews [[Jonah Barnes]] on the identity of the **Nephilim / giants** of [[Genesis 6.1-4|Genesis 6:4]], drawn from Barnes's book *The Lost Gems of Genesis*. Barnes's reconstruction is deliberately contrarian: against the [[Michael Heiser]] "supernatural fallen angels" reading, he argues the "giants" were **mortal Sethite priesthood-holders** ("overseers" / "watchers" / "bishops") who broke covenant — and that the *real* sin that triggered the Flood was **sterilization, abortion, and child sacrifice** to extend their own long lifespans. He insists "there were no giants"; the word never meant "giant."

## Summary

- **The Nephilim were apostate Sethite priesthood-holders, not supernatural giants (new).** The "sons of God" are covenant men ("sons of God" = a title for the righteous, per [[Book of Moses|Moses 6-7]]) who descend the holy mountain to the "daughters of Cain," get trapped, and break their covenants; "watcher/overseer" = *episkopos* = bishop. They are *not* fallen angels. See [[The Nephilim Were Apostate Sethite Priesthood-Holders Not Supernatural Giants]]. [^1]
- **The sin of the Nephilim was sterilization, abortion, and child sacrifice (new).** Per the *Mandean Book of Enoch*, Cave of Treasures, and Pirke de-Rabbi Eliezer, the watchers introduced a "drought" (drug) to sterilize the women and aborted or sacrificed the children to extend their multi-century lifespans — so "the end of all flesh is before me" (Gen 6:13) means *the human race was ending*, which is why God shortened lifespans after the Flood. See [[The Sin of the Nephilim Was Sterilization Abortion and Child Sacrifice]]. [^2]
- **"Nephilim" is a premasoretic triliteral pun: mighty / fallen / abortion (new).** Premasoretic Hebrew is consonantal (*npl*), so the same root yields *gibborim*-like "mighty," *nephal* "fallen ones," and "abortions/miscarriages" — and the Greek *gigantes* means "earth-born," not "giant." The "13-cubit bed" giantism is a later Deuteronomist/Ethiopic gloss. See [[Nephilim Is a Premasoretic Triliteral Pun Meaning Mighty Fallen and Abortion]]. [^3]
- **Reframes the conquest of Canaan (apologetic payoff).** If the "men of great stature… sons of Anak" the spies saw ([[Numbers 13]]) are the *sterilizing apostates* — and "there are no women or children in Canaan," only kidnapped sex-slaves — then Joshua was not committing genocide against ordinary humans but ending a child-killing cult. (Barnes grants this contradicts the plain "we were as grasshoppers" stature language, which he attributes to textual corruption.) [^4]
- **Contrast with the existing vault Watchers note.** This directly *opposes* the standard scholarly reading in [[Book of Enoch - The Watchers and Genesis 6 Expansion]] (Watchers as lesser divine beings / fallen angels) and the satan-origin reading in [[Book of Enoch - Origin of the Satan Figure]]; it overlaps the Cain/blood-of-Abel life-extension motif in [[Ether 8.13-18|the Gadianton/Jaredite secret-combination]] thread. [^5]
