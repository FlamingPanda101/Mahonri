# The Stick of Joseph — The True Order of Prayer and the Brother of Jared

An interview on the [[The Stick of Joseph - Are Christians Becoming Mormon with Brian Thomassen|Stick of Joseph]] channel between host [[Hayden Paul]] and recurring guest **[[Todd McLauchlin]]**, framed by the anxiety of "big problems / overwhelming solutions" in a tumultuous political moment. Reading [[D&C 123|D&C 123]] from Liberty Jail ("a very large ship is benefited very much by a very small helm"), they argue that Latter-day Saints steer the world not through political messiahs but through the [[The True Order of Prayer Is the Heartbeat of the Endowment|true order of prayer]] — which McLauchlin calls "the heartbeat of the endowment" — modeled by the [[The Brother of Jared Models the Endowment Ascent Through the Veil|brother of Jared at the veil]].

## Summary
- **The way to "steer the ship" is to chase darkness from oneself first through the true order of prayer.** Beginning from [[D&C 123|D&C 123:12-17]] (waste and wear out our lives bringing hidden things of darkness to light) and [[D&C 88|D&C 88:67-68]] ("if your eye be single to my glory, your whole *bodies* shall be full of light"), McLauchlin reads the endowment's core as learning to be *taught what to pray* — the true order of prayer that "puts you into possession of the knowledge and the mystery to overcome all things." Its effect is "concentric," radiating from spirit to body to marriage to ward to nation [^1][^2][^3]. See [[The True Order of Prayer Is the Heartbeat of the Endowment]].
- **"Calling upon the name of the Lord" is a scriptural cipher for the true order of prayer.** Tracing the phrase through [[Moses 5.10-11|Moses 5:16]] (Adam and Eve "ceased not to call upon God"), [[Ether 2|Ether 2:14]] (the brother of Jared *chastened for not* calling upon the name), and [[Ether 3.14|Ether 3]], McLauchlin argues that to "call upon the name" is to engage the true order — endowed Saints "know what that means" [^4][^5]. See [[Calling Upon the Name of the Lord Is the True Order of Prayer]].
- **A holy "garment of power" descended from Adam through the apocryphal patriarchs to Nimrod, the antichrist archetype.** Drawing on Jubilees-type apocrypha, McLauchlin narrates the garment of Adam and Eve passing to [[Enoch]], [[Noah]], then stolen by [[Ham]], to Cush, to [[Nimrod]] (the "great hunter" who uses it to spread death), recovered through Esau's birthright — a counterfeit "order" trying to replicate [[Enoch]]'s city at the Tower of [[Genesis 10|Babel]] [^6][^7]. See [[The Garment of Power Descended From Adam to Nimrod]].
- **"Murder to get gain" is the dark inversion of the priesthood order of sacrifice.** Where Jehovah teaches sacrifice (which "can go one step further than murder," drawing down *more* power than is given, à la Christ's atonement), [[Cain]] and Lucifer invert it into "murder to get gain" — a continuum from telling a white lie or "murdering the truth" on social media to literal ritual bloodshed and secret combinations [^8][^9]. See [[Murder to Get Gain Inverts the Order of Sacrifice]].
- **The Book of Mormon's "lesser portion" means the Saints are still under condemnation until they obtain the greater things.** From [[3 Nephi 19|3 Nephi 26:6-11]] (the sealed "greater portion" withheld "to try the faith of my people"), McLauchlin reasons "by deduction" that because the church does not yet possess the greater things — individually by the brother-of-Jared pattern or collectively by the sealed plates — it remains in a state of (non-final) condemnation; this, he says, is "the most avoided scripture in the Book of Mormon" [^10][^11]. See [[The Lesser Portion and the State of Condemnation]].
- **Practical call to action: record your prayers and learn to be taught what to pray.** Walking the endowment covenants (obedience, sacrifice, gospel, chastity, consecration) as a personal prayer, one reconciles to each, then asks the Lord "what shall I pray?" and writes down the words given — growing "in a principle of revelation" toward Helaman 10-style sealed faith ([[3 Nephi 19|3 Nephi 19:24]], "it was given unto them what they should pray") [^12][^2].
