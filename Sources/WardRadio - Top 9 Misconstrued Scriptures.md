# Top 9 Misconstrued Scriptures Against Mormons

Ward Radio episode where Cardon Ellis hosts three LDS apologists
(Dalton Williams of Unashamed Truth 116, Braden of Gospel Guardian, and Britney)
to rebut the nine Bible verses most commonly weaponized against the Church of Jesus
Christ of Latter-day Saints in online discourse.

## The Nine Scriptures (in order covered)

1. [[Revelation 22-19 - Adding to Scripture]] — "Do not add or take away from this book"
2. [[Galatians 1-8 - Angel Preaching Another Gospel]] — "If we or an angel preach another gospel"
3. [[John 10-30 - I and the Father Are One]] — Trinitarian proof-text
4. [[John 17-3 - Only True God]] — "That they might know thee, the only true God"
5. [[Isaiah 43-10 - No God Formed]] — "Before me there was no God formed"
6. [[Hebrews 1-1-2 - Last Days Spoken by Son]] — Jesus as the final prophet
7. [[Ephesians 2-8-9 - Grace Not Works]] — Works-based salvation accusation
8. [[Matthew 22-30 - No Marriage in Resurrection]] — Eternal marriage/sealing
9. [[John 4-24 - God Is a Spirit]] — God's corporeality

## Key Recurring Themes
- **Proof-texting**: Critics cherry-pick single verses and ignore surrounding context,
  historical context, and original language.
- **Definitions matter**: "Gospel," "one," "salvation," "God is spirit" — LDS and
  traditional Christian definitions often differ, causing people to talk past each other.
- **Internal consistency**: The same logic applied to these verses would invalidate
  other parts of the Bible (e.g., Deuteronomy 4:2 predates the NT).
- **The Bible didn't exist as a compiled book** when many of these texts were written.
