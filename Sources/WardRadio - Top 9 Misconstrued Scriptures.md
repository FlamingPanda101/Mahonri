# Ward Radio — Top 9 Misconstrued Scriptures

Ward Radio panel ([[Cardon Ellis]] hosting [[Dalton Williams]], [[Braden Gospel Guardian|Braden]], and [[Britney Ward Radio|Britney]]) walks through the nine Bible verses most often used against the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, giving the standard apologetic response to each.

## Summary
- Nine "proof texts" are each shown to collapse once read **in context** rather than cherry-picked — the recurring method is *don't proof-text* [^1].
- Two structural points recur: (a) the NT epistles/Revelation were **not written as chapter-and-verse**, so verse-isolation distorts meaning, and (b) **definitions matter** ("gospel," "one," "spirit") or interlocutors talk past each other [^2].
- A theological throughline: **orthopraxy (right action) over orthodoxy (right belief)** — Christ condemned wrong *acting*, not wrong *believing* [^3].

## The nine verses
1. [[Revelation 22 - Add or Take Away Rebutted|Revelation 22:18-19]] — "add/take away"
2. [[Galatians 1-8 - Angel Preaching Another Gospel|Galatians 1:8]] — "another gospel / an angel"
3. [[John 10-30 - I and the Father Are One Rebutted|John 10:30]] — "I and the Father are one"
4. [[1 John 5-7 - Johannine Comma Interpolation|1 John 5:7]] — the Trinity proof text
5. [[Isaiah 43-10 - No God Formed Before Me Rebutted|Isaiah 43:10]] — "no god formed"
6. [[Hebrews 1-1-2 - Jesus the Last Prophet Rebutted|Hebrews 1:1-2]] — "spoken by his Son"
7. [[Ephesians 2-8-9 - Saved by Grace Not Works Rebutted|Ephesians 2:8-9]] — grace vs works
8. [[Matthew 22-30 - No Marriage in Resurrection Rebutted|Matthew 22:30]] — marriage in heaven
9. [[John 4-24 - God Is Spirit Rebutted|John 4:24]] — "God is a Spirit"
