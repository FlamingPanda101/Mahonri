# Thoughtful Faith Apologetics Course (Jacob Hansen)

Jacob Hansen's (Thoughtful Faith) tiered apologetics course — a graduated path from the most basic human questions up through Latter-day Saint discipleship. Four "levels" of conversation, each meeting people where their worldview is. Source notes for each level carry a `course/tf-level-N` tag (auto-collected here as they're ingested).

## Level 1 — The Fundamentals
*For anyone, including those with no belief in a higher power. The most basic questions: What are you? What does it mean to be human? How should we live, and why? Is there a plan or purpose to existence?*

- **Preface** — why/how to learn this
- **1.1 Epistemology** — What can I know? How can I know? (the nature of knowledge)
- **1.3 Ethics & Morality** — What is the basis of right and wrong?
- **1.4 The Question of God** — Are there good reasons to believe there is a God?
- **1.5 Meaning & Purpose** — Does your life have meaning or purpose?
- **1.6 Common Challenges**

Recommended books (ingested separately): *The Moral Landscape* (Harris), *After Humanity* (Ward), *Stealing from God* (Turek), *Five Proofs of the Existence of God* (Feser), *The Return of the God Hypothesis / Sci. Evidence for a Creator* (Meyer), *Life After Life* (Moody).
