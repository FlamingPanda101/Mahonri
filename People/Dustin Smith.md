# Dustin Smith

**Role:** Biblical scholar (early Christology, Gospel of John); biblical unitarian
**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Biblical unitarian (non-Trinitarian Christian)

## Bio
Dustin Smith is a credentialed New Testament scholar and biblical unitarian known for debating Trinitarian theology (including with James White). He is referenced on [[Stick of Joseph - God Logic Trinity Debate Recap]] for a debate point.

## Cited on the Stream
The panel relays that in a recent debate with [[James White]], Smith cited a study claiming **fewer than 85% of professing Christians understand or believe in the Trinity** — used to blunt the objection that only Latter-day Saints find the doctrine confusing. See [[Trinity Is an Interpretation Not Explicit in Scripture]]. *(Relayed secondhand; the exact statistic should be verified against Smith's source.)*
