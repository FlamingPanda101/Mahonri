# Boyd Edwards

**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** (LDS-affiliated context; cited as a physicist, not an apologist)
**Role:** Physicist

## Bio
Boyd F. Edwards is a physicist who, with his brother [[Farrell Edwards]], co-authored a statistical study of chiasmus in the Book of Mormon, published in BYU Studies. [[Alex Arnold]] cites the pair as "two physicists, not LDS apologists."[^1]

## On the Statistics of Alma 36 Chiasmus
The Edwards brothers estimated the probability that the chiastic structure of [[Alma 36.1|Alma 36]] arose by chance at between 1 in 10,000 and 1 in a million, a figure Arnold uses as evidence of deliberate ancient design beyond Joseph Smith's knowledge. See [[Chiasmus as Evidence of Ancient Authorship in the Book of Mormon]].[^1]
