# Sam St. Laurent

Sam St. Laurent is a Latter-day Saint content creator who runs the **Through the Mist** YouTube channel and is based in North Carolina.[^1] On the [[Salty Saints - The Apologist Playbook for LDS Men|Salty Saints apologetics panel]] with [[Hayden Carroll]] and [[Roy Anderson]], he reads superchats and contributes the pastoral counsel that some attackers are simply not good-faith actors — "if they will not tell you what they believe, you should not be having the conversation," and online harassers who send abusive or pornographic messages should just be blocked.[^2] He also raises the practical question of navigating *intramural* LDS disagreements (e.g., whether the three degrees of glory are one place or three) in an elders-quorum setting.[^3]
