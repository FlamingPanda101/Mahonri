# Marvin Sweeney

**Who:** Contemporary American biblical scholar specializing in the Hebrew Bible and prophetic literature. Professor at Claremont School of Theology.

**Role in biblical studies:** Sweeney has refined and complicated the classical [[Deutero-Isaiah Theory]] originally crystallized by [[Bernhard Duhm]]. He argues that the compositional history of Isaiah is even more complex than the three-author model — with later literary layers appearing within chapters 1–39, suggesting redactors wove later material into earlier sections. He maintains that the majority of Isaiah 1–39 is probably the work of an 8th-century prophet named Isaiah, but with significant later additions.
