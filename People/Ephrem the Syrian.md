# Ephrem the Syrian (c. 306-373)

Fourth-century Syriac deacon, theologian, and hymn-writer; a revered church father.

## Cave of Treasures
His tradition is associated with the *Cave of Treasures*, one of the apocryphal sources for the [[Blood of Abel - Unburiable Tradition|unburiable body of Abel]] tradition cited in this episode. See [[Multiple Attestation - Corroborating Apocryphal Sources]] [^1].
