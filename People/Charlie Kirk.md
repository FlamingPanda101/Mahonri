# Charlie Kirk

Conservative political activist and founder of **Turning Point USA**, known for live, unscripted campus debates with students. Not a philosopher or ethicist by training; an effective popular communicator who argued a Christian, pro-life worldview in short Q&A formats.

## Abortion exchange with medical student Kai Bevon
In a viral campus exchange, Kirk argued the pro-life case against a Cambridge medical student (Kai Bevon) who claimed to have "schooled" him. [[Trent Horn]] reviews the clash and judges it a draw of people *talking past each other* rather than a defeat: Kirk correctly held that a new human organism begins at conception, but stated it imprecisely (leaning on "DNA is created," "something magical happens," and souls) and chose weaker counter-examples (Alzheimer's/dementia patients) where stronger ones were available. Horn supplies the precise versions Kirk could have used — see [[The Scientific Consensus That Human Life Begins at Fertilization]] and [[Equal Human Rights Come From What We Are Not What We Can Do]].[^1]

Horn notes the exchange is no longer answerable by Kirk himself, who is described in the video as deceased.[^2]
