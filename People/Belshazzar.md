# Belshazzar

Belshazzar is the Babylonian ruler of Daniel 5, at whose feast a disembodied hand writes MENE, MENE, TEKEL, UPHARSIN on the wall ([[Daniel 5.25|Daniel 5:25]]). [[Daniel]] interprets it as God's judgment: the kingdom is numbered, weighed, and divided; Belshazzar is slain that night.

## In the Miracle-Test Debate
His feast is the setting for the "writing on the wall" that a skeptic proposes as a star-spelled proof of God, illustrating the [[Miracle-Tests for God Commit the God-of-the-Gaps Fallacy|God-of-the-gaps]] objection.
