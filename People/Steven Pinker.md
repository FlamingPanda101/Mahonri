# Steven Pinker

**Era:** Contemporary (b. 1954)
**Tradition:** Secular humanist
**Role:** Harvard cognitive psychologist; author of *The Better Angels of Our Nature*, *Enlightenment Now*

## Bio

Steven Pinker is a Harvard psychologist known for the thesis that violence has declined and the world is, broadly, getting better.[^1]

## Topics

### "End of History" Optimism
[[Glenn Scrivener]] references "a kind of a Steven Pinker sort of sense" of inevitable progress to explain why [[Tom Holland]]'s thesis would *not* have resonated 20 years before *Dominion* — the post-Cold-War "end of history" optimism has since collapsed (9/11, immigration, dechurching), making the Dominion thesis newly resonant. See [[Why the Dominion Thesis Resonates Now]].[^1]
