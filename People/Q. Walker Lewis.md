# Q. Walker Lewis

A Black Latter-day Saint and abolitionist ordained an elder in the early Church (in Massachusetts), cited alongside [[Elijah Abel]] as evidence that Black men held the priesthood during the [[Joseph Smith]] era, before the later [[priesthood ban]].

## Significance
[[Don Bradley]] references "Elder Q. Walker Lewis as well" when establishing that Joseph Smith ordained Black men and did not treat [[Abraham 1.21-27|Abraham 1]] as a racial restriction. See [[Joseph Smith Ignored Race-Based Scriptural Distinctions]].
