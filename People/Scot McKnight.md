# Scot McKnight

Protestant New Testament scholar and commentator (notably on the letter of James). (Auto-captions render "Scott Mcnite.")

## James and works
Cited — *against* the typical Protestant reading — that "no matter how hard we Protestants might try to work this out, the bottom line for James is having works." A telling concession from within the tradition. See [[James - Different Kind of Faith Reply Rebutted]] [^1].
