# Council of Trent (YouTube Channel)

A Catholic apologetics YouTube channel and podcast (trenhornpodcast.com).
Produces detailed theological rebuttals to Protestant doctrines, particularly
around justification, sola fide, and sola scriptura.

## Style
- Scholarly but accessible — cites Protestant scholars (Schreiner, Zins,
  McKnight) when they agree with Catholic positions
- Heavy use of original Greek and patristic sources
- Self-deprecating humor (willingness to joke about "faith alone" in
  subscriber counts)
- References Council of Trent and Catechism of the Catholic Church as
  authoritative
