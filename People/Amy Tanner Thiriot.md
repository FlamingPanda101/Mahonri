# Amy Tanner Thiriot

Amy Tanner Thiriot is the author of *Slavery in Zion*, a documentary and genealogical history of Black lives and servitude in Utah Territory. [[Daniel C. Peterson]] read her book alongside *This Abominable Slavery* and credits it with beginning to change his view of slavery in Utah.[^1]

He attributes to her work the story of Green Flake — whom [[Brigham Young]] bought from his owner and set up with a farm — and Brigham's instruction that Green stop paying his wife's former owners ("You owe them no debt").[^2]
