# Farrell Edwards

**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** (LDS-affiliated context; cited as a physicist, not an apologist)
**Role:** Physicist

## Bio
W. Farrell Edwards is a physicist who, with [[Boyd Edwards]], co-authored a statistical study of chiasmus in the Book of Mormon published in BYU Studies. [[Alex Arnold]] presents the pair as neutral scientists rather than apologists.[^1]

## On the Statistics of Alma 36 Chiasmus
Their analysis of [[Alma 36.1|Alma 36]] put the odds of its 17-layer chiastic structure occurring by chance at between 1 in 10,000 and 1 in a million. Arnold cites this as quantitative support that the structure was deliberately ancient and beyond Joseph Smith's awareness. See [[Chiasmus as Evidence of Ancient Authorship in the Book of Mormon]].[^1]
