# Michael George

Investigator for the Salt Lake County Attorney's Office in the [[Brenda Miles]] / [[Russell M. Nelson]] case. He testified that "no one from the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints attempted to influence or interfere with our investigation in any way" — direct sworn rebuttal of the conspiracy claim that the church coerced law enforcement to quash the allegations.

## No church interference
George's testimony is one of three from investigators (with [[Diana Stevens]] and [[Leslie Lewis]]) refuting the cover-up theory. See [[No Evidence the LDS Church Suppressed the Investigations]]. [^1]
