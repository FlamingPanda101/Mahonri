# James E. Faust (1920-2007)

Latter-day Saint apostle (Quorum of the Twelve from 1978) and Second Counselor in the First Presidency under Gordon B. Hinckley (1995-2007).

## Cited in *Ancient Christians*
Cecilia Peek quotes Faust alongside Bruce R. McConkie and Dallin H. Oaks to illustrate the LDS conviction that the Atonement's full nature lies beyond mortal comprehension: "Our salvation depends on believing in and accepting the Atonement... The nature of the Atonement and its effects is so infinite, so unfathomable, and so profound that it lies beyond the knowledge and comprehension of mortal man." [^1] This frames the four ancient atonement models as faithful but partial human attempts. See [[Four Ancient Atonement Models]].
