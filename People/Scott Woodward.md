# Scott Woodward

Latter-day Saint religious educator and co-host, with [[Casey Paul Griffiths]], of the **Church History Matters** podcast (produced in connection with Doctrine and Covenants Central / Scripture Central). Woodward and Griffiths work through the Doctrine and Covenants section by section using a "context, content, controversies, consequences" framework, aiming for a more-than-average depth on each revelation while remaining devotional and faith-affirming.

## Key contributions in the vault
- On the **Word of Wisdom** ([[D&C 89]]): argues the revelation was not received in a cultural vacuum but interacts with the 1830s temperance and health-reform movements, and that it is a "living commandment" whose strict-abstinence form developed gradually after Joseph Smith. See [[The Word of Wisdom Was a Gradual Living Commandment Not an Instant Prohibition]] and [[The Word of Wisdom Reflects Its 1833 Context Including Outdated Health Counsel]].
- On the **apostasy** ([[D&C 86]]): presses the "Church driven into the wilderness" reading of the Parable of the Wheat and Tares, arguing the priesthood lineage and Christ's work never fully ceased. See [[The Apostasy Was the Church Driven Into the Wilderness Not Annihilated]].
