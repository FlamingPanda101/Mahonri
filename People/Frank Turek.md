# Frank Turek

**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Evangelical Protestant
**Role:** Apologist, author, debater (Cross Examined)

## Bio

Frank Turek is an evangelical Christian apologist, author, and public debater,
known for the *Cross Examined* ministry.

## "Gold Plates in My Garage"

Turek is the critic whose remark frames the episode: "Golden plates are not a
miracle. I can make golden plates in my garage."[^1] The Ward Radio panel treats
this as the central objection to rebut, arguing the quip ignores the witness
descriptions and concedes that real plates existed. See
[[Witness Descriptions Constrain Any Fabrication Hypothesis]] and
[[Joseph Smith Lacked the Metallurgical Skill and Time]].
