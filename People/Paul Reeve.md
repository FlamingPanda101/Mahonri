# Paul Reeve

W. Paul Reeve is an academic historian (Simmons Professor of Mormon Studies, University of Utah) specializing in race and early Latter-day Saint history. He is, with [[LaJean Carruth]] and [[Christopher Rich]], a co-author of *This Abominable Slavery*, the documentary study that [[Daniel C. Peterson]] credits with overturning his prior understanding of Utah's 1852 servitude legislation.[^1]
