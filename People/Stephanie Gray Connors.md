# Stephanie Gray Connors

Canadian Catholic **pro-life author and public speaker**, known for a calm, question-driven (Socratic) method of arguing the sanctity-of-life case on abortion and assisted suicide. Author of *Love Unleashes Life: Abortion and the Art of Communicating Truth* and ***Start with What: 10 Principles for Thinking About Assisted Suicide***. She has spoken to audiences including Google employees and at Harvard.

## On assisted suicide (recommended by Trent Horn)
[[Trent Horn]] closes his Counsel of Trent video "The Assisted Suicide Dystopia Is Here" by recommending Connors' *Start with What: 10 Principles for Thinking About Assisted Suicide* as further reading for the sanctity-of-life case her shares throughout the episode — the slippery-slope worry, the intrinsic-dignity grounding, and the distinction between killing and refusing extraordinary treatment. See [[Assisted Suicide Cannot Draw a Principled Line]], [[Dignity Is How You Face Death Not the Manner of It]], and [[Refusing Extraordinary Means Is Not Choosing to Suffer]] (source: [[The Counsel of Trent - The Assisted Suicide Dystopia Is Here]]).[^1]
