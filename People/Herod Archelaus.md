# Herod Archelaus

Son of Herod the Great. Ruled Judea, Samaria, and Idumea as ethnarch from 4 BCE until his deposition by Rome in 6 CE, after which Judea became a Roman province governed directly — leading to the census under [[Quirinius]]. Matthew 2:22 notes Joseph avoided Judea because Archelaus ruled there.
