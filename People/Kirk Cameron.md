# Kirk Cameron

**Full name:** Kirk Cameron (b. 1970)
**Role:** Actor, evangelist
**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Protestant (evangelical)

## Bio

Kirk Cameron is an American actor who rose to fame as a teenager on the sitcom *Growing Pains* before becoming a prominent evangelical Christian. He partnered with [[Ray Comfort]] on the "Way of the Master" evangelism program and the film *Fireproof*, and has produced documentaries and children's content promoting a conservative Christian worldview.

## Relevance to the vault

Cameron appears as a recognizable name in the popular evangelical-apologetics scene that the vault's biblical-studies material interacts with. He is referenced in connection with the kind of mass-audience evangelical content engaged (and often critiqued) by scholars discussing the composition and sources of the Pentateuch.
