# Paul the Apostle

Paul of Tarsus (c. 5–64/67 CE), apostle to the Gentiles. Author of the earliest Christian writings (~50s CE), including Romans, 1-2 Corinthians, Galatians, Philippians, 1 Thessalonians, and Philemon. Never met the historical Jesus; his letters are theological rather than biographical.
