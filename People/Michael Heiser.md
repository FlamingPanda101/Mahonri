# Michael S. Heiser (1963-2023)

Evangelical biblical scholar, Ph.D. in Hebrew Bible and Semitic languages, best known for academic work on the **divine council** in ancient Israelite religion (*The Unseen Realm*). Not a Latter-day Saint, but his work on Old Testament divine plurality is widely cited by LDS apologists.

## Divine plurality / "trial of the gods"
Cited on Ward Radio for the point that **Yahweh was the destroyer of other gods** — context that reframes [[Isaiah 43-10 - No God Formed Before Me Rebutted|Isaiah 43:10]] as a courtroom polemic against idols (Isaiah 40-48) rather than a flat denial that any other divine beings exist [^1].
