# King Noah

**Role:** Wicked Nephite king in the land of Nephi
**Era:** Nephite history (~150 BC, narrative setting)
**Tradition:** Book of Mormon figure (LDS scripture)

## Bio
Noah, son of [[Zeniff]], reigned wickedly over the people of Zeniff: he put down his father's priests, installed his own, taxed the people to support lavish living, and burned the prophet [[Abinadi]] alive when Abinadi confronted him (Mosiah 11-17). His son [[Limhi]] later inherited a people in bondage. The Ward Radio panel reads Noah ironically as a failed Davidic "rest" figure (Solomon was named a "son of rest"), heightening the contrast with the true heir Abinadi who stands before him.
