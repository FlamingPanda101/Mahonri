# Catherine Smith

Younger sister of [[Joseph Smith]] and one of the natural [[Informal Witnesses of the Golden Plates|informal witnesses]] of the golden plates.

## Encounter with the plates
She recalled "seeing a package on the table containing the gold plates," picking them up to "judge the weight" — they "were heavy like gold" — and rippling her fingers along the edges, feeling separate metal plates and hearing "the tinkle of sound that they made" [^1]. Her tactile testimony parallels that of [[William Smith]] and [[Emma Smith]]; see [[Natural vs Supernatural Witnesses]].
