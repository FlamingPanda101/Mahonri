# Ed Thomas

**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Ward Radio co-host / panelist

## Bio
Ed Thomas is a Latter-day Saint co-host on the [[Ward Radio]] apologetics podcast, appearing alongside host [[Cardon Ellis]] on Book of Mormon episodes. He co-hosted the response to critic [[Dan Vogel]] on the New York hill's stone box (with archaeologist guest [[Josh Gailey]]) and the episode on reformed Egyptian with geologist-linguist [[Jerry Grover]].

## Why he matters
He is a recurring host rather than a subject-matter expert; his role in the vault is as part of the Ward Radio panel framing technical guests' arguments for a popular audience. The substantive claims in the episodes he co-hosts are carried by the guests — e.g., the minimal-facts case for the gold plates (see [[Dan Vogel's East-Side Hole Is the Lone Source Against the West-Side Stone Box]] and [[The Minimal Facts Method Applied to the Gold Plates]]) and the "characters document" translation (see [[The Characters Document Translated as Abbreviated Hieratic and Maya]]).
