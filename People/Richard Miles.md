# Richard Miles

Husband of [[Brenda Miles]] and son-in-law of apostle [[Russell M. Nelson]]; accused alongside his wife in the [[Marion Smith]] / ISAT satanic-ritual-abuse case. The allegations against him — that he "tortured a girl by nearly drowning her," that the couple videotaped the abuse, killed animals "to show their resolve," and sedated children to keep them quiet — produced "no marks, no blood, no bodily fluids, no trace," and "no videotapes were ever found." Police searched the Miles home and found nothing.

## No evidence across three rounds
The Miles charges were raised, dropped, and re-raised over three decades, the 2018 attempt ending in dismissal with prejudice. See [[Accusations Cannot Transmute Into Evidence]] and [[No Evidence the LDS Church Suppressed the Investigations]]. [^1][^2]
