# Marcus Nash

A general authority of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, cited by historian [[Britney Chapman Nash]] (no relation indicated) for a 2015 article addressing the eternal status of plural versus monogamous sealings.[^1]

Drawing on the sealing ceremony [[Orson Pratt]] "recorded very carefully" during the era of plural marriage, Elder Nash's article concludes that "the covenants and blessings are identical between both polygamous and monogamous marriages" — so, from a ritual standpoint, "eternal exaltation does not ride on your monogamous or polygamous marriage status." Britney Chapman Nash offers this as reassurance to members who fear having to "share their spouse" in the afterlife.[^1] See [[The Covenants and Blessings Are Identical in Monogamous and Plural Sealings]].
