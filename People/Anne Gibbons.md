# Anne Gibbons

**Era:** Contemporary (1990s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Academic / science journalism (non-LDS)
**Role:** Science writer, contributing correspondent for *Science*

## Bio

Anne Gibbons is a science journalist who covers human evolution, genetics, and
archaeology for *Science* magazine.[^1]

## The Myth of Pure Genetic Heritage

Rappleye cites a 2017 *Science* article by Gibbons on "the myth of pure genetic
heritage," which surveys European origin myths (Germans, Anglo-Saxons) that portray
isolated conquest while genetics reveals admixture. He uses it to argue that origin
stories are typically *narrow*, not false — supporting the case that Book of Mormon
peoples were not the continent's only inhabitants. See
[[The Empty Continent Assumption Underlies the DNA Objection]].[^2]
