# John Turner

**Who:** John G. Turner, professor of religious studies at George Mason University and a non-LDS historian whose recent biography of [[Joseph Smith]] appeared "right about the time" [[Kyle Beshears]] was writing his own chapter on Joseph. Turner is also the author of the Harvard University Press biography *Brigham Young: Pioneer Prophet*. [^1]

## Read as Part of the Biographical Spectrum
Beshears names Turner among the biographers — with [[Fawn Brodie]], [[Richard Bushman]], and [[Dan Vogel]] — that he consulted to triangulate "a friend or a critic of the church" before turning to the primary documents. As a non-Latter-day-Saint academic, Turner represents the critical-but-scholarly end of the spectrum Beshears wanted to weigh against the Joseph Smith Papers. See [[Read the Primary Sources Before Judging a Person]] and [[Joseph Smith Was Sincere Not a Fraud]]. [^1]
