# Ben Witherington III

Protestant (Wesleyan-Arminian) New Testament scholar and prolific commentator.

## James and ongoing justification
Cited that James "is dealing with the conduct of those who already believe... the perfection of faith in its working out through good works," and that if James 2:24 refers to God's final verdict on one's life, "even Paul can be said to have agreed." Supports the Catholic ongoing-justification reading. See [[Initial vs Ongoing Justification]] [^1].
