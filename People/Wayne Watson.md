# Wayne Watson

Utah County Chief Deputy Attorney who personally witnessed one of [[Barbara Snow]]'s sessions with a Lehi child and was "appalled." He described how Snow would sit with a child amid toys and stuffed animals, remaining "very formal and stiff" when a child reacted negatively but becoming "very warm and loving" — holding the child in her lap and praising the child — when the child indicated something had happened.

## Eyewitness Critique of Snow's Method
Watson found the technique "totally unacceptable for a criminal investigation" and doubted whether the children's statements reflected reality or "ideas placed in their minds by Barbara Snow." See [[Barbara Snows Interview Methods Manufactured Abuse Allegations]] [^1].
