# Amihai Mazar

**Era:** Contemporary (1980s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Academic (non-LDS), Israeli
**Role:** Biblical archaeologist

## Bio

Amihai Mazar is a distinguished Israeli archaeologist at the Hebrew University of
Jerusalem, author of *Archaeology of the Land of the Bible* — described by Rappleye as
"a very standard reference in the field."[^1]

## Egyptian Khopesh-Scimitars

Mazar writes that "Egyptian *khopesh*-shaped scimitars are known from both the actual
finds and from Egyptian artistic depictions." Rappleye cites this as neutral,
top-tier confirmation that curved swords (scimitars) existed in the ancient Near East
long before the post-Islamic *word*, so the Book of Mormon's cimeters are not
anachronistic. See [[Scimitars Are Not an Anachronism in the Old or New World]].[^2]
