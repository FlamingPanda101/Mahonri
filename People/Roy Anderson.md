# Roy Anderson

Roy Anderson is a Latter-day Saint content creator who runs the **Iron Rod Cast** YouTube channel and is a former seminary teacher.[^1] On the [[Salty Saints - The Apologist Playbook for LDS Men|Salty Saints apologetics panel]] with [[Hayden Carroll]] and [[Sam St. Laurent]], his signature contribution is the **"apologetics as a shield"** framing: apologetics works "as a shield for weak or doubting testimonies… I can take the lashings while you figure out if Jesus Christ is your savior," cutting "the heads off" the accusations so a person can come to know Christ.[^2] He also insists a defender "must have a testimony of the gospel and of Jesus Christ before stepping into that space," lest a testimony resting on Joseph Smith "crumble" under attack.[^3] See [[Spirit Over Argument - The Limits of Apologetics]].
