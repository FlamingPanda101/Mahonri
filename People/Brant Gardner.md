# Brant Gardner

**Era:** Contemporary (1990s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Mesoamerican ethnohistorian, scholar, apologist

## Bio

Brant Gardner is a Latter-day Saint scholar trained in anthropology with a focus on
Mesoamerican ethnohistory. He is best known for the multi-volume *Second Witness*
commentary on the Book of Mormon and for work correlating the text with Mesoamerican
culture and history.[^1]

## New World Correlation Method

Rappleye names Gardner (alongside [[John Sorenson]]) as a leading practitioner of the
method of integrating the Book of Mormon's written record with the archaeology of
Mesoamerica — the same correlation method Rappleye applies to the Old World setting.
See [[No Archaeological Evidence Requires Specifying What to Look For]].[^2]
