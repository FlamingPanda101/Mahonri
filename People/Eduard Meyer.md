# Eduard Meyer

German ancient historian (1855–1930), Professor at the University of Berlin and a major figure in the modern study of antiquity. He is cited in [[All Those In Favor - Are Mormons Just White Islam]] as an originator of the scholarly "Mormonism as Islam" comparison. A Hegelian, Meyer held that similar societal conditions reincarnate the same religious "spirit of the age," and so treated Mormonism as essentially a re-run of Islam — even proposing that the well-documented Mormon movement could serve as a *proxy* for studying the poorly documented origins of Islam.

The channel's critique is that Meyer projected his Hegelian schema onto the data rather than letting the evidence speak, making his comparison agenda-driven pseudo-objectivity. See [[The White Islam Comparison Springs From Agenda-Driven Orientalism]].
