# Helena

Mother of the emperor [[Constantine]] and a devout Christian (the episode contrasts her firm belief with Constantine's uncertain/political faith). Day recounts the tradition that Helena traveled to the Holy Land asking locals where Jesus was born, died, and rose, and built sanctuaries over those sites — including the Church of the Holy Sepulchre and the Church of the Nativity.

Day uses Helena to make a chronological point: she arrives ~300 years after Jesus, so her site identifications rest on a long chain of "my grandma told me" tradition (with the wry caveat that such sites conveniently anchor local commerce). She illustrates the genuine devotion present even amid the apostasy. See [[Apostasy Preserved the Text Steelman]].
