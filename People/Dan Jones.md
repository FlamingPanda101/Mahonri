# Dan Jones

Welsh convert to the LDS Church who was with [[Joseph Smith]] in Carthage Jail the night before the martyrdom and later became a hugely successful missionary in Wales.

## The Wales Prophecy
The night before his death, Joseph Smith asked Jones, "Are you afraid to die?" and then prophesied, "You will yet see Wales and fulfill the mission appointed before you die." On the morning of 27 June 1844 Joseph sent Jones out of the jail to carry a letter to Orville H. Browning in Quincy; the guards would not readmit him, so he was absent when Joseph and Hyrum were murdered. Jones returned to Wales in 1845 and baptized thousands, helping establish the church there. The host of [[Mormonism with the Murf - Joseph Smith's Top 20 Fulfilled Prophecies]] notes this could be an "easily fulfilled" prophecy (see [[Self-Fulfilling Prophecy Objection]]), part of the [[Joseph Smith Predicts His Own Martyrdom]] cluster.[^1]
