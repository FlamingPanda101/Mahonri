# Mandy Green

**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint
**Role:** Independent scholar; teacher of adult religion classes; podcaster; tour leader

## Bio
Mandy Green is an independent Latter-day Saint scholar of the divine feminine and ancient Israelite religion. She teaches adult religion classes at Project Illumination, hosts the podcast *Reflecting Light*, and leads study tours (the "Forbidden Adventures" tour company). She studies the Hebrew Bible in the original language and credits [[Margaret Barker]] as a primary influence — "Margaret Barker taught me everything I know" — alongside earlier Mormon feminists.[^1][^2]

## On the Divine Feminine in the Torah
In her lecture for *The Stick of Joseph* lecture series, Green argues that God the Mother "has always been there" in the Hebrew text of Genesis but was "removed, redacted, altered, changed, written over," and teaches readers to recover her through her patterns and symbols — the plural *Elohim*, the feminine *ruach* hovering over creation, and personified *Wisdom*. See [[The Stick of Joseph - The Divine Feminine in the Torah with Mandy Green]], [[Wisdom Personified as the Mother in Heaven]], [[Ruach Elohim - The Feminine Spirit Hovering Over Creation]], and [[Asherah Pillar Iconography Depicts Wisdom Who Gives Sight]].[^1]
