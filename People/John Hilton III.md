# John Hilton III

**Who:** John Hilton III, a professor at Brigham Young University and author of *Voices in the Book of Mormon*, a study of the distinct speakers and voices within the text.

**Role in this source:** Mentioned at the close of the interview. [[Daniel C. Peterson]] and the host note that the discussion of multiple authorial voices in the Book of Mormon connects to Hilton's book, which the channel covered in a separate interview.[^1] His work parallels [[Grant Hardy]]'s argument that the text contains distinct voices.
