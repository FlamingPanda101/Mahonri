# Jonathan Kvanvig

Analytic philosopher of religion, author of *The Problem of Hell* (Oxford). (Auto-captions: "Kavanvik.")

## The problem of hell
Cited that hell "leads nowhere" — no future good ever makes up for it, the paradigm of pointless gratuitous suffering — and that the watchmaker analogy is "defective to the core" because the watchmaker relies on a pre-existing universe God would himself have to create. See [[Problem of Hell and Creation]] and [[Hell - Watchmaker Wind Up and Go Rebutted]] [^1].
