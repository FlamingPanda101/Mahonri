# Ezra Booth

An early convert who left the Church in 1831 and wrote a series of critical letters. Despite his hostility, his 1831 letter is valued as a *contemporaneous* witness to an otherwise unrecorded revelation.

## Witness to the 1831 Intermarriage Revelation
[[Don Bradley]] cites Booth's 1831 letter as part of the documentary basis for [[Joseph Smith]]'s July 1831 revelation promoting interracial marriage with Native Americans — a revelation dictated but not written down at the time, and reconstructed from contemporaneous and later recollections. See [[Joseph Smith Ignored Race-Based Scriptural Distinctions]].
