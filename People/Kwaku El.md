# Kwaku El

**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint

## Identity

Kwaku El is a Latter-day Saint commentator and co-host associated with Ward Radio. Of Ghanaian heritage, he is referenced in the reformed-Egyptian episode for explaining how Ghanaian names encode tribe, birth day, and birthplace — an analogy [[Cardon Ellis]] uses for the dense, name-as-record glyphs [[Jerry Grover]] finds in the characters document. He appears as a host in the temple-garments episode.
