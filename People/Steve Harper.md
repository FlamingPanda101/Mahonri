# Steve Harper

**Full name:** Steven C. Harper
**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Historian; First Vision specialist

## Bio

Steven C. Harper is a Latter-day Saint historian at Brigham Young University and a documentary editor on the Joseph Smith Papers Project. He is widely regarded as a leading specialist on the First Vision, author of *First Vision: Memory and Mormon Origins* (Oxford University Press, 2019), and is cited as the authority [[Kerry Muhlestein]] defers to on harmonizing the multiple First Vision accounts.

He is quoted (via Muhlestein) for the memorable formulation contrasting Joseph Smith's parents: for [[Lucy Mack Smith]] "it's better to go to any church than no church at all," while for [[Joseph Smith Sr]] "it's better to go to no church than to go to the wrong church" — the family tension that framed the young Joseph's religious anxiety. See [[The Smith Family Religious Division Framed the First Vision]].[^1]
