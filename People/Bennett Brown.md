# Bennett Brown

East Coast / Chicago-area psychiatrist whom the Ward Radio series calls "the Dr. Lawrence Pazder of the East Coast" — a therapist who built fame and fortune "recovering" memories of satanic abuse and appearing on television, alongside figures like Pazder, [[Barbara Snow]], and Whitehead. [^1]

## Sued, settled, and delicensed
In 1993 a former patient sued Brown for "falsely convincing her that she had engaged in satanic rituals, cannibalism, and infanticide"; he settled for $10.6 million, with another patient's case settled for $7.5 million. His medical license was suspended and he was expelled from the Illinois Psychiatric Society and the American Psychiatric Association in 2000. He is a central exhibit in [[Leading SRA Therapists Were Discredited Sued or Delicensed]]. [^2]
