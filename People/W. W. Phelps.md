# W. W. Phelps

William Wines Phelps, an early Latter-day Saint, editor, hymn-writer, and scribe to [[Joseph Smith]]. He worked alongside [[Oliver Cowdery]] on the Egyptian materials in Kirtland.

## Role in the Egyptian Papers
[[Don Bradley]] names Phelps among the scribes who worked with Joseph Smith on the papyri and the [[Grammar and Alphabet of the Egyptian Language]], the document Bradley uses to recover how the curse in [[Abraham 1.21-27|Abraham 1]] was understood. See [[Grammar and Alphabet of the Egyptian Language]].
