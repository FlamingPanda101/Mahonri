# Jerry Grover

**Era:** Contemporary (2010s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Geologist, engineer, researcher

## Bio

Jerry Grover is a licensed geologist and engineer who authored a 2015 book
analyzing the gold plates from an engineering and materials-science perspective.[^1]

## Plate Count Estimate

Grover argues the plates could have been thinner than the range
[[Josh Coats]] assumes, and through experimentation with very thin copper plates
he estimates a stack of **300–600 plates** — well above the ~40 figure assumed by
critics.[^2] See [[Translation Density Shows the Book of Mormon Text Fits]] and
[[Trent Horns Word-Count Argument Uses Wrong Parameters]].
