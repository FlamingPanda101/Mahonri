# Truman Madsen

(1926–2009) A Latter-day Saint philosopher and professor of philosophy and religion at Brigham Young University, well known for his lectures and writings on [[Joseph Smith]] and Mormon thought. Cited by [[Casey Paul Griffiths]] in the Keystone discussion of the [[King Follett Discourse]].

## On Section 93 and Free Will
Griffiths quotes Madsen's judgment that [[D&C 93]] "cuts through the Gordian knot with one slice" by establishing eternal free will and absolving God of authorship of evil. See [[Co-Eternality Solves the Problem of Evil]] and [[Intelligence Is Uncreated and Eternally Has Agency]].
