# LeGene Kuth

LeGene Kuth is named in the vault as a researcher credited (in Dan Peterson's remarks on Ward Radio) with the discovery that reporter **George D. Watt systematically made Brigham Young sound more authoritarian** when transcribing his sermons for the [[Journal of Discourses]]. The finding closely parallels the shorthand-transcription work attributed elsewhere in the vault to [[LaJean Carruth]].

## Relevance

The discovery supports the argument that the Journal of Discourses is an imperfect mirror of what Brigham Young actually said, undercutting "hero-flattening" readings that treat its harshest lines as verbatim — see [[Tabernacle Infallibility Misreads Brigham Young]] and [[Brigham Young as the Fall Guy]].
