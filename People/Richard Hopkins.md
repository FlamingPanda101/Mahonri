# Richard Hopkins

LDS attorney and author of *How Greek Philosophy Corrupted the Christian Concept of God*. Day read it ~30 years before going to graduate school (where he learned Greek), then re-examined its arguments and reports finding no fault in them. The book traces how Christian thinkers absorbed Greek metaphysics — Parmenides, the *logos*, Plato's forms, Aristotle's unmoved mover — to make Christianity "acceptable" to the educated classes, ultimately producing an immaterial, impassible God at odds with the embodied, passionate God of scripture. See [[Greek Philosophy Corrupted the Christian Concept of God]], [[Unmoved Mover Reshaped God the Father]].
