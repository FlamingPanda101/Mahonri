# Esther

**Who:** Jewish queen of Persia in the Book of Esther. She uses her position to save the Jewish people from Haman's genocidal plot, employing indirect, diplomatic strategy rather than open confrontation.

**Role:** [[Jacob Wright]] uses Esther as the paradigmatic example of the "survival model" in biblical narrative — she works within the system, using wisdom and indirection rather than martial prowess. Wright contrasts her with [[Mordecai]] and argues this shift from battlefield to survival-oriented leadership reflects the situation of a small, defeated people.
