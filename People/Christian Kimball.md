# Christian Kimball

**Who:** Host of *Dialogue Gospel Study*, the gospel-study video series of the *Dialogue: A Journal of Mormon Thought* foundation. A Latter-day Saint of the liberal/heterodox *Dialogue* milieu, he convenes conversations between Latter-day Saint audiences and scholars on the assigned Come Follow Me scripture block.

**Role here:** Moderator of the January 2026 session featuring [[Dan McClellan]] on reading the Old Testament, framing the discussion around how Latter-day Saints can engage the Hebrew Bible with academic honesty.
