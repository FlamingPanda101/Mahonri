# Adam

The first man ([[Genesis 4|Genesis]]); father of [[Cain]] and [[Abel]].

## The unburiable Abel
In the apocryphal tradition, the earth would not receive [[Abel]]'s body until **Adam** died and was buried first — "the first to be taken from the earth" should be "the first returned to it." See [[Blood of Abel - Unburiable Tradition]].
