# Adam

The first man ([[Genesis 4|Genesis]]); father of [[Cain]] and [[Abel]].

## The unburiable Abel
In the apocryphal tradition, the earth would not receive [[Abel]]'s body until **Adam** died and was buried first — "the first to be taken from the earth" should be "the first returned to it." See [[Blood of Abel - Unburiable Tradition]].

## Lucifer's envy of Adam and the selection of the first man
Jonah Barnes argues the apocrypha consistently make Lucifer's fall an act of jealousy of *Adam* (the angels were told to honor the newly created Adam), and that Abraham 3 records the premortal selection of Adam/Michael as the first man. See [[Lucifer Fell Out of Jealousy of Adam in the Apocrypha]] and [[Abraham 3 Is the Selection of Adam Not the Redeemer]]. The apocryphal Adam-and-Eve literature Joseph Smith emphasized is treated in [[Joseph Smith Chose the Adam-and-Eve Battlefield Before the Texts Surfaced]].
