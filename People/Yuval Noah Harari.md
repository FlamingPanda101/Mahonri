# Yuval Noah Harari

**Era:** Contemporary (b. 1976)
**Tradition:** Secular
**Role:** Israeli historian; author of *Sapiens*

## Bio

Yuval Noah Harari is a historian best known for *Sapiens*, where he argues many shared human values are "imagined orders" with no objective basis.[^1]

## Topics

### Equality as a Biblical Fiction
[[Glenn Scrivener]] cites Harari's argument that equality is "a fundamentally biblical idea... generated from a belief in God and souls" — but that if we no longer believe in God and souls, the grounding for equality dissolves. Scrivener uses this as a secular witness to the Christian origin of equality. See [[The Christian Origin of Equality]] and [[Equality Loses Its Grounding Without God and Souls]].[^1]
