# William Dembski

**Role:** Mathematician, philosopher, and Intelligent Design theorist
**Era:** Contemporary (b. 1960)

## Bio

William A. Dembski is a mathematician and philosopher best known for developing the Intelligent Design concepts of **specified complexity** and the **explanatory filter** for detecting design. His work appears in this vault's design-argument material alongside [[Stephen C. Meyer]] and [[The Scientific Case for Intelligent Design]].
