# John Butler (DNA scientist)

**Era:** Contemporary (2000s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Forensic DNA scientist, author

## Bio

John Butler is a forensic DNA scientist — the kind of DNA analysis used in the
courtroom and depicted in crime dramas. He brings forensic expertise alongside the
population-genetics expertise of his co-author [[Ugo Perego]]; together they wrote the
2025 Deseret Book volume *Let's Talk about DNA and the Book of Mormon*.[^1]

## DNA and the Book of Mormon

With Perego, Butler argues that to claim "no DNA evidence" one must first know what
genetic signature should be present — and that, given the relevant population genetics,
no Near-Eastern signal should be expected. See
[[No DNA Evidence Requires Specifying What Signature to Expect]].[^2] He also joins
Perego in rejecting Haplogroup X2A as valid Book of Mormon evidence — see
[[Haplogroup X2A Is Not Valid Evidence for the Book of Mormon]].[^3]
