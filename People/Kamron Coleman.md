# Kamron Coleman

**Full Name:** Kamron Coleman (often transcribed "Cameron")
**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Artist, multimedia sculptor/painter; creator of the *Apotheosis Scroll*

## Bio

Kamron Coleman is a Latter-day Saint artist — originally a sculptor, later moving into multimedia graphic art that fuses oil paintings with blueprint-style and computer-drawn elements. He served a mission in Samoa. His website is kamroncoleman.com; the artwork's site is apotheosisscroll.com.[^1]

His central project is the ***Apotheosis Scroll***, a roughly eight-year, still-unfinished work intended to reach **72 panels** (currently ~36, the cover plus 35), each 8 feet long — ultimately a continuous painting nearly two football fields long, which Coleman and [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|Dave Butler]] note is already ~25% longer than the Bayeux Tapestry (70 m).[^2] Coleman frames it as an "initiatic," experiential covenant path to be traversed in order — explicitly "not a doctrine" — drawing heavily on the **Gospel of Thomas** as the "right-brain" counterpart to the New Testament.[^3]

## Thesis

Coleman's governing claim is that **ancient astronomy is a lost universal language** in which the world's scriptures were encoded, recovered through the restored gospel and the Doctrine and Covenants. He developed it after an intense spiritual experience reading a [[Margaret Barker|Barker]]-adjacent article by LDS author Val Larsen depicting the El–Asherah–Anat–Baal council on the Mari cylinder seal, then spent months studying (including Yale's online Old Testament lectures) and painting the scene full-size.[^4]

Key positions, each developed in his interview on [[The Stick of Joseph - The Lost Scripture Language of the Stars]]:
- The seven gospel ordinances are seven constellations (mazzaroth = constellation = ordinance); the body is a fractal of the cosmos — see [[The Seven Constellations Are the Seven Ordinances of the Temple Ascent]].
- Zodiacal precession (Taurus → Aries → Pisces) decodes the lamb/ram/fish of salvation history — see [[The Precession of the Equinoxes Decodes the Lamb Ram and Fish]].
- The pole-star shift maps the Star of Bethlehem and the Second Coming — see [[The Pole Star Shift Marks the Star of Bethlehem and the Second Coming]].
- The cosmic mill grinds the bread of life — see [[The Cosmic Mill Grinds the Bread of Life]].

He repeatedly characterizes the work as imaginative and provisional ("my hypothesis," "according to my study, what my imagination has supplied me"), says he would "rather have a couple people study this per century than 100,000 people slightly interested right now," and invites disagreement rather than acceptance.[^5]
