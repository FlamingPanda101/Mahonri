# Adele Reinhartz

**Role:** New Testament scholar (University of Ottawa); specialist in the Gospel of John
**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Academic biblical studies

## Bio
Adele Reinhartz is a leading scholar of the Gospel of John and of Jewish-Christian relations in early Christianity. [[Hugo Mendez]] invokes her work on the Johannine prologue.

## The Logos Hymn as Hermeneutical Key
Méndez cites Reinhartz to support keeping the prologue (the Logos hymn) with the original Gospel: she brings out that the prologue functions as something like a hermeneutical key to the rest of John, so that without it the Gospel "wouldn't feel as complete." See [[Logos Hymn Belongs to the Original Gospel]] [^1].
