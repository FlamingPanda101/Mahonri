# Alex Rosenberg

**Full Name:** Alexander Rosenberg
**Era:** Contemporary (1946–present)
**Tradition:** Atheist, naturalist
**Role:** Philosopher of science, R. Taylor Cole Professor of Philosophy at
Duke University

## Bio

Alex Rosenberg is an American philosopher specializing in the philosophy of
science, particularly biology. He is the R. Taylor Cole Professor of Philosophy
at Duke University and the author of *The Atheist's Guide to Reality* (2011)
and other works articulating a scientistic, naturalistic worldview.

## Topics

### Nihilism and Morality
Rosenberg is quoted in the video for his starkly consistent statement on the
moral implications of atheism:

> "There's no moral difference between the acts of Mother Teresa and those of
> Hitler. Nihilism rejects the distinction between acts that are morally right
> and ones that are morally wrong."[^1]

[[Hayden Carroll]] notes that Rosenberg explicitly identifies atheism with
nihilism, agreeing that atheism "essentially leads logically leads you to it"
— a concession from a prominent atheist philosopher that atheism entails moral
nihilism.[^2]
