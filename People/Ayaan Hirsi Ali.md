# Ayaan Hirsi Ali

**Era:** Contemporary (b. 1969)
**Tradition:** Former Muslim, former New Atheist, now professes Christianity
**Role:** Somali-Dutch-American author and activist

## Bio

Ayaan Hirsi Ali (mis-rendered "Iron Hers Ali"/"Ayan") was once "the most famous female atheist in the world" before announcing in 2023 that she had become a Christian.[^1]

## Topics

### The Atheist-to-Christian Turn
[[Justin Brierley]] cites her as a case of someone newly "convinced about the value of Christianity" — that a culture like ours cannot be sustained "without the Christian story at the bottom of it." [[Alex OConnor|Alex O'Connor]] reads her conversion as driven by personal depression plus political worry that New Atheism failed to deliver a humanist utopia. Both note she "thought the Netherlands was a Christian country." See [[Why the Dominion Thesis Resonates Now]].[^1]
