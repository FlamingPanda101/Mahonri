# Jesus of Nazareth

Jesus (c. 4 BCE – 30/33 CE), Jewish teacher from Galilee. The gospels consistently refer to him as "Jesus of Nazareth" or "the Nazarene" outside the nativity accounts. Scholarly consensus holds he was likely born in Nazareth, not Bethlehem.
