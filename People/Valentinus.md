# Valentinus

Second-century Christian Gnostic teacher, an academic from Egypt who moved to Rome and (per the episode) was a candidate for bishop but never elected. Butler notes his doctrines were "very influential for a few decades and then lose out" — a concrete example of how a teaching could nearly become orthodoxy before losing the political contest. See [[Heresy Was Decided by Votes Not Demonstrable Truth]], [[Docetism and Early Christological Diversity]].
