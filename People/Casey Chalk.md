# Casey Chalk

Catholic author and journalist, author of *The Obscurity of Scripture: Disputing Sola Scriptura and the Protestant Notion of Biblical Perspicuity*. Argues against Protestant perspicuity while granting that "scripture interprets scripture" had ancient pedigree (e.g., in Augustine, the inspiration for Luther's Augustinian order).[^1]

## Perspicuity of Scripture
Cited by [[Trent Horn]] for the concession that early fathers like [[Augustine]] *did* affirm a version of "scripture interprets scripture" — used to press [[Anthony Rogers]] on whether Augustine therefore held sola scriptura. See [[Perspicuity Fails the Hard-Cases Test]] and [[The Early Church Did Not Operate Under Sola Scriptura]].[^1]
