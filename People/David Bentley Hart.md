# David Bentley Hart

Eastern Orthodox philosophical theologian and prominent **universalist** (*That All Shall Be Saved*).

## Against eternal hell
Cited twice: that unending punishment for a finite being's finite actions is "disproportionate, unjust, and... sheer pointless cruelty" ([[Hell - Finite Sin vs Infinite Punishment]]), and that "the moral destiny of all creation and the moral nature of God are absolutely inseparable" — so creation *ex nihilo* makes an eternal hell impossible ([[Hell - Watchmaker Wind Up and Go Rebutted]], [[Problem of Hell and Creation]]) [^1].

## Pre-existence
Cited by [[Ward Radio - Is Mainstream Christianity Accidentally Becoming Mormon|Ward Radio]] as a Christian theologian who "has talked about pre-existence"[^2] — an example of mainstream Christian scholars engaging with a doctrine traditionally dismissed as uniquely Mormon.
