# Nebuchadnezzar

Babylonian king who conquered Jerusalem and destroyed Solomon's temple (586 BC).

## The boiling blood of Zechariah
In the Mishnah Sanhedrin 96b tradition, when Nebuchadnezzar entered the defiled temple he found Zechariah's blood still **boiling** after ~200 years; he killed priests until it finally stilled. A vivid element of the [[Blood of Zechariah - Perpetual Boiling Blood]] tradition.
