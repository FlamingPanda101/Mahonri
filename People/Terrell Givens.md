# Terrell Givens

LDS scholar and professor of religion. Per Benjamin Park, Givens is the likely
author of the LDS Church's Gospel Topics essay "Becoming Like God," which
represents the current institutional approach to the [[King Follett Discourse]]'s
radical doctrines.

## The Theosis Framing

The essay connects Mormon exaltation to broader Christian traditions of theosis
(divinization) — particularly Eastern Orthodoxy — rather than presenting it as a
uniquely LDS innovation. This frames [[Human Deification in Mormonism]] as part of
a broader Christian conversation while retaining Mormonism's distinctive materialist
framework.[^1]

Per Park, this approach "emphasizes both the exceptionalism of Mormonism as well as
its connections to the broader Christian world — which is where I think the
Latter-day Saints currently are today in viewing the King Follett Discourse."[^1]
