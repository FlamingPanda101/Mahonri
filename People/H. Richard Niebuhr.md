# H. Richard Niebuhr (1894-1962)

American theologian, historian, and sociologist of religion. Argued that, over generations, **sects tend to evolve into churches** — the second generation is born into the group, standards relax to include more people, and internal hierarchies professionalize.

## Sect-to-church evolution
This dynamic explains why a group like the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints can move along the continuum over time. See [[Where Mormons Rank on the Church-Sect Continuum]] and [[Church Sect Cult Distinction]] [^1].
