# Presendia Huntington Buell

**Role:** Plural wife of [[Joseph Smith]]; sister of [[Zina Huntington Jacobs]]
**Era:** 1810–1892
**Tradition:** The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

## Bio
Presendia Huntington, sister of [[Zina Huntington Jacobs]], was already married to Norman Buell when she was sealed to [[Joseph Smith]] in mid-December 1841 — about six weeks after Joseph sealed her sister Zina. She bore a child in 1842; because the exact birth date is unrecorded (the child did not survive), [[Don Bradley]] notes there is roughly a two-in-three chance she too was pregnant at the time of the sealing — strengthening the pregnant-bride pattern. See [[Joseph Smith Married Pregnant Women Refuting the Sexual-Motive Thesis]].
