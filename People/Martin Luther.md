# Martin Luther (1483-1546)

German friar who launched the Protestant Reformation; originator of *sola fide* (justification by faith alone).

## The Epistle of Straw
Luther had a famously conflicted relationship with the letter of James because of its teaching on works — calling it "an epistle of straw," musing about "throwing Jimmy into the stove," and claiming "some Jew wrote it." He never actually removed it from the Bible. See [[Luther and the Epistle of Straw]] [^1].

## Sola fide
His "faith alone" formula is the position the Catholic argument from James 2 targets — see [[Faith Alone but Never Alone]] and [[James 2-24 - Justified by Works Not Faith Alone]].
