# Jordan Bowman

**Era:** Contemporary (active 2020s)
**Tradition:** The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS / Mormon)
**Role:** Filmmaker (Spectra Films), member of the Stick of Joseph crew

## Bio

Jordan Bowman is a Latter-day Saint filmmaker with Spectra Films who appears with the [[Stick of Joseph]] crew. In the Book of Mormon anachronisms episode hosted by [[Cardon Ellis]], he plays the conciliatory "let's all get along" foil to Cardon's deliberately combative bit, repeatedly arguing that critics who compiled anachronism lists actually did Latter-day Saints a favor by giving them artifacts to go investigate, and pressing the question of what arbitrary threshold of "solved" anachronisms would ever satisfy a skeptic. He had recently worked as a camera operator on a Mr. Beast Squid Game shoot.
