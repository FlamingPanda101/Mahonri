# Christian (Holy Rebellion)

Christian is the host of *The Holy Rebellion*, a Latter-day Saint podcast/YouTube channel. In this episode he interviews the historian [[Mark (Holy Rebellion)|Mark]] — a neighbor and ward member — about [[Joseph Smith]] and treasure hunting.[^1] Christian states he works in finance, which he uses to draw an analogy between the 1826 court case and the church's SEC fine.[^2]

His role in the conversation is to surface the common online critiques (Joseph as "convicted con man," money digger, seer-stone user) and press Mark on whether they are accurate or overclaims, and he frames the closing theme of being gracious toward other faiths' supernatural claims.[^3]
