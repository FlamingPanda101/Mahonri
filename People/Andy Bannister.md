# Andy Bannister

**Full Name:** Andy Bannister
**Era:** Contemporary (active 2010s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Evangelical Christianity
**Role:** Islamic-studies scholar and apologist

## Bio

Andy Bannister is a Christian apologist and academic with a doctorate in Islamic studies, whose dissertation applied oral-formulaic analysis to the Quran. He is director of the Solas Centre for Public Christianity and an adjunct speaker/scholar with several apologetics organizations.

[[Wes Huff]] cites Bannister as a personal friend with "a PhD in Islamic studies from Oxford University" who does computer coding and has worked on **Quran Gateway**, software attempting to begin the kind of computer-assisted textual-critical analysis of the Quran that the New Testament has enjoyed for centuries. (Huff's attribution of the degree to Oxford is approximate; Bannister's doctorate is from the University of London.)
