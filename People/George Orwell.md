# George Orwell (1903-1950)

English novelist and essayist (pen name of Eric Arthur Blair), author of *1984* and *Animal Farm*. Famous for analyzing how political control of language ("Newspeak") shapes and constrains what people are able to think.

## Language as power
Invoked as having understood "100 years ago" the postmodern point that controlling vocabulary controls thought — if you can control how people talk about things, you control what they think about them. Cited in support of [[Linguistic Relativity Shapes Thought]] [^1].
