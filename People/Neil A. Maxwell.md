# Neil A. Maxwell

Latter-day Saint apostle (the transcript's "Neil A. Maxwell"; usually spelled Neal A. Maxwell) named in the 2018 relitigation of the [[Brenda Miles]] case. [[Marion Smith]] claimed that Maxwell, a family friend, offered the children a priesthood blessing and encouraged them to "forgive others of their trespasses" — which Smith reinterpreted as proof he knew of and was covering up satanic ritual abuse on behalf of [[Russell M. Nelson]]. The episode treats this as a textbook unfalsifiable inference: an ordinary pastoral act of encouraging forgiveness recast as conspiracy.

## Forgiveness recast as cover-up
Maxwell's inclusion illustrates how the SRA narrative converts benign clerical conduct into evidence of guilt. See [[The Nelson Skull and Bones Claim Confuses Utah's Owl and Key With Yale]] and [[No Evidence the LDS Church Suppressed the Investigations]]. [^1]
