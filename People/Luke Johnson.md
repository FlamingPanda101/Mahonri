# Luke Johnson

One of the original members of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles who left the church after the Kirtland bank crisis, counted among the supernatural [[Informal Witnesses of the Golden Plates|informal witnesses]].

## Reaffirmed witness after leaving
Per a second-hand account by John D. Lee (in Eber Howe's *Mormonism Unveiled*), Johnson, when asked whether his signed statement about "seeing the angel and the plates" was true, said it was, explaining: "I have not denied the truth of the Book of Mormon, but myself and several others were overtaken in a fault at Kirtland" [^1]. The episode treats this disaffected-but-affirming pattern as evidence against [[Witnesses Never Recanted Despite Estrangement|recantation]].
