# David Sword of Laben

David runs the **Sword of Laben** Latter-day Saint YouTube channel and appears as a guest on [[Hayden Holm]]'s **Salty Saints** show. In the [[Salty Saints - Why Water Is Better Than Wine in the Sacrament|sacrament episode]] he presents a slide deck arguing three theses: that several early Christian groups used water rather than wine in the Eucharist (citing Irenaeus, Epiphanius, Clement of Alexandria, Cyprian, and the scholar Paul Bradshaw); that water is a *better* symbol of Christ than wine; and that the sacrament sustains the new life entered at baptism.[^1]
