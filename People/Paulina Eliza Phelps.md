# Paulina Eliza Phelps

Early Latter-day Saint, daughter in the Phelps family (associated with [[W. W. Phelps]]'s circle), who as a small child was reportedly blessed by [[Joseph Smith]] in Jackson County, Missouri.

## The Rocky Mountains Childhood Blessing
Phelps is the witness behind the [[Eliza Will Go to the Rocky Mountains Prophecy]]. Around 1832, at age five, she was reportedly told by Joseph that she "should live to go to the Rocky Mountains" — recalling, "I did not know at the time what the term Rocky Mountains meant." Because the affidavit dates to roughly 1903, ~70 years later and from a childhood memory, the host of [[Mormonism with the Murf - Joseph Smith's Top 20 Fulfilled Prophecies]] is "very skeptical of this particular source," citing memory distortion. See [[The Late-Reminiscence Problem in Joseph Smith Prophecy Claims]].[^1]
