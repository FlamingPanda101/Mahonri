# David Snell

**Who:** David Snell, Latter-day Saint apologist and friend of the Thoughtful Faith channel host. Cited as someone who has thoroughly addressed the [[Joseph Smith and Helen Mar Kimball Sealing]] question. The Thoughtful Faith video references Snell's work as prior coverage of the same material, with the video itself taking a different angle (the [[Edgar Allan Poe]] comparison and presentism argument).

**Role:** Apologist covering historical LDS topics including early polygamy and Joseph Smith's plural marriages.

## Helen Mar Kimball Defense
Snell has "dealt with" the Helen Mar Kimball sealing in detail, arguing — as the Thoughtful Faith video summarizes — that the sealing was dynastic and not sexual. See [[Joseph Smith and Helen Mar Kimball Sealing]].
