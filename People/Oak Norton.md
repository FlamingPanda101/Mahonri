# Oak Norton

Latter-day Saint creator of **Scripture Notes**, the online scripture-study tool (with an AI assistant named "Daniel") that sponsors Ward Radio and is used on-screen to display the [[Concentric Chiasmus in Alma 24|Alma 24 chiasmus]] charts in the episode. The hosts credit him directly ("throw a dime to our boy Oak Norton") and say "anybody can go read this in Scripture Notes" [^1][^2].

## Scripture Notes and the chiasmus charts
The visual layouts of the [[Stain Chiasmus in Alma 24|stain]] and [[Brethren Chiasmus in Alma 24|brethren]] chiasms shown in the video are built in Scripture Notes; the platform is presented as the way listeners can independently inspect the claimed structures [^2].
