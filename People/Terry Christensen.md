# Terry Christensen

Investigator on the 1985 Joyce Yost disappearance. A therapist had relayed a patient's claim that Yost was slowly and ritualistically killed by her father and a coven, giving places, dates, and gruesome details. After enormous investigative effort, the actual confessed murder turned out to be "absolutely nothing like" the satanic account.

## The Yost False Tip
Christensen recalled: "I can't tell you the hours that we put into trying to prove it or disprove it. She thinks Joyce Yost was killed by her dad and the coven and it just didn't happen." See [[The Joyce Yost Satanic Murder Tip Was Entirely False]] [^1].
