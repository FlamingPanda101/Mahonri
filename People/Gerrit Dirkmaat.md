# Gerrit Dirkmaat

Historian and BYU faculty member, formerly of the Joseph Smith Papers Project. Co-author (with Michael Hubbard MacKay) of *From Darkness unto Light: Joseph Smith's Translation and Publication of the Book of Mormon*.

## Role in the witness case
*From Darkness unto Light* is one of the two main scholarly sources behind [[Brett McDonald]]'s framework (alongside Richard Lloyd Anderson's *Investigating the Book of Mormon Witnesses*), supplying the documentary basis for the translation narrative and witness statements used throughout the episode [^1]. See [[BICC Method of Impeachment]].
