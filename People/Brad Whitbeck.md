# Brad Whitbeck

**Who:** A Latter-day Saint actor, writer, and comedian who hosts episodes of "Reading Books with Mike and Dave" on the [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|Stick of Joseph]] channel. He describes his role as "to help translate the smart things that they say into understandable things for everyone else."

**Role in this discussion:** In the *Prolegomena* episode, Whitbeck serves as the lay foil — asking the "so what?" questions, reading scripture passages aloud (e.g., [[2 Samuel 8.18|2 Samuel 8:18]] and the Goliath texts in [[1 Samuel 17.4-7|1 Samuel 17]] / [[2 Samuel 21.19|2 Samuel 21:19]]), and prompting [[Mike Day]] and [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|Dave Butler]] to unpack the documentary hypothesis for a general audience. A walk-in named Austin (a video-marketing-agency owner who "literally showed up with a pizza") co-hosts as a newcomer to the material.
