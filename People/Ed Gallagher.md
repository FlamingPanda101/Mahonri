# Ed Gallagher

**Full name:** Edmon L. Gallagher
**Role:** Biblical scholar specializing in the canon and textual history
**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Protestant

## Bio

Edmon (Ed) L. Gallagher is a professor of Christian Scripture at Heritage Christian University and a specialist in the formation of the biblical canon and patristic biblical interpretation. He co-authored *The Biblical Canon Lists from Early Christianity* (with John Meade) and has written extensively on Jerome, the Hebrew canon, and the deuterocanon.

## Relevance to the vault

Gallagher is cited as the scholar who confirms that Jerome "consistently excluded the deuterocanonical books" throughout his career. This supports the vault's rebuttal to the charge that Protestants "removed books from the Bible": a major early authority held a narrower 22-book Hebrew canon, and the only live question is whether his earlier view was even wider — never that he later accepted the deuterocanon as first-tier scripture.
