# Christopher Hitchens

**Full Name:** Christopher Eric Hitchens
**Era:** 1949–2011
**Tradition:** Atheist (one of the "Four Horsemen" of New Atheism)
**Role:** Author, journalist, literary critic, debater

## Bio

Christopher Hitchens was an English-American author, journalist, and one of the
most prominent public intellectuals of the late 20th and early 21st centuries.
His book *God Is Not Great: How Religion Poisons Everything* (2007) was a major
New Atheist text. He was one of the "Four Horsemen" alongside [[Sam Harris]],
[[Richard Dawkins]], and [[Daniel Dennett]].
