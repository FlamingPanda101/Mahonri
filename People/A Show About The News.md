# A Show About The News

**Type:** Secular comedy / explainer YouTube channel
**Tradition:** Secular

## Bio

A Show About The News is a secular YouTube channel that produces comedic deep-dive explainers on economics, politics, and society, fronted by a host who goes by "Trace" and featuring recurring sketch bits (including an "Abraham Lincoln" character). The single video catalogued here treats *location inequality* and the clustering of economic activity, drawing on Malcolm Gladwell's *Revenge of the Tipping Point* and the medical "small area variation" literature. It has no religious or apologetic content.
