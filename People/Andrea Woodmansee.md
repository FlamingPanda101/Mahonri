# Andrea Woodmansee

**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint
**Role:** Independent teacher / scripture expositor; recurring guest on [[Stick of Joseph|The Stick of Joseph]] YouTube channel

## Bio

Andrea Woodmansee is a Latter-day Saint teacher and a popular returning guest on the Stick of Joseph channel, where she delivers devotional, **temple-theology-driven** readings of scripture. A mother of five, she frames her work as faith-building exposition rather than academic argument, and is candid when a thesis is conjectural — in the wisemen episode she introduces her central claim as a theory "some Bible scholars would probably discount." Her method leans on named scholars: [[Margaret Barker]] (*Christmas: The Original Story*), [[Hugh Nibley]] (*Temple and Cosmos*), and Mircea Eliade on cross-cultural initiation rites.[^1]

## On the magi as temple-covenant bearers

In "The Secret Identity of the Wisemen," Woodmansee argues the [[Matthew 2.1-12|Matthew 2]] magi were not Persian astrologers but [[The Wise Men Were Bearers of Preserved First-Temple Covenant|bearers of preserved first-temple covenant]], descended from priests carried to Babylon in 600 BC, who came to [[The Gifts of the Magi Were Temple Items for Anointing the High Priest|anoint the infant Jesus as the great High Priest]] — performing an ordinance, not merely worshipping. She reads the Bethlehem star as the [[Numbers 24.17|"Star out of Jacob"]] sign of the Son of Man, and applies the pattern pastorally: covenant-keepers, like the magi, must hunger for the temple and heed living prophets to be ready for the Second Coming.[^2]
