# Matt Walsh

**Era:** Contemporary
**Role:** Conservative commentator, author, filmmaker (The Daily Wire)

## Bio
Matt Walsh is an American conservative political commentator and author at The Daily Wire. He is best known for the 2022 documentary *What Is a Woman?*, which popularized the question and which Walsh answers with the definition **"a woman is an adult human female."**

## Relevance
Walsh's "adult human female" definition is the position [[Trent Horn]] defends against [[Steven Woodford]] (Rationality Rules). Horn argues the definition is not merely defensible but *more useful* than any competing definition, and that Woodford's intersex counterexamples do not defeat it. See [[Intersex Conditions Do Not Prove Gender Is Separate From Sex]] and [[Appearance Is a Defeasible Guide to Sex Not Its Definition]]. Horn also faults Walsh-adjacent figures (e.g. Andrew Tate) for sharing the *opposite* error of reducing womanhood to appearance. See [[The Utility Argument Cuts Against Severing Gender From Sex]].
