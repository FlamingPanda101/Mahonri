# Jackson Paul

One of the two Paul brothers who host [[Ward Radio]] (with [[Hayden Paul]]). In the Great Apostasy episode he co-moderates and contributes commentary on the divergent gospel communities, the names of the disciples (Thomas/Didymus), and the parallels between early Christian chaos and the post-1844 Restoration breakoffs.
