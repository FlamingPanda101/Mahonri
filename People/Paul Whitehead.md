# Paul Whitehead

Utah therapist who worked alongside [[Barbara Snow]] in the Lehi cases and beyond, helping expand the accusations to 40 adults in the Lehi 8th Ward. He was Hadfield's first court-ordered therapist (treatment was reassigned to [[Robert Card]] after Hadfield refused to admit guilt). Whitehead interpreted the community's massive support for Hadfield as proof of a wide conspiracy, reasoning that since "a lot of people" were involved, "to protect one is to protect all."

## Conspiracy Reasoning
His reinterpretation of exculpatory community support as evidence of guilt exemplifies the unfalsifiable logic of the panic. See [[Community Support Reinterpreted as Conspiracy]] and [[Barbara Snows Interview Methods Manufactured Abuse Allegations]] [^1].
