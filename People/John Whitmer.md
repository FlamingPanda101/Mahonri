# John Whitmer

**Who:** John Whitmer (1802–1878), one of the **Eight Witnesses** to the Book of Mormon and a member of the Whitmer family.

**Role in this source:** [[Daniel C. Peterson]] cites John Whitmer as the *closest* any witness comes to qualifying his testimony — and even that is not a retraction. Whitmer reportedly said he saw the plates but didn't know about "the translation... whether that's correct." Peterson stresses this is not backing away from what he saw and handled; Whitmer simply limited his claim to his actual experience: he didn't hear a voice attesting the translation and couldn't read the characters.[^1]

## On the Limits and Persistence of Testimony
The most cautious witness still affirmed the physical experience, which underwrites the "they didn't recant" case.
