# Dieter F. Uchtdorf

**Era:** Contemporary (1940–present)
**Tradition:** The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
**Role:** Apostle (Quorum of the Twelve); former Second Counselor in the First Presidency; former commercial airline pilot

## Bio
Dieter F. Uchtdorf is a German-born apostle of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, called to the Quorum of the Twelve in 2004 and serving in the First Presidency 2008–2018. A former Lufthansa pilot, his addresses frequently draw on aviation and science imagery.

## Positions in this source
A clip of an Uchtdorf address supplies the "greater light" thread of [[Jacob Hansen]]'s "witness of authority" video:

- **Greater light reveals pre-existing truth.** Using the astronomical discovery of other galaxies, Uchtdorf argues "the immensity of the universe didn't suddenly change, but our ability to see and understand this truth changed." Truth "exists beyond belief… even if nobody believes it"; we "see through a glass darkly" and trust the Lord, the one "complete, correct, and incorruptible" source of truth.
