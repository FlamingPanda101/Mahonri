# Benjamin Park

Historian specializing in Mormon history and American religion. President of the
Mormon History Association. Host of a YouTube channel exploring the intersections
of American religion, politics, and culture with a focus on Mormon history.

## Credentials

- PhD in history
- President of the Mormon History Association
- Author of academic publications on Mormon history
- Self-described "unapologetic history geek"

## The King Follett Discourse Video

Park's February 2026 video "Did Joseph Smith Teach Men Can Become God?" provides
a scholarly overview of the [[King Follett Discourse]] — its background, contents,
and complicated reception history. He traces:

- Joseph Smith's development as a preacher and radical theologian
- The four contemporaneous accounts of the sermon
- The 1912 suppression by [[Joseph F. Smith]] and [[Charles Penrose]]
- [[BH Roberts]]'s fight to restore it
- Modern tensions between Mormon exceptionalism and Christian ecumenism

He recommends further reading: the Joseph Smith Papers Project accounts, William V.
Smith's *The King Follett Sermon: A Biography*, and Faulconer & Morrison's "Pinnacle
or Peripheral?" in BYU Studies.
