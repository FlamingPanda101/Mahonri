# Kaleb Jones

**Who:** Latter-day Saint media personality and host of [[Ward Radio]]. Introduces and moderates the panel episodes, including the Galatians 1:8 discussion with [[Jonah (Plain and Precious Publishing)|Jonah Barnes]] and [[Austin Falter]].

**Role:** Podcast host / commentator (not a credentialed scholar). Provides apologetic framing and the "counter-sound-bite" emphasis in discussion.

## Galatians 1:8
As host, Jones frames the central apologetic problem — that critics' sound bites are short and "very digestible" while rebuttals take many words — and presses the panel for a "counter sound bite" to [[Galatians 1.8|Galatians 1:8]]. See [[The Amen Brother Apologetic Strategy]] and [[Galatians 1-8 Latter-day Saints Preach Pauls Gospel]].[^1]
