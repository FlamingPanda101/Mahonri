# Miles Coverdale

**Full name:** Miles (Myles) Coverdale
**Era:** 1488–1569
**Tradition:** Protestant (English Reformation)
**Role:** Bible translator, reformer

## Bio

Miles Coverdale was an English reformer who produced the first complete printed English Bible (1535), building on the work of [[William Tyndale]]. His translations, like Tyndale's, were carried forward into later English Bibles including the King James Version.

[[Dan McClellan]] names Coverdale alongside Tyndale to argue that the King James Version (1611) inherited language reaching back nearly a century — making its English archaic at the moment of publication. See [[The KJV Was Already Out of Date in 1611]].[^1]
