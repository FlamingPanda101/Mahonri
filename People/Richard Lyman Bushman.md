# Richard Lyman Bushman

Eminent American historian (Columbia University, emeritus) and author of *Joseph Smith: Rough Stone Rolling*, the standard scholarly biography. A practicing Latter-day Saint, he is nonetheless regarded across the field as a rigorous academic historian.

## On the witnesses
The episode closes with Bushman's summary that secular historians have not reckoned with the witnesses' refusal to recant, used to indict the [[Mass Hallucination Hypnosis Theory Rebutted|mass hallucination theory]]:

> "Secular historians have never come to grips with the fact that none of the 11 who saw the plates in addition to Joseph Smith ever recanted. Narrations that... try to tell another story such as mass hallucination must suppress these sources and disregard the consistent and detailed accounts from the people who knew Joseph Smith best." [^1]

This connects to [[Witnesses Never Recanted Despite Estrangement]].
