# Stephen Jones

Host of the *Let's Get Real* podcast/interview series. He conducts the long-form conversation with Dr. [[Daniel C. Peterson]] on [[Brigham Young]], signing off the episode with "This is Stephen Jones. This is Let's Get Real."[^1]

In the interview he repeatedly plays a sympathetic devil's-advocate, voicing the objections of skeptical listeners (e.g., "at what cost?", "are we making excuses?", "if prophets made mistakes back then, what mistakes are they making now?") to draw out Peterson's responses.[^2] He connects the discussion to his personal *Come, Follow Me* study of Genesis and the theme of [[Identity Boxes as a Source of Division]].
