# Callie Baker

A young Latter-day Saint content creator who runs the **About Our Hope** Instagram account with her husband [[Austin Baker]]. She appears as the featured guest on the [[Ward Radio]] livestream hosted by [[Cardon Ellis]], where she presents a list of distinctive LDS doctrines she believes Gen Z members should know and embrace: eternal/forever families, the [[Pre-existence Was a Mainstream Early-Christian Belief Later Purged|pre-existence]], eternal progression and exaltation, a Heavenly Mother, the redeemed view of [[Eve]], an open canon, and vicarious work for the dead.[^1]

Her thesis is that members who grow up in the Church often fail to recognize how distinctive their theology is, and that recovering that sense of "unique theology" would give Gen Z confidence and gratitude rather than embarrassment.[^1]
