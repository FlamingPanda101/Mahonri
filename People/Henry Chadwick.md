# Henry Chadwick

Anglican church historian, Regius Professor of Divinity at both Oxford and Cambridge, author of the widely used survey *The Early Church*.

## Cited by Moroni's Standard
[[Alex Arnold]] cites Chadwick's *The Early Church* to establish that Arianism "had substantial support across the Eastern Empire" — that [[Arius]]'s position was not an outlier — supporting the claim that Nicaea resolved a genuinely open question by imperial means rather than ratifying an existing consensus. Listed among the tradition's own non-LDS authorities. See [[The Trend Line Runs the Wrong Way for the Recovery Narrative]] and [[Council of Nicaea]].[^1]
