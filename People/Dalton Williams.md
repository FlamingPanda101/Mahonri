# Dalton Williams

Online Latter-day Saint apologist behind **Unashamed Truth 116** (Instagram/TikTok). Self-describes as "the Galatians 1:8 guy" because the verse comes up so often against the Church [^1].

## Galatians 1:8
Argues Paul's "another gospel / an angel" is about the **Judaizers** (circumcision controversy) running through the whole letter and the [[Acts 15]] council — not about [[Angel Moroni]] or later revelation. See [[Galatians 1-8 - Angel Preaching Another Gospel]] [^2].

## 1 John 5:7
Walked through the **Johannine Comma** as an interpolation absent from the earliest manuscripts. See [[1 John 5-7 - Johannine Comma Interpolation]] [^3].
