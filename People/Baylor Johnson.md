# Baylor Johnson

**Who:** Latter-day Saint content creator associated with the "All Those in Favor" channel; appears as a panelist on [[Ward Radio]].

**Role:** Podcast panelist (not a credentialed scholar).

## Appearances
Panelist on the Ward Radio episode reacting to [[Trent Horn]]'s "substitutional patriarchy" argument; raises the case of faithful single members who "don't fit the mold," prompting the hosts to distinguish intentional self-prioritization from involuntary singleness. See [[Substitutional Patriarchy - The Government Replaces the Husband]].[^1]
