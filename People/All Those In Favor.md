# All Those In Favor

**Type:** Independent LDS-themed YouTube channel / podcast
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (independent commentary)

## Bio

All Those In Favor is an independent Latter-day Saint media outlet on YouTube and podcast platforms, hosted by figures including [[Kwaku El]] and [[Cade (All Those In Favor)|Cade]]. Its episodes mix apologetics, scripture discussion, and reaction/commentary, and it regularly brings on LDS scholars and apologists to answer critics. It is not an official organ of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
