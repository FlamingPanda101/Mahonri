# Jane Goodall

**Role:** Primatologist and ethologist
**Era:** Contemporary (b. 1934)

## Bio

Jane Goodall is a British primatologist renowned for her decades-long study of wild chimpanzees at Gombe Stream, which reshaped scientific understanding of animal tool use, cognition, and social behavior. She is referenced in this vault where apologetic arguments touch on animal behavior and what, if anything, distinguishes human morality, reason, and dignity from the animal.
