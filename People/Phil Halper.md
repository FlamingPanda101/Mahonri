# Phil Halper

**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Atheist
**Role:** Science/philosophy YouTuber and documentary-maker ("Skydivephil")

## Bio
Phil Halper is a British atheist science-and-philosophy communicator known for the "Skydivephil" YouTube channel, which produces long-form interview documentaries with cosmologists and philosophers on fine-tuning, the multiverse, Big Bang cosmology, and arguments for and against God. He is a frequent critic of the [[Chance, Necessity, or Design - The Fine-Tuning Trilemma|fine-tuning]] and [[The Cosmological Argument - The Universe Began to Exist|cosmological]] arguments as deployed by apologists.

## Contribution in This Video
Halper does not appear directly; [[Alex OConnor|Alex O'Connor]] relays one of his arguments — that fine-tuning fits the **evil demiurge of early Christian Gnosticism** better than the God of traditional theism, because the "meta conditions" make a material world "really, really difficult to bring about," implying the true source of being did not want matter to exist. This is developed as its own concept note, [[Fine-Tuning Fits a Gnostic Demiurge Better Than Traditional Theism]].[^1]
