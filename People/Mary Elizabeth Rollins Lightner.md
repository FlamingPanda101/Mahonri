# Mary Elizabeth Rollins Lightner

**Role:** Plural wife of [[Joseph Smith]]
**Era:** 1818–1913
**Tradition:** The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

## Bio
Mary Elizabeth Rollins, already married to Adam Lightner, was sealed to [[Joseph Smith]] in January or February 1842. Because she bore a child in March 1842, she was roughly seven or eight months pregnant at the time of the sealing — the fourth early-Nauvoo wife who fits [[Don Bradley]]'s pregnant-bride pattern. See [[Joseph Smith Married Pregnant Women Refuting the Sexual-Motive Thesis]].
