# Daniel

Daniel is the Judean exile and prophet of the eponymous Old Testament book, known for interpreting dreams and visions in the Babylonian and Persian courts. At [[Belshazzar]]'s feast he reads and interprets the supernatural "writing on the wall" ([[Daniel 5.25|Daniel 5:25]]), MENE, MENE, TEKEL, UPHARSIN, pronouncing judgment on the king.

## In the Miracle-Test Debate
The Belshazzar writing supplies a skeptic's hypothetical sign (stars rearranging to spell those words), used to illustrate the [[Miracle-Tests for God Commit the God-of-the-Gaps Fallacy|God-of-the-gaps]] problem.
