# Ishmael (son of Abraham)

[[Abraham]]'s firstborn son by Hagar (Genesis 16). Treated within the vault as scriptural narrative. (Distinct from the Book of Mormon figure [[Ishmael]] whose family joins Lehi.)

## The firstborn passed over for the son of promise
[[Stick of Joseph|The Stick of Joseph]] presents Ishmael/[[Isaac]] as "the strongest" instance of the displaced-firstborn motif: Ishmael is technically Abraham's firstborn, yet [[Isaac]] — the unambiguous type of Christ in the Akedah — is the chosen heir, and Hebrews 11:17 even calls Isaac Abraham's "only begotten son" though Ishmael lived. The hosts use this to argue the firstborn-by-birth need not be the chosen son, a pattern they extend to premortal Christ and Lucifer. See [[Two Brothers Where the Younger Is Chosen Are Types of Christ and Lucifer]].
