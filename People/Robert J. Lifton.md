# Robert J. Lifton

American psychiatrist who wrote the foundational 1961 academic study of Chinese **thought reform** ("brainwashing") of prisoners during the Korean War era.

## Brainwashing
His work launched serious study of the brainwashing claim. Latter scholarship — including the declassified CIA POW study — concluded brainwashing as popularly imagined does not exist. See [[Brainwashing Does Not Exist]] [^1].
