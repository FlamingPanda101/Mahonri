# Jaroslav Pelikan

Eminent Yale historian of Christian doctrine, author of the five-volume *The Christian Tradition*. Originally Lutheran, he later converted to Eastern Orthodoxy. Cited by [[Trent Horn]] for the claim that "in the ante-Nicene church there was no notion of sola scriptura."[^1]

## Sola Scriptura in the early church
Horn appeals to Pelikan's authority as a renowned (then-Lutheran) scholar to argue the doctrine of sola scriptura is absent from the earliest church, supporting his third argument that early Christians did not operate under it. See [[The Early Church Did Not Operate Under Sola Scriptura]] and [[Sola Scriptura Could Not Have Been True During the Apostolic Age]].[^1]
