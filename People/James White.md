# James White

Prominent Reformed Baptist apologist (Alpha & Omega Ministries), author of a book defending *sola fide*.

## Deedless faith
Cited for the Reformed thesis that "deedless faith is not saving faith" — i.e. James contrasts dead and living faith. Trent Horn argues the Greek and context don't support a two-kinds-of-faith reading. See [[James - Different Kind of Faith Reply Rebutted]] [^1].
