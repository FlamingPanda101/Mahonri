# Tad Callister

A Latter-day Saint author and former general authority (a former Sunday School general president), known for doctrinal books such as *The Infinite Atonement*. He is cited secondhand by [[Casey Paul Griffiths]] in the Keystone discussion of the [[King Follett Discourse]].

## On the God–Man Relationship
Griffiths attributes to Callister the acorn-and-oak framing of theosis: the difference between God and man is significant but is like the difference between an oak tree and an acorn — the potential is all there. See [[God and Man Are the Same Species]] and the existing note [[Acorn and Oak Analogy for Theosis]].
