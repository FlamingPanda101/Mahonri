# Mike Day

LDS educator and apologist, longtime teacher in the Church Educational System (seminary and institute). On Ward Radio he presents multi-hour "deep dive" slide presentations; this one covers the Great Apostasy from 33 AD to ~400 AD. He describes 30+ years of self-directed study of early Christianity, a graduate degree with Greek training, and reading primarily in non-LDS sources (Catholic, Eastern Orthodox, evangelical histories) plus the anti-Nicene fathers. He runs church-history tours to Rome and Jerusalem.

## On the Great Apostasy

He frames apostasy as a **gradual falling away** rather than a single mutiny, builds his case from "evidences" rather than asserted causes, and steelmans opposing views — even arguing the apostasy "preserved the text." He presents the [[Greek Philosophy Corrupted the Christian Concept of God|Hellenization of God]] as the central corruption while explicitly refusing to denigrate other faiths. See [[Apostasy as Gradual Falling Away Not Single Event]], [[Car Wreck Analogy for Diagnosing Apostasy]], [[Apostasy Preserved the Text Steelman]].
