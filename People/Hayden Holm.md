# Hayden Holm

Hayden Holm is the host of **Salty Saints**, a casual Latter-day Saint men's YouTube channel ("a channel for men"). He moderates panel-style conversations with LDS content creators, fielding superchats and steering the discussion rather than arguing positions himself.[^1] In [[Salty Saints - The Apologist Playbook for LDS Men]] he convenes [[Hayden Carroll]], [[Roy Anderson]] (Iron Rod Cast), and [[Sam St. Laurent]] (Through the Mist) for a practical discussion of apologetic posture for "the average LDS dad," opening with a riff on monetized "Think Celestial" merchandise and closing by asking whether every LDS father should learn apologetics.[^2]

Not to be confused with his guest [[Hayden Carroll]]; the episode plays on the two "Haydens" sharing a first name.[^3]
