# Brett McDonald

LDS apologist whose lecture series **"LDS Truth Claims"** is the basis for the Ward Radio episode on the Book of Mormon witnesses. [[Jonah Barnes]] credits "very little of this is my original work... all credited to Brett McDonald" and describes the goal as repackaging McDonald's material for "wider reach" [^1].

## The witness framework
McDonald's organizing tools — the [[BICC Method of Impeachment|BICC method of impeachment]] (Bias, Inconsistent statement, Character, Competency) and the [[Natural vs Supernatural Witnesses|natural/supernatural witness]] taxonomy — structure the entire episode [^2]. His three "LDS Truth Claims" are that Joseph possessed plates, that they were translated, and that Joseph and [[Oliver Cowdery]] received divine authority [^3].

## Sources used
He builds primarily on Richard Lloyd Anderson's *Investigating the Book of Mormon Witnesses* and MacKay/[[Gerrit Dirkmaat]]'s *From Darkness unto Light*, and on the [[Mass Hallucination Hypnosis Theory Rebutted|hypnosis rebuttal]] he attributes to figures including [[Richard Lyman Bushman]] [^2].
