# Terrell Given

Professor of religion and Latter-day Saint academic.

## Convergence of Christianity toward LDS doctrines
Given argues that Christians across the denominational spectrum are converging on what used to be considered uniquely Mormon heresies.[^1] He observes that LDS doctrines once "outside the margins of mainstream Christianity are now being reinvestigated just vigorously by theologians."[^2]
