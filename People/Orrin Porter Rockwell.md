# Orrin Porter Rockwell

Early Latter-day Saint, longtime bodyguard to [[Joseph Smith]] and later [[Brigham Young]], and a noted frontier lawman and gunman in early Utah. Despite a violent career he was never killed by gunfire and died of natural causes.

## The Cut-Not-Thy-Hair Blessing
Rockwell is the subject of the [[Orrin Porter Rockwell Cut Not Thy Hair Prophecy]], in which Joseph Smith is reported to have promised that, so long as Rockwell remained faithful and did not cut his hair, "no bullet or blade can harm thee." The host of [[Mormonism with the Murf - Joseph Smith's Top 20 Fulfilled Prophecies]] notes Rockwell served as bodyguard and "never [got] shot," while observing the conditional, Samson-like form of the blessing.[^1]
