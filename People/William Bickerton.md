# William Bickerton

William Bickerton (1815-1905) was the founding prophet of **The Church of Jesus Christ** (headquartered in Monongahela, Pennsylvania), one of the restoration branches sometimes called "Bickertonites." Originally associated with [[Sydney Rigdon]]'s following after [[Joseph Smith]]'s death, he later organized his own church, which accepts the Book of Mormon and Joseph Smith's prophetic role but rejected many later Utah developments. [[Kyle Beshears]] cites the Bickertonites as an example that "Mormonism" is broader than the Salt Lake church. [^1]

## In Beshears's account
Listed among the non-Utah restoration branches. See [[Mormonism Is More Than the Utah Church]].
