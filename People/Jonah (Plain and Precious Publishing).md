# Jonah (Plain and Precious Publishing)

LDS author and frequent Ward Radio guest. Specializes in apocryphal and
pseudepigraphal texts that illuminate the Bible, particularly demonstrating
how Jesus Himself referenced extra-biblical traditions.

## Core Thesis
The Old Testament was redacted by the Deuteronomistic school — the same
group that murdered the prophets and suppressed messianic prophecy.
Deuteronomy was "found" in the blood-soaked temple of Solomon and used to
rewrite Israel's history. The Brass Plates (taken by Lehi) preserved the
uncorrupted tradition, and the Book of Mormon restores what was lost.
