# Jonah (Plain & Precious Publishing)

Latter-day Saint author and researcher with **Plain & Precious Publishing**; author of *The Lost Gems of Genesis* and *The Key to the Keystone*. Specializes in apocryphal and extra-biblical traditions and their connections to LDS scripture.

## Apocryphal blood traditions
Walks through the [[Blood of Abel - Unburiable Tradition|blood of Abel]] and [[Blood of Zechariah - Perpetual Boiling Blood|blood of Zechariah]] traditions and argues Jesus referenced them in [[Luke 11.47-51|Luke 11:47-51]]. See [[Jesus Referenced the Apocrypha]] [^1]. Stresses [[Multiple Attestation - Corroborating Apocryphal Sources|multiple attestation]] as the test of reliability [^2].

## The Key to the Keystone (brass plates)
In *The Key to the Keystone* he mines ~40 apocryphal texts to argue the Book of Mormon's pre-Christian messianism — plausibly carried on the brass plates — is no anachronism. He contends [[Testament of Naphtali Was Vindicated by the Dead Sea Scrolls|historic-manuscript dating is a glorified guess]] and that [[Prophets Were Killed for Preaching the Messiah Before 600 BC|prophets were martyred for Messiah-preaching]] before 600 BC. See [[The Stick of Joseph - The Key to Unlocking Lehis Brass Plates]] [^3].
