# Nephi

Son of [[Lehi]] and a founding prophet of the Book of Mormon people. He sought and obtained the same vision his father saw, exemplifying the pattern of seeking God.

## As a Seeker
[[Don Bradley]] lists Nephi (with [[Lehi]] and others) among those who, like [[Abraham]], found God *because* they sought Him — supporting the claim that divine election is responsive, not arbitrary. See [[Seeking God Versus Arbitrary Election]] and [[Abraham's First Vision Pattern]]. Bradley also notes Nephi's argument that earlier peoples were not less "chosen" for lack of righteousness — God is not arbitrary.
