# King Ahaz

King of Judah (~735-715 BCE). During his reign, a coalition of northern kingdoms (Aram and Israel) threatened Judah. The prophet Isaiah delivered the oracle of Isaiah 7:14 — "the young woman shall conceive and bear a son" — as a sign to Ahaz that the threat would pass before the child grew up. Not a messianic prophecy.
