# Russell Brunson

**Full Name:** Russell Brunson
**Era:** Contemporary (1980s–present)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Entrepreneur, author, co-founder of ClickFunnels

## Bio

Russell Brunson is an American entrepreneur and author best known as the
co-founder of ClickFunnels. He is also an LDS Church member who has appeared on
the Stick of Joseph podcast. [[David Butler]] recounts a conversation with
Brunson in which he used a geometry analogy to illustrate presuppositional
apologetics.

## Topics

### Geometry Analogy for Unprovable Axioms
Brunson told Butler that in geometry, "there are three constants that are
unprovable and that's a point, a line, and a plane." Once you accept these
unprovable axioms, "everything else works in geometry."[^1] Butler extends the
analogy: "We will never be able to prove the existence of God... But if you
accept those three proofs, everything else will be in harmony. Zion will be
built."[^1] See [[Presuppositional Apologetics - CS Lewis Sun Analogy]].
