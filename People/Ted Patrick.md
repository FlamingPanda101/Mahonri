# Ted Patrick

The best-known **"deprogrammer"** of the 1970s anti-cult movement — hired by parents to forcibly abduct and confine adult children who had joined new religious movements, in order to "decondition" them.

## Deprogramming and the brainwashing myth
Deprogramming presupposed the (false) brainwashing model; courts ultimately rejected both the practice and the pseudo-expertise behind it. See [[Brainwashing Does Not Exist]] [^1].
