# Frederick G. Williams

Early Latter-day Saint who served as a counselor in the First Presidency under [[Joseph Smith]] and owned a farm in Kirtland, Ohio.

## The Kirtland Stronghold Revelation
Williams is the named recipient in [[D&C 64.30-33|Doctrine and Covenants 64:30-33]] (4 September 1831), directed not to sell his Kirtland farm because "I, the Lord, will retain a stronghold in the land of Kirtland for the space of five years." This is the basis for the [[Kirtland a Stronghold for Five Years Prophecy]], discussed in [[Mormonism with the Murf - Joseph Smith's Top 20 Fulfilled Prophecies]].[^1]
