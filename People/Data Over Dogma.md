# Data Over Dogma

**Type:** Podcast / YouTube channel
**Hosts:** [[Dan McClellan]] and [[Dan Beecher]]
**Tagline:** "Increasing public access to the academic study of the Bible and religion, and combating the spread of misinformation about the same."
**Tradition:** Academic biblical studies (non-confessional)

## About

*Data Over Dogma* is a podcast and YouTube channel co-hosted by biblical scholar [[Dan McClellan]] and [[Dan Beecher]]. Its recurring "It's the Law" segment examines specific Hebrew biblical texts and commandments, exposing how mainstream translations and traditions diverge from what the original-language evidence supports. The show emphasizes historical-critical method, textual criticism, and archaeology over apologetic or confessional harmonization, and supports a Patreon community at patreon.com/dataoverdogma.
