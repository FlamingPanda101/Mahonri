# Sam Shamoun

**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Christian (apologist/polemicist)
**Role:** Online apologist and debater, frequently in anti-Mormon and inter-religious polemics
**Authority:** D — combative lay polemicist; cited as opinion/polemic, not scholarship

## Bio

Sam Shamoun is a Christian apologist known for an aggressive, confrontational debate style. On the [[Ward Radio]] livestream he is discussed as a co-host (with [[Cameron Bertuzzi]] on Capturing Christianity) of a "Refuted" response stream attacking [[Jacob Hansen]]'s debate with God Logic. The panel — [[Cardon Ellis]], [[Luke Hansen]], and [[Hayden Carroll]] — characterizes his contribution as personal insult and "Gish gallop" rather than substantive argument, and references reported admissions of domestic violence and his insulting viewers in chat ("your mom is a prostitute").[^1] Because he is cited only as a hostile commentator and his remarks are presented as polemic rather than argument, he is assigned Tier D.

## Topics

### The Trinity vs Godhead "Refuted" Response
Shamoun appears as the principal antagonist in the post-debate commentary cycle the stream is reacting to, "yelling at the screen about the Old Testament" while Bertuzzi looked uncomfortable. The panel treats his "challenge" to Ward Radio as something they'd accept only if it became "a conversation," not Gish-galloping.[^2] See [[Why the Trinity vs Godhead Debate Actually Matters]], [[Calvinism Is the Logical Conclusion of Classical Theism]].
