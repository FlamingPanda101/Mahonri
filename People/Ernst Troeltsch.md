# Ernst Troeltsch (1865-1923)

German theologian and sociologist of religion who, alongside [[Max Weber]], worked out the church/sect typology — and **introduced the "cult" category** as a residual type for mystical/metaphysical groups that fit neither box.

## Cult as a category
His "cult" was a fuzzy, non-pejorative category (what we might now call new-age/metaphysical movements) — long before the term acquired its negative popular sense. See [[Cult - A Contaminated Pejorative Term]] and [[Church Sect Cult Distinction]] [^1].
