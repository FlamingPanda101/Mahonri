# Steven L. Peck

**Role:** Co-editor of *The Restored Gospel of Jesus Christ and Evolution* (BYU, 2025) and a contributing author on the scientific epistemology of evolution.

## Bio
Steven L. Peck is one of the four editors of *The Restored Gospel of Jesus Christ and Evolution*, a BYU College of Life Sciences volume arguing that accepting organic evolution is compatible with the restored gospel of Jesus Christ.[^1] Within the volume's structure he contributes to the section on "what we know from a scientific epistemology."[^2] As an editor he affirms the book's governing convictions — that God created purposefully as part of a divine plan, that [[Nothing Has Been Revealed Concerning Evolution|"nothing has been revealed concerning evolution"]], and that truth is sought [[Learning by Study and Also by Faith|by study and also by faith]].[^3]
