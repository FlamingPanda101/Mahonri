# St. Isaac of Nineveh (7th c.)

Syriac bishop and ascetic theologian, revered across Eastern Christianity for his writings on divine mercy.

## Against unending punishment
Quoted: "It is not the way of a compassionate maker to create rational beings in order to deliver them over mercilessly to unending affliction and punishment... aware of how they would turn out when he created them." See [[Problem of Hell and Creation]] [^1].
