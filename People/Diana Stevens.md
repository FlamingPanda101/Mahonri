# Diana Stevens

Bountiful Police detective in the [[Brenda Miles]] / [[Russell M. Nelson]] case. She testified that "no church officials attempted to interfere with the criminal investigations" — one of three investigators whose sworn statements directly contradict the claim that the LDS Church pressured law enforcement to suppress the ritual-abuse allegations.

## No church interference
With [[Michael George]] and [[Leslie Lewis]], Stevens's testimony anchors the rebuttal of the cover-up theory. See [[No Evidence the LDS Church Suppressed the Investigations]]. [^1]
