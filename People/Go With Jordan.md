# Go With Jordan

**Role:** Calvinist Protestant apologist and content creator ("Go With Jordan")
**Era:** Contemporary (active 2020s)
**Tradition:** Reformed/Calvinist Protestant; affirms the Athanasian Creed

## Bio
"Go With Jordan" is a Reformed Protestant YouTuber who posted *"God Logic DEFEATED Jacob Hansen on the Trinity... Here's Why,"* the rebuttal the [[Stick of Joseph - God Logic Trinity Debate Recap]] panel responds to clip-by-clip. The panel notes he affirms the **Athanasian Creed** whereas [[Avery (God Logic)]] is more of a monarchical Trinitarian — i.e. the two critics don't even share one Trinity model [^1].

## Position Reported and Rebutted
Jordan argued Avery did demonstrate biblically that the Trinity is one being, pressing "one God" texts ([[James 2.19|James 2:19]], the Shema, [[1 Corinthians 8.4-6|1 Cor 8:4]]) and objecting that "a court is not a being." He also charged Hansen with failing to use "proper categories." Hansen replies that the very categories (three whos / one what) were laid out explicitly, and that Jordan presupposes "one God = one being" without proving it. See:
- [[The Court Analogy for the Godhead]]
- [[Three Whos and One What Framework]]
- [[Latter-day Saints Are Not Tritheists]]
