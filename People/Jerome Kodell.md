# Jerome Kodell

Benedictine monk and Catholic biblical scholar known for work on Luke–Acts, including the development of the "word of God" theme across Luke's two volumes.

## The Word of God in Luke–Acts
Quoted by [[Trent Horn]] for a three-stage development: "The word of God is first God's promise in the Old Testament, then the preaching of Jesus himself, and finally the preaching about Jesus." Kodell also argues that after the resurrection "the word of God is embedded in the Christian community… he can say the word of God grew when the church adds new members" ([[Acts 6.7|Acts 6:7]]). See [[Luke Identifies the Word of God With the Apostolic Preaching and the Church]] [^1].
