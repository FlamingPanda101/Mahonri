# St. Nicholas

**Who:** 4th-century bishop of Myra (modern Turkey). Attended the Council of Nicaea (325 CE). Later became the figure behind the Santa Claus tradition.

**At Nicaea:** Tradition holds that Nicholas physically slapped [[Arius]] during the Council of Nicaea — as [[Dan McClellan]] quips, "Santa Claus — calm down, we're giving out gifts and punching heretics, and we're all out of gifts."[^1]
