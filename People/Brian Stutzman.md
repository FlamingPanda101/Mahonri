# Brian Stutzman

Latter-day Saint author of *A Timeline of Joseph Smith's Prophecies: His Prophecies Fulfilled*, a faith-promoting compilation of prophecies attributed to [[Joseph Smith]].

## Source for the Murf's Prophecy List
Stutzman's book is the primary source the host of [[Mormonism with the Murf - Joseph Smith's Top 20 Fulfilled Prophecies]] draws from, including the [[Falling of the Stars Prophecy]], the [[Eliza Will Go to the Rocky Mountains Prophecy]], and others. As a popular compilation, its accounts inherit the [[The Late-Reminiscence Problem in Joseph Smith Prophecy Claims|late-reminiscence problem]] the host repeatedly flags. The host pairs Stutzman's material with a Jeff Roundy video on *Larry Saints Q&A*.[^1]
