# John A. Tvedtnes

**Era:** 20th–21st century
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Hebraist; Book of Mormon researcher

## Bio
John A. Tvedtnes is a Latter-day Saint scholar trained in Hebrew and ancient Near Eastern languages who published prolifically on Hebraisms and name studies in the Book of Mormon, much of it through FARMS and the Maxwell Institute.[^1]

## Book of Mormon Onomastics
[[Matthew Bowen]] lists Tvedtnes among the "giants" — alongside [[Hugh Nibley]], [[Paul Hoskisson]], and [[John Gee]] — whose foundational name research he builds upon.[^1] See [[Book of Mormon Names Show Authorial Wordplay]].
