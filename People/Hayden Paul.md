# Hayden Paul

One of the two Paul brothers who host [[Ward Radio]] (with [[Jackson Paul]]). In the Great Apostasy episode he co-moderates, asking framing questions about apostasy, authority, the Eucharist, baptism, and the canon, and frequently voicing the LDS reaction to the historical material. He also references his channel "Latter Day Live."
