# Caleb Williams (Godwin)

Latter-day Saint **illustrator and author**, known online as **"Godwin"** (the *Library of Godwin*). He is the creator of *Doctrines and the Mysteries*, an illustrated compilation pairing 48 original illustrations of doctrinal images from the **Doctrine and Covenants** with matched primary sources — revelations, journal entries, visions, and discourses — from the early voices of the Restoration (published through Plain and Precious Publishing). He previously produced an illustrated **Book of Mormon** (a Come Follow Me illustration each week) and appeared on the [[The Stick of Joseph - Are Christians Becoming Mormon with Brian Thomassen|Stick of Joseph]] channel discussing Kolob.

## Contribution
In the [[The Stick of Joseph - Sacred Temple Connection to Priesthood Keys|Sacred Temple Connection to Priesthood Keys]] interview, Williams walks through his "priesthood plate" — an illustration narrating the whole story of the priesthood from [[Adam]] to the latter days, paired with a discourse by [[George Q. Cannon]]. His stated method is to "link up those words we gloss over" (keys, dispensations, garments, robes) by attaching concrete imagery to them, surfacing symbolism in the Doctrine and Covenants that readers skip. The plate encodes the seven dispensations (seven keyholes on the globe; Joseph's seven-sealed scroll) and the Aaronic/Melchizedek dualism, and supplied the visual prompt for several arguments in the episode. See [[Priesthood Keys Frame the Seven Dispensations]] and [[The Aaronic-Melchizedek Dualism Maps the Temple's Kings and Priests]].
