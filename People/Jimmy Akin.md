# Jimmy Akin

Senior Catholic Answers apologist and author of *The Drama of Salvation*. (Ward Radio-style auto-captions spell it "Aken.")

## Justification
Source of two key points in the James 2 argument: "the faith isn't the problem — it's being alone that's the problem," and the **lamp analogy** (baptism gives pure righteousness; good works increase its *brightness*).
