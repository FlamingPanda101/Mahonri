# Luke the Evangelist

Traditional author of the Gospel of Luke and the Acts of the Apostles (~80-85 CE). Claims in Luke 1:1-4 to have "carefully investigated everything from the beginning." His nativity account (Luke 1-2) includes the census under Quirinius, the shepherd's visitation, and temple rituals — but no flight to Egypt or slaughter of innocents.
