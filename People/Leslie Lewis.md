# Leslie Lewis

Deputy in the Salt Lake County Attorney's Office in the [[Brenda Miles]] / [[Russell M. Nelson]] case. She testified that "no church officials attempted to influence their work" — the third of three investigator statements (with [[Michael George]] and [[Diana Stevens]]) refuting the claim that the LDS Church coerced investigators, the FBI, or the courts to bury the allegations.

## No church interference
Lewis's testimony completes the sworn record against the cover-up theory; the judge subsequently dismissed the 2018 case with prejudice. See [[No Evidence the LDS Church Suppressed the Investigations]]. [^1]
