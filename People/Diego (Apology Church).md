# Diego (Apology Church)

## Who
Diego is an **evangelical open-air ("street") preacher** associated with a group he calls **"Apology Church,"** who evangelizes Latter-day Saints at the **Mesa Temple Christmas lights** in Arizona. He says he is "out here most" nights, has appeared in YouTube debate clips ("I'm on YouTube… debates sometimes… at Apology Church"), and distinguishes himself from the "Calvinists" who preach alongside him.[^1] His self-described soteriology is **salvation by grace through faith, the cross alone**, the Bible as the sole authority, and the Book of Mormon and LDS Church as false.[^2]

## Why he matters here
In [[Lets Talk w Hayden Carroll - Street Preachers Refuse to Talk with a Mormon|"Street Preachers Refuse to Talk with a Mormon"]] Diego is the preacher who *does* engage [[Hayden Carroll]] at length (unlike the colleague who refuses outright). The exchange illustrates two recurring apologetic patterns:
- His **"Bible only"** stance, which Carroll presses into a [[The Circularity Charge Against Self-Attesting Scripture|circle]] ("How do you know it's the word of God?" → "It's in scripture").[^3]
- His affirmation of **inherited guilt + required profession**, which Carroll turns into [[The Infant-Damnation Reductio Against Inherited Guilt Plus Required Profession|the infant-damnation reductio]].[^4]
He also raises the objection that the Book of Mormon (Nephi) names New-Testament disciples "600 years before," which he counts as evidence of fabrication.[^5]

## Key arguments (linked concepts)
- [[The Circularity Charge Against Self-Attesting Scripture]] — his "the Bible is the word of God because scripture says so" loop.
- [[The Infant-Damnation Reductio Against Inherited Guilt Plus Required Profession]] — both premises supplied by Diego.
- [[Street Preachers Engage the Unprepared but Refuse the Prepared]] — Diego is the engaging counterexample to his refusing colleagues.
