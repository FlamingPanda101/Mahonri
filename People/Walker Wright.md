# Walker Wright

**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint

## Identity

Walker Wright is a Latter-day Saint writer and researcher who, with [[Don Bradley]], co-authored a *BYU Studies* article on the First Vision. The article argues that Joseph Smith's 1832–33 revisions to the Bible (the Joseph Smith Translation, e.g. Psalm 14) already reflect the Father's involvement in the First Vision — undercutting the skeptical claim that Joseph added the Father to the story only later (1835+).
