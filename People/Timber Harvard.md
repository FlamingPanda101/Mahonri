# Timber Harvard

**Full Name:** Timber Harvard
**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Online minister, LGBTQ+/LDS voice

## Bio

Timber Harvard is an LDS man who experiences same-sex attraction and is open
about his journey online. He is married to a woman in the temple and has
children. [[Hayden Carroll]] cites him as an example of someone who "is using
his free will to keep covenants with God" despite experiencing same-sex
attraction.[^1] Harvard's social media presence focuses on his experience
navigating faith and sexuality within an LDS framework.

## Topics

### Free Will and Same-Sex Attraction
Carroll presents Harvard as a counterexample to the claim that same-sex
attraction determines identity and destiny within a deterministic framework:
"He experiences same-sex attraction and he is married to a woman — to his
wife — in the temple. He has kids."[^1] Carroll acknowledges the controversy:
"A lot of people hate him or hate on him rather because they would say, 'Oh,
you can't love your wife. You can't have a fulfilling marriage because you're
denying yourself of what your inclinations are.'"[^1] See [[Homosexuality
Problem for Atheistic Natural Selection]].
