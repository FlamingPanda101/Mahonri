# Ward Radio

**Type:** Independent LDS-themed YouTube channel / podcast
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (independent commentary)
**Tagline:** "Independent News, Humor, and Commentary on the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints"

## Bio

Ward Radio is an independent Latter-day Saint media outlet producing news,
humor, and apologetic commentary on YouTube and podcast platforms. Its programs
frequently host LDS scholars and apologists to respond to critics of the Church.
It is not an official organ of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

## Gold Plates

Hosted [[Neal Rappleye]] and [[Stephen Smoot]] to rebut the claim that
[[Joseph Smith]] could have fabricated the Book of Mormon gold plates "in his
garage," surveying the witness descriptions, the [[Tumbaga Alloy Explains Golden Appearance and Green Oxidation|tumbaga alloy theory]],
and [[Trent Horn]]'s text-capacity argument.[^1]
