# Judy Johnson

The original accuser whose 1983 complaint launched the [[McMartin Preschool Trial Produced No Convictions|McMartin preschool case]] — the largest single episode of the satanic panic.

## The McMartin accusation
Johnson accused workers at her local McMartin preschool of satanic ritual abuse, alleging her children were abused and tortured. The accusation snowballed into charges involving over 1,400 alleged victims. Johnson "was revealed to have been a diagnosed paranoid schizophrenic." See [[McMartin Preschool Trial Produced No Convictions]] and [[Satanic Panic Is a Recurrence of Salem-Style Hysteria]] [^1][^2].
