# Hans Thimme

An early-twentieth-century Protestant clergyman and amateur sociologist cited in [[All Those In Favor - Are Mormons Just White Islam]] as another source of the "Mormonism is Islam" comparison. Though he claimed to be impartial, Thimme used the parallel to argue that both faiths were *Christian heresies* — on the grounds that they supposedly undervalue human sinfulness and divine sovereignty, and lack "the message of the cross."

The channel treats his work as revealing "an agenda of criticism rather than authentic academic inquiry." See [[The White Islam Comparison Springs From Agenda-Driven Orientalism]].
