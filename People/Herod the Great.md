# Herod the Great

Herod I (c. 72 – 4 BCE), client king of Judea under Rome. Known for massive building projects including the expansion of the Second Temple. Matthew's gospel places Jesus's birth during his reign and attributes the "slaughter of the innocents" (Matthew 2:16-18) to him. Historical sources (Josephus) confirm his death in 4 BCE — creating a chronological contradiction with Luke's census under Quirinius (6 CE).
