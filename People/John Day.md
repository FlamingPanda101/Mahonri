# John Day

**Tradition:** Christian (British)
**Role:** Oxford Old Testament scholar; Canaanite religion and the divine council

## Bio
John Day is Emeritus Professor of Old Testament Studies at the University of Oxford, known for work on Canaanite religion and its relationship to ancient Israelite belief (*Yahweh and the Gods and Goddesses of Canaan*). [[Alex Arnold]] names him in the closing roster of non-LDS scholars — alongside [[Mark Smith]], [[Michael Heiser]], [[Frank Moore Cross]], and [[Margaret Barker]] — who concluded from the primary sources that the oldest layer of the biblical tradition is a populated divine council rather than the God of the Nicene Creed.[^1]
