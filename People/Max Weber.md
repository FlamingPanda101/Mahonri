# Max Weber (1864-1920)

German sociologist, a founding figure of the discipline. With Ernst Troeltsch he developed the **church / sect** typology of religious organization at the turn of the 20th century.

## Church / sect distinction
His sect-vs-church categories underlie the modern sociological analysis of where groups like the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints fall. See [[Church Sect Cult Distinction]] [^1].
