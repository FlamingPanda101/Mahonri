# Eve

The first woman; wife of [[Adam]] and "mother of all living" ([[Genesis 4|Genesis 3-4]]). Within the vault Eve is treated as scriptural narrative.

## The garments of skin and the fall as birth
[[Stick of Joseph|The Stick of Joseph]] hosts read Eve's choice in Eden as a knowing decision to enter mortality, after which God clothes the couple in "coats of skins" — a figure both of the [[The Garment Is the Covering of Christ|atonement's covering]] and of mortal flesh, so that the fall reads as a birth requiring a [[The Veil Is Christ Reversing the Day of Atonement|second birth]] back through the veil. The matchmaking step of the Hebrew wedding is also read onto Eden: "who selected the bride for Adam? God the Father." See [[Ancient Hebrew Weddings Are a Three-Phase Type of the Temple]].
