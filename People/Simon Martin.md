# Simon Martin

**Era:** Contemporary (1990s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Academic (non-LDS)
**Role:** Maya epigrapher and historian

## Bio

Simon Martin is a leading Maya epigrapher and historian, known for reconstructing Maya
dynastic history from inscriptions. Rappleye describes him and co-author [[Mary Miller]]
as "absolute top tier" specialists in Mesoamerican studies.[^1]

## Maya Scimitar-Like Blade

With Mary Miller, Martin described a Maya warrior depiction holding a "scimitar-like
flint blade," supporting the point that curved/scimitar-like weapons are not
anachronistic in the New World. See
[[Scimitars Are Not an Anachronism in the Old or New World]].[^2]
