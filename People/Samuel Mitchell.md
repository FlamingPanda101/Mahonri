# Samuel Mitchell

Prominent early-American scientist and physician (often spelled Mitchill) whom [[Martin Harris]] visited with the transcript of the plate characters in 1829, before or around the visit to [[Charles Anthon]].

## Role in the transcript episode
Mitchell "confessed that he had been unable to understand" the characters and "sent Harris with a note requesting Charles Anthon to decipher if possible" [^1]. The sequence of his and Anthon's involvement is uncertain in the record. See [[Charles Anthon Transcript Episode]].
