# Elon Musk

**Full Name:** Elon Reeve Musk
**Era:** Contemporary (1971–present)
**Tradition:** Secular / non-religious (culturally Anglican background)
**Role:** Entrepreneur (Tesla, SpaceX, xAI), technology public intellectual

## Bio

Elon Musk is a South African-born entrepreneur and the CEO of Tesla, SpaceX,
and xAI. He has been one of the most prominent popularizers of the simulation
hypothesis, suggesting publicly that the odds we are living in \"base reality\"
are extremely low — perhaps one in billions — and that we are more likely living
in a computer simulation created by a more advanced civilization.

## Topics

### Simulation Hypothesis
Musk's endorsement of [[Nick Bostrom]]'s simulation argument has brought the
idea into mainstream popular discourse. [[David Butler]] notes the irony:
atheists who take Musk's simulation hypothesis seriously have already conceded
the conceptual framework of a created, non-ultimate reality with a higher
intelligence behind it — the same structure as the LDS plan of salvation's
\"second estate.\"[^1] See [[Simulation Theory Aligns with LDS Second Estate]].
