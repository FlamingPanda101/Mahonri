# Pope Benedict XVI (1927-2022)

Pope (2005-2013) and theologian (Joseph Ratzinger), one of the most significant Catholic theological minds of the era.

## On "faith alone"
Offered an irenic reading: "Luther's phrase 'faith alone' is true if it is not opposed to faith in charity, in love" — because true faith is being united and conformed to Christ, whose life is love. Used to show the Catholic position can affirm a *properly understood* faith-alone. See [[Faith Alone but Never Alone]] [^1].
