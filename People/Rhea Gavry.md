# Rhea Gavry

Filmmaker who produced a 1992 documentary about the Hadfield case titled *Promise Not to Tell*. Although Gavry provided no explicit commentary, the film was widely taken to be sympathetic to [[Allen Hadfield]] and skeptical of the therapists [[Barbara Snow]] and [[Paul Whitehead]].

## Promise Not to Tell
The documentary contributed to the public reassessment of the Lehi cases. See [[The Allen Hadfield Trial as a Miscarriage of Justice]] [^1].
