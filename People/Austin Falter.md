# Austin Falter

**Who:** Latter-day Saint media producer of "Falter Media," a frequent panelist and editor collaborating with [[Ward Radio]]. Returned missionary (served partly in Buffalo and Palmyra, NY).

**Role:** Podcast panelist / video editor (not a credentialed scholar). Contributes the "Amen, brother" counter-apologetic framing in the Galatians 1:8 episode.

## Galatians 1:8
Falter proposes the disarming "Amen, brother / high five / here's a strudel" response: enthusiastically agreeing that anyone preaching against Christ's resurrection is accursed, then noting Latter-day Saints affirm the resurrection — using agreement to defuse the [[Galatians 1.8|Galatians 1:8]] attack and open conversation. See [[The Amen Brother Apologetic Strategy]].[^1]
