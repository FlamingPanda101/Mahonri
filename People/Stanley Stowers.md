# Stanley Stowers

**Role:** Emeritus professor of religious studies (Brown University)
**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Academic biblical studies

## Bio
Stanley Stowers is an influential New Testament scholar, especially on Paul and on the social and literary setting of early Christianity. He is the doctoral adviser of [[Robin Faith Walsh]].

## Literary, Not Communal, Origins of Early Christian Texts
[[Hugo Mendez]] credits Stowers (before Walsh) with shifting attention away from ancient churches as the formative place of early Christian literature toward educated literary production. See [[Early Christian Writers Were Literate Networks Not Faith Communities]] [^1].
