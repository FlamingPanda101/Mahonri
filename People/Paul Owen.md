# Paul Owen

**Era:** Contemporary (1990s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Evangelical Protestant
**Role:** Biblical scholar, theologian

## Bio
Paul Owen is an Evangelical scholar of biblical studies and theology who, with [[Carl Mosser]], co-authored a widely cited critical assessment of how Evangelicals engage Mormonism. (The [[All Those In Favor - Three Tactics to Evangelize Mormons|All Those In Favor]] hosts render the surname "Owens.")

## Topics

### "Are We Losing the Battle?" (1997)
With Mosser, Owen wrote *Mormonism and the Problem of Loss: Are We Losing the Battle? And Do We Not Know It?* (1997). The hosts cite it as a hostile-witness concession — these are authors hostile to Mormonism ("they are not on our team at all… everyone should read this") who nonetheless judged Evangelical responses to be falling behind rising LDS scholarship.[^1] It anchors the episode's recurring point that the scholarship gap has widened since 1997 — see [[Carl Mosser]] and [[All Those In Favor - Three Tactics to Evangelize Mormons]].
