# Mike Warnke

Evangelical author of *The Satan Seller*, one of the satanic-ritual-abuse memoirs the Ward Radio series identifies as a source of the stock "coven of 13" motif. The episode notes that *The Satan Seller*, *The Satanic Underground*, and especially *Michelle Remembers* all feature the coven-of-13 figure that [[Glenn L. Pace]] reproduces in his memo when he assumes "each [victim] comes from a coven of 13." [^1]

## Role in the genre
Warnke is cited as one of three named book sources (with [[Lauren Stratford|Lauren Wright/Stratford]] and Pazder–Smith's *Michelle Remembers*) whose shared tropes prove the Pace memo was a "late-stage synthesis of already well-established tropes" rather than an original discovery. See [[The Pace Memo Recycled Existing Satanic Panic Tropes]]. [^1]
