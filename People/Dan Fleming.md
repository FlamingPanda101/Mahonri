# Dan Fleming

**Who:** Professor of Hebrew Bible and Ancient Near Eastern Studies at New York University (NYU). Author of *The Legacy of Israel in Judah's Bible: History, Politics, and the Reinscribing of Tradition* (Cambridge University Press, 2012).

**Role:** Fleming argues that the Hebrew Bible was shaped by the legacy of the northern kingdom of Israel on the southern kingdom of Judah — that Judah's Bible incorporated and adapted Israelite traditions. [[Jacob Wright]] builds on Fleming's thesis but diverges on one key point: Fleming sees some ethnic commonality between Israel and Judah, while Wright argues the connection was "purely political."
