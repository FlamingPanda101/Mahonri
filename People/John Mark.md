# John Mark

Traditional author of the Gospel of Mark (~70 CE), the earliest gospel. According to Papias (via Eusebius), Mark served as Peter's interpreter and wrote down Peter's recollections accurately but not in chronological order. Mark's gospel contains no nativity account — Jesus's story begins at his baptism.
