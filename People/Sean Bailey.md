# Sean Bailey

**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint

## Identity

Sean Bailey is a Latter-day Saint commentator who appears on Ward Radio. In the Don Bradley seer-stone/Freemasonry episode he offers a speculative reading ("Sean Bailey Doctrine") of the First Vision: that Joseph saw Jesus Christ first, whose touch then enabled him to see the Father — paralleling passing through the veil (which represents Christ) to reach the Father.[^1]
