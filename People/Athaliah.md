# Athaliah

**Who:** Queen of Judah (c. 841–835 BCE), daughter of Ahab and Jezebel of the northern kingdom of Israel. Her reign is recorded in 2 Kings 11 and 2 Chronicles 22–23. She was the only reigning queen of either Israel or Judah.

**Role:** [[Jacob Wright]] cites Athaliah as evidence that the northern kingdom of Israel dominated Judah politically, placing one of its own members on Judah's throne. Her reign illustrates the historical North-South relationship that Wright argues the biblical "united monarchy" narrative was designed to retroactively reverse.
