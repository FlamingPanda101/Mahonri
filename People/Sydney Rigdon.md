# Sydney Rigdon

Early LDS leader (1793–1876) who served as a counselor in the [[Joseph Smith]]
First Presidency. He was present with Smith when Doctrine and Covenants Section
76 ("The Vision") was received in 1832.

## Role in D&C 76

Rigdon was a co-recipient with Joseph Smith of the 1832 revelation known as
"The Vision" ([[D&C 76]]), which introduced the three degrees of glory (Celestial,
Terrestrial, Telestial) and laid early groundwork for the concept of exaltation
that would later be developed fully in the [[King Follett Discourse]].
