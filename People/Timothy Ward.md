# Timothy Ward

Evangelical (Anglican) theologian and author writing on the doctrine of Scripture (*Words of Life*; *Word and Supplement*). Cited by [[Trent Horn]] for the formula that "in general the fathers assert the material sufficiency of scripture but deny its formal sufficiency" — a Protestant scholar conceding a distinction Horn uses against [[Anthony Rogers]].[^1]

## Material vs formal sufficiency
Horn deploys Ward's summary to argue the patristic consensus grants Scripture contains all needed doctrine *materially* but not that Scripture alone is *formally* sufficient to interpret itself without the church. See [[Material vs Formal Sufficiency - The Home Depot Analogy]].[^1]
