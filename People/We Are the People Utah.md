# We Are the People Utah

Anti-Mormon podcast identified across Ward Radio Parts 5–6 as the principal contemporary vehicle for the satanic-ritual-abuse conspiracy aimed at LDS leadership. Its "Of Pedophiles and Prophets" segments allege that [[Russell M. Nelson]], [[Ezra Taft Benson]], [[Thomas Monson]], [[Gordon B. Hinckley]], [[Neil A. Maxwell]], "and hundreds of other leaders" are implicated in child abuse — claims the series argues have "no evidence, no motive, and no shame."

## Promoting the SRA conspiracy (Part 5)
The podcast is named as the channel keeping the discredited recovered-memory narrative alive against LDS leaders, alongside [[Tim Ballard]]. See [[Accusations Cannot Transmute Into Evidence]] and [[The Nelson Skull and Bones Claim Confuses Utah's Owl and Key With Yale]]. [^1]

## "All abuse is satanic" as a Trojan horse (Part 6)
Part 6 charges that the podcast uses the slogan "all abuse is satanic" to fold ordinary abuse cases (like the [[Paul Adams]] case) back into the satanic-ritual-abuse frame — "a Trojan horse for 'prophets are eating babies like popcorn chicken'" — collapsing the distinction between abuse and SRA. See [[The Spotlight Analogy Equates a Celibate Cardinal With a Volunteer Bishop]]. [^2]
