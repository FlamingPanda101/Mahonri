# Abel

Second son of Adam and Eve, murdered by his brother [[Cain]] ([[Genesis 4|Genesis 4]]) — the first murder and the first martyr.

## The blood of Abel
Jesus invokes "the blood of Abel" as the beginning of prophet-murder ([[Luke 11.47-51|Luke 11:47-51]]). Apocryphal tradition holds the earth refused to receive his body (and his blood would not dry) until Adam was buried first. See [[Blood of Abel - Unburiable Tradition]].
