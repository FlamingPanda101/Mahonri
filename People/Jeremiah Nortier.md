# Jeremiah Nortier

Reformed (Calvinist) Protestant apologist and host of **The Apologetic Dog** ministries on YouTube, "where it's our heart's desire to contend for the gospel of grace." He hosts and moderates debates between Christians of differing traditions.

## Significance in the vault
Nortier moderated the debate "Does Water Baptism Save?" between [[Ryan (NeedGod)|Ryan]] (Reformed Baptist, NeedGod.net) and [[Joe Heschmeyer]] (Catholic Answers / Shameless Popery), the basis for the vault's baptism-debate notes. As moderator his own positions are largely implicit, but the channel's framing ("gospel of grace") aligns with the faith-alone side.
