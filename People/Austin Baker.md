# Austin Baker

A Latter-day Saint content creator, husband of [[Callie Baker]] and co-runner of the **About Our Hope** Instagram account. He appears (largely as a quieter co-panelist) on the [[Ward Radio]] livestream on "unique LDS theology every Gen Z should know," hosted by [[Cardon Ellis]].[^1] He is distinct from [[Austin Falter]] (Fault Media), a separate Ward Radio guest.
