# Cameron Bertuzzi

Christian apologist and founder of **Capturing Christianity**, who converted to Catholicism. In the Ward Radio source he is named alongside [[Joe Heschmeyer]] as a Catholic figure who, per [[Cardon Ellis]], left Latter-day Saints feeling more comfortable with their Catholic neighbors after a prior debate — though Ellis says he would "love to be neighbors" with Bertuzzi only "until he walks back the burning of my books thing," referencing Bertuzzi's stated willingness to burn the Book of Mormon.[^1]

## Relationship to LDS Apologetics
Treated by the panel as a relatively good-faith interlocutor (in contrast to the harsher reception of some Daily Wire figures), and part of the broader Catholic-vs-LDS new-media dialogue that includes [[Trent Horn]] and [[Thomas Aquinas|Aquinas]]-based contingency arguments. See [[Latter-day Saints Are Atheists Charge Rebutted]].
