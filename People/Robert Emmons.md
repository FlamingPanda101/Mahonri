# Robert Emmons

UC Davis psychologist, the world's foremost researcher on the **psychology of gratitude** (author of *Thanks!* and *Gratitude Works!*).

## Gratitude as recognizing reality, not a forced feeling
[[Don Bradley]] — who reached out to Emmons and became a friend — relays Emmons's point that gratitude "shouldn't be something that we try to make ourselves feel"; rather it "flows naturally from recognizing the truth of our lives" (none of us created ourselves; we were sustained for years by others; "our lives are just loaded with gifts"). Bradley builds on this to argue that **consecration** follows from gratitude: if everything is a gift ultimately from God through [[Jesus of Nazareth|Christ]], one naturally wants to give back. See [[The Creation Story Is a Model for How to Live]].[^1]
