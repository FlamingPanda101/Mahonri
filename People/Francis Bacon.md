# Francis Bacon

**Era:** 1561–1626 (16th–17th century)
**Tradition:** Early Enlightenment / scientific revolution
**Role:** Philosopher, statesman

## Bio

English philosopher and statesman. His *Novum Organum* (1620) is, on [[Brian Thomassen]]'s account, "a fundamental text in establishing what is going to become modern science," expanding [[Aristotle]]'s inductive logic into what becomes the **scientific method**.[^1]

## Role in the Argument

One of three foundational Enlightenment figures Thomassen names (with [[Galileo Galilei|Galileo]] and [[René Descartes|Descartes]]) as making "the Enlightenment what it is." His induction underwrites the empiricist disposition Thomassen ties to the Constitution as an Enlightenment document. See [[The Constitution Is an Enlightenment Document]].[^2]
