# Brenda Miles

Daughter of apostle (later church president) [[Russell M. Nelson]] and a central accused figure in the [[Marion Smith]] / ISAT case. A friend of Smith's, she and her husband [[Richard Miles]] were accused of being part of a child sex ring shortly after her father was ordained to the Quorum of the Twelve in 1985 — a coincidence Ward Radio argues made the accusation irresistible for headlines and book sales ("implicating a new apostle would be absolutely earth-shattering"). The charges (near-drowning, feces-drinking, rape, videotaped abuse) never produced any physical evidence.

## The case dismissed with prejudice
A 2018 relitigation (filed months after Nelson became church president) re-alleged the Miles charges and added Nelson by name as an alleged cover-up accomplice; the judge "dismissed the case with prejudice," barring a third attempt. See [[Accusations Cannot Transmute Into Evidence]] and [[No Evidence the LDS Church Suppressed the Investigations]]. [^1][^2]
