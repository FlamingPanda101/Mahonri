# Alva Beeman

A friend of [[Joseph Smith]] counted among the natural [[Informal Witnesses of the Golden Plates|informal witnesses]].

## Encounter with the plates
He "lifted and felt the plates to help hide them" from a mob attempting to steal them, and later recorded that he "heard them" rustle/clink. He subsequently joined the church [^1]. His account is one of the incidental, protective handlings the episode cites; see [[Natural vs Supernatural Witnesses]].

> Note: distinct from "Alvah Beeman Culpepper," a separate supernatural informal witness named in the episode who reported seeing angels with the Book of Mormon [^1].
