# Voddie Baucham

Reformed Baptist pastor, author, and conference speaker (rendered "Vad Bacham" in the auto-transcript), known for forceful preaching of [[Calvinism Is Consistent but Built on a Single Bad Premise|Calvinist soteriology]]. In a sermon clip, Baucham identifies the **double imputation** of sin and righteousness as "the gospel that we preach."

## Used in / Role in the argument
[[Trent Horn]] uses Baucham as a lead example of an "unpleasant Protestant" who conflates a Calvinist plan of salvation with the gospel itself. Protestant theologian [[Randal Rauser]] critiques Baucham by name, saying his articulation "is not the gospel — it's an interpretation of the gospel," and warning that the conflation treats dissenting Christians as sub-Christian. See [[Double Imputation Is Not the Gospel]] and [[Two Mistakes That Turn a Denomination's Distinctives Into the Gospel]]. [^1]
