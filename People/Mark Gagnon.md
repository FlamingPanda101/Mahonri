# Mark Gagnon

A comedian and podcaster whose channel *Religion Camp* published a video on the history of Joseph Smith and Mormonism. [[Keystone]] reviews it favorably — grading it "90 to 95% accuracy" and praising Gagnon as "dedicated to being kind and respectful," not "actively trying to tear [the truth claims] down" — while correcting five specifics.[^1]

## Role in the vault
Gagnon is the foil for [[Keystone - What Mark Gagnon Gets Wrong About Joseph Smith]]. Keystone credits him with getting polygamy's motivation, the organic "Mormon" nickname, and a tribute to missionary service right, while flagging errors: overstating treasure-digging as the family's primary income (refuted by [[Lucy Mack Smith]]'s memoir), asserting an [[Joseph Smith Was Never Charged With Necromancy|arrest and charge for necromancy that never happened]], treating the [[The New York Hill Is Not the Book of Mormon Cumorah|New York hill as the Book of Mormon Cumorah]], portraying a multi-year translation, and saying "no one knows" why [[Moroni Took the Plates to Preserve the Sealed Portion|Moroni took the plates back]].[^2]
