# Matthew the Evangelist

Traditional author of the Gospel of Matthew (~80-90 CE). Matthew's nativity account (Matthew 1-2) includes the virgin birth, the magi, the flight to Egypt, the slaughter of the innocents, and Joseph and Mary's settlement in Nazareth. Matthew frequently uses fulfillment citations to tie Jesus to Hebrew Bible prophecies.
