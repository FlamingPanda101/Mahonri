# A.T. Robertson

Southern Baptist Greek grammarian and New Testament scholar (1863–1934), author of the monumental *A Grammar of the Greek New Testament in the Light of Historical Research* and *Word Pictures in the New Testament*.

## Romans 3:2 — Commands and Promises, Not Scripture
Cited by [[Trent Horn]] (a Protestant authority enlisted against a Protestant proof-text): the *logia* of [[Romans 3.2|Romans 3:2]] "may refer to the commands and promises of God rather than scripture itself." See [[Romans 3.2 Says Oracles Logia Not Words Logos]] [^1].
