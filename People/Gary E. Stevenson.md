# Gary E. Stevenson

Member of the LDS **Quorum of the Twelve Apostles**. Mentioned in this video in the context of the church's recent push toward observing Easter.

## Easter emphasis and "Lent-adjacent" devotion
[[Luke Hansen]] notes Stevenson, "a year or more ago," led a churchwide effort to put "a heavier emphasis on Easter" with specific observances. While the church has not adopted Lent as such, members have been encouraged toward "increased devotion during the Easter season" — a "Lent-adjacent" or "proto-Lent" practice that [[Joe Heschmeyer]] flagged as encouraging common ground between Catholics and Latter-day Saints [^1].
