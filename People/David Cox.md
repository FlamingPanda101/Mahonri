# David Cox

Adult who, as a child, was taken to therapist [[Barbara Snow]] by his mother and later gave a first-hand account of her coercive interviewing. He recalls: "Barbara vigorously tried to get me to believe in fictitious memories that I had been sexually abused by my father. She would yell, threaten, and berate me if I didn't admit that I had been abused … She came up with the most outlandish ideas and then tried to convince me that they were my own memories. She destroyed my family with her allegations. She does use recorders, but conveniently loses them when it makes her look bad." [^1]

## Eyewitness to the method
Cox's account is one of the firsthand corroborations behind [[Barbara Snows Interview Methods Manufactured Abuse Allegations]] — describing the same yelling, leading, and selective non-recording documented by other observers such as [[Wayne Watson]]. [^1]
