# Matt Fradd

Catholic podcaster and host of **Pints with Aquinas** (on the Daily Wire). His earlier episode featuring [[Joe Heschmeyer]]'s critique of Mormonism is the conversation this video responds to.

## Context for the Adam-God dialogue
On *Pints with Aquinas*, Heschmeyer delivered the four-point case that Adam-God was once "official, binding, ex cathedra" LDS teaching. LDS commenters asked why Catholics were "talking about Mormons, but not with Mormons," prompting [[Luke Hansen]] to invite Heschmeyer onto Ward Radio for this follow-up — in which Heschmeyer revisits and refines several claims he made on Fradd's show. See [[Adam-God Doctrine - What Brigham Young Taught]] and [[Tabernacle Infallibility - Elders of Israel Misread]] [^1].
