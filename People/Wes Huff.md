# Wes Huff

Christian apologist. In this video he represents the **appeal to sinfulness** defense of hell.

## Appeal to sinfulness
Argues "there's no such thing as an innocent person... I deserve hell 100% of the time... the wages of sin is death," and that no one is saved by works — only by what Jesus did. This is the position rebutted in [[Hell - Appeal to Sinfulness Rebutted]] and [[Hell - Finite Sin vs Infinite Punishment]] [^1].
