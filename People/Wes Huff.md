# Wes Huff

Evangelical Christian apologist used as the primary interlocutor / example of
the "appeal to sinfulness" defense of hell in the All Those In Favor video.

## Key Arguments (as presented in the video)

### On Universal Sinfulness

> "There's no such thing as an innocent person... I deserve hell, me, Wes
> Huff, I deserve hell 100% of the time... I am not going to heaven because
> of anything I've done." [^1]

Huff argues that all humans are sinful and deserve hell; God saving anyone is
an act of mercy. This position is explicitly rebutted in [[Hell - Appeal to
Sinfulness Rebutted]] and [[Hell - Finite Sin vs Infinite Punishment]].

### On Grace vs Works

> "You cannot earn your way to heaven. It's not about what I'm doing. It's
> about what Jesus did." [^1]
