# Cade (All Those In Favor)

LDS content creator and host of the YouTube reaction channel **All Those In Favor**, co-hosting with [[Baylor Johnson (Ward Radio)|Baylor Johnson]]. A returned missionary (served in West Texas) who recently moved into a new ward, he narrates and drives most of the commentary in [[All Those In Favor - Pastor Destroys Mormon Missionaries Former Missionaries React]], reacting to a viral "pastor owns Mormon missionaries" clip alongside Baylor and guest [[Kwaku (All Those In Favor)|Kwaku]].

## In this source
He supplies the bulk of the apologetic pushback: that the pastor's "part God, part man" trap presupposes the [[Hypostatic Union]] (Chalcedon, 451) and a Greek-philosophical doctrine of [[Divine Impassibility Is Classical-Theist Heresy|impassibility]]; that the JST does not read as the pastor claimed (see [[The Pastor Misattributed the Anarthrous Reading to the Joseph Smith Translation]]); and that missionaries should function as guides, not debaters (see [[Missionaries Are Tour Guides Not Question Answerers]]). He cites [[Blake Ostler]] on the Godhead as a "relationship of indwelling unity" and [[Daniel Wallace]] on the anarthrous *theos* of [[John 1.1|John 1:1]].
