# Democritus (c. 460-c. 370 BC)

Ancient Greek pre-Socratic philosopher, with his teacher Leucippus the founder of **atomism** — the view that all reality reduces to indivisible particles (Greek *atomos*, "uncuttable") moving in void.

## Atomism as the Greek instinct to deconstruct
Named (alongside the etymology of "atom") as the archetype of the Greek epistemology: to know a thing, break it into its smallest parts and define it by those parts. Contrasted with the Hebrew holistic/functional way of knowing. See [[Atomizing vs Holistic Knowing]] [^1].
