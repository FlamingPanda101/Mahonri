# Randal Rauser

Canadian Protestant (Baptist) systematic theologian and professor of historical theology (rendered "Randall Rouseer" / "Rouser" in the auto-transcript). Known for irenic, philosophically careful engagement and for criticizing fellow evangelicals who over-narrow the gospel.

## Used in / Role in the argument
[[Trent Horn]] enlists Rauser as a **Protestant witness against** "unpleasant Protestants." Rauser argues that [[Voddie Baucham]] "conflated his summary of the Calvinist plan of salvation with the gospel," insisting "it's not the gospel — it's an *interpretation* of the gospel," and warns the conflation "leads to people... treating Christians who disagree with him on doctrines like the specific nature of the atonement as being less Christian than him or not even Christian at all." See [[Double Imputation Is Not the Gospel]] and [[Mistaking Tradition for the Gospel Sets Up Brittle Faith]]. [^1]
