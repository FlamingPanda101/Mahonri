# Harold Camping

**Who:** American Christian radio broadcaster and former civil engineer (1921–2013). President of Family Radio.

**Role in biblical studies / apologetics:** Camping famously predicted the Rapture would occur on May 21, 2011, followed by the end of the world on October 21, 2011. When neither event occurred, followers rationalized the failure. [[Dan McClellan]] cites Camping as an example of how, without [[Methodological Naturalism in Biblical Scholarship|methodological naturalism]], there is no principled way to distinguish between genuine and false prophecy — any failed prediction can be explained away by motivated reasoning.
