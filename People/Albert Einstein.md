# Albert Einstein

**Full Name:** Albert Einstein
**Era:** 1879–1955
**Field:** Theoretical physics (relativity, quantum theory)
**Affiliation:** Institute for Advanced Study, Princeton

## Bio

Albert Einstein was the foremost physicist of the twentieth century, author of
the special and general theories of relativity and a Nobel laureate (1921, for
the photoelectric effect).

## Topics

### "God Does Not Play Dice" — Resistance to Indeterminism
[[Inspiring Philosophy|Michael Jones]] cites Einstein as the famous holdout
against quantum indeterminism. Einstein "hated" the uncertainty principle
because, as he put it, "God doesn't play dice with the universe."[^1] Jones'
verdict, via [[Michio Kaku]], is that on this point "Einstein was wrong. God
does play dice" — the experimental verdict went against Einstein's hope for a
hidden deterministic order, which the video reads as good news for free will.
