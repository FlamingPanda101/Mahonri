# Bruce Metzger

**Who:** Prominent American biblical scholar and textual critic (1914–2007). Professor at Princeton Theological Seminary. Authored *A Textual Commentary on the Greek New Testament* (1971).

**Key citation:** Metzger's textual commentary addresses the variant in [[John 1.18|John 1:18]] between *monogenes huios* (only/unique Son) and *monogenes theos* (only/unique God). Metzger notes that *lectio difficilior* (the more difficult reading) favors *theos* as earlier, BUT the editors of the volume added a note that this is unlikely "because this would require the author actually have written this — and that just doesn't make any sense."[^1]

**Significance:** Even the standard scholarly textual commentary acknowledges the tension: a literal "only God" reading conflicts with the rest of John's Gospel, making the variant an unreliable proof text for Jesus's deity.
