# Deborah

**Who:** Prophetess and judge of Israel in the Book of Judges (chapters 4–5). She leads Israel in battle against the Canaanite king Jabin and his commander Sisera, alongside Barak.

**Role:** [[Jacob Wright]] cites Deborah (along with [[Esther]]) as an example of women in biblical narrative who model the survival-oriented leadership a small, defeated people needs — contrasting her with [[Gideon]]'s more direct military model.
