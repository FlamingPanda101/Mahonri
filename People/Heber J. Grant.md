# Heber J. Grant

Seventh president of the LDS Church (1856–1945; president 1918–1945).

## Recording the "Whole United States" Prophecy
Grant is the source cited for the [[Whole United States Arrayed Against the Saints Prophecy]] — his recollection that [[Joseph Smith]] predicted a day when not just a city, county, or state but "the whole United States should be arrayed against" the Saints, at which "men laughed at the absurdity." Because Grant recorded this long after the fact, the host of [[Mormonism with the Murf - Joseph Smith's Top 20 Fulfilled Prophecies]] flags it for possible retrofitting. See [[Retrofitting a Prophecy After the Fulfillment]].[^1]
