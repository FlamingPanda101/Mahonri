# Tim Ballard

Public figure named in Ward Radio Part 5 as a promoter of the satanic-ritual-abuse conspiracy narrative aimed at LDS leaders. The episode charges that "Tim Ballard thinks peddling these theories can somehow substitute for his own repentance," grouping him with the We Are the People Utah podcast's "Of Pedophiles and Prophets" segments that implicate [[Russell M. Nelson]], [[Ezra Taft Benson]], [[Thomas Monson]], [[Gordon B. Hinckley]], and [[Neil A. Maxwell]].

## Promoting the SRA conspiracy
Ballard is cited as an example of how the discredited recovered-memory/SRA narrative is recycled without evidence or motive. See [[Accusations Cannot Transmute Into Evidence]] and [[The Nelson Skull and Bones Claim Confuses Utah's Owl and Key With Yale]]. [^1]
