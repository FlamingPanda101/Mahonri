# Thomas Schreiner

Reformed New Testament scholar (auto-captions: "Shriner"). Significant because, though Reformed, he concedes points against the standard Protestant defense.

## Justification before God, not men
Schreiner says there is no evidence James's "justify" means before *people* rather than God — undercutting the Calvinist "vindication before men" reading. See [[James - Justification Before Men Reply Rebutted]] [^1].
