# Ray Comfort

**Full name:** Ray Comfort (b. 1949)
**Role:** Evangelist, author, apologist
**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Protestant (evangelical)

## Bio

Ray Comfort is a New Zealand-born evangelist and founder of the Living Waters ministry, best known for street-evangelism using the moral law ("the Way of the Master" method) and for films such as *Evolution vs. God*. He has frequently worked with actor [[Kirk Cameron]]. His ministry produces interview-style evangelistic videos engaging members of other faiths.

## Relevance to the vault

Comfort is named as the head of Living Waters, the ministry whose representative [[Mark Spence]] interviews an under-catechized Latter-day Saint convert. The vault critiques that exchange for misrepresenting LDS beliefs about grace and salvation and for abusing scripture (sola scriptura, Deuteronomy 18), making Comfort's ministry the institutional backdrop for the rebuttal.
