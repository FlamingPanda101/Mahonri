# Lauren Stratford

Author of *The Satanic Underground* and, per the Ward Radio series, the originator of the "breeder" narrative used to explain why no sacrificed-baby evidence ever surfaced. She claimed to have been impregnated through ritual rape so her babies could be sacrificed — children who "would never have had a birth certificate." Her claims were embraced by the evangelical community; she was presented as a witness in the McMartin trial and appeared on Oprah in 1989. See [[The Breeder Myth Was Invented to Explain Missing Bodies]]. [^1]

## Exposed as a serial fabricator
When journalists investigated, the story collapsed: she was never pregnant in the years claimed, suffered undisclosed severe mental illness, practiced self-mutilation, and had been hospitalized 40+ times. Her real name was Lauren Wilson (born 1941 in Washington state). After her publisher dropped her books, she reinvented herself as Holocaust survivor "Lauren Grabowski," claiming Nazi medical experiments rendered her blind and infertile, and even filed claims with the Swiss Fund and the World Jewish Restitution Organization. Her recurring tell — that she had "never been able to tell" her secret before — and her line "only the individual can decide if he or she is a survivor" anchor the episode's thesis about self-identified victimhood. See [[Lauren Stratford Was a Serial Fabricator of Victim Identities]]. [^2][^3]
