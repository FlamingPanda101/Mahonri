# Steven Woodford

**Era:** Contemporary
**Role:** Atheist YouTuber and commentator ("Rationality Rules")

## Bio
Steven Woodford runs the atheist YouTube channel **Rationality Rules**, covering arguments for and against God and various social-issues debates. He has taken a comparatively nuanced line on transgender questions — he was criticized by the Atheist Community of Austin for opposing males competing in women's sports — and distinguishes sex (biological) from gender (a social construct).

## Contribution in This Video
Woodford argues against [[Matt Walsh]]'s "adult human female" definition of *woman*. His case: (1) definitions are judged only by utility, not correctness; (2) intersex conditions — especially complete androgen insensitivity syndrome (his "Jane" example) — show some biological males are women, since re-classifying such a person as male would be cruel and "untenable"; (3) sex relates to gamete type/chromosomes while gender is a social construct correlating with secondary sexual characteristics. [[Trent Horn]] rebuts each move. See [[Intersex Conditions Do Not Prove Gender Is Separate From Sex]], [[Boundary Cases Do Not Dissolve the Sex Binary - The Fallacy of the Beard]], and [[The Utility Argument Cuts Against Severing Gender From Sex]].
