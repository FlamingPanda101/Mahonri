# Philosophy Tube

**Tradition:** Secular; popular philosophy education
**Role:** Philosophy YouTube channel / educator (the "Gentleman Thinker" persona)

## Bio

Philosophy Tube is a philosophy education YouTube channel known for explaining
mainstream philosophical concepts in an accessible, often comic register. The
early *Gentleman Thinker* shorts present topics "in brief in a posh English
accent." The content cited here is a standard introductory treatment of the two
basic forms of reasoning.

## Cited Contribution

In the short [[Philosophy Tube - Deduction and Induction|Deduction and Induction]],
the channel lays out the distinction between deduction (top-down, logically
certain) and induction (bottom-up, probable), notes that science runs on
induction, and flags the classic **problem of induction** — that while it is
clear what makes a good deduction (the premises entail the conclusion), "it
isn't really clear what makes a good induction." See
[[Deduction Is Top-Down and Induction Is Bottom-Up Reasoning]] and
[[No Clear Criterion Separates Good Induction from Bad]].[^1]
