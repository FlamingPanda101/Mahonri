# Zechariah son of Jehoiada

Old Testament priest and prophet, son of the high priest Jehoiada, **stoned to death in the temple court** at the king's command for rebuking Judah's apostasy ([[2 Chronicles 24.20-21|2 Chronicles 24:20-21]]).

## The blood of Zechariah
Jesus names him as the terminus of prophet-murder ("unto the blood of Zacharias") in [[Luke 11.47-51|Luke 11:47-51]]. Apocryphal tradition (Mishnah Sanhedrin 96b) holds his blood **boiled in the temple for ~200 years** until Nebuchadnezzar. See [[Blood of Zechariah - Perpetual Boiling Blood]]. He may be the same figure as the messianic prophet of [[Zechariah 9.9|Zechariah 9:9]].
