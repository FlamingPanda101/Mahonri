# Jonathan Edwards (1703-1758)

Puritan theologian and philosopher, famous for *Sinners in the Hands of an Angry God*.

## Infinite-being argument
With [[William Wainwright]], argued that **all sin warrants eternal punishment because it is committed against an infinite being (God)**. Rebutted: this makes God the creator of beings who can commit "infinite sins" easily and unknowingly. See [[Hell - Finite Sin vs Infinite Punishment]] [^1].
