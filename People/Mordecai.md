# Mordecai

**Who:** Jewish figure in the Book of Esther, cousin and guardian of Esther. He refuses to bow to Haman, triggering the plot against the Jews, and later becomes second in rank to King Ahasuerus.

**Role:** [[Jacob Wright]] contrasts Mordecai with [[Esther]] to illustrate the shift from "battlefield" models of honor (bold confrontation) to "survival" models (indirect, diplomatic action). Esther's approach — working within the system — is the model for a small, defeated people.
