# Tacitus

Cornelius Tacitus (c. 56 – 120 CE), Roman senator and historian. Author of the *Annals* and *Histories*. His account of the Cleitae rebellion (*Annals* 6.41) is misused by some apologists ([[Inspiring Philosophy]]) to argue Rome imposed censuses on client kingdoms — a claim classicists reject.
