# Jeff Borders

Jeff Borders is a Latter-day Saint author (*The Antichrist Playbook*) and a frequent guest panelist on [[Hayden Holm]]'s **Salty Saints** YouTube show. He appears on multiple episodes built from these transcripts, including the [[Salty Saints - Why Water Is Better Than Wine in the Sacrament|sacrament water-vs-wine]] discussion, the [[Salty Saints - Fact-Checking Johnny Harris's Harm Story|Johnny Harris "harm story" rebuttal]], and the [[Salty Saints - Why Gay Temple Marriage Will Never Happen|matrimony / same-sex temple marriage]] episode, where he typically plays the measured, note-taking foil to the more animated hosts.
