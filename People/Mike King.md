# Mike King

Investigator invited by the Utah Attorney General to join the state's ritual-abuse crime unit due to his experience investigating cult activity. He described the assignment — "we're going to pursue Satan" — as "way outside of normal police work." His unit was given almost 300 cases of alleged ritual sexual abuse with a Satanism component.

## The Hit Rate
Asked how many cases proved out, King answered: "Three or four" — roughly 99% "wild goose chases." See [[Ritual Abuse Crime Unit Found Ninety-Nine Percent Wild Goose Chases]] [^1].
