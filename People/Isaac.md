# Isaac

Son of [[Abraham]] and Sarah, the son of the covenant. The near-sacrifice of Isaac (the "Binding of Isaac," [[Genesis 22|Genesis 22]]) is a central test-of-faith narrative and, in Christian and LDS reading, a type of the Father's offering of His Son.

## As a Type of Christ
[[Don Bradley]] notes that Genesis 22 calls Isaac Abraham's "only son" though Ishmael exists — "portending the Only Begotten." Read against the backstory of Abraham's own near-sacrifice, the event reveals Abraham's trust and points to the Atonement. See [[The Binding of Isaac as a Type of Christ]].
