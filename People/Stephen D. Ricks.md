# Stephen D. Ricks

**Stephen D. Ricks** is a Latter-day Saint scholar of biblical and ancient Near Eastern studies (Hebrew Bible, Semitic languages), long associated with BYU and Maxwell-Institute/FARMS scholarship. He is cited in Latter-day Saint apologetics for his work on temple themes, ritual, and apocryphal traditions.

## Relevance
On Ward Radio he is the named source for the **Garment of Adam** thesis: his essay *"The Garment of Adam in Jewish, Muslim, and Christian Tradition"* (in the BYU scholarship archive) compiles the apocryphal and rabbinic lore in which a celestial garment passes from Adam down the patriarchal line and is tied to priestly investiture and atonement-as-covering.
