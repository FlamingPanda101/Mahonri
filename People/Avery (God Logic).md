# Avery (God Logic)

**Role:** Trinitarian YouTube debater (channel: *God Logic*)
**Era:** Contemporary (active 2020s)
**Tradition:** Trinitarian Christian

## Bio

Avery is a Trinitarian content creator who runs the YouTube channel *God Logic* and frequently debates the Trinity. He is the scheduled debate opponent for whom [[Jacob Hansen]] prepares in this interview; Hansen role-plays Avery's expected opening and follow-up arguments so that [[Blake Ostler]] can rebut them. [^1]

## Arguments Anticipated and Rebutted

Hansen attributes to Avery the standard Trinitarian/biblical-unitarian-style case: the Isaiah negation texts prove there is only one God; [[Michael Heiser]]'s "species-unique" reading of Yahweh among the *elohim*; Yahweh as multi-personal; and Christ's "I am" / "alpha and omega" claims as proof of his being Yahweh. Ostler answers each. See:
