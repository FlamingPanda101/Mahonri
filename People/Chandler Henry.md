# Chandler Henry

**Role:** Latter-day Saint apologist and content creator (Restored Truth); nicknamed "Chuck"
**Era:** Contemporary (active 2020s)
**Tradition:** The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

## Bio
Chandler "Chuck" Henry runs **Restored Truth**, focusing on church history, the early Christian fathers, and "the Great Apostasy." On [[Stick of Joseph - God Logic Trinity Debate Recap]] he supplies the historical and linguistic background to the Trinity debate [^1].

## Greek Categories and the Development of the Trinity
Henry argues the creeds imported *Greek* thought-categories (ontology, *homoousios* vs *homoiousios*, consubstantiality) into a Hebrew text that had no such framework, and that the Fathers even coined *new* definitions of Greek terms to make the doctrine work. See:
- [[Greek Categories Are Anachronistic to the Hebrew Bible]]
- [[Being and Person Are the Same in Aristotle]]
- [[Hebrews 1-3 Express Image Is the Imprint Not the Stamp]]

## Charity Toward the Creeds
Distinctively, Henry refuses to call the Trinity "dishonest," casting it instead as a good-faith effort to make sense of scripture under persecution — drawing a controversial analogy to the modern redefinition of "gender" vs "sex." See:
- [[Trinity Was a Good-Faith Effort Not Dishonesty]]
- [[Persons-and-Beings Were Redefined Like Gender and Sex]]
- [[Subordination Within the New Testament]]
