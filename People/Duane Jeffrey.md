# Duane Jeffrey

Duane Jeffrey (Jeffery) is a Latter-day Saint biologist and long-serving professor in the Department of Biology at Brigham Young University, associated with the formal teaching of evolution there. In the vault he marks a milestone in the institutional history that runs from Vasco M. Tanner's 33-year chairmanship to Jeffrey's formal evolution course.

## Relevance

He is cited as evidence that evolutionary biology has been taught at [[Brigham Young University]] as a mainstream academic discipline — supporting the argument in [[Evolutionary Biology at BYU Is an Academic Success Story]] that Latter-day Saint faith does not require creationism.
