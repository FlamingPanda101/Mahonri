# Pat Madrid

**Patrick Madrid** is a veteran Catholic apologist, radio host, and author. He is referenced here for his classic **1993 debate with [[James White]]** on *sola scriptura*.

## The "Who Is the Man of God?" Question
[[Trent Horn]] recounts that when White argued from **2 Timothy 3:17** that Scripture alone equips "the man of God" for every good work, Madrid pressed in rebuttal: *who is the man of God?* Is the Lutheran who believes in baptismal regeneration a man of God? Is White, who disagrees with him? How does anyone know they are the man of God Scripture is equipping in the first place? The point exposes that *sola scriptura* presupposes a prior, extra-scriptural judgment about who rightly interprets Scripture. See [[No Final Interpreter - The Protestant Adjudication Problem]] [^1].

> "Mr. White, who is the man of God?... How do you know that you are the man of God that scripture's equipping in the first place?" (Madrid, paraphrased by Horn) [^1].
