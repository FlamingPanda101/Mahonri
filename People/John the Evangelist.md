# John the Evangelist

Traditional author of the Gospel of John (~90-100 CE), three epistles, and Revelation. John's gospel contains no nativity account; instead opens with the pre-existent Logos (John 1:1-18). John 7:42 references the messianic prophecy of Bethlehem but does not claim Jesus was born there.
