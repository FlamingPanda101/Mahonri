# Zenas Gurley

A Reorganized Church of Jesus Christ (RLDS) figure who recorded testimony from Book of Mormon witness [[David Whitmer]] late in Whitmer's life. The [[Jeremy Runnells|CES Letter]] cites Gurley's record of Whitmer describing his experience as a "vision."[^1]

[[Keystone]] grants the description but denies the inference: calling the experience a vision "does not mean the experience wasn't real," just as Latter-day Saints call the First Vision a vision while affirming it was a real physical event. Whitmer himself, asked about it, replied that the vision "was as factual as" seeing a nearby apple tree.[^1]
