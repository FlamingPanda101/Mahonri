# Anne Draffkorn

Anne Draffkorn Kilmer, Assyriologist and Professor emerita of Near Eastern Studies at the University of California, Berkeley. In [[Alex OConnor - There Are Multiple Gods in the Bible Not Just One - Dan McClellan]], [[Dan McClellan]] cites Draffkorn (alongside [[Cyrus Gordon]], in papers decades apart) as having argued that the Hebrew *ha-elohim* in Exodus 21–22 refers to "the gods," **not** "the judges."

McClellan flags that a NET Bible note cites both scholars to bolster the "judges" reading, when in fact their work argues against it. See [[Exodus 21-22 Ha-Elohim Means the Gods Not the Judges]].
