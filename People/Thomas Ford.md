# Thomas Ford

Governor of Illinois during the Nauvoo period and author of *A History of Illinois* (1852). His book is the earliest source for the [[Empty Box Story Rebutted|empty-box anecdote]] about the Eight Witnesses.

## The empty-box source problem
Ford attributes the story only to "men who were once in the confidence of the prophet," giving no names. The episode traces how later authors — Harry Beardsley, then [[Fawn Brodie]] (who upgraded the source to "Joseph's key men"), then Edmund Wilson — progressively inflated this anonymous report into a confident account [^1][^2]. See [[Empty Box Story Rebutted]].
