# Limhi

**Role:** Nephite king; son of [[King Noah]]
**Era:** Nephite history (~145 BC, narrative setting)
**Tradition:** Book of Mormon figure (LDS scripture)

## Bio
Limhi inherited the throne of the people of Zeniff after his father [[King Noah]] was killed, leading his people under Lamanite bondage until they escaped to Zarahemla. It is Limhi who reports the death of the prophet [[Abinadi]] to [[Ammon]] and his brethren. The detail that [[Ammon]]'s party "did mourn for the death of Abinadi" — twenty years after the fact, for a prophet they never met — is used in the *Key to the Keystone* reconstruction as evidence that Abinadi was a recognized royal figure (the lost heir of the Jacobite line).
