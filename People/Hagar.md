# Hagar

**Role:** Handmaid of [[Sarah]]; mother of [[Ishmael (son of Abraham)|Ishmael]]
**Era:** Patriarchal period (biblical setting)
**Tradition:** Hebrew Bible / Old Testament

## Bio
Hagar, Sarah's Egyptian handmaid, bore [[Abraham]]'s firstborn son [[Ishmael (son of Abraham)|Ishmael]] (Genesis 16). After [[Isaac]]'s birth, Sarah had Hagar and Ishmael sent into the wilderness (Genesis 21). In the Ward Radio discussion of [[Genesis 22]], the existence of Ishmael — Abraham's older son by Hagar — is what makes the Akedah's "thine only son Isaac" language conspicuous, which the hosts read as a Deuteronomist fingerprint on the text.
