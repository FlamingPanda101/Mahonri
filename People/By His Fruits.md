# By His Fruits

**Type:** Independent LDS-themed YouTube channel
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (independent commentary)

## Bio

By His Fruits is an independent Latter-day Saint YouTube channel producing short, illustrated explainer videos defending Restoration scripture and history. Its content synthesizes published LDS apologetic scholarship (such as [[Matt Roper]]'s anachronism research) and General Authority counsel for a general audience. It is not an official organ of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
