# Devin Barnes

Latter-day Saint content creator who runs the **Latter-day Logic** project (latterdaylogic.org) and built a following doing live-stream apologetics on TikTok, where he debates atheists and other Christians one-on-one under a "civil, no-contention" format. He maintains a personal ~120-page Obsidian database of common questions and answers, and frames the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints as "the most logical" / most rationally defensible theology because its doctrines function as interconnected [[You Are What You Act Out - Implicit Axioms as Religion|axioms]] with "no isolated beliefs."

His central apologetic is that the [[Book of Mormon]] is "undebunkable" — naturalistically inexplicable on grounds of complexity, dictation feat, and the witnesses (see [[No Naturalistic Source Accounts for the Whole Book of Mormon]], [[Book of Mormon Witnesses Are the Strongest Evidence]]). Distinctive arguments he advances include [[You Would Believe Abraham as Your Neighbor but Not Joseph Smith]] and [[Moses Received Genesis by Revelation as Joseph Smith Received the Book of Moses]].

Interviewed by host Cardell on [[Ward Radio - The Book of Mormon Is Undebunkable]].
