# Colton Miller

**Role:** Latter-day Saint apologist and content creator (YouTube channel "Colton Miller")
**Era:** Contemporary (active 2020s)
**Tradition:** The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

## Bio
Colton Miller is a Latter-day Saint apologist who produces video responses defending the LDS view of God against Trinitarian critiques. After [[Jacob Hansen]]'s "God Logic" debate, Miller posted a clip explaining the LDS "three whos and one what" view, which Hansen reshared — a clip the panel discusses on [[Stick of Joseph - God Logic Trinity Debate Recap]] [^1].

## The Godhead and Mosiah 15
Miller defends the LDS reading of "oneness" texts ([[John 17.20-23|John 17]], "one flesh") as covenant unity, not numerical identity of being, and has produced a verse-by-verse breakdown of [[Mosiah 15.1-5|Mosiah 15]] responding to the "Modalist proof-text" objection. See:
- [[Three Whos and One What Framework]]
- [[Oneness Means Unity Not Numerical Identity of Being]]
- [[Mosiah 15 Father and Son Explained]]
