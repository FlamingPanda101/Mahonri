# Ludwig Wittgenstein (1889-1951)

Austrian-British philosopher, central to 20th-century philosophy of language. His idea of **"family resemblance"** — that some concepts have no single defining essence but a network of overlapping features — is used to define contested terms.

## Family resemblance and "cult"
Scholars apply this approach to "cult"/NRM: rather than a strict definition, assemble characteristic features and judge whether a group has enough of them. See [[Family Resemblance Definition of Cults]] [^1].
