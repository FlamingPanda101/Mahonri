# Lyman Wight

Early Latter-day Saint apostle (the "Wild Ram of the Mountains"), imprisoned with [[Joseph Smith]] in Liberty Jail.

## Corroborating the Five-Year Death Prophecy
Per [[Wilford Woodruff]]'s journal, Wight said Joseph told him in Liberty Jail in 1839 that "he would not live to see 40 years," but that Wight "was not to reveal it till the prophet was dead" (Joseph died at 38). The host of [[Mormonism with the Murf - Joseph Smith's Top 20 Fulfilled Prophecies]] treats this as corroboration of [[Lucy Mack Smith]]'s account in [[Joseph Smith Predicts His Own Death Within Five Years]], while noting the obvious objection that the reveal-after-death condition allows post hoc construction.[^1]
