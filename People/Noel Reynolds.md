# Noel Reynolds

Retired BYU professor and editor of *Early Christians in Disarray: Contemporary LDS Perspectives on the Christian Apostasy*. Day quotes Reynolds's **car-wreck analogy**: doctrinal changes and schisms are the *debris* of an accident — evidence that an apostasy occurred, but not its *cause*. This framing governs Day's method of arguing from "evidences" rather than asserted causes. See [[Car Wreck Analogy for Diagnosing Apostasy]].
