# Barry Bickmore

Author of *Restoring the Ancient Church: Joseph Smith and Early Christianity*, a book Day praises for assembling **actual quotations from the early church fathers** (sourced to the *Ante-Nicene Fathers* collection) showing pre-Nicene beliefs that resemble LDS doctrine — deification, pre-existence, an embodied/plural Godhead. Day's one caveat: Bickmore's cover image implies "one perfect thing" that fragmented, whereas Day insists early Christianity was a hodgepodge from the start. See [[Greek Philosophy Corrupted the Christian Concept of God]], [[Loss of the Divine Family Is the Core Apostasy]].

Bickmore especially highlights [[Origen]] on the pre-existence of souls.
