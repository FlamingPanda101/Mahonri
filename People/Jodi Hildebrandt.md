# Jodi Hildebrandt

Jodi Hildebrandt is a Utah counselor associated (with Ruby Franke) in widely-publicized child-abuse cases that drew media and Netflix-style attention to "devious behavior" in an LDS-adjacent context. [[Kyle Beshears]] cites her as an example of a genuinely **cultic faction** that exists *within* the umbrella of Mormonism — "she was definitely building her own cult" — while insisting such factions are not representative of the LDS church as a whole. [^1]

## In Beshears's account
The concession case proving that cultic groups exist within Mormonism without the whole being a cult. See [[Mormonism Is Not a Cult]].
