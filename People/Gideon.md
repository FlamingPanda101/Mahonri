# Gideon

**Who:** Judge of Israel in the Book of Judges (chapters 6–8). He leads a small force of 300 men to victory against the Midianites, after reducing his army at God's command.

**Role:** [[Jacob Wright]] contrasts Gideon with [[Deborah]] and [[Esther]] — Gideon represents the "battlefield" model of honor and direct confrontation, while the women in biblical narrative model the indirect, diplomatic, survival-oriented approach required by a defeated people.
