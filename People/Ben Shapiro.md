# Ben Shapiro

**Era:** Contemporary (2010s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Orthodox Jewish
**Role:** Political commentator, media personality, attorney

## Bio
Ben Shapiro is an American conservative political commentator, attorney (Harvard Law), and founder/host at The Daily Wire. He is cited in [[Hayden Carroll]]'s course only for a rhetorical illustration, not for any theological expertise.

## Topics

### Argument from Authority
Carroll praises Shapiro as "really good at this" — declining to use religious arguments when speaking to an atheist ("I don't use religious arguments when I'm talking to an atheist; it means nothing to you"). Carroll uses the example to teach that a premise resting on an authority the interlocutor rejects (e.g., "the Bible says...") is an unjustified appeal — an **argument from authority** / hidden faith-based premise. See [[Validity vs Soundness - Why the Catholicism Syllogism Fails]], [[I'm Right So You're Wrong - The Hidden Presupposition Pattern]].[^1]
