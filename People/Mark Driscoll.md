# Mark Driscoll

**Era:** Contemporary (2000s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Evangelical Protestant
**Role:** Pastor, author

## Bio
Mark Driscoll is an American evangelical pastor (founder of the former Mars Hill Church; later Trinity Church, Arizona), known for forceful, combative preaching.

## Topics

### "Demonically Inspired" Charge Against the LDS
[[Hayden Carroll]] cites Driscoll as an example of the "I'm right, so you're wrong" pattern: Driscoll can only call Joseph Smith / the Latter-day Saints "demonically inspired" by first presupposing his own worldview, which Carroll argues is unjustified. The underlying syllogism — "any miracle that invalidates my worldview was caused by the devil" — is the target. See [[I'm Right So You're Wrong - The Hidden Presupposition Pattern]], [[God Can Lead People Through Other Religions]].[^1]
