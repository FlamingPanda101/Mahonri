# Braden (Gospel Guardian)

Online Latter-day Saint apologist behind **Gospel Guardian** (Instagram/TikTok/YouTube). Panelist on Ward Radio's misconstrued-scriptures episode.

## Topics covered
- **Revelation 22:18-19** — opened with this "add/take away" verse; argued Revelation predates the compiled Bible. See [[Revelation 22 - Add or Take Away Rebutted]] [^1].
- **Isaiah 43:10** — framed it via the "trial of the gods" (Isaiah 40-48). See [[Isaiah 43-10 - No God Formed Before Me Rebutted]] [^2].
- **Hebrews 1:1-2** — rebutted "Jesus the last prophet" with [[Acts 11.27|Acts 11:27]] and [[Acts 21.10|Acts 21:10]]. See [[Hebrews 1-1-2 - Jesus the Last Prophet Rebutted]] [^3].
- Articulated [[Orthopraxy over Orthodoxy]] strongly [^4].
