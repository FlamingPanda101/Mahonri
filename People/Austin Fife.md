# Austin Fife

**Role:** Latter-day Saint lay apologist and author
**Era:** Contemporary

## Bio

Austin Fife is a Latter-day Saint who lost his faith in January 2014 and spent roughly ten years reading the CES Letter, listening to Mormon Stories, and engaging ex-member and critical-Christian material before reexamining and reaffirming his belief. The fruit of that search is the **[[Light and Truth Letter - Austin Fife|Light and Truth Letter]]** (*My Search for More Light and Truth*, 2024; updated 2025), a point-by-point response to the [[CES Letter]] and other critics that emphasizes the logical fallacies and missing context he argues underlie the critical case. He writes as a private member, not as a representative of the Church. [^1]
