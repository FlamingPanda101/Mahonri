# Mark Goodacre

**Role:** New Testament scholar (Duke University); author of *The Fourth Synoptic Gospel*
**Era:** Contemporary
**Tradition:** Academic biblical studies

## Bio
Mark Goodacre is a New Testament scholar known for his work on the Synoptic Problem (notably arguments against the hypothetical source Q). [[Hugo Mendez]] cites Goodacre's forthcoming book, *The Fourth Synoptic Gospel*, as part of the recent wave of scholarship arguing that the Gospel of John knew and used the Synoptics.

## John Knew the Synoptics
Named with [[James Barker]] as a scholar "really great at... demonstrating" that John used the Synoptic Gospels; the very title *The Fourth Synoptic Gospel* presses the claim. See [[John Knew the Synoptics]] [^1].
