# Inspiring Philosophy

YouTube apologetics channel run by **Michael Jones**. Produces scripted,
source-cited videos on philosophy of religion, the existence of God,
consciousness, and biblical apologetics. Cited approvingly across the vault's
free-will and consciousness clusters; also cited critically by [[Dan McClellan]]
as an example of an apologist who misuses the Tacitus Cleitae passage (*Annals*
6.41) to argue Rome imposed censuses on client kingdoms.
