# Gregory of Nyssa (c. 335-395)

Fourth-century Cappadocian church father, one of the most important early theologians (often associated with universal restoration / *apokatastasis*).

## Creation ex nihilo as a moral claim
Cited for the point that creation from nothing is not merely metaphysical but a **moral claim about God's nature** — if God is the only uncreated being, he bears full moral responsibility for what he creates. See [[Hell - Watchmaker Wind Up and Go Rebutted]] [^1].
