# Daniel Constantino

**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Formerly Reformed Baptist / Calvinist (later left Christianity, per Carroll)
**Role:** Apologist (former)

## Bio
Daniel Constantino is a former Reformed Baptist / Calvinist apologist whom [[Hayden Carroll]] and Jacob Hansen debated (alongside Oscar Dunlap) at an Apologia-affiliated setting. Carroll reports that Constantino "is no longer a Christian because he realized how unjustified the presuppositions of Apologia Church and reformed Christianity" were.

## Topics

### Circular Self-Attestation of the Bible
Carroll plays a clip in which Constantino concedes the reasoning is admittedly **circular**: "We believe the Bible is true because the Bible says it's true... the only reason we believe it's true is because God makes us believe... it's our ultimate authority... there's no higher authority than that." Carroll uses this as a self-admission that Protestants cannot justify their standard. See [[The Three Unjustified Protestant Canon Presuppositions]], [[The Circularity Charge Against Self-Attesting Scripture]].[^1]

### Proverbs 26 Method
Carroll says he learned the apologetic application of [[Proverbs 26.4-5|Proverbs 26:4-5]] ("answer not / answer a fool") from Constantino. See [[Proverbs 26 Two-Verse Apologetic Method]].[^2]
