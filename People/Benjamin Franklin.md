# Benjamin Franklin

**Era:** 1706–1790
**Tradition:** Deist
**Role:** U.S. founding father, polymath

## Bio

Benjamin Franklin, polymath and founder, is cited in the dialogue as one of "the two least Christian" founders alongside [[Thomas Jefferson]].[^1]

## Topics

### The "Self-Evident" Edit
[[Glenn Scrivener]] identifies Franklin's change of Jefferson's "sacred and undeniable" to "**self-evident**" as the paradigm of secularization — turning a supernaturally grounded claim into an unexamined assumption, i.e. the "air we breathe." See [[Secularized Founders Used Christian Language]].[^1]
