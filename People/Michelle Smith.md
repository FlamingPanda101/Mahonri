# Michelle Smith

Canadian woman whose recovered-memory "testimony," published as *Michelle Remembers* (1980) with her psychiatrist [[Lawrence Pazder]], helped ignite the satanic panic.

## *Michelle Remembers*
Under recovered memory therapy, Smith claimed to recall childhood satanic ritual abuse — cages, sexual assault, human sacrifices, infant blood, an 81-day torture ritual, and supernatural beings. None of it was ever corroborated; she was proven to have attended school during her alleged captivity, no missing persons or police records exist, and she implicated only her deceased mother by name. See [[Michelle Remembers Sparked the Satanic Panic]] [^1][^2].

## Aftermath
Smith and Pazder divorced their spouses, married each other, and earned millions in royalties. See [[The Satanic Panic Was Driven by Financial and Reputational Incentives]] [^3].
