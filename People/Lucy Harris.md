# Lucy Harris

Wife of [[Martin Harris]], counted (with her daughter) among the natural [[Informal Witnesses of the Golden Plates|informal witnesses]].

## Encounter with the plates
After being shown the plates in a dream, Lucy and her daughter were handed the wooden box containing them. Martin reported that his daughter said they "were about as much as she could lift," and his wife said they were "very heavy" [^1]. The episode lists her among the incidental, unstaged witnesses; see [[Natural vs Supernatural Witnesses]].
