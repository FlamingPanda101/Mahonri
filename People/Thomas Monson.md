# Thomas Monson

Sixteenth President of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (2008–2018). In Ward Radio Part 5 he appears only as one name in the roster of LDS leaders the We Are the People Utah podcast's "Of Pedophiles and Prophets" segments accuse, without evidence, of involvement in satanic ritual abuse — alongside [[Russell M. Nelson]], [[Ezra Taft Benson]], [[Gordon B. Hinckley]], and [[Neil A. Maxwell]].

## Named in the SRA conspiracy roster
His inclusion illustrates the indiscriminate scope of the accusations ("hundreds of other leaders") that the episode argues have "no evidence, no motive, and no shame." See [[Accusations Cannot Transmute Into Evidence]]. [^1]
