# Johann Christoph Döderlein

**Who:** German Protestant theologian and biblical scholar (1745–1792). Early contributor to the critical study of Isaiah's composition.

**Role in biblical studies:** Along with [[Johann Benjamin Koppe]] and [[Johann Gottfried Eichhorn]], Döderlein was among the late-18th-century scholars who began identifying passages in Isaiah that appeared to come from a later period, laying groundwork for the [[Deutero-Isaiah Theory]].
