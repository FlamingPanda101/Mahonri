# John MacArthur (1939-2025)

Prominent American Reformed/Calvinist pastor and author, a leading popular voice for *sola fide* and "lordship salvation."

## James 2 — "if a man says"
MacArthur argues the governing phrase in James 2:14 is "if a man says" — i.e. a mere *claim* of faith. He also holds that good works inevitably *follow* (not earn) salvation. Trent Horn rebuts both and presses the assurance problem. See [[James - Different Kind of Faith Reply Rebutted]] and [[Assurance of Salvation - Catholic vs Protestant]] [^1].
