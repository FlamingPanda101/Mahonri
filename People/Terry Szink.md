# Terrence L. Szink

**Era:** 20th–21st century
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Scholar of the ancient Near East

## Bio
Terry Szink is a Latter-day Saint scholar who has published on the Book of Mormon onomasticon, working in the lineage of [[Hugh Nibley]] and [[Paul Hoskisson]].[^1]

## The Name Alma at Ebla
[[Matthew Bowen]] credits Szink with the specific follow-up work examining **attestations of Alma at Ebla** (the third-millennium-BC archive in Syria), which helped establish *Alma* as a genuine ancient male Semitic name.[^1] See [[Alma Means Young Man - Wordplay on Hiddenness]] and [[Attestation Versus Wordplay - Two Levels of Evidence]].
