# Matthew Roper

**Era:** Contemporary (1990s–2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Researcher on Book of Mormon studies

## Bio
Matt Roper is a Latter-day Saint researcher long associated with FARMS and BYU's research projects, known for empirical studies of Book of Mormon names, historicity, and authorship claims.[^1]

## On the Nephi "Fair Ones" Wordplay
[[Matthew Bowen]] notes that Roper was "really convinced" by Bowen's argument that the recurring "Oh ye fair ones" lament plays on **Nephi/nfr** meaning "good, fair, beautiful" — the Nephites seeing themselves as "the good/fair ones."[^1] See [[Nephi Means Good and Fair - The Goodliness Inclusio]].
