# Dr. Ryan Burge

American political scientist specializing in the **quantitative study of religion and religious demography**, widely cited for his work on the rise of the religiously unaffiliated ("the nones") and for translating survey data into accessible public analysis. [[Justin Dyer]] describes him as "one of the most important scholars in this area."[^2]

## Spiritual Seismology dataset
Burge collected the "spiritual seismology" survey of **over 100,000 respondents**, the large-sample dataset Dyer's team mined to identify the four distinct groups of Latter-day Saints who leave the Church. See [[Four Distinct Groups of Those Who Leave the LDS Church]].[^1]

## Politics as a disaffiliation predictor
Dyer cites Burge's finding that **one of the best indicators of disconnecting from faith is one's politics**, especially when political identity is taken to an extreme. See [[Politics Predicts Religious Disaffiliation]].[^2]
