# Andrew Huberman

Andrew Huberman is an American neuroscientist, associate professor of neurobiology and ophthalmology at Stanford University, and host of the popular *Huberman Lab* podcast. He is cited by [[Ether's Elephant]] from a conversation with psychologist [[David DeSteno]] in which Huberman affirmed his belief in God and argued that "all the elements of science are entirely compatible with the idea of there being a God." Ether's Elephant uses these statements as the spine of a video reframing belief in God as compatible with — even an application of — the scientific method.

In the cited clip Huberman states he has believed in God "since I was a kid," holds that a "really rational" person looking at the data finds "nothing to refute" God, and characterizes a felt "welling up of neural activity, chemical activity" as a spiritual experience — a framing Ether's Elephant aligns with the Latter-day Saint emphasis on personal revelation.
