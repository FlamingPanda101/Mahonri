# Informed Saints

A faithful Latter-day Saint podcast and YouTube channel whose motto is "study deeply and believe boldly." It interviews scholars and discusses doctrine, data, and apologetics for an LDS audience.

## Why People Are Leaving The Church
Hosted a long-form interview with BYU social scientist Dr. [[Justin Dyer]] examining his peer-reviewed paper on LDS retention, well-being, and the demographic landscape of disaffiliation. See [[Informed Saints - The Surprising REAL Reason People Are Leaving The Church]] and concepts such as [[Church Is Hemorrhaging Members at a Unique Rate]] and [[The Four Groups of LDS Disaffiliates]].
