# Archelaus of Cappadocia

Client king of Cappadocia under Rome. Not to be confused with [[Herod Archelaus]] of Judea. The Cleitae tribe rebelled against his census, which was conducted "in the Roman fashion" (Tacitus, *Annals* 6.41). Apologists mistakenly cite this passage as evidence of Rome imposing censuses on client kingdoms.
