# Henry Jacobs

**Role:** Early Latter-day Saint; legal husband of [[Zina Huntington Jacobs]]
**Era:** 1817–1886
**Tradition:** The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

## Bio
Henry Bailey Jacobs was married to [[Zina Huntington Jacobs]] and living with her in Nauvoo when she was sealed to [[Joseph Smith]] in October 1841. Their son **Zebulon Jacobs** (born January 2, 1842) was conceived before the sealing; DNA testing of descendants confirms Zebulon was Henry's biological child, not Joseph's — a key proof in [[Don Bradley]]'s case that the early Nauvoo "polyandrous" sealings were not sexual. Henry remained an active member; [[Brian Hales]] notes there are no recorded complaints from the legal husbands in these sealings. See [[Joseph Smith Married Pregnant Women Refuting the Sexual-Motive Thesis]].
