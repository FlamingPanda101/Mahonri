# Steven Pynakker

Evangelical host and creator of the **Mormon Book Reviews** YouTube channel, whose tagline is "where an evangelical encounters the restoration." Raised in Northwest Indiana (the same region as [[Kyle Beshears]]), he is committed to interfaith dialogue with the Latter-day Saint and broader restoration traditions, including off-camera friendships with figures like [[Jacob Hansen]]. He describes himself as "a fan of the Book of Mormon," fascinated by both its material/plates history and its narrative and literary dimensions. He champions a two-way, golden-rule mode of engagement over debate and contention. [^1][^2]

## On dialogue and the Book of Mormon
He pressed Beshears on the "could God vs would God" framing and on the believer's inability to summarily dismiss the Book of Mormon. See [[Could God vs Would God on the Book of Mormon]], [[Would God Not Could God]].

## On restoration origins
He raised the [[Alvin Smith]] funeral story and the broader 19th-century reprobation context as a key to Mormonism's origins. See [[The Alvin Smith Funeral Story Is Misremembered]].
