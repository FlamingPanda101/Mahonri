# Jeremy Runnells

Jeremy Runnells is the ex-Mormon author of *Letter to a CES Director* (the "CES Letter"), one of the most widely circulated lay critiques of the LDS Church. The document compiles objections to Latter-day Saint truth claims, including a section on the [[Book of Mormon]]'s alleged dependence on [[Ethan Smith]]'s *View of the Hebrews*.[^1]

## The CES Letter's View of the Hebrews Argument

Runnells reproduces a dual-column parallel list drawn from [[B.H. Roberts]]'s unpublished study. Smoot critiques this on two grounds: (1) the list provides no textual analysis establishing the legitimacy of any parallel, so it imposes no burden of refutation (see [[A Parallel List Without Textual Analysis Carries No Burden]]); and (2) on the "Ethan/Ether" item, Runnells quotes Roberts's name-comparison but omits Roberts's very next sentence repudiating it as "not a serious suggestion" (see [[The Ethan and Ether Parallel Roberts Rejected]]). Smoot also notes Runnells faulted FAIR for not debunking "every single parallel" despite Runnells himself doing nothing to establish a single one.[^2]
