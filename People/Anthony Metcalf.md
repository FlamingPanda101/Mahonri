# Anthony Metcalf

A disaffected nineteenth-century Latter-day Saint whose 1880 book reported an interview with Book of Mormon witness [[Martin Harris]]. The [[Jeremy Runnells|CES Letter]] draws on Metcalf for the claim that Harris saw the plates only in a "visionary or entranced state," and presents two excerpts as though they were independent quotations even though they come from the **same paragraph**.[^1]

[[Keystone]] flags Metcalf's reliability problems: he dated the interview to the winter of 1875–76, which "would have been an interview with a corpse" since Harris had died, so the real interview was likely 1873 when Harris was about 90; and the word "entrancement" appears in no other source, making it a probable embellishment on Metcalf's part.[^1]
