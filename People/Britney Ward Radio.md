# Britney (the Shadow)

Latter-day Saint panelist on Ward Radio's misconstrued-scriptures episode.

## Top 9 Misconstrued Scriptures
Pressed the panel on *why* the Trinity remains such a sticking point and made the point that members "need another book" (the Book of Mormon) to clarify what readers of the same Bible understand so differently [^1]. Noted that Latter-day Saints do **not** deny others the label "Christian" — the accusation runs only one direction [^2].
