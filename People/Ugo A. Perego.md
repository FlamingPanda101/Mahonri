# Ugo A. Perego

**Role:** Co-editor of *The Restored Gospel of Jesus Christ and Evolution* (BYU, 2025) and a contributing author on reconciling evolution with religion.

## Bio
Ugo A. Perego is one of the four editors of *The Restored Gospel of Jesus Christ and Evolution*, a BYU College of Life Sciences volume making the in-faith case that accepting organic evolution is compatible with the restored gospel.[^1] Within the volume he contributes to the section offering "perspectives on what and how Latter-day Saints (and others) have sought ways to reconcile evolution with religion."[^2] As an editor he affirms the book's central commitments — purposeful divine creation, the principle that [[Nothing Has Been Revealed Concerning Evolution|"nothing has been revealed concerning evolution"]], and the pursuit of truth [[Learning by Study and Also by Faith|by study and also by faith]].[^3]
