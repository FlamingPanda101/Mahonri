# John Calvin (1509-1564)

French Reformer, founder of the Reformed (Calvinist) tradition.

## Two meanings of "justify"
Calvin's reconciliation of Paul and James: Paul means the "gratuitous imputation of righteousness before the tribunal of God," while James means "the manifestation of righteousness... before men." Trent Horn rebuts this "justification before men" reading. See [[James - Justification Before Men Reply Rebutted]] [^1].

## Predestination and God's glory
Quoted that God "pre-ordained for his own glory... a part of the human race... to eternal salvation, and... to eternal damnation" — the basis of the "for God's glory" defense of hell, rebutted in [[Hell - For Gods Glory Defense Rebutted]] [^2].
