# Steven Prothero

Non-LDS scholar of American religion (Boston University), author of *Religious Literacy* and *American Jesus*, among others. Cited by the Ward Radio panel as an outside academic voice on the First Vision controversy.

## On First Vision Discrepancies
Prothero judges that critics "have delighted in the discrepancies" between the canonical First Vision account and earlier renditions (notably the 1832 one in Smith's own hand), but that "such complaints, however, are much ado about relatively nothing. Any good lawyer or historian would expect to find contradictions in competing narratives written down years apart" — and that despite the differences, key elements abide (Jesus appears, Smith receives revelation, he is told to remain aloof from existing denominations). See [[First Vision Accounts Are No More Contradictory Than the Gospels]].[^1]
