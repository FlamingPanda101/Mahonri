# Bruce Porter

A Latter-day Saint authority on the Pearl of Great Price whom [[Jonah Barnes]] repeatedly invokes as a corroborating expert on the volume's textual and publication history. On [[Ward Radio - Pearl of Great Price Explained|Ward Radio]], Barnes notes that Bruce Porter quoted [[Hugh Nibley]]'s line that "the Pearl of Great Price is the book that answers all of the questions," and cites Porter as someone who would "corroborate a lot of what I'm saying" about the origins of the [[Book of Moses]] — including the point that no one is certain who first applied the title "Book of Moses" to the 1830 revelations.[^1]

This is a passing attribution within an apologetics podcast; the note exists only to resolve the wikilink. (Not to be confused with [[James P. Porter]] in the vault.)
