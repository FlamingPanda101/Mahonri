# Angel Moroni

In LDS belief, the angel who appeared to [[Joseph Smith]] and directed him to the golden plates from which the Book of Mormon was translated. As a heavenly messenger delivering scripture, Moroni is the figure critics have in mind when they apply [[Galatians 1.8|Galatians 1:8]] ("though... an angel from heaven, preach any other gospel").

## Galatians 1:8 objection
The argument that Moroni's visitation is the "accursed angel" of Galatians 1:8 is rebutted in [[Galatians 1-8 - Angel Preaching Another Gospel]]: angels are God's ordinary messengers throughout scripture, and Paul's target is Judaizing legalism, not angelic revelation.
