# Larry Murf

Host of the YouTube channel *Mormonism with the Murf*, whose tagline is that it "explores church history and the church's truth claims." A believing Latter-day Saint based in England (he mentions visiting friends in Northern Ireland and getting home "to England"), he produces apologetics and church-history content.[^1]

## On Evaluating Joseph Smith's Prophecies
In his episode on [[Joseph Smith]]'s top 20 fulfilled prophecies, the Murf adopts an unusually self-critical stance for an apologist, repeatedly putting on his "skeptic hat." He articulates [[The Charitable-but-Critical Believer Posture]] — refusing both to assume the early Saints fabricated accounts and to accept dramatic claims uncritically.[^2] He foregrounds [[The Late-Reminiscence Problem in Joseph Smith Prophecy Claims]] as the master caveat over nearly the entire list, and distinguishes an [[Apologetic vs Evidence Distinction for Prophecy|apologetic device from genuine evidence]].[^3] He draws his material from Brian Stutzman's book *A Timeline of Joseph Smith's Prophecies* and a Jeff Roundy video on *Larry Saints Q&A*.[^4]
