# Dr. David Rubin

Duke University cognitive psychologist specializing in **autobiographical and flashbulb memory**.

## The 9/11 flashbulb-memory study
The day after the towers fell, Rubin gathered subjects and had them write in detail everything they remembered about that day; a year later he had the same subjects do it again. The later accounts "remembered things they had not witnessed" and contradicted the originals — demonstrating that even the most vivid "flashbulb" memories are reconstructed, not recorded. See [[Flashbulb Memories Are Reconstructed Not Recorded]] [^1].
