# Britney (Ward Radio)

Referred to as "The Shadow" and "backed by popular demand." Panelist on Ward Radio
and married to host Cardon Ellis. Provides supporting apologetic points and
interpersonal dynamic as the lone female voice on the panel.

## Notable Contributions
- "Jesus can be salty and I love it" — on Jesus's comeback to the Samaritan woman
  (John 4).
- [[John 4-24 - God is a Spirit]] — We have both spirit and body; same is true of God.
