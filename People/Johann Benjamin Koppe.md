# Johann Benjamin Koppe

**Who:** German Protestant theologian and biblical scholar (1750–1791). One of the earliest scholars to identify passages in the book of Isaiah that appeared to come from a period later than the 8th-century prophet.

**Role in biblical studies:** Along with [[Johann Christoph Döderlein]] and [[Johann Gottfried Eichhorn]], Koppe was among the late-18th-century scholars who laid the groundwork for the [[Deutero-Isaiah Theory]] by questioning the unity of Isaiah's authorship on textual and historical grounds.
