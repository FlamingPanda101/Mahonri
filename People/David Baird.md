# David Baird

**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Role:** Metallurgist

## Bio

David Baird is a metallurgist who constructed a replica set of "gold plates,"
trying to be as authentic as possible. Because real gold and metals were
unaffordable, his replica is made of brass.[^1] He was interviewed by the
Interpreter Foundation for their *Witnesses* documentary project.

## Difficulty of Fabrication

Baird's firsthand testimony is used to show how hard the task is: he stated that
making pages thin and consistent, cutting and shaping them, and engraving/etching
them "would have been extremely difficult," that it would have taken
[[Joseph Smith]] "quite a while," and that there is no evidence Smith had the
required skills.[^2] See [[Joseph Smith Lacked the Metallurgical Skill and Time]]
and [[Gold Plates Fabrication Was Prohibitively Expensive]].
