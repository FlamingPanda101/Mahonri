# Cardon Ellis

Host of **Ward Radio**, a Latter-day Saint commentary and apologetics podcast/YouTube channel. Hosts panels of LDS creators and apologists discussing doctrine, culture, and responses to criticism.

## Top 9 Misconstrued Scriptures
Hosted the panel walking through nine commonly weaponized verses, framing each discussion and reading the texts. Tied the recurring theme of [[Orthopraxy over Orthodoxy]] to the Good Samaritan — the priest had right belief but failed at right action [^1].

## Jesus Referenced the Apocrypha
Hosted [[Jonah (Plain and Precious Publishing)|Jonah]] on how Jesus's "blood of Abel to Zechariah" ([[Luke 11.47-51|Luke 11:47-51]]) draws on apocryphal blood traditions. See [[Jesus Referenced the Apocrypha]] [^2].

## Reacting to Anti-Mormon Books
Hosted [[Baylor Johnson (Ward Radio)|Baylor Johnson]] and [[Michael Myrrono (OrthoCardia)|Michael Myrrono]] reacting to a stack of anti-Mormon books, driving the on-air critique of [[Mark J. Cares]]' Truth and Love Ministry "Five Pillars" and the "rebranding" charge. See [[Ward Radio - We Bought Every Anti-Mormon Book and React]].

## Book of Abraham "Nightmare" Debunked Again
Hosted [[Jonah Barnes]] in a line-by-line reaction to an evangelical video calling the Book of Abraham "the biggest apologetic nightmare for Mormons," surfacing the **provenance-vs-content double standard** and the post-1842 apocrypha parallels. See [[The Book of Abraham Debate Plays a Provenance vs Content Double Standard]], [[Ancient Egyptian Is Not Easily Translatable]], [[Lion-Couch Legs Apart Signal a Living Exalted Figure Not a Forgery]], from [[Ward Radio - Biggest Apologetic Nightmare Book of Abraham Debunked Again]].

## Who Are the Giants in the Bible?
Hosted [[Jonah Barnes]] on the identity of the Genesis 6 Nephilim, drawing out Barnes's reconstruction (apostate Sethite priesthood-holders, not supernatural giants; the sin as sterilization/abortion). See [[The Nephilim Were Apostate Sethite Priesthood-Holders Not Supernatural Giants]] and [[Ward Radio - Who Are the Giants in the Bible (Jonah Barnes)]].

## Was Alma a High Priest of Heavenly Mother?
Hosted [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|David Butler]] arguing that Alma the Elder baptized by the authority of El Shaddai (the divine Mother) at the Waters of Mormon. See [[Alma Baptizes by the Authority of El Shaddai at the Waters of Mormon]] and [[Ward Radio - Was Alma a High Priest of Heavenly Mother]].

## Responding to Cliffe Knechtle, Cain and Abel, Lucifer's Fall, Ehrman, the Apostasy, and the Great Replacement
Hosted a cluster of further episodes: reacting to evangelical [[Cliff Knechtel|Cliffe Knechtle]]'s "Jesus and Lucifer are brothers" comments on the PBD podcast (see [[Moses 1 Shows Satan Falsely Claims to Be the Only Begotten]], [[The Layman Groups Latter-day Saints as Christian]]); two [[Jonah Barnes]] interviews on his book *The Lost Gems of Genesis* — how Cain killed Abel (see [[The Apocrypha Reveals How Cain Killed Abel by the Throat]], [[Cain Weaponizes Empathy to Lure Abel - The First Crybully]]) and why Lucifer fell (see [[Abraham 3 Is the Selection of Adam Not the Redeemer]]); a [[Jonah Barnes]] / [[Dave Butler]] episode on Bart Ehrman and the canon (see [[Ehrman Is Right That the Canon Lacks the Suffering Messiah]]); a [[Jacob Hansen]] episode on the Great Apostasy (see [[The Creeds Won by State Power Not Persuasion]]); and a panel on [[Trent Horn]]'s "substitutional patriarchy" (see [[Substitutional Patriarchy - The Government Replaces the Husband]]).
