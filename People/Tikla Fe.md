# Tikla Fe

Tikla Fe is a contributor to **Ward Radio Women** and a guest panelist on [[Hayden Holm]]'s **Salty Saints** show. In the [[Salty Saints - Why Gay Temple Marriage Will Never Happen|matrimony episode]] she advances the central argument that "matrimony" derives from *mater* (mother) and therefore names a "holy order of making a mother," which she uses to argue that the temple sealing is intrinsically oriented toward motherhood and cannot accommodate a same-sex union. She frames her approach as deliberately unemotional and doctrinal, and prefaces it with a plea (drawn from her late aunt, a lesbian-community leader in Portland) to "meet people where they are" rather than vilify those who try to make others feel welcome.[^1]
