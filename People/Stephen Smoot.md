# Stephen Smoot

**Full Name:** Stephen O. Smoot
**Era:** Contemporary (2020s)
**Tradition:** Latter-day Saint (LDS)
**Role:** Scholar, apologist, lecturer

## Bio

Stephen Smoot is an LDS scholar and apologist who participates in the Stick of
Judah lecture series hosted by [[David Butler (Stick of Joseph)|David Butler]].
He holds academic training in ancient Near Eastern studies and has presented on
topics including the Book of Abraham and ancient astronomy. The video mentions
an upcoming lecture by Smoot titled "Abraham the Astronomer."[^1]
