# Harrison Burgess

An early convert counted among the supernatural [[Informal Witnesses of the Golden Plates|informal witnesses]] of the golden plates.

## Vision of the plates
After being asked whether the Book of Mormon was true, Burgess was "tormented in the spirit," went to the woods to pray, and reported: "The vision of my mind was opened, and a glorious personage clothed in white stood before me and exhibited to my view the plates from which the Book of Mormon was taken" [^1]. His account is one of the supernatural informal testimonies the episode uses against the [[Metallurgical Fraud Theory Rebutted|metallurgical fraud theory]].
