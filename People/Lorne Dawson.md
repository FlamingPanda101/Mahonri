# Dr. Lorne Dawson

Canadian sociologist of **new religious movements** and **radicalization** (emeritus, University of Waterloo), prominent in the foundational 1980s-2000s period of NRM scholarship; later worked on terrorist radicalization after 9/11. Author of academic books on the sociology of NRMs.

> Note: Ward Radio's auto-captions render his name "Lauren Dawson"; it is **Lorne Dawson**.

## Cult terminology
Explains that "cult" was a neutral scholarly term (ritual focus) until mid-20th-c. evangelicals weaponized it against the LDS and others; scholars now say "new religious movement." See [[Cult - A Contaminated Pejorative Term]] [^1].

## Brainwashing
Marshals the empirical and legal record that **brainwashing does not exist** — the declassified CIA POW study, two large independent studies, and US v. Fishman. See [[Brainwashing Does Not Exist]] [^2].

## Where Mormons rank
Places the Church on the church/sect continuum and stresses the categories are relative to time and place. See [[Where Mormons Rank on the Church-Sect Continuum]] [^3].
